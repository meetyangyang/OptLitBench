An algorithm to solve the generalized linear complementarity problem with a vertical block z-matrix
 Aniekan   Ebiefung ;   M.M.   Kostreva ;   V.   Ramanujam ;
Abstract
 Chandrasekaran's algorithm for solving the linear complementarity problem with a Z-matrix is extended to solve the Generalized Linear Complementarity Problem (GLCP) when the is a vertical block Z-matrix of type (m 1,…,m n). The extended scheme solves the GLCP in at most n cycles by either finding a solution or declaring that none exists. Numerical examples are given to demonstrate the effectiveness of the algorithm  
Keywords
Algorithm, Generalized LCP, Z-Matrices
