Optimal flight trajectories for the validation of aerodynamic models
 I.   Gherman ; V.   Schulz ; J.   T.   Betts ;
Abstract
 For the validation of aerodynamic models, certain flight trajectories have to be chosen. In the current paper, the methodology of optimum experimental design is employed for the solution of this task. Aerodynamic models are highly nonlinear and so far no optimum experimental design approaches have been applied to them. We show that the methods of optimum experimental design are efficiently applicable to such models and considerably improve the statistical accuracy of the parameter estimates. 
Keywords
Optimum experimental design, Optimal control, Trajectory optimization
