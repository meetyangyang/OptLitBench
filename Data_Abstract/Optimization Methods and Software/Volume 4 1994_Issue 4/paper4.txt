The global convergence of the ABS methods for a class of nonlinear problems
 Aurél   Galántai ;
Abstract
 We prove the monotone convergence of a wide subclass of the nonlinear ABS methods. The convergence conditions are essentially those of the Newton-Baluev theorem [10,11]. Two members of the ABS class are shown to be at least as fast as Newton's method in the partial ordering. 
Keywords
Monotone convergence,  ABS method, nonlinear equations
