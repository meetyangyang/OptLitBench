On CG algorithms as objects of scientific study: An appendix
 J. L.   Nazareth ;
Abstract
 In this short note, a numerical illustration is provided that brings a study of nonlinear conjugate gradient algorithms initiated by Nazareth [J.L. Nazareth (2003). On conjugate gradient algorithms as objects of scientific study. Optimization Methods and Software, 18, 555–565; J.L. Nazareth (2003). Differentiable Optimization and Equation Solving: A Treatise on Algorithmic Science and the Karmarkar Revolution. Springer-Verlag, New York.] to a conclusion.  
Keywords
Conjugate gradients, Nonlinear optimization
