A framework for interior methods of linear programming ∗  
   J.L.   Nazareth ;
Abstract
 An approach to interior methods for linear programming premised on the optimality condition and two fundamental optimization principles (homotopy and symmetry) is described. It provides a concise, yet general introduction to the new area of optimization research germinated by Karmarkar, and a useful alternative to the current logarithmic Barrier-based perspective on the subject
