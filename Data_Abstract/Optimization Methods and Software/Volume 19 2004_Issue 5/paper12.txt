A nonlinear complementarity approach to multiuser power control for digital subscriber lines
 Nobuo   Yamashita *  ; Zhi-Quan   Luo ‡  ;
Abstract
 In this article, we formulate the problem of multiuser power control for digital subscriber lines (DSL) as a nonlinear complementarity problem (NCP). We study conditions under which the resulting NCP belongs to the class P 0 and its solution is B-regular. The NCP formulation makes it possible to use the Newton-type smoothing methods (SMs) to efficiently compute a Nash equilibrium solution. In our computer simulations, the SM appears much more robust to the presence of strong interference than the existing synchronous water-filling method. We also present an extension of the NCP formulation, which can lead to substantial increase in the rate sum performance of the DSL system.  
Keywords
Multiuser power control, Digital subscriber lines, Nash equilibrium, Nonlinear complementarity problem, P0-function, B-regular
