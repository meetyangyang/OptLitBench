Solving nonconvex SDP problems of structural optimization with stability control
 Michal   Kočvara ; Michael   Stingl ;
Abstract
 The goal of this article is to formulate and solve structural optimization problems with constraints on the global stability of the structure. The stability constraint is based on the linear buckling phenomenon. We formulate the problem as a nonconvex semidefinite programming problem and introduce an algorithm based on the augmented Lagrangian method combined with the trust-region technique. The algorithm is implemented in a code PENNON. The article is concluded by a series of numerical examples. 
Keywords
Structural optimization, Buckling, Stability control, Nonconvex semidefinite programming
