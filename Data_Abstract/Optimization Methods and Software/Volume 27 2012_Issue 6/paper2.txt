A damped semismooth Newton iterative method for solving mixed linear complementarity problems
 Lei   Wu ; Zhe   Sun ; Jinping   Zeng ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we propose a damped semismooth Newton iterative method for mixed linear complementarity problems. Under appropriate conditions, we prove that the method exhibits monotone and superlinear convergence properties. 
Keywords
complementarity problem, non-smooth equation, semismooth Newton iterative method, monotone convergence MR(2000)Subject Classifications : 90C33, 65H10, 90C53
