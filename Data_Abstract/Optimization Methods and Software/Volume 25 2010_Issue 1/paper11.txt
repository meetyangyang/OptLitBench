Second-order analysis of the Moreau–Yosida and the Lasry–Lions regularizations
 N.   Ovcharova ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we investigate the second-order differentiability properties of the Moreau–Yosida and the Lasry–Lions regularization of a locally Lipschitz continuous function defined on . We give a relationship between the second-order properties of the regularized function and the approximated function itself. These results are useful for convergence analysis of approximate numerical methods for solving non-smooth optimization problems.
