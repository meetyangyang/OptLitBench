On the amount of minimal pairs of convex sets
 Jerzy   Grzybowski ; Diethard   Pallaschke ; Ryszard   Urbański ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we determine the cardinality of various subsets of the family of all minimal pairs of compact convex subsets of ℝ n  (Theorems 2.1 and 2.8). Furthermore, we express minimality properties of a pair of closed bounded convex sets in set–theoretical terms (Theorem 3.3). 
Keywords
convex analysis, minimal pairs of compact convex sets AMS Subject Classification : 52A07, 26A27
