Mordukhovich subdifferential of pointwise minimum of approximate convex functions
 Vera   Roshchina ;
Abstract
 We prove an inclusion formula for the Mordukhovich subdifferential of a pointwise minimum of approximate convex functions in finite-dimensional setting and show that this result provides a much sharper estimate than the existing general one. 
Keywords
approximate convex functions, subdifferential of minimum function, Mordukhovich subdifferential, calculus of subdifferentials AMS Subject Classification : 46N10, 65K10
