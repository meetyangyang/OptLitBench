An optimization-based approach for data classification
 St.   Busygin ; O.   A.   Prokopyev ; P.   M.   Pardalos ;
Abstract
 In this paper we discuss an optimization-based approach to handling data classification problems. The main idea of the proposed method is to reformulate the classification problem as a mathematical programming problem, whose solution implies relevant feature selection and forms the classes of data. Encouraging computational results on two DNA microarray data mining problems are reported. 
Keywords
Classification, Gene expression data, Optimization approach
