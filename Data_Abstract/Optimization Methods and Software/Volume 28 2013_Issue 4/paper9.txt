Trust region methods for solving multiobjective optimisation
 Shaojian   Qu ; Mark   Goh ; Bing   Liang ;
Abstract
 This paper first proposes a trust region algorithm to obtain a stationary point of unconstrained multiobjective optimisation problem. Under suitable assumptions, the global convergence of the new algorithm is established. We then extend the trust region method to solve the non-smooth multiobjective optimisation problem.
