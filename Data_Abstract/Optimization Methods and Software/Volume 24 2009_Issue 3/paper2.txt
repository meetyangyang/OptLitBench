Solving generalized Nash equilibrium problem with equality and inequality constraints
 Qing   Xu ; Xi   Dai ; Bo   Yu ;
Abstract
 In this paper, a homotopy mapping for a generalized Nash equilibrium problem (GNEP) with equality and inequality constraints is constructed. From proving the existence of a homotopy path, a globally convergent homotopy method is developed to find a solution to the GNEP. Several numerical examples are presented. 
Keywords
generalized Nash game, homotopy method, global convergence AMS Subject Classification : 90C30, 91A10, 91B50
