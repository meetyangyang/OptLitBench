Variationally derived ABS methods
   N.Y.   Deng ;   Y.   Xiao ;   F.J.   Zhou ;
Abstract
 This paper studies the minimum norm correction methods for solving a linear system of equations which can be viewed as an extension to [3]. The minimum norm class of algorithms and its properties are studied. Furthermore, the equivalence between the minimum norm class and the ABS algorithm class[4] is proved. At last, numerical stability of a minimum norm algorithm and numerical experiments are reported. 
Keywords
Linear systems, minimum norm corrections, ABS algorithms, numerical stability
