The local convergence property of the nonlinear Voevodin-ABS algorithm
 Naiyang   Deng ; Meifang   Zhu ;
Abstract
 We consider a subclass of the nonlinear ABS algorithm for solving nonlinear systems of equations corresponding to a generalization of the Voevodin method for linear systems which includes all conjugate gradient type methods. A local Q-order convergence theorem is obtained under only the standard conditions on the mapping, without additional conditions on the parameters defining the class.  
Keywords
Nonlinear ABS algorithm, Voevodin method, local convergence, Q-order convergence, conjugate gradient type methods
