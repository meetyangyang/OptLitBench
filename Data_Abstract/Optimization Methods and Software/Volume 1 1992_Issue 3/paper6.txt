An orthogonally scaled ABS method for nonlinear least squares problems
 Emilio   Spedicato ; Zhijian   Huang ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we propose an approximate implicit QR method for solving nonlinear least squares problems. Under some standard assumptions, we prove that the sequence {xk} generated by the new method is linearly convergent for small residual problems and is quadratically convergent for zero-residual problems.  
Keywords
ABS methods, orthogonally scaled ABS method, Implicit QR algorithm, Nonlinear least squares problems, Linear convergence, Quadratic convergence
