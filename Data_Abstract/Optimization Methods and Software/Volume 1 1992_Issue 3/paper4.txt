Further results on the local convergence of the nonlinear ABS algorithms
 Naiyang   Deng ; Meifang   Zhu ;
Abstract
 In this paper we consider the conditions on the parameters of the class of nonlinear ABS algorithms  which guarantee super linear convergence. While previous results assumed that the angle between the search direction and the normal to the to the hyperspace of all solutions of the current linearized equation was uniformly away from л/2 , we show now that it can tend to л/2 but not too fast.  
Keywords
Nonlinear ABS algorithm, Q-order convergence
