Antipodal pairs, critical pairs, and Nash angular equilibria in convex cones
 Alfredo   Iusem ; Alberto   Seeger ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we discuss three related geometric concepts that arise in connection with the angular analysis of a convex cone K⊂ℝ d . Although antipodal pairs and critical pairs in convex cones have been studied in our earlier works, several additional results are presented here for the first time. The concept of Nash angular equilibrium is new, and we set up the basic ingredients of the theory. 
Keywords
antipodal pairs, convex cones, maximal angle, critical angle, Nash angular equilibria, polyhedral cones
