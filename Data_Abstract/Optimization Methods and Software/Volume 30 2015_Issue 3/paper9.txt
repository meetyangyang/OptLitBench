Characterizing ϵ-properly efficient solutions
K. Khaledian;E. Khorram;B. Karimi;
Abstract
In this paper, new necessary and sufficient conditions for characterizing ϵ-properly efficient solutions and new necessary conditions for characterizing ϵ-(weakly) efficient solutions of multiple objective optimization problems are presented. Note that all of the provided results are established without any convexity and boundedness assumptions on the problem under consideration.
Keywords
multiple objective optimization, ϵ-(proper, weak) efficiency, direction scalarization
