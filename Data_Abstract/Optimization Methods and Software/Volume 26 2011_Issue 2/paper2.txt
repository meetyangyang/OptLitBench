A damped semismooth Newton method for mixed linear complementarity problems
 Zhe   Sun ; Jinping   Zeng ;
Abstract
 The mixed linear complementarity problem is reformulated as a non-smooth equation by the use of the median operator. On the basis of slant differentiability, a damped semismooth Newton method for solving the non-smooth equation is presented. Both monotone and finite termination properties of the proposed method are proved. 
Keywords
non-smooth equation, semismooth Newton method, complementarity problem, monotone convergence AMS Subject Classification : 90C33, 65H10, 90C53
