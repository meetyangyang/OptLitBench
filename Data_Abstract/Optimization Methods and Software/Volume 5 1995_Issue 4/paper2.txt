On the convergence behavior of the truncated GMRES method for nonlinear equations
 Marco   Gaviano ; Huang   Zhijian ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we discuss the convergence behavior of truncated GMRES method for systems of nonlinear equations. A local convergence theorem with three special schemes is proved. We present two examples to show the effect of the truncated GMRES method. Finally, we give an efficiency analysis that allows to choose the algorithm parameters optimally  
Keywords
Newton's Method, GMRES Method, Inexact Newton's Methods, Truncated GMRES Methods, Local convergence, Efficiency
