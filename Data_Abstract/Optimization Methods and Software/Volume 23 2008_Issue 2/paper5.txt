On the global convergence of a nonmonotone proximal bundle method for convex nonsmooth minimization
 Liusheng   Hou ; Wenyu   Sun ;
Abstract
 In this paper, an algorithm for convex nonsmooth minimization that employs both proximal bundle method and nonmonotone technique is proposed. The global convergence of the proposed algorithm is established. 
Keywords
convex programming, nonsmooth optimization, proximal bundle methods, nonmonotone algorithm AMS Subject Classifications : 65K05, 90C30
