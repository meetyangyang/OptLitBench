An estimate of solution setperturabations for a system of linear inequalities
   A.V.   Lotov ;
Abstract
 Hausdorff distance between solution sets of original and perturbed linear inequalities systems in real partially ordered Banach space is estimated. The estimation receives a particular simple expression for finite dimensional case. The concept of condition number for solution set of a linear inequalities system is suggested. Explicit expressions of Lipschitz constants are given  
Keywords
Linear Inequalities, Solution Set, Perturbations, Hausdorff Distance, Lipschitz Continuity, Condition Number
