On comparison of solution trajectories between dantzig-wolfe decomposition and basis factorization
   V. E.   Krivonozhko ;
Abstract
 Variants of Dantzig-Wolfe decomposition and basis factorization are proposed as solution techniques for block angular systems. It is shown that the two methods follow the same solution path to the optimum. The proposed approach allows us to compare Dantzig-Wolfe decomposition and basis factorization and to reveal some differences and similarities between them. The results also have implications for implementation of decomposition and factorization algorithms. 
Keywords
Decomposition, Basis factorization, Block angular systems
