GloptiPoly 3: moments, optimization and semidefinite programming
 Didier   Henrion ; Jean-Bernard   Lasserre ; Johan   Löfberg ;
Abstract
 We describe a major update of our Matlab freeware GloptiPoly for parsing generalized problems of moments and solving them numerically with semidefinite programming. 
Keywords
moments, polynomials, global optimization, semidefinite programming AMS Subject Classification : 44A60, 90C26, 90C22
