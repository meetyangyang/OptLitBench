One-operator, two-machine open shop and flow shop problems with setup times for machines and weighted number of tardy jobs objective 
 Mohammed Fazle   Baki ; R. G.   Vickson†  ;
Abstract
 One-operator two-machine open shop and flow shop problems are 𝒩𝒫-hard in the ordinary sense even if all the weights are the same. We discuss two different pseudo polynomial dynamic programming recursions for each of the open shop and flow shop case. Some of the running times are better than those of the best known algorithms in the literature.  
Keywords
Scheduling, Computational complexity, Pseudopolynomial algorithm, One-operator, Two-machine
