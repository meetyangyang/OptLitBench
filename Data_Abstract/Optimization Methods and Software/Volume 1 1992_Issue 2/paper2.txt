On self-dual update formulae in the Broyden family
 Ya-Xiang   Yuan ;
Abstract
 Quasi-Newton methods are a class of numerical methods for unconstrained optimization, where the Hessian of the objective function is approximated by an n × n matrix which is updated from iteration to iteration. A self-dual update formula has the property that the update formula and the inverse update formula are the same except “dual” interchanges. In this paper, we give a general condition for self-dual formulae in the Broyden family. Consequently two known self-dual formulae can be easily obtained. We also derive a general formula for self-dual update methods, which gives three new self-dual formulae. One of these new formulae is in the convex Broyden family. Numerical results are also represented. 
Keywords
self-dual, quasi-Newton method, unconstrained optimization.
