Effect of dimensionality on the Nelder–Mead simplex method
 Lixing   Han ; Michael   Neumann ;
Abstract
 The effect of dimensionality on the widely used Nelder–Mead simplex method for unconstrained optimization is investigated. It is shown that by using the quadratic function f(x)=x T x, the Nelder–Mead simplex method deteriorates as the dimension increases. 
Keywords
Nelder–Mead method, Simplex, Effect of dimensionality, Convergence, Optimization
