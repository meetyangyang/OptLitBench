Convergence of Barrier-projection methods of optimization via vector Lyapunov functions
 Georgi V.   Smirnov ;
Abstract
 The convergence of barrier-projection methods of optimization is investigated on the basis of vector Lyapunov functions. Continuous and discrete variants of barrier-projection methods are considered. 
Keywords
Trajectory following method, Barrier-projection method, Nonlinear programming, Vector Lyapunov function
