On global optimization in R using interval arithmetic
   M.A.   Wolfe ;
Abstract
 Interval arithmetic algorithms A.l and A.2 that determine computationally rigorous bounds on the global minimizers of continuous functions  without inequality constraints and with inequality constraints  respectively are described. It is assumed that, for A.l and that for  and. Computational experience indicates that A.l and A.2 always succeed, although in some cases more than one interval containing a global minimizer is found. Numerical results obtained from Sun Pascal implementations of A.l and A.2 are presented. 
Keywords
Global optimization, Interval mathematics, Inequality constraints
