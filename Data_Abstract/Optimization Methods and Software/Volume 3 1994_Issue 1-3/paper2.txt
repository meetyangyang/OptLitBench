Local properties of a newton-like direction for equality constrained minimization problems
 Francisco   Facchinei ; Stefano   Lucidi ;
Abstract
 In this paper we establish the q-uadratic convergence rate of a local Newton-like method for equality constrained minimization problems. We also consider a wide class of differentiable exact penalty functions and show that, in principle, they can be used to globalize the local algorithm without destroying its fast convergence rate. 
Keywords
Equality constrained optimization, Newton's method, quadratic convergence, Multiplier functions, Penalty functions.
