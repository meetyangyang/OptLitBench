The existence of dual prices for the linear integer programming problem
 SØren   Holm ;
Abstract
 Strong duality has been proven for the integer linear programming problem, where a super-additive function prices out the activities and closes the duality gap. In this paper an alternative, equivalent formulation of the dual problem there exist dual prices, which activities and close the duality gap, and where the dual objective function is an affine variables. 
Keywords
Integer Programming, Dual Prices
