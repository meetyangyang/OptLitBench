A derivative-free PRP method for solving large-scale nonlinear systems of equations and its global convergence
Min Li;
Abstract
An extended Polak-Ribière-Polyak conjugate gradient method for solving nonlinear systems of equations is proposed, which is suitable to large-scale problems for the lower storage requirement. Under some mild conditions, the global convergence of the proposed method is established. It is shown from the numerical results that the proposed method is practically effective.
Keywords
