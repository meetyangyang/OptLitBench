Remarks on Convergence of the Matrix Splitting Algorithm for the Symmetric Linear Complementarity Problem
Wu  Li;
Abstract
In this paper it is shown how to remove a restriction on the perturbation vectors $h^i$ imposed by Mangasarian and Luo and Tseng in their respective convergence analyses of the matrix splitting algorithms.
Keywords
matrix splitting algorithm, linear complementarily problem, linear convergence, error estimate, power method, 90C33, 90C20,
