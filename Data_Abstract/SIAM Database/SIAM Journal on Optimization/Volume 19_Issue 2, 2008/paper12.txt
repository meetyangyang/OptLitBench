Pitchfork Bifurcations in Nonlinear Least Squares Problems–Applications to TOA Geolocation
Louis A.  Romero and Jeff  Mason;
Abstract
We prove a general result concerning nonlinear least squares problems in the neighborhood of a singular Hessian. Assuming the Hessian has a one-dimensional null space, we show that in the neighborhood of such a point, there will be three solutions with small residuals. Counter to our intuition, assuming certain transversality conditions are satisfied, the solution with the largest residual is actually the most accurate solution. We illustrate this general theory by applying it to a time of arrival (TOA) geolocation problem.
Keywords
