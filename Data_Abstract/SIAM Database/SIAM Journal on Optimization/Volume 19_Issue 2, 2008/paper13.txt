A Characterization of Efficient and Weakly Efficient Points in Convex Vector Optimization
Kristin  Winkler;
Abstract
We present efficiency criteria for multicriteria problems with $\EuScript{C}(T)$-valued maps via directional derivatives and subdifferentials. It turns out that the essential structure of similar results known for $\mathbb{R}^n$-valued optimization problems remains valid. As an improvement, our formulas allow us to distinguish between efficient and weakly efficient elements.
