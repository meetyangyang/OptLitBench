Generalizations of the Dennis--Moré Theorem
Asen L.  Dontchev;
Abstract
A theorem of Dennis and Moré is generalized to characterize the $q$-superlinear convergence of  quasi-Newton methods applied to nonsmooth equations and generalized equations under strong metric subregularity.
Keywords
quasi-Newton method, strong metric subregularity, nonsmooth equations, $q$-superlinear convergence, generalized equation, 49J53, 49M37, 65J15, 90C30,
