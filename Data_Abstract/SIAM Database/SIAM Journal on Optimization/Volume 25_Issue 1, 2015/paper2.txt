Risk-Averse Optimization in Two-Stage Stochastic Models: Computational Aspects and a Study
Csaba I.  Fábián, Christian  Wolf, Achim  Koberstein, and Leena  Suhl;
Abstract
We extend the on-demand accuracy approach of Oliveira and Sagastizábal to constrained convex optimization. The resulting method is applied to risk-averse two-stage stochastic programming problems. We present a survey of risk-averse models. The appropriate oracle is formulated for the case of a conditional value-at-risk constraint. We discuss computational aspects and compare different approaches in a study.
Keywords
