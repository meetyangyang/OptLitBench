A Structure-Exploiting Algorithm for Nonlinear Minimax Problems
Andrew  R.  Conn and Yuying  Li;
Abstract
In this paper, some basic concepts are generalised which characterise the best linear Chebyshev approximation in one variable to general nonlinear minimax problems. A new method for solving a nonlinear minimax problem is presented, which exploits the structure and characterisation of the solution whenever possible. The algorithm is globally convergent with a superlinear convergence rate. Numerical results indicate the efficacy of the new method.
