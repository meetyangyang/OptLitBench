Practical Update Criteria for Reduced Hessian SQP: Global Analysis
Y. F.  Xie and R. H.  Byrd;
Abstract
In this paper, a new update criterion is proposed to improve the Nocedal--Overton update criterion for reduced Hessian successive quadratic programming (SQP). Global and R-linear convergence is proved for the new criterion and the Nocedal--Overton criterion using nonorthogonal basis matrices, which allow efficient implementations of the reduced Hessian SQP for solving large-scale equality constrained problems.
