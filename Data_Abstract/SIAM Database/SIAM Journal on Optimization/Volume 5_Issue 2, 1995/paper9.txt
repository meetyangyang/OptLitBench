Ladders for Travelling Salesmen
Sylvia C.  Boyd, William H.  Cunningham, Maurice  Queyranne, and Yaoguang  Wang;
Abstract
We introduce a new class of valid inequalities for the symmetric travelling salesman polytope. The family is not of the common handle-tooth variety. We show that these inequalities are all facet-inducing and have Chvátal rank 2.
Keywords
polyhedra, facets, travelling salesman problem, 90C08,
