Corrigendum: Semidefinite Programs: New Search Directions, Smoothing-Type Methods, and Numerical Results
Christian  Kanzow and Christian  Nagel;
Abstract
We correct an error in Lemma 4.2 of [C. Kanzow and C. Nagel,  Semidefinite programs: New search directions, smoothing-type methods, and numerical results, SIAM J. Optim., 13 (2002), pp. 1--23]. With this correction, all results in that paper remain true.
Keywords
semidefinite programs, smoothing-type methods, Newton's method, global convergence, superlinear convergence, 90C22, 90C46,
