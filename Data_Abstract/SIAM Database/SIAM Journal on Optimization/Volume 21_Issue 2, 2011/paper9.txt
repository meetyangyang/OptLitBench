Erratum: Global Convergence of a Nonsmooth Newton Method for Control-State Constrained Optimal Control Problems
Matthias  Gerdts;
Abstract
This erratum points out a mistake in Lemma 2.5(i) in the author’s article [SIAM J. Optim., 19 (2008), pp. 326–350], which fails to hold in the present form. Lemma 2.5(i) was needed as an auxiliary result in the investigation of local convergence properties of semismooth Newton methods for control-state constrained optimal control problems subject to ordinary differential equations. Lemma 2.5(i) stated that a superposition operator mapping essentially bounded functions to essentially bounded functions is semismooth, which is not true. As a consequence, the locally superlinear convergence results in the author’s article mentioned above that use Lemma 2.5(i) have to be considered an open problem.
