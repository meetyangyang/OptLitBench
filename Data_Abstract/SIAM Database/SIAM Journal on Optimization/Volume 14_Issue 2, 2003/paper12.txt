Sharp Estimates for Hoffman's Constant for Systems of Linear Inequalities and Equalities
C.  Zualinescu;
Abstract
We extend the formulae given by Azé and Corvellec, Belousov and Andronov, and Ng and Zheng for the Hoffman constant associated to the system of linear inequalities $Ax\leq b$ and relate them to that established by Li.
Keywords
convex function, Hoffman constant, linear inequality, Lipschitz constant, multi\-function, 90C25, 90C48,
