The Monotonic Diameter of the Perfect 2-matching Polytope
Fred J.  Rispoli;
Abstract
The monotonic diameter of the perfect 2-matching polytope associated to the complete graph with n nodes is shown to be $[ n/2 ]$ for all $n \geq 3$, except for $n = 3,4,8$, and 9, in which case the monotonic diameter is $[ n/2 ] - 1$. The result establishes the monotonic Hirsch conjecture for this polytope.
