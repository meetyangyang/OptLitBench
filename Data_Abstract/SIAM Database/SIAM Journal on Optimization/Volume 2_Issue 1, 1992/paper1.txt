Classification of Critical Stationary Points in Unconstrained Optimization
 Stefan  Schäffler;
Abstract
A stationary point of an unconstrained optimization problem is called critical if the Hessian matrix at this point is positive semidefinite. Such a point cannot be classified using second-order optimality conditions. In this paper the problem of classifying a critical stationary point of a given objective function is reduced to the application of higher-order optimality conditions for a special auxiliary function.
Keywords
