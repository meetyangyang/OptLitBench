Facially Exposed Cones Are Not Always Nice
Vera  Roshchina;
Abstract
We address the conjecture proposed by Gábor Pataki that every facially exposed cone is nice. We show that the conjecture is true in the three-dimensional case; however, there exists a four-dimensional counterexample of a cone that is facially exposed but is not nice.
Keywords
facially exposed cone, nice cone, Pataki's conjecture, 90C25, 52A20, 52A15,
