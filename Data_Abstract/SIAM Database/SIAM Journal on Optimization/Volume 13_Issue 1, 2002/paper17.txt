On the Distance between Two Ellipsoids
Anhua  Lin and Shih-Ping  Han;
Abstract
We study the fundamental geometric problem of finding the distance between two ellipsoids. An algorithm is proposed for computing the distance and locating the two closest points. The algorithm is based on a local approximation of the two ellipsoids by balls. It is simple, geometric in nature, and has excellent convergence properties.
