On the Choice of the Regularization Parameter in Nonlinear Inverse Problems
K.  Ito and K.  Kunisch;
Abstract
This paper focuses on regularization techniques for nonlinear ill-posed inverse problems. Tikhonov regularization and regularization due to the use of norm constraints are analyzed. A model function technique is proposed to iteratively determine an optimal regularization parameter or the parameter characterizing the norm constraint, and to estimate the error in the data if it is not known a priori.
Keywords
