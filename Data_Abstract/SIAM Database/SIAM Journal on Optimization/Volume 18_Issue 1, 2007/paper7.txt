Representation of Sets of Lattice Points
Raymond   Hemmecke and Robert   Weismantel;
Abstract
Original algorithmic approaches in integer programming rely on the availability of different representations for all the lattice points in the feasible region. We present three results that are applicable to fairly general sets of lattice points and characterize a nonnegative integer linear representation.
