Stable Minimizers of $\varphi$-Regular Functions
Jen-Chih  Yao, Xi Yin  Zheng, and Jiangxing  Zhu;
Abstract
In this paper, given an admissible function $\varphi$, we consider $\varphi$-regularity and find it to be  a suitable qualification for a function to satisfy the $\varphi$-growth condition and uniform $\varphi$-growth condition. We establish the relationships between $\varphi$-growth condition/uniform $\varphi$-growth condition of a $\varphi$-regular function and the corresponding strong metric subregularity/regularity of its  subdifferential mapping. The results presented in this paper improve and generalize some existing ones on  stable second-order local minimizers in the literature.
Keywords
