Regularity Properties of a Semismooth Reformulation of Variational Inequalities
Francisco  Facchinei, Andreas  Fischer, and Christian  Kanzow;
Abstract
Variational inequalities over sets defined by systems of equalities and inequalities are considered. A new reformulation of the KKT conditions for the variational inequality as a system of equations is proposed. A related unconstrained minimization reformulation is also investigated. As a by-product of the analysis, a new characterization of strong regularity of KKT points is given.
Keywords
variational inequality problem, KKT conditions, strong regularity, 90C33, 65K05, 90C31,
