Global Analysis of the Dennis--Wolkowicz Least-Change Secant Algorithm
Lixing  Han and Guanghui  Liu;
Abstract
This paper explores the global convergence of the least-change secant method proposed by Dennis and Wolkowicz[SIAM J. Numer. Anal., 30 (1993), pp. 1291--1314]. This method is a member of the Broyden family, but it doesn't necessarily belong to the Broyden convex family. Furthermore, it can be very close to the DFP method. We will prove that this method, with the Wolfe line search on uniformly convex objective functions, is globally convergent.
Keywords
Broyden family, DW algorithm, Wolfe line search, global convergence, 90C30, 65K05,
