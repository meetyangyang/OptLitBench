Gradient Flows, Second-Order Gradient Systems and Convexity
Tahar Z.  Boulmezaoud, Philippe  Cieutat, and Aris  Daniilidis;
Abstract
We disclose an interesting connection between the gradient flow of a $\mathscr{C}^2$-smooth function $\psi$ and strongly evanescent orbits of the second-order gradient system defined by the square-norm of $\nabla\psi$, under an adequate convexity assumption. As a consequence, we obtain the following surprising result for two $\mathscr{C}^2$, convex, and bounded from below functions $\psi_1$, $\psi_2$: if $||\nabla\psi_1||=||\nabla\psi_2||$, then $\psi_1=\psi_2 + k$ for some $k\in \mathbb{R}$.
