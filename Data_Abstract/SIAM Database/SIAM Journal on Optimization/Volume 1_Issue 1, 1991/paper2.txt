A New Variational Result for Quasi-Newton Formulae
R.  Fletcher;
Abstract
The recent measure function of Byrd and Nocedal [SIAM J. Numer. Anal., 26 (1989), pp. 727–739] is considered and simple proofs of some of its properties are given. It is then shown that the BFGS and DFP formulae satisfy a least change property with respect to this new measure.
Keywords
quasi-Newton method, BFGS formula, DFP formula, 65K, 90C,
