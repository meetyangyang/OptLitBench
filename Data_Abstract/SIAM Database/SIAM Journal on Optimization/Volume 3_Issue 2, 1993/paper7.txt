Quantitative Stability of Variational Systems II. A Framework for Nonlinear Conditioning
Hedy  Attouch and Roger J.-B.  Wets;
Abstract
Stability results of Lipschitz and Hölder type are obtained for the solutions and optimal values of optimization problems when perturbations are measured in terms of the $\rho $-epi-distance.
Keywords
quantitative stability, epi-convergence, epi-distance, projection, optimization, variational problems, 65K10, 65K05, 49J40, 58E35,
