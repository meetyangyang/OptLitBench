A $(k+1)$-Slope Theorem for the $k$-Dimensional Infinite Group Relaxation
Amitabh  Basu, Robert  Hildebrand, Matthias  Köppe, and Marco  Molinaro;
Abstract
We prove that any minimal valid function for the $k$-dimensional infinite group relaxation that is continuous piecewise linear with at most $k+1$ slopes and does not factor through a linear map with nontrivial kernel is extreme. This generalizes a theorem of Gomory and Johnson for $k=1$ and of Cornuéjols and Molinaro for $k=2$.
Keywords
integer programming, cutting planes, corner polyhedron, group relaxation, subadditive functions, 90C10, 90C11,
