Existence of Minimizers on Drops
R.  Correa, P.  Gajardo, L.  Thibault, and D.  Zagrodny;
Abstract
For a boundedly generated drop $[a,E]$ (a property which holds, for instance, whenever $E$ is bounded), where $a$ belongs to a real Banach space $X$ and $E \subset X$ is a nonempty convex set, we show that for every lower semicontinuous function $h:X\longrightarrow {\mathbb{R}}\cup  \{+\infty\}$ that satisfies $\sup_ {\delta>0} \inf_{x\in E+\delta B_X }h(x)>h(a)$ ($B_X$ being the unitary open ball in $X$), there exists $\bar x \in [a,E]$ such that  $h(a)\ge h(\bar x)$ and $\bar x$ is a strict minimizer of $h$ on the drop $[\bar x,E]$.
Keywords
variational principle, variational inequalities, drop, minimizers, 65K10, 49J40,
