A Distributed Boyle--Dykstra--Han Scheme
Soham R.  Phade and Vivek S.  Borkar;
Abstract
We present a provably convergent distributed analog of Han's algorithm for computing the projection of a point on the intersection of a finite collection of convex sets.
Keywords
Han's algorithm, projection on convex sets, distributed algorithm, 90C25, 65K10, 49M99,
