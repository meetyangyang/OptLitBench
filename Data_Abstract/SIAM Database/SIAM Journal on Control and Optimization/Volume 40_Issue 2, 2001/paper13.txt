New Second-Order Optimality Conditions for Variational Problems with C2 -Hamiltonians
Vera  Zeidan;
Abstract
The generalized problem of Bolzawith a C2 -Hamiltonian is considered. Necessary and sufficient conditions are obtained, respectively, in terms of the accessory problem, the existence of conjoined basis, and the existence of a solution to a Riccati equation with boundary conditions. No strengthened Legendre--Clebsch condition is required. When applied to a general optimal control problem, these results include and generalize known results.
Keywords
