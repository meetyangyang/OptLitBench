Un Algorithme de Minimisation en Chaine en Optimisation Convexe
C.  Carasso and P. J.  Laurent;
Abstract
We consider the problem of minimization of a convex, nondifferentiable function on a linear manifold in $\mathbb{R}^n $. We give a general algorithm of “exchange” type which does not need any Haar assumption and can be continued indefinitely.
