Optimality Conditions for the Average Cost per Unit Time Problem with a Diffusion Model
H. J.  Kushner;
Abstract
Defining the solution to a stochastic differential equation to be the solution to the martingale problem of Strook and Varadhan, we obtain results on the existence of an optimal stationary control for the average cost per unit time problem, a necessary and sufficient condition for optimality of a control, and a number of other related results.
