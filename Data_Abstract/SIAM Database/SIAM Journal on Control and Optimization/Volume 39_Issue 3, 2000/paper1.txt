A Noncontrollability Result for Systems of Mixed Order
Giuseppe  Geymonat and Vanda  Valente;
Abstract
In this paper we prove an abstract theorem of noncontrollability for the evolution equation associated to a Douglis--Nirenberg elliptic system of mixed order with nonempty essential spectrum. In particular, we show by Weyl's characterization of the essential spectrum that the condition of exact controllability does not hold true. We discuss examples concerning the elasticity operator in the framework of the linear membrane shell theory.
Keywords
