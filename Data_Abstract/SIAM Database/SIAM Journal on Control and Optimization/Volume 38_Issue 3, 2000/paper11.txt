A Class of Team Problems with Discrete Action Spaces: Optimality Conditions Based on Multimodularity
Peter R.  de Waal and Jan H.  van Schuppen;
Abstract
In this paper we discuss a class of team problems with discrete action spaces. We introduce multimodularity into team theory as a natural alternative to convexity in continuous spaces. The main result relates coordinatewise-optimal (cw-optimal) points to the optimal team decision for a class of team problems. The method is based on a characterization of coordinatewise minima of multimodular functions.
Keywords
