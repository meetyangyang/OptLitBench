Exact Boundary Controllability of a Maxwell Problem
N.  Weck;
Abstract
We consider the problem of steering some initial state of the time-dependent Maxwell equations to rest by controlling the lateral boundary condition. Depending on the topology (Betti numbers) of the given domain $\Omega$, we identify completely those subspaces which can be steered to rest and those which cannot. In the positive case it is shown that each time interval which is longer than diam $\Omega$ suffices. This result is almost sharp for the class of regions $\Omega$ which contain a segment of length diam $\Omega $.
