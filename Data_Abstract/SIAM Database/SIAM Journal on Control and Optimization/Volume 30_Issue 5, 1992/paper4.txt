A Generalized Estimate of the Number of Zeros for Solutions of a Class of Linear Differential Equations
A. V.  Dmitruk;
Abstract
For a class of triangular systems of Nth-order linear ordinary differential equations with measurable coefficients, a simple proof of the following property is given: On every interval of an a priori prescribed length, which depends only on the norms of coefficients, the first component of every nontrivial solution of the system has at most $N - 1$ zeros.
Keywords
differential equation, measurable coefficients, zeros of solutions, 34A30, 34C10,
