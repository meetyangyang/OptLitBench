Controllability of $l^2 $-Systems
Fabio  Fagnani and Jan C.  Willems;
Abstract
This paper is devoted to an investigation of controllability and almost controllability of $l^2 $-systems. These concepts are defined in terms of the possibility of steering one system trajectory to another. It is proved that a controllable $l^2 $-system always has finite memory. The main result on almost controllability states that this is equivalent to the existence of a scattering representation. The paper ends with an investigation of the relation of almost controllability and state representations.
Keywords
controllability, l2-systems, state representation, linear systems, 93B05,
