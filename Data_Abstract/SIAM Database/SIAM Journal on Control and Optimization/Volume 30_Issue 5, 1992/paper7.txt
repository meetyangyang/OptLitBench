Algebraic Differential Equations and Rational Control Systems
Yuan  Wang and Eduardo D.  Sontag;
Abstract
An equivalence is shown between realizability of input/output (i/o) operators by rational control systems and high-order algebraic differential equations for (i/o) pairs. This generalizes, to nonlinear systems, the equivalence between autoregressive representations and finite-dimensional linear realizability.
