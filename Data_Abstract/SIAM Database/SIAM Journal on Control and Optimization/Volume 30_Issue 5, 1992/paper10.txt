On Multivariate Robust Stability
Charles K.  Chui and H. N.  Mhaskar;
Abstract
The notion of robust stability in the multidimensional setting is carefully investigated. In particular, the perturbation function is defined to measure the maximal stability region. Applications to robust stability tests of Kharitonov and Barmish types are obtained.
Keywords
robust stability, perturbation functions, Kharitonov test, 93D05, 93C35, 93D20, 93D25,
