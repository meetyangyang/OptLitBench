Necessary Conditions for Optimality of Elliptic Systems with Positivity Constraints on the State
P.  Michel;
Abstract
Recently, necessary conditions for optimality were established in the case of nonlinear elliptic multi-dimensional systems with integral constraints [P. Michel, Condition nécessaire d’optimalité pour des systèmes d’équations elliptiques non linéaires, Quatrièmes journées de contrôle (Metz, 18–21 Mai 1976), polycopié Université de Metz, 1976]. Here this problem is solved in the case of additional constraints of positivity of the state’s components on measurable sets.
