Difference Approximations in Optimal Control Problems
B. M.  Budak, E. M.  Berkovich, and E. N.  Solov’eva;
Abstract
The convergence of solutions of discrete extremum problems to the solution of the continuous optimal control problem is considered, both in the sense of the optimal value of the functional and of the optimal control. In the latter case “nonwell-posed” extremum problems are discussed.
