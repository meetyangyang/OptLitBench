A Simple Free Boundary Problem in ${\bf R}^d $
Guillermo  Ferreyra and Omar  Hijab;
Abstract
A multidimensional deterministic singular control problem is posed and solved. The interest here is the explicitness of the result and the novelty of the gradient constraint.
Keywords
