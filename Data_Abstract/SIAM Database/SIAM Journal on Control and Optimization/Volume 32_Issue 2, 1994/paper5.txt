Stabilization and Exact Boundary Controllability for Maxwell’s Equations
B. V.  Kapitonov;
Abstract
This paper considers Maxwell’s equations with dissipative boundary conditions. Under certain geometric conditions imposed on the domain $\Omega $, the results on uniform stabilization of the solutions are established. Exact boundary controllability is then obtained through Russell’s “controllability via stabilizability” principle.
Keywords
