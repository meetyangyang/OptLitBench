Global Controllability of Nonviscous and Viscous Burgers-Type Equations
Marianne  Chapouly;
Abstract
In this paper, we deal with both nonviscous and viscous Burgers-type equations on a bounded interval. We study the global exact controllability of these equations when we have three controls: one control is the right member of the equation and is constant with respect to the space variable; the two others are the boundary values. For a first time, we are interested in nonviscous Burgers-type equations and we prove their global exact controllability for every time $T>0$ with such controls thanks to the return method. Then we prove the global exact controllability of the viscous Burgers equation for every time $T>0$.
Keywords
global exact controllability, Burgers-type equations, return method, 93B05, 93C20, 35Q53,
