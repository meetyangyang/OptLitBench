Measure-Theoretic Invariance Entropy for Control Systems
Tao  Wang, Yu  Huang , and Hai-Wei  Sun;
Abstract
We introduce two types of measure-theoretic invariance entropies for topological time-invariant control systems. It is shown that these entropies are invariant under conjugacies and there exists a generator for them. We also illustrate that invariance entropies are determined by measure-theoretic invariance entropies. More importantly, we establish two variational principles between topological and measure-theoretic invariance entropies in some special situations.
