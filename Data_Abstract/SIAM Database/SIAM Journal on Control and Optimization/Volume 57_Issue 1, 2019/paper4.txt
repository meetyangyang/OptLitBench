Consistent Discretization of Finite-Time and Fixed-Time Stable Systems
Andrey  Polyakov , Denis  Efimov, and Bernard  Brogliato;
Abstract
Algorithms of implicit discretization for generalized homogeneous systems having a possible discontinuity only at the origin are developed. They are based  on the transformation of the original system to an equivalent one which admits an implicit or a semi-implicit discretization scheme preserving the stability properties of the continuous-time system. Namely, the discretized model remains finite-time stable (in the case of negative homogeneity degree) and practically fixed-time stable (in the case of positive homogeneity degree). The theoretical results are supported with numerical examples.
Keywords
