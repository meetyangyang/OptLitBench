Differential Games with Asymmetric Information
P.  Cardaliaguet;
Abstract
We investigate a two-player zero-sum differential game in which the players have asymmetric information on the random terminal payoff. We prove that the game has a value and characterize this value in terms of dual solutions of some Hamilton–Jacobi equation. We also explain how to adapt the results to differential games where the initial position is random.
