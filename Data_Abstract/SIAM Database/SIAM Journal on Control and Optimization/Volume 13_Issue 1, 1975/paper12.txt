Plane Motion of a Particle Subject to Curvature Constraints
E. J.  Cockayne and G. W. C.  Hall;
Abstract
A particle P moves in the plane with constant speed and subject to an upper bound on the curvature of its path. This paper studies the classes of trajectories by which P can reach a given point in a given direction and obtains, for all t, the set $R(t)$ of all possible positions for P at time t, thus extending the results of several recent authors.
