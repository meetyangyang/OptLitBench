Comparison of Differential Games of Fixed Duration
A. N. V.  Rao;
Abstract
The upper and lower Values of two differential games, one described by a system of n differential equations and the other, by a system of m differential equations, are compared. The approach taken depends upon the use of Lyapunov-like functions and the theory of differential inequalities. It is suggested that such comparison results are of interest because they enable us to obtain information about a given game by studying a simpler game.
