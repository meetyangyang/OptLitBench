Controllability of Linear Systems on Lie Groups with Finite Semisimple Center
Víctor  Ayala and Adriano Da  Silva;
Abstract
This paper studies controllability for a given linear system $\Sigma $ on a connected Lie group $G$ by taking into consideration the eigenvalues of an associated derivation $\mathcal{D}$. If we assume that the Lie group $G$ has finite center and, for some $\tau >0$, the identity element of $G$ is an interior point of its reachable set at time $  \tau $, then the system is controllable if $\mathcal{D}$ has only eigenvalues with zero real part.
