Classical Solutions of Nonlinear Beam Equations: Existence and Stabilization
Gareth  Hegarty and Stephen  Taylor;
Abstract
In this paper we consider the large amplitude planar motion of a cantilevered beam with a feedback force and/or torque (proportional and acting opposite to the velocity/angular velocity, respectively) applied at the free end. We derive nonlinear equations of motion for such a beam based upon certain natural geometric assumptions. We then prove the existence of classical solutions for these equations and show that all classical solutions are uniformly exponentially stable.
