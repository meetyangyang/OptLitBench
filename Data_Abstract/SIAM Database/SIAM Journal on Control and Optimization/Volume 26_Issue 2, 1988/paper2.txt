Optimal Control of Strongly Monotone Variational Inequalities
ShuZhong  Shi;
Abstract
By using the penalty method and Ekeland’s variational principle, this paper proves the optimality condition for a solution to a nonconvex optimal control problem, in which the system is governed by a strongly monotone variational inequality.
Keywords
optimal control, strongly monotone variational inequalities, optimality condition, penalty method, Ekeland’s variational principle, 49B99, 49A29,
