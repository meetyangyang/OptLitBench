Galerkin Approximation for Optimal Linear Filtering of Infinite-Dimensional Linear Systems
A.  Germani, L.  Jetto, and M.  Piccioni;
Abstract
In this paper an implementable approximation of the infinite-dimensional Kalman filter is proposed for linear distributed systems corrupted by white noise. It relies on a Galerkin-type treatment of both the Riccati and the filter equation, and it is shown to converge to the best linear estimate of the state for each sample path of the noise. A basic tool is the study of the Riccati equation on the Hilbert space of Hilbert-Schmidt (HS) operators on the state space. Numerical results are given for a case concerning delay-differential equations.
Keywords
