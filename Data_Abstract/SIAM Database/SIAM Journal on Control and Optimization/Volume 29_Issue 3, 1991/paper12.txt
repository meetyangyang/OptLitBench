Invariance of the Approximately Reachable Set under Nonlinear Perturbations
Koichiro  Naito and Thomas I.  Seidman;
Abstract
This paper considers nonlinear perturbations of control systems with linear dynamics and seeks to analyze whether the approximately reachable set may be left unchanged by this perturbation. Under suitable conditions it is shown that this analysis may be reduced to the presumably simpler analysis of such invariance for a family of affine perturbations. Interest centers on the context of infinite-dimensional state spaces so the system may, for example, correspond to a hyperbolic or parabolic partial differential equation.
