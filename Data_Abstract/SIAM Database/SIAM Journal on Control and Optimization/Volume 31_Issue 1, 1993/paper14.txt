A Subspace Decomposition Principle for Scaled Gradient Projection Methods: Local Theory
J. C.  Dunn;
Abstract
This paper is a sequel to an earlier work on a modification of the Gafni–Bertsekas scaled gradient projection method. Global convergence theorems proved in the first paper are complemented here by a local convergence analysis for the general SGP schemes and for special SGP algorithms with Newtonian scaling operators.
Keywords
