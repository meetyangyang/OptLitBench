Maximum Likelihood Estimator for Two-Point Boundary Value Process
Shin ichi  Aihara and Arunabha  Bagchi;
Abstract
The problem of identifying unknown parameters in two-point boundary value (TPBV) processes is studied. Using the explicit form of the likelihood functional, the conistency property of the maximum likelihood estimator is analyzed under a large number of independent experiments.
Keywords
two-point boundary value (TPBV) process, maximum likelihood estimator, consistency, likelihood functional, 93E12, 60H99, 62M09,
