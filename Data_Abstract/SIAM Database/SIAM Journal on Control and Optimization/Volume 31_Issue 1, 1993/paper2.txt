Existence Theorems for Positive Semidefinite and Sign Indefinite Stabilizing Solutions of $H_\infty  $ Riccati Equations
Gary  Hewer;
Abstract
The existence of both positive semidefinite and sign indefinite stabilizing solutions of $H_\infty  $-type Riccati equations is determined via joint eigenvalue and Hamiltonian tests. These tests determine the inertia properties of the solutions. They are dependent on a formula—derived in this paper—that shows how the solution of game Riccati equations can be decomposed into a sum of two well-known Riccati equations, namely, the standard filter Riccati equation and the bounded real Riccati equation. Other properties of stabilizing solutions such as solution partial order, spectral radius monotonicity, and rank are also discussed.
Keywords
