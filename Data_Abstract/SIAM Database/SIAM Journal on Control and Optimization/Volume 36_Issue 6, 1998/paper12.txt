Dissipativity and the Lur'e Problem for Parabolic Boundary Control Systems
L.  Pandolfi;
Abstract
Dissipativity is studied for a class of boundary control systems whose free evolution is described by a holomorphic semigroup. It is proved that the system is dissipative if and only if there exists a (bounded selfadjoint) solution of the corresponding linear operator inequality. In addition, the Lur'e problem is solved under particular assumptions.
Keywords
