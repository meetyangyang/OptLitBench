Null Controllability of a Fluid-Structure System
Julien  Lequeurre;
Abstract
We study a coupled fluid-structure system. The structure corresponds to a part of the boundary of a domain containing an incompressible viscous fluid. The structure displacement is modeled by an ordinary differential equation. We prove the local null controllability of the system when the control acts on a fixed subset of the fluid domain.
