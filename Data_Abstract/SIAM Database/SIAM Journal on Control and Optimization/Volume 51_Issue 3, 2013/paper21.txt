Optimal Harvesting in the Stochastic Logistic Growth Model with Finite Horizon
Hiroaki  Morimoto;
Abstract
We consider a singular control  problem of optimal harvesting to maximize the expected total value of the harvested individuals in the logistic model with finite horizon. Using the viscosity solution technique, we develop the penalty method  to solve the parabolic variational inequality associated with this problem and analyze the behaviors of the moving free boundary for the variational inequality. The optimal harvesting policy is shown to exist.
Keywords
