Sufficient Conditions in Free Final Time Optimal Control Problems
Atle  Seierstad;
Abstract
A direct sufficient condition for the optimality of a control in standard control problems with free final time is proved. The basic tool is superderivative properties of the optimal value of the criterion as a function of the final time and the final point. Sufficient conditions arise if the superderivatives have proper signs.
Keywords
