On Differential Evasion Games
Jiongmin  Yong;
Abstract
The notions of evadability and strict evadability are defined for a nonlinear differential game with general closed convex terminal set. Sufficient conditions for strict evadability and evadability and a necessary condition for evadability are proved. The results are strengthened for the linear cases, including the heretofore untreated case in which the terminal set is an $(n - 1)$dimensional subspace.
