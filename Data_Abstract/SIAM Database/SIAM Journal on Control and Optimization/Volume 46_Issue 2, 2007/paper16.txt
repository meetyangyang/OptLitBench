Comparison Results and Estimates on the Gradient without Strict Convexity
A.   Cellina;
Abstract
In this paper we establish a comparison result for solutions to the problem $$\mbox{minimize}\int_\Omega f(\nabla u(x))\,dx \quad \hbox{ on } \{u: u-u _0 \in W^{1,1}_0(\Omega)\}.$$
Keywords
strict convexity, comparison theorem, bounded slope condition, 49K20,
