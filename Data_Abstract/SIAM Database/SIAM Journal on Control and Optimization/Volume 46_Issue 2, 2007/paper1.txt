Null Controllability of Some Systems of Two Parabolic Equations with One Control Force
Sergio   Guerrero;
Abstract
In this paper we establish some exact controllability results for systems of two parabolic equations. First, we prove the existence of insensitizing controls for the $L^2$ norm of the gradient of solutions of linear heat equations. Then, in the worst situation where null controllability for a system of two parabolic equations can hold, we prove this result for some general couplings.
Keywords
