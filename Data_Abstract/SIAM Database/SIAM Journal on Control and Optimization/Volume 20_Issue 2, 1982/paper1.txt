On Necessary and Sufficient Conditions for Regulation of Linear Systems over Rings
E.  Emre;
Abstract
Necessary and sufficient conditions are given for regulation of linear systems over rings using observers and causal dynamic state feedback systems with the polynomial fractional representation property. The results are then used to obtain stabilizability conditions for systems over integers, delay-differential systems, systems over polynomial rings, and to obtain conditions to make a 2-D system nonrecursive.
