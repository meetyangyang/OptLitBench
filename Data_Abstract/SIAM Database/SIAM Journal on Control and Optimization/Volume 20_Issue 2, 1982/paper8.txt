On the Computation of the Reachable/Observable Canonical Form
R. E.  Kalman;
Abstract
This note is concerned with questions related to the definition of “canonical form” for linear systems. Some misunderstandings in a previous note by Boley in this journal [SIAM J. Control Optim., 18 (1980), pp. 624–626], are clarified and some new research problems are suggested.
