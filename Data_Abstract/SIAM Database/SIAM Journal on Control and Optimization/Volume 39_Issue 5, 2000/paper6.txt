A Frequency Domain Robust Stability Theorem for Infinite Dimensional Systems with Parametric Uncertainty
Coleman  Brosilow and Marshall J.  Leitman;
Abstract
A robust stability theorem of non-Kharitonov type for parametric uncertainty is formulated and proved: A closed loop system depending on a connected set of parameters whose maximum frequency response is bounded is either stable for all the parameters or for none. Realistic illustrative examples are provided.
Keywords
