A “Simplest Possible” Property of the Generalized Routh–Hurwitz Conditions
B. D. O.  Anderson and E. I.  Jury;
Abstract
To decide whether a prescribed complex polynomial has all its zeros with negative real parts, there are available many tests involving the checking of rational or polynomial inequalities in the coefficients. It is shown that the generalized Routh–Hurwitz conditions are from a certain point of view not replaceable by simpler conditions.
