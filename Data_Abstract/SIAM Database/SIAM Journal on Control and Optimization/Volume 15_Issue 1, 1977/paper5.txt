A Convergent Scheme for Boundary Control of the Heat Equation
William C.  Chewning and Thomas I.  Seidman;
Abstract
It is desired to steer the solution of the heat equation in $\mathcal{D} \subset \mathbb{R}^n $ from a given initial state $u_0  = u(0, \cdot )$ exactly to a given terminal state $u_T  = u(T, \cdot )$ by controlling the Dirichlet data $\varphi $. An algorithm is presented which, when such control is possible, provides a sequence converging in $L_2 $ to the optimal control.
