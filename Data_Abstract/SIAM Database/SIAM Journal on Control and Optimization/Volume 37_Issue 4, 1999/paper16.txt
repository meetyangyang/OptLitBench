Erratum: "Existence of Markov Controls and Characterization of Optimal Markov Controls"
Thomas G.  Kurtz and Richard H.  Stockbridge;
Abstract
A compactness condition used to establish the equivalence of infinite-dimensional linear programming problems with stochastic control problems in [SIAM J. Control Optim., 36 (1998), pp. 609--653.] was misstated. The correct statement of the condition is given, as well as an example to show that the erroneous condition is not sufficient to obtain equivalence.
