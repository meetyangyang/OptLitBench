1-Determinacy of Feasible Sets
Toshihiro  Matsumoto, Susumu  Shindoh, and Ryuichi  Hirabayashi;
Abstract
Germs of feasible sets, 1-determinacy of feasible sets, (strongly) restricted A-equivalence of map germs, and (strongly) restricted 1-determinacy of map germs are defined. Then each concept of 1-determinacy is characterized by a necessary and sufficient condition. Finally, it is demonstrated that all of these concepts are equivalent in some sense.
Keywords
feasible sets, 1-determinacy, (strongly) restricted A-equivalence, singularity,
