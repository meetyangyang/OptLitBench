Viability Problems for Nonautonomous Differential Inclusions
Peter  Tallos;
Abstract
In this paper the existence of viable solutions to nonautonomous differential inclusions is proved. The set-valued map on the right-hand side is assumed to be integrably bounded, measurable in t, and upper semicontinuous in x with nonempty convex, compact values. The cases of convex and nonconvex viability domains and also the time-dependent case are considered. An example for control systems is also given.
Keywords
