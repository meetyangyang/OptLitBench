Convergence of Viable Solutions of Differential Inclusions with Convex Compact Graphs
Arie  Leizarowitz;
Abstract
A convergence property for the viable solutions of a differential inclusion with convex and compact graph is established. We show that if there is a unique stationary point and no elliptic solutions, then all the viable solutions converge uniformly to the stationary point. We show that the convergence property is generic for the set of differential inclusions with a unique stationary point.
Keywords
differential inclusions, set valued functions, viable solutions, stationary points,
