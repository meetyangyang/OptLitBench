Exact Boundary Controllability for Quasilinear Hyperbolic Systems on a Tree-Like Network and Its Applications
Qilong  Gu and Tatsien  Li;
Abstract
In this paper we establish the local exact boundary controllability on a star-like network under certain interface conditions. By means of this result, the local exact boundary controllability of unsteady flows with fewer controls is realized in a tree-like network of open canals with general topology.
Keywords
exact boundary controllability, Saint-Venant system, tree-like network of open canals, 35L50, 93B05,
