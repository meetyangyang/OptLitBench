Reachability of a Class of Discrete-Time Positive Switched Systems
Ettore  Fornasini and Maria Elena  Valcher;
Abstract
In this paper, monomial reachability and reachability properties for the special class of single-input discrete-time positive systems that switch among p subsystems, sharing the same state transition matrix, are investigated. Necessary and sufficient conditions for these properties to hold, together with some examples, are provided.
