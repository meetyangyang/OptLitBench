Feedback Spreading Control under Speed Constraints
K.  Kassara;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with the control of spread in semilinear parabolic systems. It first introduces a formula in order to measure the speed of a spread. Then feedback spreading control laws under speed constraints are studied by a set-valued approach. The optimality of such laws is examined in the case in which the system is affine dependent upon the control.
Keywords
