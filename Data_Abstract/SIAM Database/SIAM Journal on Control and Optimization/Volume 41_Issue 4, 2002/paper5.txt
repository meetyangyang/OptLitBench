The Solution of the $\mbox\boldmath$H^2/H^\infty$$ Problem by Direct Methods
M. A.  Da Silveira and R.  Ades;
Abstract
The $H^2/H^{\infty}$ problem is formulated in a Hilbertian context. It has a unique solution, which is the strong limit of sequences generated by Galerkin methods based on convenient, but not necessarily orthogonal, generator sets. Using these results, a methodology to solve the problem by a Galerkin method is proposed, and an example is solved and compared to other approaches.
