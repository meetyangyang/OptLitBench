Observability and Observers for Nonlinear Systems
J. P.  Gauthier and I. A. K.  Kupka;
Abstract
This paper deals with observability and observers for general nonlinear systems. In the non-control-affine case, we characterize systems that are observable independently of the inputs. An exponential observer for these systems is also exhibited.
