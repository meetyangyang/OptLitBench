Optimal Design for Heat Conduction Problems with Hysteresis
Martin  Brokate and Avner  Friedman;
Abstract
A model of controlling the temperature in heat conduction is considered; the control mechanism is accomplished by means of a hysteresis operator that depends on some parameters. A cost functional is introduced that depends on these parameters and then properties for the optimal parameters are derived.
Keywords
heat equation, hysteresis, thermostat control, optimal design, Preisach operator, 93C20, 49A36, 49B22,
