Ritz–Galerkin Approximation of the Time Optimal Boundary Control Problem for Parabolic Systems with Dirichlet Boundary Conditions
Irena  Lasiecka;
Abstract
This paper deals with Ritz–Galerkin approximations of the following two problems: (i) boundary-value problems with $L_2 $-boundary data given in the form of Dirichlet boundary conditions; (ii) time optimal control problems for parabolic systems with control acting on the boundary.In both cases, optimal rates of convergence of approximation are obtained. Also, the specialization is considered to approximating with nonconformal elements which are not required to satisfy boundary conditions. Corresponding rates of convergence are also proved.
Keywords
Ritz–Galerkin approximations, finite element approximations, bang-bang principle, analytic  semigroups, Dirichlet map,
