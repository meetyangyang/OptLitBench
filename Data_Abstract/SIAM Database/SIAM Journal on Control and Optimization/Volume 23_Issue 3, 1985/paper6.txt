Connections Between Optimal Stopping and Singular Stochastic Control II. Reflected Follower Problems
Ioannis  Karatzas and Steven E.  Shreve;
Abstract
The stochastic control problem of following a Brownian path by a process of bounded variation, and subject to a reflecting barrier at the origin (reflected follower), is reduced to a question of optimal stopping with absorption. Direct probabilistic arguments are used to establish the equivalence of the two problems under suitable conditions on the cost functions.
Keywords
Brownian motion, optimal stopping, stochastic control, reflection of discontinuous trajectories,
