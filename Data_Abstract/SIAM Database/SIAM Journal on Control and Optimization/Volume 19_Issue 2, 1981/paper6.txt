Spectral Theory of the Linear-Quadratic Optimal Control Problem: Analytic Factorization of Rational Matrix-Valued Functions
Edmond A.  Jonckheere and Leonard M.  Silverman;
Abstract
The inversion of the Toeplitz operator $T_\Phi  $, associated with the operator-valued function $\Phi $ defined on the unit circle, is well known to involve a special factorization, called analytic factorization, of the function $\Phi $. New results and algorithms concerning this factorization are presented, in the special case where $\Phi $ is rational and matrix-valued.
