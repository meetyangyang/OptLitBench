Kantorovich Estimates for Some Convex Programs
A. A.  Goldstein and R. A.  Tapia;
Abstract
If C is a closed convex subset of a Hilbert space H, and f a real-valued function defined and twice Frechet differentiable on C, we consider whether, given a point $x_0  \in H$, it is possible to determine if a local minimizes of f in C lies nearby, and if so, to estimate its distance.
