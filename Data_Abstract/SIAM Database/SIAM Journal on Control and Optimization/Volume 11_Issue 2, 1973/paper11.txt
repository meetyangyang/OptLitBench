Extensions of the Concept of Regular Synthesis as a Sufficient Condition for Optimality
Thomas G.  Hack;
Abstract
A generalized regular synthesis is defined and examined using the approach of Boltyanskii [2]. Special attention is given to the action of the marked trajectories on the lower order manifolds. The yield of this analysis is twofold. First, sufficient conditions for the general autonomous control problem are obtained. In addition, the notion of a modified regular synthesis is exhibited as a second sufficient condition for optimality. The subsequent discussions show how the latter result can be applied to differential games.
