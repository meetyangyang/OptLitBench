The Stabilizing Solution of the Algebraic Riccati Equation
B. P.  Molinari;
Abstract
This paper investigates solutions $\hat X$ of the algebraic Riccati equation $F'X + XF - XGG'X + Q = 0$ with the property $\operatorname{Re} \lambda (F - GG'\hat X) \leqq 0$. The uniqueness and existence of such a solution is completely characterized. In addition, the conditions are strengthened to characterize the important stabilizing solution $(\operatorname{Re} \lambda (F - GG'\hat X) < 0)$. All standard existence results are recovered as special cases.
