Exact Boundary Controllability for 1‐D Quasilinear Wave Equations
Li   Tatsien and Yu   Lixin;
Abstract
In this paper we present a unified constructive method to establish the local exact boundary controllability for 1‐D quasilinear wave equations with boundary conditions of various types.
