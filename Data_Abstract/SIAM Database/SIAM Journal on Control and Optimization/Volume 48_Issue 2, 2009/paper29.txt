Semigroups of Affine Groups, Controllability of Affine Systems and Affine Bilinear Systems in $\mathrm{Sl}(2,\mathbb{R})\rtimes\mathbb{R}^{2}$
Osvaldo G. do  Rocio, Alexandre J.  Santana, and Marcos A.  Verdi;
Abstract
Let $G=B\rtimes V$ be an affine group given by a semidirect product of connected Lie group B and vector space V, where B has a transitive representation on V. We study semigroups of G with nonempty interior. As an application, we obtain a characterization of controllability of bilinear systems in the case of $\mathrm{Sl}(2,\mathbb{R})\rtimes\mathbb{R}^{2}$.
Keywords
controllability, affine systems, semigroups, semisimple Lie group, 93B05, 22E15, 20M20,
