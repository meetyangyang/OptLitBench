$H_\infty $ Boundary Control with State Feedback: The Hyperbolic Case
Viorel  Barbu;
Abstract
Necessary and sufficient conditions of existence for the $H_\infty $ control problem associated with infinite dimensional boundary control systems of hyperbolic type are given.
Keywords
boundary control systems, feedback controller, differential game, exponentially stable semigroup, 93B50, 93C35, 49A40,
