A Generalized Lagrange Multiplier Rule for Equality Constraints in Normed Linear Spaces
D. O.  Norris;
Abstract
It is shown that a necessary and sufficient condition for a differentiable function to have a critical point on an affine constraint set is that a Lagrange multiplier rule hold. The rule applies to problems which are not covered by the standard multiplier theorem. The results are extended to the determination of necessary conditions for a convex function to have a minimum on a nonaffine constraint set.
