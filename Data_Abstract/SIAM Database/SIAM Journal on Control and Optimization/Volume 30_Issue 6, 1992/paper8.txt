Extreme Points for Linear Optimal Control Problems with Diagonal Structure
Edward J.  Anderson and Andrew B.  Philpott;
Abstract
This paper discusses the extreme points of the feasible set for a certain type of optimal control problem with constraints on both the state and control variables. The problem is posed as a continuous-time linear program in the space of bounded measurable functions. The extreme points can be characterized by a certain full rank condition.
Keywords
