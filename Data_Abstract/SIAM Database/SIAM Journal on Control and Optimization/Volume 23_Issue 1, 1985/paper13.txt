Approximate Stabilizability via the Algebraic Riccati Equation
N.  Levan;
Abstract
This note will study stabilizability of Hilbert space $C_0 $ semigroups by means of a state feedback involving a solution of the algebraic Riccati equation. The notion of “approximate” stability is introduced and it is shown that stabilizability in this case is, in general, only approximate in the sense that the feedback semigroup is stable on a dense subspace—instead of on the whole space.
Keywords
stabilizability, algebraic Riccati equation, quasi-affine transforms of contraction semigroups,
