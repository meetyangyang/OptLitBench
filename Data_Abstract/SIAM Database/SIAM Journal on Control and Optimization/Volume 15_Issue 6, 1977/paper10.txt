Invertibility of Control Systems on Lie Groups
Ronald M.  Hirschorn;
Abstract
This paper gives necessary and sufficient conditions for the invertibility of a class of nonlinear systems which includes matrix bilinear systems. Lie algebraic invertibility criteria are obtained for bilinear systems in $R^n $ which generalize the standard tests for single input linear systems. These results are used to construct nonlinear systems which act as left-inverses for bilinear systems.
