On Lower Semicontinuity of Integral Functionals. II
A. D.  Ioffe;
Abstract
A necessary and sufficient condition for the integral functional $\smallint _G f(t,x(t),y(t))d\mu $ to be lower semicontinuous with respect to norm convergence of $x( \cdot )$-components in $L_s (G,R^m )$ and weak convergence of $y( \cdot )$-components in $L_q (G,R^n )(1 < q < \infty )$ is established.
