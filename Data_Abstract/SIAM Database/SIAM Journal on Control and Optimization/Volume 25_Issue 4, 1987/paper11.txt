A Class of Optimization Problems with Noncompact Constraints: General Results and Applications
Kjell  Holmåker and David  Stewart;
Abstract
tion problems recently studied in liver kinetics feature noncompact constraints and highly nonunique solutions. Here a wider class of problems with these properties is considered. It is shown that it is sufficient to solve the problem with an additional, compact constraint. From the solutions of this restricted problem all solutions of the original problem can be obtained. By means of this result and Pontryagin’s maximum principle, solutions are found for some problems of liver-kinetic interest.
