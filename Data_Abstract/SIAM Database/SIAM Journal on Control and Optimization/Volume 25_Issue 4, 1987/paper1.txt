Linear Control Theory with an ${\bf H}_\infty  $ Optimality Criterion
Bruce A.  Francis and John C.  Doyle;
Abstract
This expository paper sets out the principal results in ${\bf H}_\infty  $ control theory in the context of continuous-time linear systems. The focus is on the mathematical theory rather than computational methods.
Keywords
H∞ control theory, linear systems, 93,
