Optimization of “$\log x$” Entropy over Linear Equality Constraints
Yair  Censor and Arnold  Lent;
Abstract
In this paper we develop a special-purpose iterative algorithm, of the row-action type, for solving the problem of maximizing the “$\log x$” entropy functional over linear equality constraints. The algorithm employs “projections” onto hyperplanes which we call “$\log x$” entropy projections. A complete proof of convergence is given.
