A Study of Minimizing Sequences
E.  Polak and Y. Y.  Wardi;
Abstract
Differentiable and nondifferentiable optimization problems in normed spaces may fail to have solutions. Even when they have solutions, optimization algorithms may produce minimizing sequences that have no accumulation points. To deal with this difficulty, this paper examines optimization problems as problems on sequences, in an extended normed space, and derives first and second order optimality conditions for them.
Keywords
optimality conditions, extension of spaces, nondifferentiable optimization, nonlinear programming, optimal control,
