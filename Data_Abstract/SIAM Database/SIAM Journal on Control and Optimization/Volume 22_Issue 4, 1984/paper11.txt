Existence and Uniqueness of Minimal Realizations for a Class of $C^\infty  $ Systems
J. P.  Gauthier and G.  Bornard;
Abstract
The main result is relative to the quotient of a manifold M by a closed discrete equivalence relation when the first homotopy group of M has no infinite order element.As a direct consequence of the main theorem, the existence and uniqueness of minimal realizations is proved for a class of $C^\infty $ completely controllable weakly observable systems. This extends: • Some results of Sussmann available in the analytic or symmetric cases. • Some results of the authors obtained in the compact case.
