Linear Hilbert Networks Containing Finitely Many Nonlinear Elements
Vaclav  Dolezal;
Abstract
In this paper we establish conditions for the existence and uniqueness of a regime in a linear (finite or infinite) Hilbert network which contains several nonlinear elements. These conditions are given in terms of the driving point set impedance of the linear network, and the operator describing the nonlinear elements. They are easy to test in specific cases. Two examples illustrating the application are given.
