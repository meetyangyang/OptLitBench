State Convergence of Dissipative Nonlinear Systems Given Bounded-Energy Input Signal
Bayu  Jayawardhana;
Abstract
In this paper, we show the state convergence of a class of dissipative nonlinear systems given bounded-energy input functions $u:\mathbb{R}_+\to\mathbb{R}^m$, where the energy function is related to the dissipation inequality (or the supply rate).
