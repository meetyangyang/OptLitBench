Approximate Controllability for a Class of Semilinear Abstract Equations
Hong Xing  Zhou;
Abstract
In this paper a class of control systems governed by the semilinear abstract equation: $\dot y + Ay = F(y) + Bv$ is considered. A sufficient condition for the approximate controllability is obtained which improves J. Henry’s results on a nonlinear parabolic control systems with some more serious restrictions. It is suitable not only to the infinite-dimensional case but also to the finite-dimensional case. Two examples are given to explain the applications of the theory.
Keywords
semilinear abstract system, approximate controllability theory, infinite dimension examples,
