Sufficient Optimality Conditions for Dirichlet Boundary Control of Wave Equations
Andrzej  Nowakowski;
Abstract
We study optimal control problems for wave equations (focusing on the multidimensional wave equation) with control functions in the Dirichlet boundary conditions under pointwise control (and we admit state—by assuming weak hypotheses) constraints.
Keywords
