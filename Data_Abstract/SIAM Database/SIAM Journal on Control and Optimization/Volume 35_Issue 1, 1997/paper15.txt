A Farkas lemma Without A Standard Closure Condition
Jean B.  Lasserre;
Abstract
We present a setting in which we derive a new Farkas lemma for the system $\{Ax=b, x\in S\}$ without the standard closure condition on $A(S)$. Further characterization in Hilbert spaces is also presented.
Keywords
