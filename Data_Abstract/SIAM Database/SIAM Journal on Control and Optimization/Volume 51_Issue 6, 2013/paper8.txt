All Solutions of a Bitangential Interpolation Problem that Includes Boundary Points
Jovan  Stefanovski;
Abstract
All solutions of a matrix interpolation problem with purely imaginary interpolation points are given, by a descriptor system realization, if necessary and sufficient conditions are satisfied. The first step is to reduce the interpolation problem to a minimal realization. No assumptions are needed in the paper.
Keywords
interpolation, poles and zeros of rational matrices, matrix pencil, finite generalized eigenvalues, 41A05, 15A22, 15A18,
