Necessary Optimality Conditions with Application to a Variational Problem
Norbert  Christopeit;
Abstract
For an abstract optimization problem with operator constraints and vector-valued objective function, necessary optimality conditions of Kuhn–Tucker type are developed, and are then used to obtain a generalized Euler–Lagrange condition for the problem of Bolza with phase restrictions in the nonconvex and nondifferentiable case.
