Some Analytic and Measure Theoretic Properties of Set-Valued Mappings
Mary  Bradley and Richard  Datko;
Abstract
A theory of differentiation for set-valued mappings in a separable reflexive Banach space X is presented. The investigation is centered around the differentiability of the support functionals and thus only computation of limits of real functions is required. Our results include a Radon–Nikodym theorem for set-valued measures taking closed bounded convex values.
