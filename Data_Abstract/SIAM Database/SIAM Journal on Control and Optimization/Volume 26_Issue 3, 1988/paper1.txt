A Product Formula Approach to Nonlinear Optimal Control Problems
Viorel  Barbu;
Abstract
In this paper we prove the convergence of an iterative scheme of Lie–Trotter type for optimal control problems governed by nonlinear systems. The numerical implementation of the resulting algorithm is also discussed.
Keywords
Lie-Trotter product formula, maximal monotone operators, nonlinear semigroups, distributed control systems, free boundary problem, optimal control, Stefan problem, 35,
