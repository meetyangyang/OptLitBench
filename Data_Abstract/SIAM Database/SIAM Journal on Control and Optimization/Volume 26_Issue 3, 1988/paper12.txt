On the Mathematical Model for Linear Elastic Systems with Analytic Damping
Falun  Huang;
Abstract
In the present paper we investigate linear elastic systems with damping $\ddot y + B\dot y + Ay = 0$ in Hilbert spaces, where A is a positive definite unbounded linear operator and B is a closed linear operator related in various ways to $A^\alpha  (\frac{1}{2} \leqq \alpha  \leqq 1)$. We discuss the spectral property of these systems and obtain some fundamental results for the holomorphic property and the exponential stability of the semigroups associated with these systems.
Keywords
elastic systems, damping, exponential stability, holomorphic property, 34G, 93A, 93D,
