Behavior of Observations of Linear Control Systems
Roger C.  McCann;
Abstract
Given two linear control systems with reachable sets $R_1 (t)$, $R_2 (t)$, respectively, and a constant matrix M, directly verifiable necessary and sufficient conditions are obtained for the coincidence $R_1 (t) = MR_2 (t)$ for all $t > 0$.
