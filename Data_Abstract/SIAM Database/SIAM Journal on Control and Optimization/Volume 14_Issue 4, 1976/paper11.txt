A Boundary Value Criterion for Semipassive Hilbert Ports
L. P.  D’Amato and A. H.  Zemanian;
Abstract
Necessary and sufficient conditions on the boundary values of an operator-valued analytic function Y defined on the open right half-plane are given which ensure that Y is the Laplace transform of the unit-impulse response of a linear time-invariant causal semipassive Hilbert port. Similar results are also obtained for the more restricted case where the Hilbert port is linear time-invariant and passive.
