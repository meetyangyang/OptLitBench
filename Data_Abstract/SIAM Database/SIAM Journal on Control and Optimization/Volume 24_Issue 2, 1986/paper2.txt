The Generic Local Time-Optimal Stabilizing Controls in Dimension 3
Alberto  Bressan;
Abstract
This paper studies the control system \[\dot x(t) = X(x(t)) + Y(x(t))u(t),\qquad X(p_0 ) = 0,| {u(t)} | \leqq 1,\] where X and Y are $\mathcal{C}^\infty  $ vector fields on a 3-dimensional manifold $\mathcal{M}$. Under generic assumptions on X, Y, the structure of the time-optimal stabilizing controls is completely determined in a neighborhood of $p_0 $. The proofs rely on a systematic use of a local asymptotic approximation of X and Y by means of vector fields which generate a nilpotent Lie algebra.
