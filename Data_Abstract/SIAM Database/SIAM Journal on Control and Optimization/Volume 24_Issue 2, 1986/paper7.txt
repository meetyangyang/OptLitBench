Strong Controllability of Nonlinear Systems
Ronald M.  Hirschorn;
Abstract
The shape of the reachable set for an affine control system is studied by introducing trace vector fields, freely-controlled and semi-controlled vector fields. Properties of these classes of vector fields are established and they are related to the structure of the reachable set of states at time t. A method for generating semi-controlled vector fields from a trace vector fields and a freely-controlled vector field is presented.
