Quasi-Newton Methods and Unconstrained Optimal Control Problems
C. T.  Kelley and E. W.  Sachs;
Abstract
We prove a mesh-independence result for the BFGS method in Hilbert space and apply it to a class of unconstrained optimal control problems. A new fourth order discretization scheme is used in the implementation. Observations from numerical experiments are presented.
Keywords
