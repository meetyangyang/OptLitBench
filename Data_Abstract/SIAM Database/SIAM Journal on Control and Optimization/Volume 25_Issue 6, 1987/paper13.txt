Optimal Stabilization of Families of Linear Stochastic Differential Equations with Jump Coefficients and Multiplicative Noise
William E.  Hopkins, Jr.;
Abstract
Stabilization of families of finite-dimensional linear systems by constant linear feedback is studied by introducing a sequence of finite horizon parameter optimization problems. If the set of stabilizing constant linear feedback controllers is not empty and a regularity condition holds, a subsequence of solutions to the optimization problems converges to a constant stabilizing controller.
