On Nonlinear Optimal Control Problems with State Constraints
Monica  Motta;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with an optimal control problem where the state is constrained to stay either in a smooth open set $\Omega $ or in its closure $\bar \Omega $ Under a “higher-order” sufficient condition for the viability of $\Omega $ and $\bar \Omega $, we prove that the optimal cost function $v_\Omega  $ is the unique continuous constrained solution of the Hamilton–Jacobi–Bellman equation. Furthermore, we show that $v_\Omega  $ coincides with the optimal cost function $v_{\bar \Omega } $ on $\Omega $
