Stabilizability Does Not Imply Homogeneous Stabilizability for Controllable Homogeneous Systems
Rodolphe  Sepulchre and Dirk  Aeyels;
Abstract
This paper presents an example of a homogeneous planar controllable system which is stabilizable while not stabilizable by homogeneous feedback. Addition of an integrator to the system provides a three-dimensional affine system with the same properties.
