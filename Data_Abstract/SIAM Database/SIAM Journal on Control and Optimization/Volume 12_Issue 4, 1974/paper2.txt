A General Theory of Convergence for Constrained Optimization Algorithms that Use Antizigzagging Provisions
R.  Klessig;
Abstract
A general convergence theory for algorithms that use an antizigzagging parameter is presented. Certain relationships between this new theory and convergence theories of Polak and Zangwill are established. Applications of the theory are briefly discussed and these applications indicate that the new theory has a wider range of applicability than either the Polak or Zangwill theories.
