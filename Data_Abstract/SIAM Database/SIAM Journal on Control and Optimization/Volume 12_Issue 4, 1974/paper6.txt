An Interior Penalty Method for Minimax Problems with Constraints
Hitoshi  Sasai;
Abstract
In this paper the interior penalty function method is applied to the minimax problems. Fiacco and McCormick’s penalty technique is used in order to solve the minimax problems with side constraints.As a result, the constrained minimax problem is reduced to the one solving a sequence of unconstrained approximation problems and a new computational algorithm is established.
