On a Class of Diffeomorphic Matching Problems in One Dimension
Alain  Trouvé and Laurent  Younes;
Abstract
We study a class of functional which can be used for matching objects which can be represented as mappings from a fixed interval, I, to some "feature space." This class of functionals corresponds to "elastic matching" in which a symmetry condition and a "focus invariance" are imposed. We provide sufficient conditions under which an optimal matching can be found between two such mappings, the optimal matching being a homeomorphism of the interval I. The differentiability of this matching is also studied, and an application to plane curve comparison is provided.
