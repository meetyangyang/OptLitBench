Multiscale Singularly Perturbed Control Systems: Limit Occupational Measures Sets and Averaging
Vladimir  Gaitsgory and Minh-Tuan  Nguyen;
Abstract
An averaging technique for nonlinear multiscale singularly perturbed control systems is developed. Issues concerning the existence and structure of limit occupational measures sets generated by such systems are discussed. General results are illustrated with special cases.
Keywords
