On Differential Games of Fixed Duration with Phase Coordinate Restrictions on One Player
K.  Haji-Ghassemi;
Abstract
This paper considers differential games of fixed duration in which state constraints described by a given closed set ${\bf E}$ are imposed on one of the players. Using Berkovitz’s definition of a game, the existence of the value is obtained first and, under mild conditions, the existence of saddle points. Sufficient conditions are then given for the value to be continuous or Lipschitz continuous.
