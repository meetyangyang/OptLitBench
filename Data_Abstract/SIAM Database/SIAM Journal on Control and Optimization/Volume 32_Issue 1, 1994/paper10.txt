On the Existence of Optimal controls of Hilbert Space-Valued Diffusions
Dariusz  Gatarek and Jarosław  Sobczyk;
Abstract
An optimal control problem is studied for a Hilbert space-valued diffusion. Existence of an optimal control in the class of relaxed controls is proved. As a tool, the factorization method is used. Also, a simple example is given.
Keywords
optimal control, diffusion processes in Hilbert spaces, existence theory, relaxed controls,
