Redheffer’s Lemma and $H_\infty  $-Control for Infinite-Dimensional Systems
Bert  van Keulen;
Abstract
An infinite-dimensional version of a lemma that has been crucial in the theory of $H_\infty  $-control with measurement-feedback for finite-dimensional systems is proved. This extension is used to parametrize all controllers that solve the suboptimal $H_\infty  $-control problem for a large class of infinite-dimensional systems.
Keywords
Redheffer’s lemma, H∞-control, infinite-dimensional state-space systems, 93B36, 93C25,
