A Finite Set of $n \times n$ Stochastic Matrices Generating all n-Dimensional Probability Vectors Whose Coordinates Have Finite Binary Expansion
A.  Paz;
Abstract
For any integer n, it is proved that there exists a finite set of stochastic matrices such that any n-dimensional probabilistic vector, the entries of which have finite binary expansion, can be realized as a row in a finite product of those matrices. The matrices are given explicitly and an algorithm is provided for finding the realization of a given vector.
