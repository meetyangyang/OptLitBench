Stabilizability of Time-Periodic Parabolic Equations
Alessandra  Lunardi;
Abstract
Second-order parabolic equations with time-periodic coefficients in $[0, + \infty [ \times \Omega $ ($\Omega $ is a regular bounded domain in ${\bf R}^n $) are considered, with input acting either in the interior or on the boundary of $\Omega $. Stabilizability results that are similar to some of the known results concerning the autonomous case are given. Abstract evolution equations techniques are employed.
