Some Results for an Optimal Control Problem with Semilinear State Equation
Fausto  Gozzi;
Abstract
A control problem governed by a semilinear state eqution depending on a small parameter $\varepsilon $ is considered. It is proven that, for $\varepsilon $ sufficiently small, the associated Hamilton–Jacobi equation has a unique strict solution; consequently, the control problem can be solved by dynamic programming method.
Keywords
