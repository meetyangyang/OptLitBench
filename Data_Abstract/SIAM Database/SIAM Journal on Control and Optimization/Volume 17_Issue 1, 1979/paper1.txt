A Note on the Existence of a Nash Equilibrium Point in Stochastic Differential Games
Kenko  Uchida;
Abstract
Using the implicit function lemma of Beneš [SIAM J. Control, 1970] we derive a sufficient condition for the existence of a Nash equilibrium point in a feedback form in N-person stochastic differential games, which is an addendum to the existence theorem given by the author [SIAM J. Control, 1978].
