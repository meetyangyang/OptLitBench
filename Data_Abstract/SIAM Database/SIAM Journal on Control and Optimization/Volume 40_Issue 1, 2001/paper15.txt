Uniform Robust Performance Against Quasi-LTI Uncertainty in Sampled-Data Systems
Sean E.  Bourdon and Geir E.  Dullerud;
Abstract
Uniform robust performance of sampled-data systems is studied in the context ofarbitrarily slowly time-varying structured perturbations. Exact conditions for robustness are obtained. These conditions are convex but inherently infinite dimensional in nature.
Keywords
sampled-data systems, quasi-LTI, structured uncertainty, periodic systems, 93C57, 93D09,
