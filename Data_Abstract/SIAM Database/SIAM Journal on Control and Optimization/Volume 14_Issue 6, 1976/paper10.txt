Relations Among the Multipliers for Problems with Bounded State Constraints
I. Bert  Russak;
Abstract
In previous articles, the author established certain necessary conditions for control problems with constraints of the form $\psi ^\alpha  (t,x) \leqq 0$$\alpha  = 1, \cdots ,m$. These conditions involve certain multiplier functions $\mu _\alpha  (t)$ of the derivatives of the above constraints together with multiplier constants $K^\alpha  $ used in the transversality relation. In this paper, it is shown that these terms satisfy $\mu _\alpha  (t^0 ) \leqq K^\alpha  $ with $\mu _\alpha  (t^0 ) = K^\alpha  $ if $\psi ^\alpha  (t^0 ) < 0$.
