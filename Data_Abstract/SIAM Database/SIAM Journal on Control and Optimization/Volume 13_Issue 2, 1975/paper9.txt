On the Concept of Symmetry in Pontryagin’s Maximum Principle
Mineo  Ikeda and Kentaro  Sakamoto;
Abstract
From the standpoint of Lie group theory, the concept of symmetry (invariance) is introduced for a class of dynamical control problems which are treated on the basis of Pontryagin’s maximum principle. The so-called linear first integrals of a holonomic dynamical system are extended to the framework of the maximum principle, leading to the concept of special linear first integrals. The symmetry group of a control system can be generated by a set of these integrals. Particular attention is paid to the case where the dynamical system is a natural one, and it is shown that the kine-matical symmetry group of a natural system is locally isomorphic to the symmetry group of the corresponding control system. These results are applied to the problem of optimum trajectories for space navigation.
