Second Order Necessary Conditions for Problems with State Inequality Constraints
I. Bert  Russak;
Abstract
A set of second order necessary conditions is obtained for solution to a basic problem in optimal control involving the state constraints \[\psi ^\alpha  (t,x) \leqq 0,\quad alpha  = 1, \cdots ,m.\] For the case of a normal solution arc, the methods of Hestenes are used to show that the second variation of a certain functional is nonnegative on a class of variations of the solution arc. The results for this problem are basic in the sense that they can be extended to include analogous results for much more complicated problems.
