The Singularly Perturbed Linear State Regulator Problem. II
R. E.  O’Malley and C. F.  Kung;
Abstract
We consider regulator problems for the singularly perturbed system \[\begin{gathered}  \frac{{dx}}{{dt}} = A_1 (t,\varepsilon )x + A_2 (t,\varepsilon )z + B_1 (t,\varepsilon )u, \hfill \\  \varepsilon \frac{{dz}}{{dt}} = A_3 (t,\varepsilon )x + A_4 (t,\varepsilon )z + B_2 (t,\varepsilon )u \hfill \\ \end{gathered} \] on $0 \leqq t \leqq 1$, seeking their asymptotic solution as $\varepsilon  \to 0$. The hypotheses used are simple. Results are related to the current literature.
