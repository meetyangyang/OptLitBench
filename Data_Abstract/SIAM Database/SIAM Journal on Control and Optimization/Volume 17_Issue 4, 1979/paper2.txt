Determination of Unknown Functions for a Class of Distributed Parameter Systems
Toshihiro  Kobayashi;
Abstract
The aim of this paper is to investigate the determination of unknown functions which give the input distributions for distributed parameter systems. After the problem formulation, the identifiability of input-distribution functions is discussed. The identifiability of the system does not necessarily assure that the problem of input-function determination is well posed. A well-posed approximation method is presented and discussed from the standpoint of regularization. The relation between the observability and the identifiability is also clarified for the system described by a linear parabolic partial differential equation.
