Asymptotic Stability of Infinite-Dimensional Discrete-Time Balanced Realizations
Raimund  Ober and Yuanyin  Wu;
Abstract
The question of power and asymptotic stability of infinite-dimensional discrete-time state space systems is investigated. It is shown that every balanced realization is asymptotically stable. Conditions are given for balanced, input normal, or output normal realizations to be asymptotically and/or power stable.
Keywords
