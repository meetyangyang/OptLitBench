Representations of Symmetric Linear Dynamical Systems
Fabio  Fagnani and Jan  Willems;
Abstract
The purpose of this paper is to study static symmetries in linear time-invariant differential dynamical systems. The main result is a representation theorem which brings the symmetry strongly into evidence. This result is then applied to a number of examples involving permutations and rotations. We close by proving a general result on the representation of compact groups on the ring of unimodular polynomial matrices.
Keywords
