Convexity of the Range of Certain Integrals
Lamberto  Cesari;
Abstract
In the line of Lyapunov’s convexity theorem, the author considers the subsets H and K obtained from any number h of given integrable n-vector functions by all possible set decompositions and by all possible convex combinations respectively and integration. Both sets are closed and convex, and the author gives a simple proof that $H = K$.
