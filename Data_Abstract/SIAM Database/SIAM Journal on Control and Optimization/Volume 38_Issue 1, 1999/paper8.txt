Indirect Obstacle Control Problem for Semilinear Elliptic Variational Inequalities
Qihong  Chen;
Abstract
An optimal control problem for a coupled system of a semilinear elliptic equation and an obstacle variational inequality is considered. Existence and optimality conditions of optimal triple are established.
Keywords
optimal control, existence, optimality condition, elliptic obstacle variational inequality, 49J20, 49K20,
