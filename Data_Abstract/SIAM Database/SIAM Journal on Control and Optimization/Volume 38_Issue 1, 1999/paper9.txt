Some Results on the Bellman Equation of Ergodic Control
Hiroaki  Morimoto and Masami  Okada;
Abstract
We study the multidimensional Bellman equation of ergodic control for diffusion processes without invariant measures and establish the existence of a unique solution in a certain class.
Keywords
