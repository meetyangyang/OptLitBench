Infinite Horizon Stochastic Programs
Richard C.  Grinold;
Abstract
This paper considers a discrete time infinite horizon stochastic program with the objective of minimizing the expected present value of a sum of convex cost functions. In any finite time we assume that only a finite number of outcomes can occur. The paper presents a sequence of solvable finite horizon problems that converge in value and decision to the value and decision of the infinite problem.
Keywords
