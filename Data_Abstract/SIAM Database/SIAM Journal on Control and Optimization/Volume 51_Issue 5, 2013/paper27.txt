Maximum Principle and Bang-Bang Property of Time Optimal Controls for Schrödinger-Type Systems
Jérôme  Lohéac and Marius  Tucsnak;
Abstract
We consider the time optimal control problem, with a point target, for a class of infinite dimensional systems with a dynamics governed by an abstract Schrödinger-type equation. The main results establish a Pontryagin-type maximum principle and give sufficient conditions for the bang-bang property of optimal controls. The results are then applied to some systems governed by partial differential equations. The paper ends with a discussion of possible extensions and by stating some open problems.
Keywords
