State Constrained Control Problems Governed by Variational Inequalities
Zheng-Xu  He;
Abstract
We will consider various types of problems of minimizing some given cost functional over all pairs of states and controls subject to some parabolic or elliptic variational inequality and some state constraints. Our aim is to derive the first order optimality conditions for optimal pairs of these problems.
Keywords
optimal control, state constraints, parabolic variational inequality, elliptic variational inequality, optimal pair, optimality conditions, 49B22, 49A29,
