Optimal Control for a Backward Parabolic System
P. H.  Rivera and C. F.  Vasconcellos;
Abstract
In this paper the authors study two problems of optimal control for systems governed by a backward parabolic equation, when the $\mathcal{U}_{{\text{ad}}} $ has nonempty interior.
Keywords
optimal control, backward equation, nonwell-posed problem, 49A22, 35K99,
