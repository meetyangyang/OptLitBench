Finite-Time Global Stabilization by Means of Time-Varying Distributed Delay Feedback
Iasson  Karafyllis;
Abstract
The paper contains certain results concerning the finite-time global stabilization for triangular control systems described by retarded functional differential equations by means of time-varying distributed delay feedback. These results enable us to present solutions to feedback stabilization problems for systems with delayed input. The results are obtained by using the backstepping technique.
Keywords
