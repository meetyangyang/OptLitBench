Solution of Optimal Control Problems by a Pointwise Projected Newton Method
C. T.  Kelley and E. W.  Sachs;
Abstract
In the context of optimal control of ordinary differential equations, we prove local superlinear convergence and constraint identification results for an extension of the projected Newton method of Bertsekas. The estimates are also valid for discretized versions of the method-problem pair
