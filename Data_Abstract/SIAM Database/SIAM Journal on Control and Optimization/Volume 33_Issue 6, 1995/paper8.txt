Periodic Stability of Nonlinear Flexible Systems with Damping
Koichiro  Naito;
Abstract
We study periodic stability of solutions of nonlinear elastic systems with damping under periodic perturbations. Investigating the sufficient conditions for the stability, we find some inequality relations between the system parameters of its linear term and those of its nonlinear term.
Keywords
stability, periodic perturbation, nonlinear beam equation, damping, 35B10, 73D35, 73K05,
