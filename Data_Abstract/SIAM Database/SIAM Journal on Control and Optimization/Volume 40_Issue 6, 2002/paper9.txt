A Constraint on the Maximum Reflectance of Rapidly Oscillating Dielectric Gratings
Gang  Bao, David C.  Dobson, and Karim  Ramdani;
Abstract
When considering optimal design problems involving diffraction gratings, it is useful to have some a priori characterization of the range of possible reflectances one can achieve for given material parameters. Here we consider the limiting case of a rapidly oscillating dielectric grating and show that such gratings can have reflectance no greater than that of a flat interface, regardless of the shape of the grating interface.
