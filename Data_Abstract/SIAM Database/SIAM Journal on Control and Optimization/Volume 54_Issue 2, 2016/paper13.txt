$H_\infty$-Stability Analysis of Fractional Delay Systems of Neutral Type
Le Ha Vy  Nguyen, Catherine  Bonnet, and André Ricardo  Fioravanti;
Abstract
In this paper we consider linear fractional systems of commensurate orders and with commensurate delays, whose characteristic equation is a polynomial in the two variables $s^\alpha$ $(0 < \alpha < 1)$ and $e^{- s \tau}$ ($\tau > 0$). These systems may have single or multiple chains of poles asymptotic to the imaginary axis. Location of poles of large modulus belonging to these chains are determined by approximation and simple necessary and sufficient $H_\infty$-stability conditions are derived.
Keywords
fractional systems, neutral systems, delay effects, $H_\infty$-stability, 93C23, 93D25, 93C05,
