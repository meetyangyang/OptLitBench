Admissibility of the Observation Operators for Volterra systems with Exponential Kernels
Jian-Hua  Chen;
Abstract
We study admissibility of the observation operators for Volterra systems in Hilbert spaces with exponentially decaying kernels. Sufficient conditions for infinite-time admissibility of the observation operators are established and some illustrating examples are given.
Keywords
Volterra equation, operator semigroup, resolvent, admissibility, exponential memory kernel, 45K05, 45N05, 93B99,
