Stationary  Mean Field Games Systems Defined on Networks
Fabio  Camilli and Claudio  Marchi;
Abstract
We consider a stationary mean field games system constrained on a network. According to the optimal control interpretation of the problem, some transition conditions on the vertices are imposed. We prove separately the well-posedness for each of the two equations composing the system. Finally, we prove  existence and uniqueness of the solution of the mean field games system.
Keywords
mean field games, networks, transition conditions, 35R02, 35B30, 49N70,
