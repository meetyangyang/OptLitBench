A Bang-Bang Theorem with Bounds on the Number of Switchings
Héctor J.   Sussmann;
Abstract
For systems of the form $\dot x = f(x) + ug(x)$, with f and g analytic, and $ - 1 \leqq u \leqq 1$, we prove a bang-bang theorem with a priori bounds on the number of switchings, provided that the following condition is satisfied: in a neighborhood of every point x, it is possible to express, for each j, the vector field $[g,({\text{ad }}f)^j (g)]$ as a linear combination of the $({\text{ad }}f)^i (g)$, $i \leqq j + 1$, in such a way that the coefficient of $({\text{ad }}f)^{^j  + 1} (g)$ in this expression is bounded in absolute value by a constant $c < 1$.
