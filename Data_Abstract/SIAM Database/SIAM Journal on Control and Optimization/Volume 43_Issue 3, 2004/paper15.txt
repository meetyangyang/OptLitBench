Elimination of Strict Convergence in Optimization
Dusan  Bednarík and Karel  Pastor;
Abstract
We present second-order optimality conditions (sufficient and necessary) and the characterization of convexity in terms of generalized second-order derivatives. Some conclusions obtained by R. Cominetti and R. Correa are proved under weakened assumptions.
Keywords
