Controllability of Poisson Systems
Petre  Birtea, Mircea  Puta, and Tudor S.  Ratiu;
Abstract
Sufficient conditions for the controllability of affine nonlinear control systems on Poisson manifolds are given. The important special case when the Poisson manifold is the reduced space of a symplectic manifold by a free Lie group action is studied. The controllability of the reduced system is linked to that of the given affine nonlinear system. Several examples illustrating the theory are also presented.
