A Necessary and Sufficient Condition for Bounded-Input Bounded-State Stability of Nonlinear Systems
Andrea  Bacciotti and Luisa  Mazzi;
Abstract
The main purpose of this work is to prove a converse theorem for bounded-input bounded-state stability of nonlinear systems in the framework of Liapunov's second method. The construction gives rise to an upper semicontinuous time-dependent Liapunov function. In certain cases, the monotonicity conditions can be checked by means of contingent directional derivatives.
Keywords
Liapunov functions, external stability, nonlinear systems, 93D25, 93C10, 93D05,
