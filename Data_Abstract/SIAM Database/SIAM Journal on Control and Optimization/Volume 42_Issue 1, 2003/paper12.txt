Existence of Solutions to Differential Inclusions and to Time Optimal Control Problems in the Autonomous Case
Arrigo  Cellina and António  Ornelas;
Abstract
We prove existence of solutions to upper semicontinuous differential inclusions and to time optimal control problems under conditions that are strictly weaker than the usual assumption of convexity.
Keywords
differential inclusions, time optimal control problems, 49J15, 34A34, 34A36,
