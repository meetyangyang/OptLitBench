Variational Inclusions under State Constraints
Moulka  Tamzali-Lafond;
Abstract
We extend variational equations of ODEs to nonconvex differential inclusions under state constraints. As an application, we provide a direct proof of the maximum principle of the Pontryagin type for an optimal control problem with state constraints.
Keywords
