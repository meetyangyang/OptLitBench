A Case of Regularity of Solutions to Nonregular Problems
Arrigo  Cellina;
Abstract
We consider the regularity of solutions to the problem of minimizing $\int_{\Omega} [ L(\nabla v(x)) + g(x,v(x))]dx  \mbox { on }   u^0+W^{1,2}_0(\Omega),$ where $L$ is  not necessarily differentiable at the origin.
Keywords
regularity of solutions, nondifferentiable Lagrangians, higher differentiability, 49K10, 76M30,
