Asymptotic Properties of Feedback Solutions for a Class of Quantum Control Problems
Kazufumi  Ito and Karl  Kunisch;
Abstract
Control of quantum systems described by the Schrödinger equation is considered. Feedback control laws are developed for orbit tracking via controlled Hamiltonians, and their asymptotic properties are analyzed. Numerical integration via time splitting is also investigated and used to demonstrate the feasibility of the proposed feedback laws.
Keywords
feedback control, asymptotic tracking, Schrödinger equations, 93B11, 93B52, 49N35,
