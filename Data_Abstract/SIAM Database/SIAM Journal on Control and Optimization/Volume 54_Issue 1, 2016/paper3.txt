Criteria of Kalman's Type to the Approximate Controllability and the Approximate Synchronization for a Coupled System of Wave Equations with Dirichlet Boundary Controls
Tatsien  Li and Bopeng  Rao;
Abstract
In this paper the authors obtain  necessary conditions, presented as criteria of Kalman's type, to the approximate null controllability, the approximate synchronization, and the approximate synchronization by groups, respectively, for a coupled system of wave equations with Dirichlet boundary controls. The sufficiency of these conditions is also  established for $N\times N$ cascade systems,  for some $2\times 2$ systems,  as well as  for diagonalizable systems  in the one-space-dimensional case.
