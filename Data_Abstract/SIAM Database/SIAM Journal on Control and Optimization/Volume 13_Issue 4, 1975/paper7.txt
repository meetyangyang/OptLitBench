Bilinear and Nonlinear Realizations of Input-Output Maps
Arthur J.  Krener;
Abstract
Given a nonlinear realization of an input-output map, sufficient conditions are given for the existence of an equivalent bilinear realization for small t. It is also shown that every nonlinear realization can be approximated by a bilinear realization, with an error that grows like an arbitrary power of t.
