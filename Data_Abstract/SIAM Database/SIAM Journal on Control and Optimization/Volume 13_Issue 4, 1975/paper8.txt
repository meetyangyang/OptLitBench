Differential Games and a Nash Equilibrium Searching Algorithm
L. F.  Pau;
Abstract
Nonlinear nonzero sum differential games with fixed playing time are considered. They are transformed into a two-level hierarchical game, with a supremal referee optimizing an overall objective function in order to improve the respect of the Nash playing rule. We propose an algorithm, based on constraint coordination, in order to approximate open-loop Nash equilibrium controls. The theory illustrating this algorithm is presented.
