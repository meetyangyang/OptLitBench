Regulation of Linear Stochastic Systems
J.  Snyders and W. M.  Wonham;
Abstract
Linear constant dynamic systems with noisy measured output y and a second output z (the one to be regulated), are considered. Sufficient conditions for the existence of an estimated-state feedback that assures mean-square boundedness on $[0,\infty )$ of z are derived, the estimation being based on y. The part of the problem related to boundedness of the estimation error is more thoroughly investigated, and necessary and sufficient conditions are obtained. Necessary and sufficient conditions for boundedness of the solution to a Riccati equation are included in this result.
