Recursive System Identification and Adaptive Control by Use of the Modified Least Squares Algorithm
H. F.  Chen;
Abstract
In this paper we first use a weaker than persistent excitation condition to establish the strong consistency of MLS for MIMO stochastic systems without monitoring, and give a comparison between various conditions for consistency. Then we show the global convergence for adaptive tracking based on MLS and finally the adaptive tracking and strong consistency results are combined.
Keywords
system identification, strong consistency, least squares, adaptive tracking,
