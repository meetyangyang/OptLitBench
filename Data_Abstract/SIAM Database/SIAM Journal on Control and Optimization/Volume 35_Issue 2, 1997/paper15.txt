Partial Exact Controllability for Spherical Membranes
Paola  Loreti and Vanda  Valente;
Abstract
In this paper the partial exact controllability of an elastic spherical membrane is proved. The reachability problem for the integrodifferential equation, introduced for the vibrations of the meridional displacement, is solved. The main result is a generalization of a Ingham's theorem on nonharmonic Fourier series.
Keywords
partial exact controllability, reachability problem, almost periodic functions, 35Q99, 49E15,
