Comments on “Identifiability of Spatially Varying and Constant Parameters in Distributed Systems of Parabolic Type”
M.  Courdesses;
Abstract
In the paper [SIAM J. Control and Optim., 15 (1977), pp. 785–802], S. Kitamura and S. Nakagiri gave results on identifiability of constant parameters of a system described by a linear, one-dimensional parabolic partial differential equation with pointwise measurement. Unfortunately, their expression of the solution seems to be incorrect and consequently some of their results are, too. A new expression for the solution is given here, as well as a necessary and sufficient condition for identifiability.
