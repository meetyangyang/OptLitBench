Regularity of the Optimal Stopping Problem for Jump Diffusions
Erhan  Bayraktar and Hao  Xing;
Abstract
The value function of an optimal stopping problem for jump diffusions is known to be a generalized solution of a variational inequality. Assuming that the diffusion component of the process is nondegenerate and a mild assumption on the singularity of the Lévy measure, this paper shows that the value function of this optimal stopping problem on an unbounded domain with finite/infinite variation jumps is in $W^{2,1}_{p, loc}$ with $p\in(1, \infty)$. As a consequence, the smooth-fit property holds.
Keywords
optimal stopping, variational inequality, Lévy processes, regularity of the value function, smooth-fit principle, Sobolev spaces, 60G40, 60J75, 35R35, 45K05,
