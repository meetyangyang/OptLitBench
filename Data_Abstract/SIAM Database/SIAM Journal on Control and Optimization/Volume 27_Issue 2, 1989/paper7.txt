Exact Boundary Controllability of Maxwell’s Equations in a General Region
John E.  Lagnese;
Abstract
By the Hilbert uniqueness method, it is proved that the evolution of solutions of Maxwell’s equations in a general region can be exactly controlled by means of currents flowing tangentially in the boundary of the region.
Keywords
