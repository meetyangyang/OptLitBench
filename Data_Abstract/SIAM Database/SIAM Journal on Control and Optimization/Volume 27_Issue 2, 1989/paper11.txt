Optimal Control of Semilinear Multistate Systems with State Constraints
Joseph Frederic  Bonnans and Eduardo  Casas;
Abstract
This paper deals with state-constrained optimal control problems governed by a semilinear multistate equation. The authors prove the existence of solutions and derive optimality conditions.
Keywords
optimal control, subdiflerential calculus, optimality conditions, elliptic operators, semilinear equations, multistate systems, 49B22, 49A22,
