Optimal Control of Linear Periodic Resonant Systems in Hilbert Spaces
Viorel  Barbu;
Abstract
This work concerns existence, the maximum principle, and synthesis for optimal control problems with linear periodic dynamics in Hilbert spaces in the resonant case. Applications to distributed and boundary periodic control problems are given.
Keywords
distributed optimal control problems, maximum principle, closed range, p-stabilizable, 93B50, 93C35, 93C05,
