Local Controllability and Infinitesimal Generators of Semigroups of Set-Valued Maps
Halina  Frankowska;
Abstract
We study the semigroup properties of reachable sets of a differential inclusion \[x' \in F(x)\] using the derivative of the set-valued map which associates to each initial state the set of solutions. The results are applied to the local controllability problem.
Keywords
differential inclusion, local controllability, reachable set, derivative of solution with respect to initial condition, derivative of a set-valued map, generalized tangent cone, Clarke’s tangent cone, 49A34, 49A50, 93B05,
