A Constructive Algorithm for Sensitivity Optimization of Periodic Systems
Tryphon T.  Georgiou and Pramod P.  Khargonekar;
Abstract
In a recent paper (A. Feintuch, P. P. Khargonekar and A. Tannenbaum, On the sensitivity minimization problem for linear time-varying periodic systems, this Journal, 24 (1986), pp. 1076–1085), the problem of weighted sensitivity optimization was considered for linear, discrete-time, periodic time-varying systems. Here we present a constructive algorithm for solving this problem.
