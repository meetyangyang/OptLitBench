Pathwise Nonlinear Filtering for Nondegenerate Diffusions with Noise Correlation
M. H. A.  Davis and M. P.  Spathopoulos;
Abstract
We consider the filtering problem of calculating $E[f(x_t )|y_s ,s \leqq t]$ where $x_t $ is a nondegenerate diffusion process on a finite dimensional manifold with given generator and $y_t  = \int_0^t {h(x_s )ds + w_t^0 } $ is a real valued observation process. $w_t^0 $ is a scalar Brownian motion whose correlation with $x_t $ is specified by a given vector field Z. It is shown that this filtering problem is robust, i.e., the estimates for $x_t $ can be calculated separately for each observation sample path $y_t $.
Keywords
nonlinear filtering, noise correlation, conditional densities, Markov process, extended generator, stochastic differential equation, manifold, horizontal lifting, 93E11, 60H20, 60J60,
