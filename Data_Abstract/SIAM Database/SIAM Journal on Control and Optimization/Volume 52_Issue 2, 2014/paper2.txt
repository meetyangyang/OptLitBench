The Multiplayer Nonzero-Sum Dynkin Game in Continuous Time
Said  Hamadène and Hassani  Mohammed;
Abstract
In this paper we study the N-player nonzero-sum Dynkin game $(N\geq 3)$ in continuous time, which is a noncooperative game where the strategies of the players are stopping times or pure strategies. We show that the game has a Nash equilibrium point for general payoff processes.
