Sub-Riemannian Geometry of Stiefel Manifolds
Christian  Autenried and Irina  Markina;
Abstract
In the paper we consider the Stiefel manifold $V_{n,k}$ as a principal $U(k)$-bundle over the Grassmann manifold and study the cut locus from the unit element. We give the complete description of this cut locus on $V_{n,1}$ and present a sufficient condition on the general case. At the end, we study the complement to the cut locus of $V_{2k,k}$.
Keywords
