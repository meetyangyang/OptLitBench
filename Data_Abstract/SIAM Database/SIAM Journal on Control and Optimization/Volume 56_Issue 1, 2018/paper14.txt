Solvability of the Nonlinear Dirichlet Problem with Integro-differential Operators
Erhan  Bayraktar  and Qingshuo  Song;
Abstract
This paper analyzes the solvability of a class of elliptic nonlinear Dirichlet problems with jumps. The contribution of the paper is the construction of the supersolution required in Perron's method. This is achieved by solving the exit time problem of an Itô jump diffusion. The proof of this relies on the proof of continuity of the entrance time and point with respect to the Skorokhod topology.
Keywords
