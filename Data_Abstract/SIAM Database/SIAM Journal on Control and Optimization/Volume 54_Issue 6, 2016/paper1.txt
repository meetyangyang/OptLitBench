Bang-Bang Property of Time Optimal Null Controls for Some Semilinear Heat Equation
Lijuan  Wang and Qishu  Yan;
Abstract
In this paper, we derive a bang-bang property of  a kind of time optimal control problem for some semilinear heat equation on bounded $C^2$ domains (of the Euclidean space), with homogeneous Dirichlet boundary condition and controls distributed on an open and nonempty subset of the domain where the equation evolves.
Keywords
