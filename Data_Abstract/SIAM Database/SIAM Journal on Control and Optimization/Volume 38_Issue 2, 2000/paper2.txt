Minimization of Functionals of the Gradient by Baire's Theorem
Sandro  Zagatti;
Abstract
We give sufficient conditions for the existence of solutions of the minimum problem $$ {\cal P}_{u_0}: \qquad \hbox{Minimize}\quad \int_\Omega g(Du(x))dx, \quad u\in u_0 + W_0^{1,p}(\Omega,{\Bbb R}), $$ based on the structure of the epigraph of the lower convex envelope of g, which is assumed be lower semicontinuous and to grow at infinity faster than the power p with p larger than the dimension of the space. No convexity conditions are required on g, and no assumptions are made on the boundary datum $u_0\in W_0^{1,p}(\Omega,{\Bbb R})$.
Keywords
