Optimal Input Design for Dynamic System Parameter Estimation: The $D_s$-Optimality Case
Z. H.  Qureshi and T. S.  Ng;
Abstract
The problem of optimal input design for estimating part of the parameters of a dynamic system is considered. We show that two design criteria, $G_s $-optimality and $D_s $-optimality are equivalent. Furthermore, a proven sequential design algorithm is extended to cover this case.
