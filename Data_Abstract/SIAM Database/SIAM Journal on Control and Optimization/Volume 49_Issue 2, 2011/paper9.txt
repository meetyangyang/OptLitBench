Stability of Neutral Systems with Commensurate Delays and Poles Asymptotic to the Imaginary Axis
Catherine  Bonnet, André R.  Fioravanti, and Jonathan R.  Partington;
Abstract
This paper addresses the $H_\infty$-stability analysis of neutral time-delay systems with multiple commensurate delays, including those with poles asymptotic to the imaginary axis. The location of asymptotic poles is completely described, and easy-to-check necessary and sufficient conditions of $H_\infty$-stability are derived. Robustness relative to a change in the delay or the parameters is discussed. Moreover, $H_\infty$-stabilizability by rational controllers is considered, and, finally, several examples are given.
Keywords
