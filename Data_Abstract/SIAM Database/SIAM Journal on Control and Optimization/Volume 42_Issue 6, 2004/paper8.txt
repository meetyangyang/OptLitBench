Necessary Optimality Conditions in Multiobjective Dynamic Optimization
SaÏd  Bellaassali and Abderrahim  Jourani;
Abstract
We consider a nonsmooth multiobjective optimal control problem related to a general preference. Both differential inclusion and endpoint constraints are involved. Necessary conditions and Hamiltonian necessary conditions expressed in terms of the limiting Fréchet subdifferential are developed. Examples of useful preferences are given.
Keywords
