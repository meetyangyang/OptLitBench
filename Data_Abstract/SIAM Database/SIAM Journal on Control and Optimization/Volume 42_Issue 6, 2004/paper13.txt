Wiener--Hopf Factorization Indices and Infinite Structure of Rational Matrices
A.  Amparan, S.  Marcaida, and I.  Zaballa;
Abstract
The relationship between the infinite structure and the Wiener--Hopf factorization indices of polynomial matrix representations of controllable systems is investigated. On the way, local Wiener--Hopf factorization indices must be defined. In addition, the finite and infinite structure of a rational matrix is characterized given its Wiener--Hopf factorization indices.
Keywords
matrix polynomial, rational matrix, Wiener--Hopf factorization indices, finite invariant factors, infinite structure, majorization, 15A23, 93B55, 15A45,
