Acausal Realization Theory, Part I; Linear Deterministic Systems
A. J.  Krener;
Abstract
We study acausal linear systems, their controllability and observability properties and the weighting patterns that they realize. A complete classification is given of all minimal real analytic realizations of a given weighting pattern and of all minimal autonomous realizations of a stationary weighting pattern.
Keywords
