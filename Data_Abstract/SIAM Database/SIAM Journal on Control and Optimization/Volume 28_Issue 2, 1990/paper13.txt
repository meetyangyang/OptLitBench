A New Proof of the Lyapunov Convexity Theorem
Fabio  Tardella;
Abstract
A new proof of the Lyapunov Theorem is given, based on the Shapley–Folkman Theorem, that does not require any tools of functional analysis.
Keywords
vector-valued measures, Lyapunov Convexity Theorem, convex analysis, integrals of multi-functions, 28B05, 46010,
