Optimal Control for an Infinite-Dimensional Periodic Problem under White Noise Perturbations
Constantin  Tudor;
Abstract
In this paper a result of the type of “law of large numbers” is obtained for the infinite-dimensional version of the linear quadratic cost problem in the periodic case if the deterministic optimal feedback law is used in the presence of white noise perturbations.
Keywords
optimal control, periodic problem, law of large numbers, 49B, 93E,
