Hardy Spaces of Analytic Functions and Feedback Stability of Scalar Convolution Systems
S.  Mossaheb;
Abstract
By use of the theory of $H^p $-spaces of analytic functions, stability of single input single output convolution systems under constant gain feedback is studied. Various $L^p $-stability results are discussed. These are considerable generalizations of the known results on stable systems. It is shown that corresponding to the classical Nyquist diagram the gain space may be partitioned into mutually disjoint sets, on each of which the stability of the closed-loop system is independent of the gain. It is proved how some seemingly different systems fall into the category of those which are considered in this paper. Finally, the extension of some of the above stability results to more general systems is pointed out.
