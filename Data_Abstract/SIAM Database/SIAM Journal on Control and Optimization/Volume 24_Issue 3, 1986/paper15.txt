Découplage des Systèmes Non Linéaires, Series Génératrices Non Commutatives et Algèbres de Lie
Daniel  Claude;
Abstract
The joint use of noncommutative generating power series and Lie algebras gives an algebraic approach for decoupling problem of nonlinear systems. We produce a coherent decoupling method using the additional notion of the immersion of a system into another, a concept which makes clear the fact that two systems have a same input–output behaviour. Thus, a judicious choice of feedback which gives decoupling is able to immerse a system into a system defined on $\mathbb{R}^n $, with $\eta $ depending on the characteristic numbers of the system.
Keywords
nonlinear systems, decoupling, noninteracting control, disturbance rejection, noncommutative generating series, Lie algebras,
