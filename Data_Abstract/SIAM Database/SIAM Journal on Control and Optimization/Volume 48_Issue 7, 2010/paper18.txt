Singularly Perturbed Evolution Inclusions
T.  Donchev;
Abstract
Singularly perturbed differential inclusions in evolution triple are studied with the help of the averaging method. The upper and lower limits of the slow solution set are estimated when the small parameter tends to zero and the upper or the lower limit averaged system exists. Sufficient conditions of the existence of the limit of the (full) solution set are presented. Under these conditions some properties of the limit occupational measure sets are studied. An illustrative example is provided.
Keywords
