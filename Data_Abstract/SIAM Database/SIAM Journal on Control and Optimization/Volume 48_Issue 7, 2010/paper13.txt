Some Equivalent Results with Yakubovich's S-Lemma
Yan  Zi-zong and Guo  Jin-hai;
Abstract
This paper presents an elementary proof of nonstrict Finsler's theorem and discusses five closely related theorems: Yakubovich's S-lemma, Yuan's alternative theorem, nonstrict Finsler's theorem, strict Finsler's theorem, and Finsler–Calabi's theorem. This paper discusses how one is related with another. At the end of this article we present some extensions of these results to an infinite-dimensional vector space.
