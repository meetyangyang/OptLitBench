Sufficient Conditions for Observation-Innovation Equivalence in White Gaussian Channels with Feedback
Kenko  Uchida;
Abstract
This paper deals with the problem of establishing conditions for the informational equivalence between observation processes and their innovation processes in white Gaussian channels with feedback. It is shown that the informational equivalence holds in either case that there is a nonzero time delay in the feedback loop or that a time integral operation is included in the feedback loop.
