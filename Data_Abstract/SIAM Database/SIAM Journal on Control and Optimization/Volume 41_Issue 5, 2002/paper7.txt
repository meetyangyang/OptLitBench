Flow Stability of Patchy Vector Fields and Robust Feedback Stabilization
Fabio  Ancona and Alberto  Bressan;
Abstract
The paper is concerned with patchy vector fields, a class of discontinuous, piecewise smooth vector fields that were introduced by the authors to study feedback stabilization problems. We prove the stability of the corresponding solution set w.r.t. a wide class of impulsive perturbations. These results yield the robustness of patchy feedback controls in the presence of measurement errors and external disturbances.
Keywords
