Feedback Stabilization and Boundary Controllability  of the  Korteweg--de Vries Equation on a Star-Shaped Network
Kaïs  Ammari and Emmanuelle  Crépeau;
Abstract
In this article, we propose a new model using  the Korteweg--de Vries  equation on a finite star-shaped network. We first prove the well-posedness of the system and give some regularity results. Then we prove that the energy of the solutions of the dissipative system decays exponentially to zero when the time tends to infinity. Last we show an exact boundary controllability result.
Keywords
star-shaped network, KdV equation, stabilization, controllability, 35L05, 35M10,
