The Matrix Riccati Equation and the Noncontrollable Linear-Quadratic Problem with Terminal Constraints
Pavol  Brunovský and Jozef  Komorník;
Abstract
It is proved that each positive semidefinite symmetric solution of the matrix Riccati equation corresponds to an optimal control problem with suitable terminal cost and constraints. The approximation scheme for the computation and characterization of the optimal cost and optimal controls of the problem with terminal constraints is extended to the noncontrollable case.
Keywords
matrix Riccati, linear-quadratic, terminal constraints,
