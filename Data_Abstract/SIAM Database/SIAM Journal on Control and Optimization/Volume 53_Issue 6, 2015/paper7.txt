Stochastic Well-Posed Systems and Well-Posedness of Some Stochastic Partial Differential Equations with Boundary Control and Observation
Qi  Lü;
Abstract
We generalize the concept “well-posed linear system” to stochastic linear control systems and study some basic properties of such kinds of systems. Under our generalized definition, we show the well-posedness of the stochastic heat equation and the stochastic Schrödinger equation with suitable boundary control and observation operators, respectively.
Keywords
stochastic well-posed linear system, stochastic heat equation, stochastic Schrödinger equation, Primary, 93E03; Secondary, 60H15,
