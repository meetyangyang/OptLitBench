Note on Boundary Stabilization of Wave Equations
John E.  Lagnese;
Abstract
An energy decay rate is obtained for solutions of wave type equations in a bounded region in $\mathbb{R}^n $ whose boundary consists partly of a nontrapping reflecting surface and partly of an energy absorbing surface. Unlike most previous results on this subject, the results presented here are potentially valid for regions having connected boundaries.
Keywords
