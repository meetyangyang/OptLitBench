Addendum: The Circle Criterion and the $L^p$ Stability of Feedback Systems
S.  Mossaheb;
Abstract
In an earlier paper [SIAM J. Control. Optim., 20 (1982), pp. 144–152] it was shown that under certain mild conditions the circle theorem of Sandberg and Zames implies the $L^p$ stability of a broad class of scalar feedback systems for all $p \geq 1$. In this addendum it is shown how such stability theorems can be extended to multivariable systems.
