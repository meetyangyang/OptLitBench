A Remark on Existence of Solutions of Infinite-Dimensional Noncompact Optimal Control Problems
H. O.  Fattorini;
Abstract
Without compactness of the semigroup, optimal controls for semilinear infinite dimensional control problems may not exist even under the usual convexity--lower semicontinuity assumptions. This is shown with two counterexamples, and two fixes for nonexistence are discussed.
Keywords
