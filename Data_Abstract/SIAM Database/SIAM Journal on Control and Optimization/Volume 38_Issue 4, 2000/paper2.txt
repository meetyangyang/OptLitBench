Linear Systems with Prescribed Similarity Structural Invariants
I.  Baragaña, V.  Fernández, and I.  Zaballa;
Abstract
The problem of the existence of linear systems $\dot{x}(t)=Ax(t)+Bu(t)$ with prescribed structural invariants for system similarity is studied. Namely, we solve the problem of the existence of such a system with prescribed controllability indices, Hermite indices, and invariant factors when the invariant factors of A (which are also invariants under system similarity) are given.
Keywords
system similarity, controllability indices, Hermite indices, Brunovsky indices, invariant factors, majorization, 93B05, 93B10,
