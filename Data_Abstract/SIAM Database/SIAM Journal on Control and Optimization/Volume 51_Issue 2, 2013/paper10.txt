The Euler Equation in the Multiple Integrals Calculus of Variations
Pierre  Bousquet;
Abstract
For a multiple integrals problem in the calculus of variations, we establish the validity of the Euler equation when the Lagrangian L satisfies a mild growth assumption from below at infinity. We do not assume that the map L is  differentiable or convex.
Keywords
