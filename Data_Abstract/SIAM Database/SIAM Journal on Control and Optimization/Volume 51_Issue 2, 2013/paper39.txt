Transformation Operators of the Sturm--Liouville Problem in Controllability Problems for the Wave Equation on a Half-Axis
Larissa V.  Fardigola;
Abstract
In this paper necessary and sufficient conditions of $L^\infty$-controllability and approximate $L^\infty$-controllability are obtained for the control system $ w_{tt}=w_{xx}-p(x)w$, $w(0,t)=u(t)$, $x>0$, $t\in(0,T)$. Here $p(x)=q^2+r(x)$, $|r(x)|\le\alpha e^{-x}$, $x>0$; $u$ is a control; and $q\ge0$, $\alpha>0$, $T>0$ are constants. These problems are considered in the Sobolev spaces. Using the transformation operator of the Sturm--Liouville problem on the positive half-axis, we see that the control system with an exponentially perturbed potential $q^2$ replicates the controllability properties of the system with the constant potential $q^2$. Conditions of controllability for the system with the potential $p$ are obtained from the conditions for the system with the constant potential $q^2$.
