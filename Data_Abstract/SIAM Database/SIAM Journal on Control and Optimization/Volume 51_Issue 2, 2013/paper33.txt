The Nonoccurrence of the Lavrentiev Phenomenon for a Class of Variational Functionals
Giovanni  Bonfanti and Arrigo  Cellina;
Abstract
We prove that the Lavrentiev phenomenon does not occur for the functional $\int_\Omega [l(|\nabla u(x)|)+ G(u)]\, dx$ on $u- u^0\in W^{1,1}_0(\Omega)$, where $L$ and $G$ are convex functions subject to additional regularity assumptions, and where $\partial \Omega$ and $u^0$ are smooth.
Keywords
calculus of variations, Lavrentiev phenomenon, boundedness of solutions, 49K10,
