Optimal Control of Parabolic Differential Equations with Two Point Boundary State Constraints
Gengsheng  Wang;
Abstract
This paper deals with the necessary conditions of optimality for some optimal control problems governed by some parabolic differential equations involving monotone graphs. The two point boundary (time variable) state constraints are discussed. Some analyses on the state equations are given.
Keywords
variational inequality, optimal control, state constraint, maximum principle, 49K20, 35J65,
