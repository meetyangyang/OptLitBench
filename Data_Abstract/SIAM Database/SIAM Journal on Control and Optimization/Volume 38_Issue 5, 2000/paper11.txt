Invariant Solutions of Differential Games and Hamilton--Jacobi--Isaacs Equations for Time-Measurable Hamiltonians
Pierre  Cardaliaguet and Slawomir  Plaskacz;
Abstract
We characterize invariance of time-varying domains with respect to differential games with time-measurable dynamics. We deduce from this result a new definition of viscosity solutions to some first order Hamilton--Jacobi equations with time-measurable Hamiltonians.
