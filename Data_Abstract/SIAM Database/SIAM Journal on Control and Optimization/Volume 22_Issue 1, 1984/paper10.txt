New Results on the Interpolation Problem for Continuous-Time Stationary Increments Processes
Michele  Pavon;
Abstract
Explicit solutions to the interpolation problem for continuous-time stationary increments processes with a rational spectral density are derived. To do so we take a new approach to the problem relying on stochastic realization theory. In particular we show that the optimal interpolator is completely characterized by two steady-state Kalman–Bucy estimates.
Keywords
interpolation problem, stationary process, stochastic realization theory,
