Necessary and Sufficient Conditions for Isolated Local Minima of Nonsmooth Functions
Marcin  Studniarski;
Abstract
We consider the mathematical programming problem: find $\inf \{ f(x)\mid  x  \in C\} $ where f is an arbitrary extended-real-valued function, and C a subset of a finite dimensional space. We give necessary and sufficient optimality conditions for this problem, generalizing previous results of A. Auslendex (this Journal, 22 (1984), pp. 239–254).
Keywords
nonsmooth optimization, higher order optimality conditions, isolated local minima, lowet and upper Dini directional derivatives,
