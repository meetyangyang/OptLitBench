Similarity and Reduction for Time Varying Linear Systems with Well-Posed Boundary Conditions
I.  Gohberg and M. A.  Kaashoek;
Abstract
The classical theory of similarity and reduction of causal and anti-causal systems is generalized and extended to time varying linear systems with well-posed boundary conditions. Two main problems are solved. The first is to determine to what extent a system with boundary conditions can be simplified by similarity and reduction. The second problem is to find the invariants of such a simplification. Special attention is given to the time invariant case.
