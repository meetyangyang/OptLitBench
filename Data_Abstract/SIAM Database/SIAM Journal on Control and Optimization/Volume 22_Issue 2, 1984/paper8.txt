Extended Jacobi Sufficiency Criterion for Optimal Control
Vera  Zeidan;
Abstract
In this paper we present sufficient conditions for strong local minimality in optimal control. We do not require the dynamic f to be linear nor the control set U to be open. Moreover, these conditions extend a known sufficiency criterion in the calculus of variations involving the Jacobi condition.
Keywords
sufficient conditions, strong local minimality, optimal control problem, extended Jacobi condition,
