An Extension of the Maximum Principle for a Class of Optimal Control Problems in Infinite-Dimensional Spaces
N.  Basile and M.  Mininni;
Abstract
An extension of the Pontryagin maximum principle and of the transversality conditions for a class of optimal control problems for a system of a parabolic equation and an ordinary differential equation in a Hilbert space are given. In particular, the time optimal problem for some of these systems is considered. As an application the optimal control of the diffusion of a class of epidemics is studied.
Keywords
