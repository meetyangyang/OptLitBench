Study of the Optimal Harvesting Control and the Optimality System for an Elliptic Problem
M.  Delgado, J. A.  Montero, and A.  Suárez;
Abstract
An optimal harvesting problem with a concave nonquadratic cost functional and a diffusive degenerate elliptic logistic state equation type is investigated. Under certain assumptions, we prove the existence and uniqueness of an optimal control. A characterization of the optimal control via the optimality system is also derived, which leads to approximating the optimal control.
