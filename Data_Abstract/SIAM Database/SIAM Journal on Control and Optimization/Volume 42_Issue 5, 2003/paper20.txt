Addendum to "Generic Simplicity of the Spectrum and Stabilization for a Plate Equation"
Jaime H.  Ortega and Enrique  Zuazua;
Abstract
In this addendum we clarify a technical point of the article [J. H. Ortega and E. Zuazua, SIAM J. Control. Optim., 39 (2001), pp. 1585--1614], devoted to studying the multiplicity of the eigenvalues of the plate equation and some applications to the stabilization.
Keywords
plate equation, stabilization, spectral theory, shape differentiation, 35P05, 35J40, 93D15,
