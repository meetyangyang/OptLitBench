Erratum: Directional Sparsity In Optimal Control of Partial Differential Equations
Roland  Herzog, Georg  Stadler, and Gerd  Wachsmuth;
Abstract
This erratum corrects a mistake in the statement and proof of Lemma 3.6 in [R. Herzog, G. Stadler, and G. Wachsmuth, SIAM J. Control Optim., 50 (2012), pp. 943--963]. This mistake does not have an impact on any other results in the paper.
Keywords
sparsity, optimal control, control device placement, $L^1$-norm minimization, semi-smooth Newton, 49K20, 65K10, 49M15, 49J52,
