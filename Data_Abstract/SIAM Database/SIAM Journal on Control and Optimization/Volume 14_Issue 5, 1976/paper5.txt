A Perturbation Problem from Control Exhibiting on and off the Boundary Behavior
Dennis D.  Berkey and Marvin I.  Freedman;
Abstract
We consider a regular perturbation problem for a system arising from a control problem in which the optimal control moves on and off the boundary of the control region in a continuous way. By using a nonlinear change of time scales, we are able to give conditions under which our technique yields a solution of the perturbed system.
