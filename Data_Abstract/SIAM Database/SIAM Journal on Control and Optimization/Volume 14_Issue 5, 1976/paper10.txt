Spectral Minimality for Infinite-Dimensional Linear Systems
Avraham  Feintuch;
Abstract
Let $\{ A,b,c\} $ be an infinite-dimensional system on a Hilbert space H. Suppose $f(\lambda ) = ((\lambda I - A)^{ - 1} b,c)$ is the transfer function of the system. It is shown that if A is a compact operator or spectral operator, then the set of singularities of f is the spectrum of A.
