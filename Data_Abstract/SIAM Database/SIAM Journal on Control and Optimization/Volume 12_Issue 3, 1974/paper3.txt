Optimization of Allocations Under a Coverability Condition
Daniel H.  Wagner and Lawrence D.  Stone;
Abstract
Consider maximization of a real functional E given by $E(q) = \smallint _x e(x,q(x))d\mu x$, subject to equality or inequality constraint on $\smallint _x q\mu $. It is proved that such extrema exist and necessitate satisfaction of a pointwise multiplier rule without assuming a topology on X, but assuming a condition called coverability of e, pertaining to the concave envelope of $e(x, \cdot )$, $x \in X$. Examples show that key hypotheses may not be omitted.
