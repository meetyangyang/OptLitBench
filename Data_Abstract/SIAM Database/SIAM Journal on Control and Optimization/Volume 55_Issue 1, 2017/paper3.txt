A Deterministic Optimal Design Problem for the Heat Equation
Heiko  Gimperlein and Alden  Waters;
Abstract
For the heat equation on a bounded subdomain $\Omega$ of ${\mathbb{R}^d}$, we investigate the optimal shape and location of the observation domain in observability inequalities. A new decomposition of $L^2({\mathbb{R}^d})$ into heat packets allows us to remove the randomization procedure and assumptions on the geometry  of $\Omega$ in previous works. The explicit nature of the heat packets gives new information about the observability constant in the inverse problem.
