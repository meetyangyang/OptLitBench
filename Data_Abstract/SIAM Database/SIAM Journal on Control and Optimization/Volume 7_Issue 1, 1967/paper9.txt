On the Existence of Solutions to a Differential Game
P. P.  Varaiya;
Abstract
In this paper we consider the problem of the existence of a “min-sup” strategy to a pursuit-evasion game. The dynamics of the players have been modeled by a general dynamical system rather than by a differential system. This has helped to achieve mathematical simplicity as well as clarification of the problems involved in a competitive situation. We have discussed the relation between the two models and the relevance of our results to time-optimal control problems.
