Construction of Suboptimal Control Sequences
R. J.  Leake and Ruey-Wen  Liu;
Abstract
As an alternative to a direct solution of the Hamilton-Jacobi equation, results are presented for the determination and improvement of suboptimal controls and for obtaining bounds on the optimal value of the performance index. Treatment is restricted to the class of problems included in the well-known work of Kalman [1].
