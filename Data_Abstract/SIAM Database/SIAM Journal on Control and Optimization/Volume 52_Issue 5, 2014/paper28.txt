Optimal Control of Evolutionary Quasi-Newtonian Fluids
Nadir  Arada;
Abstract
We are interested in the necessary optimality conditions for optimal control problems of systems describing the unsteady flow of incompressible shear-thickening fluids. To overcome the difficulties related to the differentiability of the control-to-state mapping, consequence of the lack of regularity of the state variable, we first establish some intermediate optimality conditions and then pass to the limit.
Keywords
optimal control, non-Newtonian fluids, shear-dependent viscosity, necessary optimality conditions, 49K20, 76D55, 76A05,
