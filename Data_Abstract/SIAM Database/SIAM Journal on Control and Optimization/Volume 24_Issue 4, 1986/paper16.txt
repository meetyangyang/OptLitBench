Finite-Dimensional Compensators for Infinite-Dimensional Systems with Unbounded Input Operators
R. F.  Curtain and D.  Salamon;
Abstract
This paper contains a design procedure for constructing finite dimensional compensators for a class of infinite dimensional systems with unbounded input operators. Applications to retarded functional differential systems with delays in the input or the output variable and to partial differential equations with boundary input operators are discussed.
