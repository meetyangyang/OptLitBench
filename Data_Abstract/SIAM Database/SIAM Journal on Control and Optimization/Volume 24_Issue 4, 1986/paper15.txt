Open Loop Control of Water Waves in an Irregular Domain
Russell M.  Reid;
Abstract
This paper considers open loop control of linear, small amplitude waves on a fluid surface. It extends the controllability results of Reid and Russell [4] to a two-dimensional domain with irregular bottom contour, constructing a null control via its Laplace transform, provided the bottom has finite arclength and no beaches. The domain may be multiply connected, i.e. contain fixed objects, provided they do not touch the surface.
