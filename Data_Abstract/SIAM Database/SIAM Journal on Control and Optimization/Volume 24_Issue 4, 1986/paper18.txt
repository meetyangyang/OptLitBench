Iterative Techniques for the Nash Solution in Quadratic Games with Unknown Parameters
G. P.  Papavassilopoulos;
Abstract
We study adaptive schemes for repeated quadratic Nash games in a deterministic and a stochastic framework. The convergence of the schemes is demonstrated under certain conditions.
Keywords
Nash equilibrium, adaptive Games, stochastic approximation, 60G42, 62L20, 90D05, 90D15,
