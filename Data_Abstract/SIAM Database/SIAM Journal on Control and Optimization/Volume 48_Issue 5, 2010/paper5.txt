Necessary Conditions for Solutions to Variational Problems
A.  Cellina and M.  Mazzola;
Abstract
We prove necessary conditions for a solution u to the problem of minimizing $\int_\Omega[f(\|\nabla v(x)\|)+g(x,v(x))]dx$ in the form of a Pontryagin maximum principle, for f convex and satisfying a growth assumption, but without assuming differentiability.
Keywords
Euler–Lagrange equation, Pontryagin maximum principle, 49K10,
