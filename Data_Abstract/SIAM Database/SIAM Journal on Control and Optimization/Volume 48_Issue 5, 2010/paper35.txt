Corrigendum: Optimality Conditions for Degenerate Extremum Problems with Equality Constraints
Olga  Brezhneva and Alexey A.  Tret'yakov;
Abstract
We correct an error in the statement of Theorem 3.3 in our paper [SIAM J. Control Optim., 42 (2003), pp. 729–745] and slightly correct the proof of the theorem. With these corrections, all results in the paper remain true. In the corrected form, the sufficient conditions given in Theorem 3.3 complement the second order necessary conditions derived earlier in [A. F. Izmailov and A. A. Tret'yakov, Factor-Analysis of Nonlinear Mappings, Nauka, Moscow, 1994 (in Russian)].
Keywords
constrained optimization, nonregular problems, optimality conditions, 49K27, 49N60,
