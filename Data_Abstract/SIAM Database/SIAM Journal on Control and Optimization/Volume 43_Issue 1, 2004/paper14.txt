High-Order Variations for Families of Vector Fields
Ronald  Hirschorn and Andrew D.  Lewis;
Abstract
Sufficient conditions involving Lie brackets of arbitrarily high order are obtained for local controllability of families of vector fields. After providing a general framework for the generation of high-order control variations, we propose a specific method for generating such variations. The theory is applied to a number of nontrivial examples.
Keywords
