Optimality Conditions for Nonconvex Multistate Control Problems in the Coefficients
Juan  Casado-Díaz, Julio  Couce-Calvo, and José D.  Martín-Gómez;
Abstract
The purpose of this paper is to attain some optimality conditions for the identification of a diffusion matrix (material) under several restrictions. Assuming that the set of such diffusion matrices is closed for the H-convergence, we give a method to obtain admissible directions which applies to a not-necessarily convex control set. Our results permit obtaining the diffusion matrix from the state functions.
