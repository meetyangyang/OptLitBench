Job Shop Scheduling with Setup Times and Maximal Time-Lags: A Simple Constraint Programming Approach
Diarmuid Grimes;Emmanuel Hebrard;
Abstract
In previous work we introduced a simple constraint model that combined generic AI strategies and techniques (weighted degree heuristic, geometric restarts, nogood learning from restarts) with naive propagation for job shop and open shop scheduling problems. Here, we extend our model to handle two variants of the job shop scheduling problem: job shop problems with setup times; and job shop problems with maximal time lags. We also make some important additions to our original model, including a solution guidance component for search.
