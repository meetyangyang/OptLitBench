The “Not-Too-Heavy Spanning Tree” Constraint
Grégoire Dooms;Irit Katriel;
Abstract
We develop filtering algorithms for the Weight-Bounded Spanning Tree (WBST(G,T,I,W) ) constraint, which is defined on undirected graph variables G and T, a scalar variable I and a vector of scalar variables W. It specifies that T is a spanning tree of G whose total weight is at most I, where W is a vector of the edge weights.
