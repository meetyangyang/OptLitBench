Asymptotic expansions for Markov processes with lévy generators
W. H. Fleming;H. M. Soner;
Abstract
This paper considers a deterministic flow inn-dimensional space, perturbed by a Markov jump process with small variance. Asymptotic expansions are obtained for certain functionals of Feynman—Kac type, in powers of a small parameter representing a noise intensity. The methods are analytical rather than probabilistic.
Keywords
