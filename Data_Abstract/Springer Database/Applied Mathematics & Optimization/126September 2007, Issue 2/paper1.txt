Existence and Ergodicity for the Two-Dimensional Stochastic Magneto-Hydrodynamics Equations
Viorel Barbu;Giuseppe Da Prato;
Abstract
The paper proves the existence of solutions to the magneto-hydrodynamics equations driven by random exterior forcing terms both in the velocity  and in the magnetic field. The existence and uniqueness of an invariant measure is also obtained via coupling methods.
Keywords
Stokes Equation  Invariant Measure  Coupling Method  London Mathematical Society Lecture Note  Transition Semigroup
