Bounded controlling sequences, lower stability and certain penalty procedures
Szymon Dolecki;
Abstract
We present a unified approach to certain controllability and optimization questions such as approximate controllability with bounded sequences, necessary and sufficient conditions for lower stability, a problem of moments, penalty techniques. The control processes concerned are given by strongly continuous semigroups in Banach spaces, but the argument is led in the generality of continuous operators.
Keywords
Banach Space  System Theory  Control Process  Mathematical Method  Unify Approach
