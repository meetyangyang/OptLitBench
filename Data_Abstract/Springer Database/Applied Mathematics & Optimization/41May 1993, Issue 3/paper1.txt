Controlling the moving boundary of the parabolic obstacle problem
Viorel Barbu;Srdjan Stojanovic;
Abstract
We consider the controlled parabolic obstacle problem with the controlu appearing through the Neumann condition ∂y/∂v=u on the boundary ∂Ω of an open domain Ω ofR N . Given a smooth open subsetQ0 of Ω×(0,T) we prove that the problemQ0 ⊂{(x, t) εQ, y u (x, t)=0} is approximately solvable. Numerical tests are given.
Keywords
