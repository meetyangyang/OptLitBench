Controlled partially observed diffusions with correlated noise
Qing Zhang;
Abstract
This work is concerned with separated control problems for optimal stochastic controls under partial observations. Continuity properties of the unnormalized conditional distribution measure are found, and the Nisio nonlinear semigroup is formed in the case when a functionh(X t ,Y t ,U t ) of stateX t  observationY t , and controlU t  plus correlated additive white noise is observed.
Keywords
Control Problem  White Noise  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Conditional Distribution
