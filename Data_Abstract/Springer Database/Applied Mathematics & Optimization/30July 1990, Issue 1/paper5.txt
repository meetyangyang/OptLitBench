Floquet exponents and stabilizability in time-periodic parabolic systems
Giuseppe Da Prato;Alessandra Lunardi;
Abstract
We give asymptotic estimates of the Floquet exponents of a class of time-periodic parabolic systems. We apply the result to the problem of stabilizability of such systems.
Keywords
