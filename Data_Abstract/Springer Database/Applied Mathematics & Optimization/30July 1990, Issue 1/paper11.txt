Duality applied to contact problems with friction
A. Klarbring;A. Mikelić;M. Shillor;
Abstract
The duality theory of Mosco, Capuzzo-Dolcetta, and Matzeu for variational and quasi-variational inequalities is extended. Then it is applied to two problems of contact with friction of an elastic body with a rigid foundation. The more realistic normal compliance condition is used in place of Signorini's condition on the contact surface.
Keywords
