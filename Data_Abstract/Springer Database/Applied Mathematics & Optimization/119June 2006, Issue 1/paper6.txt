Viscosity Solution of the Hamilton-Jacobi Equation Arising from a Thin Film Blistering Model
Vanda Valente;Giorgio Vergara Caffarelli;
Abstract
The paper deals with  a dynamical nonlinear model
describing the self-driven delamination of compressed thin films.
Some assumptions on the buckled shape allow us to describe the moving
boundary of the film by a single Hamilton-Jacobi equation. We
prove the existence and uniqueness of a viscosity solution to the
associated evolution problem.
Keywords
Viscosity Solution  Jacobi Equation  Dynamical Nonlinear Model  Airy Stress Function  Unique Viscosity Solution
