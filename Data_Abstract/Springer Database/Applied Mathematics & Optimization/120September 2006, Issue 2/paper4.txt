Optimal Measures for Nonlinear Cost Functionals
Nicolas Varchon;
Abstract
We study an optimization problem for an elliptic PDE where the unknown is a measure. The state equation is a relaxed form of the classical Dirichlet problem and the functional to minimize is nonlinear. We prove a regularity result for the optimal measure, give a  description of it and present some numerical examples.
