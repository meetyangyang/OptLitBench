A Random Continuous Model for Two Interacting Populations
R. Manthey;B. Maslowski;
Abstract
 A system of two interacting populations is considered where each population density follow a stochastic partial differential equation. The stability behaviour of both populations is studied.
Keywords
Key words. Stochastic partial differential equation, Stability, Extinction, Predator—prey systems, Competition. AMS Classification. 60H15, 35R60, 92D25, 92D40.
