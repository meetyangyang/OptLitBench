Bang–Bang Property for Time Optimal Control of the Korteweg-de Vries-Burgers Equation
Mo Chen;
Abstract
In this paper, we obtain the existence of time optimal control of the Korteweg-de Vries-Burgers equation in a bounded domain with control acting locally in a subset. Moreover, we prove that any time optimal control satisfies the bang–bang property.
