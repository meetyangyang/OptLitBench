On the Rate of Convergence of the Finite-Difference Approximations for Parabolic Bellman Equations with Constant Coefficients
Jun Luo;N. V. Krylov;
Abstract
The error bounds of order 
\(h+\tau^{\frac{1}{2}}\) 
for two types of finite-difference approximation schemes of parabolic Bellman equations with constant coefficients are obtained, where h is x-mesh size and τ is t-mesh size. The key methods employed are the maximum principles for the Bellman equation and the approximation schemes.
Keywords
Finite-difference approximations  Bellman equations  Fully nonlinear equations  Maximum principle  Comparison principle  Bellman’s principle
