Martingale representation and the Malliavin calculus
Robert J. Elliott;Michael Kohlmann;
Abstract
Using stochastic flows and the Itô differentiation rule, the integrand in the representation of a martingale as a stochastic integral is identified. By iterating this representation result a homogeneous chaos type expansion is obtained. Using the stochastic integral representation, an integration by parts formula is obtained without using any calculus of variations in function space. If the inverse of the Malliavin matrix belongs to all spacesLp(Ω) it follows that a random variable has a smooth density.
