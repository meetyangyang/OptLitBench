Sufficiency conditions with minimal regularity assumptions
Vera Zeidan;
Abstract
The aim of this article is to provide second-order sufficiency criteria that extend known ones in [6] and [7] to the case where the control candidate and some of the data are merely essentially bounded, and/or the control setU is any convex subset of ℝ m . In the classical setting, where a restriction on the velocity is imposed\((\dot x(t) \in U)\), it is shown that whenU is compact the known strengthened Weierstrass condition is equivalent to the Weierstrass condition with strict inequality.
