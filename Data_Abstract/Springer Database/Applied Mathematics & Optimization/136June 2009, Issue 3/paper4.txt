The American Foreign Exchange Option in Time-Dependent One-Dimensional Diffusion Model for Exchange Rate
Nasir Rehman;Malkhaz Shashiashvili;
Abstract
The classical Garman-Kohlhagen model for the currency exchange assumes that the domestic and foreign currency risk-free interest rates are constant and the exchange rate follows a log-normal diffusion process.
Keywords
American put option  Time-dependent model  Optimal exercise boundary  Early exercise premium representation  Snell envelope  Optimal stopping  Zeros of semimartingales
