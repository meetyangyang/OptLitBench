Inertial Manifolds for von Karman Plate Equations 

 Igor Chueshov; Irena Lasiecka;
Abstract
Abstract.  Inertial manifolds associated with nonlinear plate models governed by dynamical von Karman equations are considered. Three different dissipative mechanisms are discussed: viscous, structural and thermal damping. Though the systems considered are subject to some dissipation, the overall dynamics may not be dissipative. This means that the energy may not be decreasing.
Keywords
Key words. von Karman equations, Long-time behaviour, Inertial manifolds, Thermal damping, Viscous damping. AMS Classification. 40A20, 74K20, 74H40, 35Q72.
