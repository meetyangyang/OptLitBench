Adapting Some Ideas from Stochastic Control Theory to Studying the Heat Equation in Closed Smooth Domains 


 Nicolai V. Krylov;
Abstract
Abstract.  Sufficient conditions on bounded domains D  ⊂  R d ={(t,x)}  of class C 4   are given under which solutions of the heat equation u t =Δ u+f  in D  have continuous second-order derivatives with respect to (t,x)  in D- . The equation is supplemented with C 4   boundary data and it is assumed that f∈ C 2  .

Keywords
Key words. Heat equation, martingale inequalities, conditional processes AMS Classification. Primary 35K05, Secondary 35J70.
