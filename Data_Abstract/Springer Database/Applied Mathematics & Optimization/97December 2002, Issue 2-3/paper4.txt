Null Controllability for the Dissipative Semilinear Heat Equation 

 Sebastian Aniţa; Daniel Tataru;
Abstract
Abstract.  We  consider the exact null controllability problem for the semi- linear heat equation with dissipative nonlinearity in a bounded domain of R n  . The main result of the article asserts that if the nonlinearity is even mildly superlinear, then global null controllability in an arbitrarily short time fails; instead we provide sharp estimates for the controllability time in terms of the size of the initial data.

Keywords
Key words. Null controllability, Heat equation, Nonlinear parabolic equation. AMS Classification. 93B05, 35K05, 35K60.
