A Sufficient Condition for a Blow-Up in the Space of Absolutely Continuous Functions for the Very Weak Solution
Jean-Michel Rakotoson;
Abstract
Given an open bounded smooth set \(\Omega \) in \(\mathrm{I}\!\mathrm{R}^N,\ N\geqslant 3\), we provide a sufficient condition on the data \(f\) integrable with respect to the distance \(\delta \), to ensure the blow-up of the gradient of the very weak solution for the Dirichlet equation.
