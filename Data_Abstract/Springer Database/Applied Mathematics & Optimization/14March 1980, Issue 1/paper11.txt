On the behaviour ast→∞ of solutions of the second mixed problem for a second-order parabolic equation
A. K. Guščin;
Abstract
In this paper is distinguished a geometric characteristic of the unbounded domain Ω, that determines the rate of stabilization fort→∞ of the solution in (t>0)×Ω of the second boundary value problem for a second-order parabolic equation, in which the initial function decreases sufficiently rapidly as |x|→∞.
Keywords
System Theory  Mathematical Method  Parabolic Equation  Geometric Characteristic  Initial Function
