State constrained control problems for parabolic systems: Regularity of optimal solutions
Irena Lasiecka;
Abstract
Quadratic control problems for parabolic equations withstate constraints are considered. Regularity (smoothness) of the optimal solution is investigated. It is shown that the optimal control is continuous in time with the values inL2(Ω) and its time derivative belongs toL2[OT×Ω].
Keywords
