A Modified Landweber Iteration for Solving Parameter Estimation Problems 
O. Scherzer;
Abstract
 In this paper a convergence analysis for a modified Landweber iteration for the solution of nonlinear ill-posed problems is presented. A priori and a posteriori stopping criteria for terminating the iteration are compared. Some numerical results for the solution of a parameter estimation problem are presented.
Keywords
Key words. Nonlinear ill-posed problems, Modified Landweber iteration, Regularization methods, Stopping rules. AMS Classification. 65J15, 65J20, 47H17.
