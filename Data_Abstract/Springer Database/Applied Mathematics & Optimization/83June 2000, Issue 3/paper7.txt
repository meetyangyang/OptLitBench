Asymptotic Expansion in Nonlinear Filtering with Homogenization 
C. Marchetti;
Abstract
 The problem of nonlinear filtering is studied for a class of diffusions whose statistics depend periodically on the state and a small parameter ε . Our purpose here is to show that, under some assumptions, the conditional density of the filtering problem admits an asymptotic expansion (see [2]).
Keywords
Key words. Nonlinear filtering, Homogenization, Feynman—Kac and Poisson equations. AMS Classification. 60H10, 60H30, 35K10.
