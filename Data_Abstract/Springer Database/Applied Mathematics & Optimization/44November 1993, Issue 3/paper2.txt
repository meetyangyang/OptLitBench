Asymptotic behavior of eigenvalues and random updating schemes
Tzuu-Shuh Chiang;Yunshyong Chow;
Abstract
For a stochastic matrix (Q  ij   T  )  i,j=1   M   withQ  ij   T  ∼ exp(−U(ij)/T) at the off-diagonal positions, we develop an algorithm to evaluate the asymptotic convergence rate of all eigenvalues ofQ  ij   T   asT ↓ 0 using Ventcel's optimal graphs. As an application we can compare the convergence rates of some random updating schemes used in image processing.
