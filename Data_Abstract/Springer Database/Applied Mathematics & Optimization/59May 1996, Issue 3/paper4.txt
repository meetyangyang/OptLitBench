Boundary control problems for quasi-linear elliptic equations:
A Pontryagin's;E. Casas;
Abstract
In this paper we prove some optimality conditions, in the form of a Pontryagin's principle, for boundary control problems governed by quasi-linear elliptic equations. Because of the presence of state constraints, we distinguish the cases of qualified and nonqualified conditions for optimality. Both cases are treated in the paper. Neither convexity of the control set nor differentiability of the functions involved in the control problem are assumed.
