Wiener Chaos and Nonlinear Filtering
Istvan Gyongy;Anton Shmatkov;
Abstract
We investigate the rate of convergence of the Wong-Zakai approximations for second-order stochastic PDEs of parabolic type driven by a multi-dimensional Wiener
process W.
Keywords
Wiener Process  Separable Hilbert Space  Area Process  Free Term  Stochastic Partial Differential Equation
