A Lattice Scheme for Stochastic Partial Differential Equations of Elliptic Type in Dimension d ≥ 4
Teresa Martinez;Marta Sanz-Sole;
Abstract
We study a stochastic boundary value problem on (0,1)d of elliptic type in dimension d ≥ 4, driven by a coloured noise. An approximation scheme based on a suitable discretization of the Laplacian on a lattice of 
(0,1)d is presented; we also give the rate of convergence to the original stochastic partial differential equation in the Lp(ΩL2(D))-norm, for some values of p.
Keywords
