Optimal Shape Design of Blazed Diffraction Gratings 
D. C. Dobson;
Abstract
 The problem of designing a periodic interface between two materials in such a way that time-harmonic waves diffracted from the interface have a specified far-field pattern is studied.
Keywords
Key words. Diffraction, Periodic structure, Optimal shape design. AMS Classification. 78A45, 49J20, 65K10, 49J50.
