Comments on “a numerical approach to the infinite horizon problem of deterministic control theory”
Mabel Maria Tidball;
Abstract
In the paper cited above a proof about an estimate of the rate of convergence for a special discretization scheme for the Hamilton—Jacobi equation is given. The proof is essentially based on the identification between functions\(\tilde v_h\) andv  h   k  . Here we give a simple example which shows that this identification is incorrect.
