A Property of Sobolev Spaces and Existence in Optimal Design 

 Tiba;
Abstract
Abstract.  We prove that for bounded open sets Ω   with continuous boundary, Sobolev spaces of type W 0  l,p (Ω )  are characterized by the zero extension outside of Ω  . Combining this with a compactness result for domains of class C, we obtain a general existence theorem for shape optimization problems governed by nonlinear nonhomogenous Dirichlet boundary value problems of arbitrary order, in arbitrary dimension and with general cost functionals.

Keywords
Key words. Open sets of class C, Stability of Sobolev spaces, Compactness. AMS Classification. 49D37, 65K10.
