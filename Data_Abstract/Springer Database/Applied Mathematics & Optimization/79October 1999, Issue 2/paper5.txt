Nonlinear Programming Problems Associated with Closed Range Operators
S. Aizicovici;D. Motreanu;N. H. Pavel;
Abstract
 Necessary conditions for the optimality of a pair  \((\overline{y}, \overline{u})\)  with respect to a locally Lipschitz cost functional L(y,u) , subject to Ay + F(y) = Cu + B(u) , are given in terms of generalized gradients. Here A  and C  are densely defined, closed, linear operators on some Banach spaces, while F  and B  are (Fréchet) differentiable maps, which are suitably related to A  and C . Various examples and potential applications to nonlinear programming models and nonlinear optimal control of partial differential equations are also discussed.
Keywords
Key words. Optimality conditions, Closed range operators, Tangent cones, Clarke's generalized gradients, Wave operator. AMS Classification. Primary 49K27, 49K30, Secondary 47A62, 47H15.
