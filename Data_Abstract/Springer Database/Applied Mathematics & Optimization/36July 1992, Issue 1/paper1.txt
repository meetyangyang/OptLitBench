Uniform stabilization of a nonlinearly perturbed Kirchoff plate
M. E. Bradley;
Abstract
This paper considers the problem of local uniform stability of certain plate equations where unstructured nonlinearities appear in the boundary conditions. Using appropriate feedback laws, we prove that the solutions to these equations possess the necessary regularity to achieve the desired stability.
Keywords
Boundary Condition  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Uniform Stability  Plate Equation
