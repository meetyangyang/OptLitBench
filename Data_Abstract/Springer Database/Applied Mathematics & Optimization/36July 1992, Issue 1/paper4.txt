Wiener distributions and white noise analysis
David Betounes;Mylan Redfern;
Abstract
The paper describes the structure of a new space of generalized Wiener functionals,\((D_\infty  )^* \), called the Wiener algebra, or space of Wiener distributions, and demonstrates its use in the white noise analysis. The concepts of derivatives and integrals for multi-time parameter generalized stochastic processθ:ℝN→\((D_\infty  )^* \) are introduced, and a derivative version of Itô's lemma is proved. The algebraic structure of\((D_\infty  )^* \) and its lattice of subspaces is elaborated, and within this framework a generalized version of the Malliavin calculus is presented.
