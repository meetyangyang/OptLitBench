Necessary and sufficient conditions for the existence of positive solutions to algebraic Riccati equations with indefinite quadratic term
Shuping Chen;
Abstract
Necessary and sufficient conditions are established in this paper for the existence of positive- and/or negative-definite solutions to the algebraic Riccati equation with indefinite coefficient. An iterative procedure is also given for computing such a solution.
