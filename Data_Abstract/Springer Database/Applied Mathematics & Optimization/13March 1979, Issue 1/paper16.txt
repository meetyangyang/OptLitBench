The Multiplier Method for optimal control problems of parabolic systems
G. Di Pillo;L. Grippo;
Abstract
In this paper the application of the Multiplier Method (also known as Augmented Lagrangian Method or Penalty Shifting Method) to quadratic optimal control problems for linear parabolic distributed systems is studied and the convergence of the method on appropriate Hilbert spaces is proved. Numerical examples are reported.
Keywords
Hilbert Space  Control Problem  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Optimal Control Problem
