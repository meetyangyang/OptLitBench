The Skorohod integral and the derivative operator of functional of a cylindrical Brownian motion
G. Kallianpur;V. Pérez-Abreu;
Abstract
The paper presents a definition of the Skorohod integral of operator-valued processes and the derivative operator for functional of a cylindrical Brownian motionW on a Hilbert space. The method is based on the chaos expansions in terms of multiple Wiener integrals ofW.
Keywords
Cylindrical Brownian motion  Skorohod integral  Sobolev derivative  Multiple Wiener integral  Chaos expansion
