The time-optimal control problem for parabolic variational inequalities
Viorel Barbu;
Abstract
We show here that a variant of the maximum principle holds for the time-optimal trajectories of control systems governed by certain variational inequalities of parabolic type. The approach is constructive and relies on the approximation of the time-optimal problem by a family of optimal control problems with infinite time horizon.
