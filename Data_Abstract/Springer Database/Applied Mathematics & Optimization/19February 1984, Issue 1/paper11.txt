Quasiconvex quadratic forms in two dimensions
Paolo Marcellini;
Abstract
Letf andg be two quadratic forms in ℝ n . Iff(ξ) is positive whereg(ξ) = 0, ξ ≠ 0, then we show that there exists a realλ such thatf — λ g is positive definite. As a consequence we obtain a new description of the old characterization by Terpstra [19] of quasiconvex quadratic forms in two dimensions.
