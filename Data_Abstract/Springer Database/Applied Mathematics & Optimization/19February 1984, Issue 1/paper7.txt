Optimal control of a variational inequality with applications to structural analysis. I. Optimal design of a beam with unilateral supports
I. Hlaváček;I. Bock;J. Lovíšek;
Abstract
A class of optimal design problems is considered, where the state problem is governed by a variational inequality. The latter includes an elliptic operator, the coefficients of which are chosen as the design (control) variables.
Keywords
Structural Analysis  Optimal Design  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Variational Inequality
