Stability of the Filter Equation for a Time-Dependent Signal on ℝd
Wilhelm Stannat;
Abstract
Stability of the pathwise filter equation for a time-dependent signal process induced by a   
d-dimensional stochastic differential equation and a linear observation is studied, using a  
variational approach introduced in [16]. A lower bound for the rate of stability is identified  
in terms of the mass-gap of a parabolic ground state transform associated with the generator of the  
signal process and the square of the observation. The lower bound can be easily calculated a priori and  
provides hints on how precisely to measure the signal in order to reach a certain rate of stability. Ergodicity  
of the signal process is not needed.
