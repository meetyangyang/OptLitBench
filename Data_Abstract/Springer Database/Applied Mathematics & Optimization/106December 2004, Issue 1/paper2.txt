A Connection between Singular Stochastic Control and Optimal 
Stopping
Fred Espen Benth;Kristin Reikvam;
Abstract

  We show that the value function of a singular stochastic
  control problem is equal to the integral of the value function
  of an associated optimal stopping problem. The connection is
  proved for a general class of diffusions using the
  method of viscosity solutions.
Keywords
Singular stochastic control  Optimal stopping  Variational inequalities  Viscosity solutions
