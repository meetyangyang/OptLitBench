Singular perturbations of variational problems arising from a two-phase transition model
Guy Bouchitte;
Abstract
Given thatα, β are two Lipschitz continuous functions of Ω to ℝ+ and thatf(x, u, p) is a continuous function of\(\bar \Omega \) × ℝ × ℝ N  to [0, + ∞[ such that, for everyx, f(x,·, 0) reaches its minimum value 0 at exactly two pointsα(x) andβ(x), we prove the convergence ofF ε (u) = (1/ε)∫ Ω f (x, u, εDu) dx when the perturbation parameterε goes to zero. A formula is given for the limit functional and a general minimal interface criterium is deduced for a wide class of two-phase transition models. Earlier results of [19], [21], and [22] are extended with new proofs.
