Stability and sensitivity of solutions to optimal control problems for systems with control appearing linearly
Kazimierz Malanowski;
Abstract
We consider a family of optimal control problems for systems described by nonlinear ordinary differential equations with control appearing linearly. The cost functionals and the control constraints are convex. All data depend on a vector parameter.
Keywords
Differential Equation  Optimality Condition  Ordinary Differential Equation  Control Problem  System Theory
