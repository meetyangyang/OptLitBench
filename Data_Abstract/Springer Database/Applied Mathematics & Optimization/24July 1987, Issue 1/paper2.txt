Justification of the averaging method for a system of functional-differential equations with variable structure and impulses
D. D. Bainov;S. D. Milusheva;
Abstract
This paper presents a justification of the averaging method for a system of functional-differential equations with variable structure, impulses, and transformed argument, depending on the time and on the unknown function.
Keywords
System Theory  Mathematical Method  Variable Structure  Unknown Function  Average Method
