On the Feynman-Kac formula and its applications to filtering theory
Rajeeva L. Karandikar;
Abstract
In this article the Feynman-Kac formula is obtained for a Markov process (Xt) whose transition probability function is not stationary. A converse to the Feynman-Kac formula is also obtained. This is used to prove the uniqueness of the solution to a measure-valued equation satisfied by the optimal filter in the white-noise approach to nonlinear filtering theory.
Keywords
System Theory  Mathematical Method  Markov Process  Probability Function  Optimal Filter
