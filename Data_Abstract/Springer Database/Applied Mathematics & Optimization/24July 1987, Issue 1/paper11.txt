A maximal distance result of interest in robotic simulation
L. -E. Andersson;G. Hurteau;N. F. Stewart;
Abstract
Given two hyper-rectangles inEn with sides having surface normals in the directions of the axes, and given a connected set in each that touches all 2n sides of its containing hyper-rectangle, it is important to have an easily calculated upper bound on the distance between the two connected sets. A result giving such a bound, necessary in branch and bound algorithms used for collision avoidance in robotic simulation, is stated and proved here.
Keywords
