Minimizing a quasi-concave function subject to a reverse convex constraint
L. V. Dien;
Abstract
Any constraintg(x)≥0 is called a reverse convex constraint ifg: R n → R1 is a continuous convex function. This paper establishes a finite method for finding an optimal solution to a concave program with an additional reverse convex constraint. The method presented is a new approach to global optimization problems since it combines the idea of the branch and bound method with the idea of the cutting plane method.
