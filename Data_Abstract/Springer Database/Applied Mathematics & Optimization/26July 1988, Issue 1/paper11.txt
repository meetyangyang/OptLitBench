Perturbation theory of nonlinear programs when the set of optimal solutions is not a singleton
Alexander Shapiro;
Abstract
Given a mathematical programming problem depending on a parameter vectorx, we consider the associated optimal value (marginal) functionϕ(x) and the optimal set-valued multifunctionM(x). The aim of this paper is to investigate continuity and differentiability properties ofϕ(x) andM(x) at a pointx0 where the corresponding setM(x0) of optimal solutions isnot asingleton. We show that under certain regularity conditions the multifunctionM(x) is upper Lipschitzian atx0 andϕ(x) possesses second-order directional derivatives.
