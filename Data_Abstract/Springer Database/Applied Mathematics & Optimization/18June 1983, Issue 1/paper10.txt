Bounds for the First eigenvalue of a spherical cap
C. Betz;G. A. Cámera;H. Gzyl;
Abstract
We study upper and lower bounds for the lowest eigenvalueλ of the Laplace operator on a spherical capC θ  inm-dimensional space (m ≥ 3).
Keywords
Lower Bound  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Laplace Operator  Asymptotic Expression
