How the Reynolds equation is related to the Stokes equations
Giovanni Cimatti;
Abstract
The Reynolds equation, which gives the pressure distribution in a thin layer of lubricating fluid, is usually motivated in a rather heuristic way. We make an attempt to clarify how the Reynolds equation can be viewed as an asymptotic solution of the Stokes equations.
Keywords
Thin Layer  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Pressure Distribution  Stokes Equation
