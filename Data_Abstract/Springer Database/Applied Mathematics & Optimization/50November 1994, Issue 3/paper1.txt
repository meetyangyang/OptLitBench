Notes on a generalized Fresnel class
Il Yoo;
Abstract
In this paper we establish a theorem for the generalized Fresnel class FA1,A2 ensuring that various functions are in FA1,A2. We also prove a translation theorem for the analytic Feynman integral of functions in FA1,A2.
Keywords
Abstract Wiener measure space  Generalized Fresnel class  Analytic Feynman integral  Primary 28C20
