On the Uniqueness ofL2-solutions in half-space of certain differential equations
Matania Ben-Artzi;Allen Devinatz;
Abstract
Square integrable solutions to the equation{−∂2/∂y2 + P(Dx)+b(y)−λ}u(x, y) = f(x, y) are considered in the half-spacey>0, x ∈ℝ n , whereP(Dx) is a constant coefficient operator. Under suitable conditions on limy→0u(x, y), b(y), f(x, y) and λ, it is shown that suppu = suppf. This generalizes a result due to Walter Littman.
Keywords
