Optimal pairs of score vectors for positional scoring rules
W. V. Gehrlein;B. Gopinath;J. C. Lagarias;P. C. Fishburn;
Abstract
Letw=(w1,⋯,w m ) andv=(v1,⋯,v m-1 ) be nonincreasing real vectors withw1>w m  andv1>v m-1 . With respect to a lista1,⋯,an of linear orders on a setA ofm⩾3 elements, thew-score ofa∈A is the sum overi from 1 tom ofw i  times the number of orders in the list that ranka inith place; thev-score ofa∈A∖{b} is defined in a similar manner after a designated elementb is removed from everya j .
