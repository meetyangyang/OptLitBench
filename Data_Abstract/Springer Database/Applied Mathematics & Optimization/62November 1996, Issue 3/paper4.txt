Local partial regularity theorems for suitable weak solutions of a class of degenerate systems
Xiangsheng Xu;
Abstract
A partial regularity theorem is established for a particular class of weak solutions to the system∂u/∂t− div(K(u)▽u)=σ(u)¦▽ϕ¦2, div(σ(u)▽ϕ)=0 on a bounded domain inR N . Under our assumptions, σ(u) may exhibit exponential decay, and thus the system may be degenerate. Our proof is based upon a blow-up argument.
