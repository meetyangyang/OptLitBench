Quadratic Functionals with General Boundary Conditions
Z. Došlá;O. Došlý;
Abstract
 The purpose of this paper is to give the Reid ``Roundabout Theorem'' for quadratic functionals with general boundary conditions. In particular, we describe the so-called coupled point and regularity condition introduced in [16] in terms of Riccati equation solutions.
Keywords
Key words. Quadratic functional, Coupled point, Regularity condition, Riccati equation, Picone's identity. AMS Classification. 49B10, 34C10.
