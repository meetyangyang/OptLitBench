The monotone follower problem in stochastic decision theory
Ioannis Karatzas;
Abstract
We consider the problem of optimally tracking the “random demand”x+wt, w. Brownian motion, by a nondecreasing processξ. adapted to the Brownian past, so as to minimize the expected lossE∫ 0 T φ(x+wt−ξt)dt. The decision problem is reduced to a free boundary one, and the latter is studied and solved for a large class of cost functionsφ(⋅).
Keywords
