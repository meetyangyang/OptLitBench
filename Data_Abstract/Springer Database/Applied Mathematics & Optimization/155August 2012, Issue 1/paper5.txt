Viscoelastic Timoshenko Beams with Occasionally Constant Relaxation Functions
Nasser-eddine Tatar;
Abstract
For a prescribed desirable arbitrary decay suitable viscoelastic materials are determined through their relaxation functions. It is shown that if we wish to have a decay of order γ(t) then the kernels should be of the same order. That is their product with this function should be summable.
Keywords
Arbitrary decay  Memory term  Relaxation function  Stabilization  Timoshenko beam  Viscoelasticity
