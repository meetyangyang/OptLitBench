Recursive integral equations for the detection of counting processes
Francois B. Dolivo;Frederick J. Beutler;
Abstract
A recursive stochastic integral equation for the detection of counting processes is derived from a previously known formula [5] of the likelihood ratio. This is done quite simply by using a result due to Doléans-Dade [4] on the solution of stochastic integral equations.
Keywords
Integral Equation  Likelihood Ratio  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Counting Process
