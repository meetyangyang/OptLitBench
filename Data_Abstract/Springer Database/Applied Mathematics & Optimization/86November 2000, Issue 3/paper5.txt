A Direct Approach to Deriving Filtering Equations for Diffusion Processes 
N. V. Krylov;A. Zatezalo;
Abstract
 Filtering equations are derived for conditional probability density functions in case of partially observable diffusion processes by using results and methods from the L  p  -theory of SPDEs. The method of derivation is new and does not require any knowledge of filtering theory. 
Keywords
Key words. Filtering, Diffusion processes, Lp -Theory of SPDEs. AMS Classification. Primary 60G35, Secondary 62M20.
