Filtering of Finite-State Time-Nonhomogeneous Markov Processes, a Direct Approach 
N. V. Krylov;A. Zatezalo;
Abstract
 A filtering equation is derived for P(x  t  =x|y  s  ,s∈[0,t])  for a continuous-time finite-state two-component time-nonhomogeneous cadlag Markov process z  t  =(x  t  ,y  t  ) . The derivation is based on some new ideas in the filtering theory and does not require any knowledge of stochastic integration.
Keywords
Key words. Finite-state Markov processes, Filtering. AMS Classification. 60G35, 62M20.
