Second order optimality conditions for theL1-minimization problem
A. Ben-Tal;J. Zowe;
Abstract
In this paper we establish necessary and sufficient second order optimality conditions for theL1-problem. The approach is based on optimality criteria in terms of a curved second directional derivative, discussed in [3]. Our conditions generalize conditions for theL1-problem given in [6]. An example demonstrates the usefulness of our criteria.
