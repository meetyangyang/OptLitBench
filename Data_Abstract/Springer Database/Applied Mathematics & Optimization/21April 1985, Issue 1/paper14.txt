TheN-membranes problem
M. Chipot;G. Vergara-Caffarelli;
Abstract
We study the equilibrium position ofN elastic membranes attached to rigid supports and submitted to the action of forces. They are constrained because they cannot pass through each other. As in the case of the obstacle problem, the solution fails to beC2 and thus fails to be classical, so we provide some new regularity results in different larger spaces using an iterative penalization technique.
