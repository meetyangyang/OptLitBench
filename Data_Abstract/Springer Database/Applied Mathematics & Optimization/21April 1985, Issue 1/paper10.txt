A Signorini problem in elasticity with prescribed contact set
Piero Villaggio;
Abstract
The following unilateral problem is considered. An elastic half-sphere is supported, without friction, by a rigid plane in possible contact with its base. The spherical portion of the surface is loaded by tractions statically equivalent to a compressive load perpendicular to the supporting plane.
Keywords
System Theory  Mathematical Method  Classical Solution  Compressive Load  Supporting Plane
