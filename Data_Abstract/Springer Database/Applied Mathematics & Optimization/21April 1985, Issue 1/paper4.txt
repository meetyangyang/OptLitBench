Optimal control for the dam problem
Avner Friedman;Daniel Yaniro;
Abstract
Consider the variational inequality for the rectangular dam problem and assume that fluid can be withdrawn from the bottom at a rate proportional tok(x). Denote byp(x, y) the pressure of the fluid in the dam corresponding to a particular choice ofk. Consideringk(x) as a control variable varying in a class {0⩽k(x)⩽N, ∫k(x)dx⩽M}, we introduce the functionalJ(k)=∫∫g(y)p(x, y) whereg(y) is a given positive and monotone nondecreasing function. We characterize the controlsk0 which minimizeJ(k).
