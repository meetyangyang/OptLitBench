The anticipative Stratonovich integral in conuclear spaces
T. Bojdecki;J. Jakubowski;
Abstract
We define an anticipative stochastic integral of Stratonovich type with respect to a nonhomogeneous Wiener process in the dual of a nuclear space and investigate its basic properties.
Keywords
Nuclear space  Generalized Wiener process  Skorohod integral  Stratonovich integral  Primary 60H05  Secondary 60B11
