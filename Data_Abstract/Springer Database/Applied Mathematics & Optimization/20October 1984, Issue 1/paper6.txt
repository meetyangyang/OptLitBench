About positive invariance and asymptotic stability
J. W. Nieuwenhuis;
Abstract
In this note we consider the differential equation\(\dot x(t) = Ax(t)\), t≧0 with initial conditionx(0)∈G, whereG is a closed pointed convex cone. Our results pertain to positive invariance and asymptotic stability. A result by Stern, [3], will be improved. Subtangentiality will also be discussed as this notion is important in deriving results about positive invariance.
Keywords
Differential Equation  System Theory  Mathematical Method  Asymptotic Stability  Convex Cone
