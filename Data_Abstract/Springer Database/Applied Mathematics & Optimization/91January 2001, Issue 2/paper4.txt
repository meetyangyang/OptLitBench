Dynamics for Linear Feedback Controlled Two-Dimensional Bénard Equations with Distributed Controls 
Hyung-Chun  Lee;Byeong Chun  Shin;
Abstract
 The long-time behavior of solutions for some feedback distributed control problems associated with the B&eacute;nard equations is studied. Some linear feedback solutions for the B&eacute;nard equations are constructed. Then we prove that these feedback solutions possess the decay (in time) properties. 
Keywords
Key words. Optimal control, Navier—Stokes equation, Bénard equations. AMS Classification. 35B40, 35B37, 35Q30, 65M60.
