Brittle Thin Films
A. Braides;I. Fonseca;
Abstract
 A two-dimensional model for brittle thin films is obtained from a three-dimensional fracture model for elastic material. Γ -convergence techniques are used to identify the limiting effective energy as the thickness of the sample approaches zero.
Keywords
Key words. Γ -Limit, Thin films, Functions of bounded variation, Fracture mechanics. AMS Classification. 35E99, 35M10, 49J45, 73C50, 73M25.
