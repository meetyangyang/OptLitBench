Periodic optimal control in Hilbert space
V. Barbu;N. H. Pavel;
Abstract
Optimal control problem governed byy′=Ay + Bu, y(0)=ɛy (T), ɛ=±1 are studied, whereA is the infinitesimal generator of a nonasymptotically stableCo semigroup andB is a linear operator from a controller spaceU into a state spaceH. Both distributed (B ε L(U, H)) and boundary cases (B εL(U, (D(A*))′)) are investigated. Some applications to periodic control of wave equations are given.
Keywords
