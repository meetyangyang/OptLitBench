Identification of nonlinear elliptic equations
V. Barbu;K. Kunisch;
Abstract
An optimization theoretic approach and algorithm for the estimation of state-dependent coefficients in nonlinear elliptic equation is presented. It is based on a splitting method combined with convex analysis techniques. Convergence of the algorithm is established and numerical examples are included.
Keywords
