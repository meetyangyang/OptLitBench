Remarks about separately convex functions
Pablo Pedregal;
Abstract
This paper shows the property of weak lower semicontinuity of separately convex integrands under more general hypothesis than boundedness inL2 of partial derivatives, and some properties of the parametrized measures associated with such sequences.
