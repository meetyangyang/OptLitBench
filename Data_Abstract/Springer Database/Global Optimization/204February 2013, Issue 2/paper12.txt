The general iterative methods for nonexpansive semigroups in Banach spaces
Rattanaporn Wangkeeree;Rabian Wangkeeree;
Abstract
Let E be a real reflexive strictly convex Banach space which has uniformly Gâteaux differentiable norm. Let \({\mathcal{S} = \{T(s): 0 \leq s  < \infty\}}\) be a nonexpansive semigroup on E such that \({Fix(\mathcal{S}) := \cap_{t\geq 0}Fix( T(t) ) \not= \emptyset}\) , and f is a contraction on E with coefficient 0 <  α <  1. Let F be δ-strongly accretive and λ-strictly pseudo-contractive with δ + λ >  1 and \({0  < \gamma  < \min\left\{\frac{\delta}{\alpha}, \frac{1-\sqrt{ \frac{1-\delta}{\lambda} }}{\alpha} \right\} }\) . When the sequences of real numbers {α n } and {t  n } satisfy some appropriate conditions, the three iterative processes given as follows :$${\left.\begin{array}{ll}{x_{n+1} = \alpha_n \gamma f(x_n) + (I - \alpha_n F)T(t_n)x_n,\quad n\geq 0,}\\ {y_{n+1} = \alpha_n \gamma f(T(t_n)y_n) + (I - \alpha_n F)T(t_n)y_n,\quad n\geq 0,}\end{array}\right.}$$and$$ z_{n+1} = T(t_n)( \alpha_n \gamma f(z_n) + (I - \alpha_n F)z_n),\quad n\geq 0 $$converge strongly to \({\tilde{x}}\) , where \({\tilde{x}}\) is the unique solution in \({Fix(\mathcal{S})}\) of the variational inequality
$${ \langle (F - \gamma f)\tilde {x}, j(x - \tilde{x}) \rangle \geq 0,\quad x\in Fix(\mathcal{S}).}$$Our results extend and improve corresponding ones of Li et al. (Nonlinear Anal 70:3065–3071, 2009) and Chen and He (Appl Math Lett 20:751–757, 2007) and many others.
