On the image space analysis for vector quasi-equilibrium problems with a variable ordering relation
G. Mastroeni;
Abstract
In this paper, we extend the image space analysis to a vector quasi-equilibrium problem (VQEP) with a variable ordering relation and we analyse scalar and vector saddle point optimality conditions arising from the existence of a vector separation in the image space associated with VQEP.
Keywords
Image space  Separation  Vector quasi-equilibrium problems
