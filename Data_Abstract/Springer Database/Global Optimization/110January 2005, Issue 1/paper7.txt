Vector Equilibrium Problems. Existence Theorems and Convexity of Solution Set
Jun-Yi Fu;
Abstract
The natural quasi-concavity of set-valued mappings in an ordered vector space is introduced. Existence theorems for vector equilibrium problems involving set-valued monotone mappings are obtained and the convexity of the solution set is shown.
Keywords
Convexity  Monotonicity  Vector equilibrium problem  Set-valued mapping
