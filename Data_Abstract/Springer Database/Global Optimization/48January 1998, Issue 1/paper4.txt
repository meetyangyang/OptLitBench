Testing the ℜ- strategy for a Reverse Convex Problem
Alexanders Strekalovsky;Ider Tsevendorj;
Abstract
This paper is devoted to solving a reverse-convex problem. The approach presented here is based on Global Optimality Conditions. We propose a general conception of a Global Search Algorithm and develop each part of it. The results of numerical experiments with the dimension up to 400 are also given.
Keywords
Global optimization  Global optimality conditions  Global search algorithm
