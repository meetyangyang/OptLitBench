Global regularity for solutions to Dirichlet problem for discontinuous elliptic systems with nonlinearity q > 1 and with natural growth
Sofia Giuffrè;Giovanna Idone;
Abstract
In this paper we deal with the Hölder regularity up to the boundary of the solutions to a nonhomogeneous Dirichlet problem for second-order discontinuous elliptic systems with nonlinearity q > 1 and with natural growth. The aim of the paper is to clarify that the solutions of the above problem are always global Hölder continuous in the case of the dimension n = q without any kind of regularity assumptions on the coefficients. As a consequence of this sharp result, the singular sets \(\Omega_0 \subset \Omega\) , \(\Sigma _0 \subset \partial \Omega\) are always empty for n = q. Moreover we show that also for 1 < q < 2, but q close enough to 2, the solutions are global Hölder continuous for n = 2.
