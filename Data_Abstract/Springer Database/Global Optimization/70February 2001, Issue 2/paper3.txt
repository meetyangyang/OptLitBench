Iterative Schemes for Multivalued Quasi Variational Inclusions
Muhammad Aslam Noor;
Abstract
In this paper, we suggest and analyze a class of iterative schemes for solving multivalued quasi variational inclusions using the resolvent operator method. As special cases, we obtain a number of known and new iterative schems for solving variational inequalities and related optimization problems. The results obtained in this represent an improvement and a significant refinement of previously known results.
Keywords
Variational inclusions  Resolvent equations  Algorithms  Iterative methods  Convergence
