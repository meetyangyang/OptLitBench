Prederivatives of convex set-valued maps and applications to set optimization problems
Michaël Gaydu;Michel H. Geoffroy;Yvesner Marcelin;
Abstract
We investigate the existence of several kinds of prederivatives for set-valued mappings enjoying convex properties. Then, we established both necessary and sufficient optimality conditions, involving such prederivatives, for set optimization problems.
