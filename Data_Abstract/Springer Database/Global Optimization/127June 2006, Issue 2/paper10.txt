Computing the Minkowski Sum of Prisms
D. Pallaschke;J. Rosenmüller;
Abstract
Within this paper we study the Minkowski sum of prisms (“Cephoids”) in a finite dimensional vector space. For a vector
\(a \in \mathbb{R}^n\) with positive components we write
\({\bar{a}} = ({1\over \bar{a}_1}, \cdots , {1\over \bar{a}_n})\) and denote by
\(\prod = \prod^{\bar{a}} = \{x \in \mathbb{R}^n | \langle \bar{a}, {\bf x} \rangle \leqslant 1, {\bf x} \geqslant 0 \}\) the associated prism. We provide a representation of a finite sum of prisms in terms of inequalities.
