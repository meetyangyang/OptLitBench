Some regularities for parametric equilibrium problems
M. Bogdan;J. Kolumbán;
Abstract
In this paper we aim to study a family of equilibrium problems governed by pseudomonotone maps depending on a parameter and the behavior of their solutions. The main result gives sufficient conditions for closedness of the solution map defined on the set of parameters.
Keywords
