Iterative approximation method for finite family of relatively quasi nonexpansive mappings and systems of equilibrium problems
Yekini Shehu;
Abstract
In this paper, a new iterative scheme by hybrid method is constructed. Strong convergence of the scheme to a common element of the set of common fixed points of a finite family of relatively quasi-nonexpansive mappings and set of common solutions to a system of equilibrium problems in a uniformly convex real Banach space which is also uniformly smooth is proved. Our results extend important recent results.
