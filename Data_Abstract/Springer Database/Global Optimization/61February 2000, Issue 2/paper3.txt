Optimal Solutions in an Allocation Process for a Continuum of Traders
Fabián Flores-Bazán;
Abstract
We prove an existence theorem in an allocation process for a continuum of traders in the absence of the convexity assumption on the cost function and under the presence of some economic parameters.
Keywords
Allocation process  Continuum of traders  Convexified problem  Liapunov theorem  Sequentially weakly lower semicontinuous function  Biconjugate function
