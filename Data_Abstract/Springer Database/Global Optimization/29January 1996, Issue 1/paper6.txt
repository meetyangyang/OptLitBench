Optimal random non-adaptive algorithm for global optimization of Brownian motion
Hisham Al-Mharmah;James M. Calvin;
Abstract
In this paper we study random non-adaptive algorithms for finding the maximum of a continuous function on the unit interval. We compare the average performance of different algorithms under the assumption of Wiener measure on the space of continuous functions. Placing the observations independently according to a Beta(2/3,2/3) density function is shown to be the optimal random non-adaptive algorithm. The performance is compared with other random and deterministic non-adaptive algorithms.
Keywords
