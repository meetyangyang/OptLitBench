Optimality Conditions for D.C. Vector Optimization Problems Under Reverse Convex Constraints
N. Gadhi;M. Laghdir;A. Metrane;
Abstract
In this paper, we establish global necessary and sufficient optimality conditions for D.C. vector optimization problems under reverse convex constraints. An application to vector fractional mathematical programming is also given.
Keywords
convex mapping  D.C. mapping  global weak minimal solution  optimality condition  reverse convex  subdifferential
