The subgradient formula for the minimal time function in the case of constant dynamics in Hilbert space
Giovanni Colombo;Peter R. Wolenski;
Abstract
The minimal time function with constant dynamics is studied in the context of a Hilbert space. A general formula for the subgradient is proven, and assumptions are identified in which the minimal time function is lower C2.
Keywords
Distance functions  Minimal time function  Subgradient formulas  Variational analysis
