Existence and stability of minimax regret equilibria
Zhe Yang;Yong Jian Pu;
Abstract
In this paper, we study minimax regret equilibria. First, existence theorem of minimax regret equilibria is proved. Further, the generic stability of minimax regret equilibria is studied. We show that the set of minimax regret equilibria for most of problems (in sense of Baire category) is a singleton set.
Keywords
Minimax regret equilibria  Essential point  Generic stability  Baire category
