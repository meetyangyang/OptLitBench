A Chain Rule for Upper Semicontinuous (CF)-Mappings
Tae-Hwan Yoon;
Abstract
A chain rule for calculating convexificators of composite functions of the type f  =  h∘g, with the inner factor g being a transformation of \(\mathbb{R}^n\), is proposed. The proof is based on a double application of a mean value theorem for (CF)-mappings due to V.F. Demyanov and V. Jeyakumar (see [4]), along with a stability property for the support of a certain ɛ-perturbation of (CF)-mappings.
