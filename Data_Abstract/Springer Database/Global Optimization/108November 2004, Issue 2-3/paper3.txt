Generalized Vector Equilibrium Problems with Trifunctions
Y. Chiang;O. Chadli;J. C. Yao;
Abstract
In this paper, we study the existence of strong and weak solutions of the generalized vector equilibrium problems for trifunctions. Two special classes of vector-valued trifunctions are introdcued, called the classes of (SPM) and (GPM), respectively. Some existence results for strong solutions associated to functions of these classes are given.
Keywords
Generalized vector equilibrium problems  Topological pseudomonotonicity  (SPM) classes and (GPM) classes
