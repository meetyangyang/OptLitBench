On constructing total orders and solving vector optimization problems with total orders
Mahide Küçük;Mustafa Soyertem;Yalçın Küçük;
Abstract
In this paper, we introduce a construction method of total ordering cone on \({\mathbb{R}^n}\) . It is shown that any total ordering cone on \({\mathbb{R}^n}\) is isomorphic to the cone \({\mathbb{R}^n_{lex}}\) . Existence of a total ordering cone that contain given cone with a compact base is shown. By using this cone, a solving method of vector and set valued optimization problems is presented.
Keywords
Nonconvex analysis  Vector optimization  Total order  Nonconvex optimization  Scalarization
