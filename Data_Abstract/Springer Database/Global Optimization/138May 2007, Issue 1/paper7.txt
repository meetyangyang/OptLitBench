A (CF)-mapping of integral functional of locally lipschitz functions
Tae-Hwan Yoon;
Abstract
We find a (CF)-mapping of the integral functional of locally Lipschitz functions f  t  parametrized by \(t \in T\). In the process of obtaining a (CF)-mapping, the hypothesis of upper semicontinuity of the set-valued map
\(t \mapsto C_{f_t} (x)\) is needed, where \(C_{f_{t}} (x)\) denotes a convexificator of f  t  at x. As a corollary of our result, we get (CF)-mappings which are obtained by Clarke subdifferentials and Michel–Penot subdifferentials, respectively. Finally, the examples specifically deriving a convexificator of the integral functional are provided.
