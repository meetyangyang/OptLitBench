Multiple solutions for semilinear resonant elliptic problems with discontinuous nonlinearities via nonsmooth double linking theorem
Kaimin Teng;
Abstract
In the present paper, some multiplicity results for semilinear resonant elliptic problems with discontinuous nonlinearities are obtained by using our extended double linking theorem.
