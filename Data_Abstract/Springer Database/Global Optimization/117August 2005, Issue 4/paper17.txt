Existence Theorems of Simultaneous Equilibrium Problems and Generalized Vector Quasi-Saddle Points
Lai-Jiu Lin;
Abstract
In this paper, we establish the existence theorems of simultaneous equilibrium problems. As consequences of our results, we establish the existence theorem of simultaneous mathematical programs and equilibrium problems and the existence theorems of generalized vector quasi-saddle point problems.
Keywords
closed multivalued map  C(x)-convex  C-diagonally quasi-convex  Fréchet derivative  generalized vector quasi-saddle point
