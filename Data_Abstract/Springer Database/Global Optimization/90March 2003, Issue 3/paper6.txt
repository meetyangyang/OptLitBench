Exceptional Family of Elements and Feasibility for Nonlinear Complementarity Problems
Nan-jing Huang;Cheng-jia Gao;Xiao-ping Huang;
Abstract
In this paper, we introduce the new concept of α-exceptional families of elements and (α,β)-exceptional families of elements for continuous functions, and utilize these notions for the study of the feasibility of nonlinear complementarity problems in Rn and an infinite-dimensional Hilbert space H without the assumption K*⫅ K.
Keywords
Exceptional family of elements  Nonlinear complementarity problem  Feasibility  Homotopy
