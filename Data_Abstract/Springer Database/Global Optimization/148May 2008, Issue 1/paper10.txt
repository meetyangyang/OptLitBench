Optimality for (h,φ)-multiobjective programming involving generalized type-I functions
Guolin Yu;Sanyang Liu;
Abstract
Based upon Ben-Tal’s generalized algebraic operations, new classes of functions, namely (h,φ)-type-I, quasi (h,φ)-type-I, and pseudo (h,φ)-type-I, are defined for a multi-objective programming problem. Sufficient optimality conditions are obtained for a feasible solution to be a Pareto efficient solution for this problem. Some duality results are established by utilizing the above defined classes of functions, considering the concept of a Pareto efficient solution.
