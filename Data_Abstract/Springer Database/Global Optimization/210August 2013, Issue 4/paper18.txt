A new iterative scheme for fixed point problems of infinite family of κ  i -pseudo contractive mappings, equilibrium problem, variational inequality problems
Atid Kangtunyakarn;
Abstract
In this paper, we modify the set of variational inequality to construct a new iterative scheme for finding a common element of the set of fixed point problems of infinite family of κ  i -pseudo-contractive mappings and the set of equilibrium problem and two set of variational inequality problems.
