Convergence criteria for generalized gradient methods of solving locally Lipschitz feasibility problems
Dan Butnariu;Abraham Mehrez;
Abstract
We prove the convergence of a class of iterative algorithms for solving locally Lipschitz feasibility problems, that is, finite systems of inequalities f i (x)≤0, (i ∈ I), where each f i  is a locally Lipschitz functional on ℝ n . We also obtain a new convergence criterion for the so-called block-iterative projection methods of finding common points of finite families of convex closed subsets of ℝ n  as defined by Aharoni and Censor ([3]).
