Finding Postal Carrier Walk Paths in Mixed Graphs
Hong Yan;Gerald L. Thompson;
Abstract
The postman problem requires finding a lowest cost tour in a connected graph that traverses each edge at least once. In this paper we first give a brief survey of the literature on postman problems including, the original Chinese postman problem on undirected graphs, the windy Chinese postman problem on graphs where the cost of an arc depends on the direction the arc is transversed, the directed postman problem on graphs with directed edges, and the mixed postman problem on graphs in which there are some directed and some undirected arcs.
Keywords
postman problem  windy postman problem  edge traversals in graphs
