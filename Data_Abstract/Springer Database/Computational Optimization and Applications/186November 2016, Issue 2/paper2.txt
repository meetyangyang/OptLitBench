Central axes and peripheral points in high dimensional directional datasets
Annabella Astorino;Manlio Gaudioso;Alberto Seeger;
Abstract
We introduce a new notion of central axis for a finite set \(\{a_1,\ldots ,a_m\}\) of vectors in \(\mathbb {R}^n\). In tandem, we discuss different ways of measuring the dispersion of the data points \(a_i\)’s around the central axis. Finally, we explain how to detect numerically the most peripheral points of the given dataset.
