On Two Applications of H-Differentiability to Optimization and Complementarity Problems
M.A. Tawhid;M. Seetharama Gowda;
Abstract
In a recent paper, Gowda and Ravindran (Algebraic univalence theorems for nonsmooth functions, Research Report, Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Maryland, Baltimore, MD 21250, March 15, 1998) introduced the concepts of H-differentiability and H-differential for a function f : Rn → Rn and showed that the Fréchet derivative of a Fréchet differentiable function, the Clarke generalized Jacobian of a locally Lipschitzian function, the Bouligand subdifferential of a semismooth function, and the C-differential of a C-differentiable function are particular instances of H-differentials.
Keywords
H-differentiability  nonlinear complementarity problem  NCP function  merit function  locally Lipschitzian function  generalized Jacobian
