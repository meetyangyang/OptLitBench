Minimization of the Tikhonov functional in Banach spaces smooth and convex of power type by steepest descent in the dual
Kamil S. Kazimierski;
Abstract
For Tikhonov functionals of the form Ψ(x)=‖Ax−y‖  Y   r  +α‖x‖  X   q   we investigate a steepest descent method in the dual of the Banach space X. We show convergence rates for the proposed method and present numerical tests.
Keywords
Convex optimization  Convergence rate  Linear convergence  Sparsity  Smooth of power type  Convex of power type
