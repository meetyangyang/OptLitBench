Lower bounds based on linear programming for the quadratic assignment problem
Zvi Drezner;
Abstract
In this paper we prove that the Classical Gilmore-Lawler lower bound for the quadratic assignment problem is equivalent to a solution of a certain linear programming problem. By adding additional constraints to this linear programming problem we derive a lower bound which is at least as good as the Gilmore-Lawler lower bound.
Keywords
