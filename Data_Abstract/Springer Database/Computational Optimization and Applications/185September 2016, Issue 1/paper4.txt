On the global convergence of the inexact semi-smooth Newton method for absolute value equation
J. Y. Bello Cruz;O. P. Ferreira;L. F. Prudente;
Abstract
In this paper, we investigate global convergence properties of the inexact nonsmooth Newton method for solving the system of absolute value equations. Global Q-linear convergence is established under suitable assumptions. Moreover, we present some numerical experiments designed to investigate the practical viability of the proposed scheme.
