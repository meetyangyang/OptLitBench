Optimization with Variable Sets of Constraints and an Application to Truss Design
Wolfgang Achtziger;
Abstract
We discuss the minimization of a continuous function on a subset of Rn subject to a finite set of continuous constraints. At each point, a given set-valued map determines the subset of constraints considered at this point. Such problems arise e.g. in the design of engineering structures.
Keywords
continuous optimization  numerical optimization  constrained programming  successive approximation methods  structural optimization  truss topology optimization
