On the Minimizing Trajectory of Convex Functions with Unbounded Level Sets
W.T. Obuchowska;
Abstract
We consider a convex function f(x) with unbounded level sets. Many algorithms, if applied to this class of functions, do not guarantee convergence to the global infimum. Our approach to this problem leads to a derivation of the equation of a parametrized curve x(t), such that an infimum of f(x) along this curve is equal to the global infimum of the function on ℝ n .
Keywords
convex function  cone of recession  unattained infimum  unbounded level set
