Conjugate Grids for Unconstrained Optimisation
D. Byatt;I.D. Coope;C.J. Price;
Abstract
Several recent papers have proposed the use of grids for solving unconstrained optimisation problems. Grid-based methods typically generate a sequence of grid local minimisers which converges to stationary points under mild conditions.
Keywords
unconstrained optimisation  grid-based methods  pattern search  conjugate directions
