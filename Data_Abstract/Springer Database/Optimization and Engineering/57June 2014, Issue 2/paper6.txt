The optimization test environment
Ferenc Domes;Martin Fuchs;Hermann Schichl;Arnold Neumaier;
Abstract
The Optimization Test Environment is an interface to efficiently test different optimization solvers. It is designed as a tool for both developers of solver software and practitioners who just look for the best solver for their specific problem class. It enables users to:  Choose and compare diverse solver routines;  Organize and solve large test problem sets;  Select interactively subsets of test problem sets;  Perform a statistical analysis of the results, automatically produced as  Open image in new window , PDF, and JPG output.  The Optimization Test Environment is free to use for research purposes.
