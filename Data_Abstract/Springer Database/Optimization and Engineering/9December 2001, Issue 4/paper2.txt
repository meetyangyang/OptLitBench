An Introduction to the Space Mapping Technique
Mohamed H. Bakr;John W. Bandler;Kaj Madsen;Jacob Søndergaard;
Abstract
The space mapping technique is intended for optimization of engineering models which involve very expensive function evaluations. It is assumed that two different models of the same physical system are available: Besides the expensive model of primary interest (denoted the fine model), access to a cheaper (coarse) model is assumed which may be less accurate.
Keywords
non-linear optimization  space mapping  surrogate modelling
