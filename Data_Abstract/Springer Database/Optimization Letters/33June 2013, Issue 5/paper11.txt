A note on lower Studniarski derivative of the perturbation map in parameterized vector optimization
Q. L. Wang;
Abstract
The note provides two results concerning sensitivity analysis in parameterized vector optimization. This is appropriate modifications for the deficiencies contained in two earlier results by Sun and Li (Optim. Lett. 5:601–614, 2011). Several examples are given for illustrating the results obtained.
Keywords
Parameterized vector optimization  Perturbation maps  Weak perturbation maps  Higher-order lower Studniarski derivatives
