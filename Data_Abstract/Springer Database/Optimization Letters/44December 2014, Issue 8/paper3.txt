Agreeable solutions of discrete time optimal control problems
Alexander J. Zaslavski;
Abstract
In this paper we study solutions of an infinite horizon discrete-time optimal control problem with a compact metric space of states which arises in economic dynamics. We consider locally maximal solutions whose prototypes were introduced and used for models of solid-state physics and in the theory of thermodynamical equilibrium for materials, good solutions and agreeable solutions which were introduced for models of economic dynamics. We show that if an objective function possesses an asymptotic turnpike property, then an admissible program is agreeable if and only if it is locally maximal and good.
Keywords
Agreeable program  Good program  Infinite horizon problem  Locally maximal program
