A reduced complexity zero-forcing beamformer design for multiuser MIMO systems
Bin Li;Zhi Guo Feng;Hai Huyen Dam;Antonio Cantoni;
Abstract
Zero-forcing beamforming under per-antenna power constraints is considered for rural applications. By exploring the structures of a deterministic line-of-sight channel matrix, some properties are found for the optimal weighting matrix. Particularly, some constraints can be removed from the original formulation. With these properties, a reduced complexity zero-forcing beamformer design is proposed. Numerical examples under several different scenarios are provided.
Keywords
Zero-forcing beamforming (ZFBF)  Per-antenna power constraint (PAPC)  Multiuser MIMO systems
