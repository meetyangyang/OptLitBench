A note on dependency between interval linear systems
Wei Li;
Abstract
This note proves a more or less surprising result: that the sets of the weak solution of interval linear equations \(\mathbf{A}x \!=\!\mathbf{b}\) and the interval linear inequalities \(\mathbf{A}x\!\le \! \mathbf{b}\), \(\mathbf{A}x\ge \mathbf{b}\) are identical.
Keywords
Interval linear equation  Interval linear inequality  Weak solution  Dependency
