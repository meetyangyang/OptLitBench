Elementary convex techniques for equilibrium, minimax and variational problems
M. Ruiz Galán;
Abstract
The aim of this paper is to generalize and unify in a single statement some results on the existence of a solution for an equilibrium problem, as well as to apply it to derive consequences in minimax theory and variational analysis.
Keywords
Equilibrium problems  Minimax inequalities  Monotone variational inequalities  Hahn–Banach theorem
