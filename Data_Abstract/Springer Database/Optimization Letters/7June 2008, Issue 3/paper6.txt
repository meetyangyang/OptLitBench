Existences of solutions for generalized vector quasi-equilibrium problems
S. J. Li;J. Zeng;
Abstract
In the paper, existence theorems for two classes of generalized vector quasi-equilibrium problems are established by using a fixed point theorem and some examples are given to illustrate them. An application to vector quasi-optimization problems is shown.
Keywords
Generalized vector quasi-equilibrium problem  Compactly open  Compactly closed  Upper semicontinuity  Fixed point theorem
