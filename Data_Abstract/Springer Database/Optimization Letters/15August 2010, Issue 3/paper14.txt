Monotonicity properties of the volume of the unit ball in \({\mathbb{R}^{n}}\) 
Cristinel Mortici;
Abstract
The aim of this paper is to establish new bounds for ratios involving the volume of the unit ball in \({\mathbb{R}^{n}}\).
Keywords
Volume of the unit n-dimensional ball  Surface area of the unit n-dimensional ball  Gamma function  Monotonicity  Inequalities
