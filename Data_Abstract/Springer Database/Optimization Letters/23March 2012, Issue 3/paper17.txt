A theorem of the alternatives for the equation |Ax| − |B||x| = b 
Jiri Rohn;
Abstract
A theorem of the alternatives for the equation \({|Ax|-|B||x|=b\ (A,B\in{\mathbb{R}}^{n\times n},\, b\in{\mathbb{R}}^n)}\) is proved and several consequences are drawn. In particular, a class of matrices A, B is identified for which the equation has exactly 2 n  solutions for each positive right-hand side b.
Keywords
