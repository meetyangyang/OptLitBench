Combined partial regularization and descent method for a generalized primal–dual system
D. A. Dyabilkin;I. V. Konnov;
Abstract
A variational inequality system, which is a generalization of the saddle point problem, is considered. The system does not possess monotonicity properties and the feasible set is unbounded in general. To solve the problem we propose a completely implementable iterative scheme, whose convergence is proved under certain coercivity type conditions.
