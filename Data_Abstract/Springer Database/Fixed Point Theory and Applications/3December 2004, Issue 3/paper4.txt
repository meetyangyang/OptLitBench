Generic convergence of iterates for a class of nonlinear mappings
Simeon Reich;Alexander J Zaslavski;
Abstract
Let  Open image in new window  be a nonempty, bounded, closed, and convex subset of a Banach space. We show that the iterates of a typical element (in the sense of Baire's categories) of a class of continuous self-mappings of  Open image in new window  converge uniformly on  Open image in new window  to the unique fixed point of this typical element.
