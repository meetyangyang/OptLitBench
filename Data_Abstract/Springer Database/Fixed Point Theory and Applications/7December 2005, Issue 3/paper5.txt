Common fixed point theorems for compatible self-maps of Hausdorff topological spaces
Gerald F Jungck;
Abstract
The concept of proper orbits of a map  Open image in new window  is introduced and results of the following type are obtained. If a continuous self-map  Open image in new window  of a Hausdorff topological space  Open image in new window  has relatively compact proper orbits, then  Open image in new window  has a fixed point. In fact,  Open image in new window  has a common fixed point with every continuous self-map  Open image in new window  of  Open image in new window  which is nontrivially compatible with  Open image in new window . A collection of metric and semimetric space fixed point theorems follows as a consequence. Specifically, a theorem by Kirk regarding diminishing orbital diameters is generalized, and a fixed point theorem for maps with no recurrent points is proved.
