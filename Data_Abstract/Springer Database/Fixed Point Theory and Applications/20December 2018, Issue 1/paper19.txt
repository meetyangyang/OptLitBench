Fixed points of fuzzy neutrosophic soft mapping with decision-making
Muhammad Riaz;Masooma Raza Hashmi;
Abstract
In this paper, we introduce some operations on a fuzzy neutrosophic soft set (\(\mathfrak{fns}\)-set) by utilizing the theories of fuzzy sets, soft sets and neutrosophic sets. We introduce \(\mathfrak{fns}\)-mappings by using a cartesian product with relations on \(\mathfrak{fns}\)-sets and establish some results on fixed points of an \(\mathfrak{fns}\)-mapping. We present an algorithm to deal with uncertainties in the multi-criteria decision making to slenderize energy crises by using an \(\mathfrak{fns}\)-average operator and a comparison table for \(\mathfrak{fns}\)-sets.
