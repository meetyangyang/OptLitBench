Nonlinear Mean Ergodic Theorems for Semigroups in Hilbert Spaces
Seyit Temir;Ozlem Gul;
Abstract
Let  Open image in new window  be a nonempty subset (not necessarily closed and convex) of a Hilbert space and let  Open image in new window  be a semigroup on  Open image in new window  and let  Open image in new window  be an almost orbit of  Open image in new window . In this paper, we prove that every almost orbit of  Open image in new window  is almost weakly and strongly convergent to its asymptotic center.
