The Equivalence between  Open image in new window -Stabilities of The Krasnoselskij and The Mann Iterations
Ştefan M Şoltuz;
Abstract
We prove the equivalence between the  Open image in new window -stabilities of the Krasnoselskij and the Mann iterations; a consequence is the equivalence with the  Open image in new window -stability of the Picard-Banach iteration.
