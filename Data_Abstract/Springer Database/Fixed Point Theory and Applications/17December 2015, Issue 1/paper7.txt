Coupled fixed point theorems for single-valued operators in b-metric spaces
Monica-Felicia Bota;Adrian Petruşel;Gabriela Petruşel;Bessem Samet;
Abstract
The aim of this paper to present fixed point results for single-valued operators in b-metric spaces. The case of scalar metric and the case of vector-valued metric approaches are considered. As an application, a system of integral equations is studied.
Keywords
single-valued operator  vector-valued metric  fixed point  ordered metric space  coupled fixed point  integral equation  Ulam-Hyers stability  47H10  54H25
