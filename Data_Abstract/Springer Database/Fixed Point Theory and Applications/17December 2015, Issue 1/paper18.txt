Existence of solutions of first-order differential equations via a fixed point theorem for discontinuous operators
Rubén Figueroa Sestelo;Rodrigo López Pouso;
Abstract
We use a recent Schauder-type result for discontinuous operators in order to look for solutions for first-order differential equations subject to initial functional conditions. We show how this abstract fixed-point result allows us to consider a nonlinearity which can be strongly discontinuous. Some examples of applications and comparison with recent literature are included.
Keywords
