 \(C^{\ast}\)-Algebra-valued b-metric spaces and related fixed point theorems
Zhenhua Ma;Lining Jiang;
Abstract
Based on the concept and properties of \(C^{\ast}\)-algebras, the paper introduces a concept of \(C^{\ast}\)-algebra-valued b-metric spaces which generalizes the concept of \(C^{\ast}\)-algebra-valued metric spaces and gives some basic fixed point theorems for self-map with contractive condition on such spaces. As applications, existence and uniqueness results for a type of operator equation and an integral equation are given.
