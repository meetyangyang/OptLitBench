Fixed point theorems for set-valued mappings on TVS-cone metric spaces
Raúl Fierro;
Abstract
In the context of TVS-cone metric spaces, we prove a Bishop-Phelps and a Caristi type theorem. These results allow us to prove a fixed point theorem for \((\delta, L)\)-weak contraction according to a pseudo Hausdorff metric defined by means of a cone metric.
