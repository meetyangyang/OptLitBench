On best proximity points of upper semicontinuous multivalued mappings
Ghada AlNemer;Jack Markin;Naseer Shahzad;
Abstract
In this paper we study the existence of best proximity points of a nonself upper semicontinuous multivalued mapping \(T:A\rightarrow2^{B}\) in a strictly convex Banach space. This multivalued mapping commutes with affine, noncyclic, and relatively u-continuous single-valued mapping \(f:A \cup B\rightarrow A\cup B\). Also, we study the case when T commutes with a family of commuting, affine, noncyclic, and relatively u-continuous single-valued mappings on \(A\cup B\). Moreover, we present some examples to illustrate our results.
