Weak and Strong Convergence Theorems for Nonexpansive Mappings in Banach Spaces
Jing Zhao;Songnian He;Yongfu Su;
Abstract
The purpose of this paper is to introduce two implicit iteration schemes for approximating fixed points of nonexpansive mapping  Open image in new window  and a finite family of nonexpansive mappings  Open image in new window , respectively, in Banach spaces and to prove weak and strong convergence theorems. The results presented in this paper improve and extend the corresponding ones of H.-K. Xu and R. Ori, 2001, Z. Opial, 1967, and others.
Keywords
Banach Space  Differential Geometry  Convergence Theorem  Nonexpansive Mapping  Strong Convergence
