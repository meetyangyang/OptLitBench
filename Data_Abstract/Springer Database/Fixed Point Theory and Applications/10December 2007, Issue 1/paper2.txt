Quasicontraction Mappings in Modular Spaces without Δ2-Condition
M.A. Khamsi;
Abstract
As a generalization to Banach contraction principle, Ćirić introduced the concept of quasi-contraction mappings. In this paper, we investigate these kinds of mappings in modular function spaces without the  Open image in new window 2-condition. In particular, we prove the existence of fixed points and discuss their uniqueness.
