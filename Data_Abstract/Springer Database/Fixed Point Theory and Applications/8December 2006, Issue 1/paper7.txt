Strong convergence to common fixed points of nonexpansive mappings without commutativity assumption
Yonghong Yao;Rudong Chen;Haiyun Zhou;
Abstract
We introduce an iteration scheme for nonexpansive mappings in a Hilbert space and prove that the iteration converges strongly to common fixed points of the mappings without commutativity assumption.
Keywords
Hilbert Space  Differential Geometry  Nonexpansive Mapping  Strong Convergence  Computational Biology
