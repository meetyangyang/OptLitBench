A degree theory for a class of perturbed Fredholm maps
Pierluigi Benevieri;Alessandro Calamai;Massimo Furi;
Abstract
We define a notion of degree for a class of perturbations of nonlinear Fredholm maps of index zero between infinite-dimensional real Banach spaces. Our notion extends the degree introduced by Nussbaum for locally  Open image in new window -contractive perturbations of the identity, as well as the recent degree for locally compact perturbations of Fredholm maps of index zero defined by the first and third authors.
