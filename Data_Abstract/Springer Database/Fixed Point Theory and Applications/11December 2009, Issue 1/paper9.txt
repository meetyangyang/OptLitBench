On Series-Like Iterative Equation with a General Boundary Restriction
Wei Song;Guo-qiu Yang;Feng-chun Lei;
Abstract
By means of Schauder fixed point theorem and Banach contraction principle, we investigate the existence and uniqueness of Lipschitz solutions of the equation Open image in new window . Moreover, we get that the solution Open image in new window  depends continuously on Open image in new window . As a corollary, we investigate the existence and uniqueness of Lipschitz solutions of the series-like iterative equation Open image in new window  with a general boundary restriction, where Open image in new window  is a given Lipschitz function, and Open image in new window  are compact convex subsets of Open image in new window  with nonempty interior.
