Critical Point Theorems and Ekeland Type Variational Principle with Applications
Lai-Jiu Lin;Sung-Yu Wang;QamrulHasan Ansari;
Abstract
We introduce the notion of  Open image in new window -spaces which is much weaker than cone metric spaces defined by Huang and X. Zhang (2007). We establish some critical point theorems in the setting of  Open image in new window -spaces and, in particular, in the setting of complete cone metric spaces. Our results generalize the critical point theorem proposed by Dancs et al. (1983) and the results given by Khanh and Quy (2010) to  Open image in new window -spaces and cone metric spaces. As applications of our results, we characterize the completeness of  Open image in new window -space (cone metric spaces and quasimetric spaces are special cases of  Open image in new window -space) and studying the Ekeland type variational principle for single variable vector-valued functions as well as for multivalued bifunctions in the setting of cone metric spaces.
