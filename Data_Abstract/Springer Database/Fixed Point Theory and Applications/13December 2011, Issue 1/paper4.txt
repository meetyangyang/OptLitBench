The Iterative Method of Generalized  Open image in new window -Concave Operators
Yanqiu Zhou;Jingxian Sun;Jie Sun;
Abstract
We define the concept of the generalized  Open image in new window -concave operators, which generalize the definition of the  Open image in new window -concave operators. By using the iterative method and the partial ordering method, we prove the existence and uniqueness of fixed points of this class of the operators. As an example of the application of our results, we show the existence and uniqueness of solutions to a class of the Hammerstein integral equations.
Keywords
Banach Space  Real Number  Integral Equation  Natural Number  Iterative Method
