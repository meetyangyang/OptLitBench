On strongly degenerate complementary cones and solution rays
N. Eagambaram;S. R. Mohan;
Abstract
In this paper we show that ifA is a matrix in the class of matricesE(d), for ad ∈R n ,d > 0, introduced by Garcia, then the boundary of the set ofq ∈R n  for which the linear complementarity problem (q, A) has a solution is equal to the union of all strongly degenerate cones of (I, -A). This is a generalization of a similar result for copositive plus matrices observed by Cottle. We also study some related questions.
Keywords
Strongly degenerate complementary cone  solution ray  boundary
