Note on the computational complexity ofj-radii of polytopes in ℝ n  
U. Faigle;W. Kern;M. Streng;
Abstract
We show that, for fixed dimensionn, the approximation of inner and outerj-radii of polytopes in ℝ n , endowed with the Euclidean norm, is in ℙ. Our method is based on the standard polynomial time algorithms for solving a system of polynomial inequalities over the reals in fixed dimension.
Keywords
