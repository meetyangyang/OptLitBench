Integral approximation sequences
Jozsef Beck;Joel Spencer;
Abstract
Letn linear formsL  i  onm variables be given, normalized so that all coefficients have absolute value at most unity. Letw 1, ...,w  m  be real numbers andx 1, ...,x  m  be integers. We sayE  i =L  i  (w 1, ...,w  m )-L  i  (x 1, ...,x  m ) is the error in approximating thew's by thex's with respect to formL  i  It is shown that given anyw's there is an integral approximation ofx's so that the errorsE  i  are small-roughly that\(E_i  = O(\sqrt i  In i )\) simultaneously for alli.
