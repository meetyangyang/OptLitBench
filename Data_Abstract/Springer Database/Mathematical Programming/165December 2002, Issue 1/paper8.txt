A feasible semismooth asymptotically Newton method for mixed complementarity problems
Defeng Sun;Robert S. Womersley;Houduo Qi;
Abstract
 Semismooth Newton methods constitute a major research area for solving mixed complementarity problems (MCPs). Early research on semismooth Newton methods is mainly on infeasible methods. However, some MCPs are not well defined outside the feasible region or the equivalent unconstrained reformulations of other MCPs contain local minimizers outside the feasible region. As both these problems could make the corresponding infeasible methods fail, more recent attention is on feasible methods.
