Some continuous functions related to corner polyhedra
Ralph E. Gomory;Ellis L. Johnson;
Abstract
Previous work on Gomory's corner polyhedra is extended to generate valid inequalities for any mixed integer program. The theory of a corresponding asymptotic problem is developed. It is shown how faces previously generated and those given here can be used to give valid inequalities for any integer program.
