Maximal domains of quasi-convexity and pseudo-convexity for quadratic functions
Jacques A. Ferland;
Abstract
We study quasi-convex and pseudo-convex quadratic functions on solid convex sets. This generalizes Martos' results in [12] and [13] by using Koecher's results in [8].
Keywords
Mathematical Method  Quadratic Function  Maximal Domain  Solid Convex
