Linear programming with spheres and hemispheres of objective vectors
B. Curtis Eaves;Alan J. Hoffman;Hui Hu;
Abstract
Given a linear program with a boundedp-dimensional feasible region let the objective vector range over ans-sphere, that is, ans-dimensional sphere centered at the origin wheres does not exceedp−1. If the feasible region and the sphere are in general position with respect to each other, then the corresponding set of all optimal solutions is a topologicals-sphere. Similar results are developed for unbounded feasible regions and hemispheres of objective vectors.
