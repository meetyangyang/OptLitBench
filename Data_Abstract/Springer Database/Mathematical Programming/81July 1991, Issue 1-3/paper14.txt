Necessary and sufficient conditions for the existence of complementary solutions and characterizations of the matrix classesQ andQ0
Faiz A. Al-Khayyal;
Abstract
We simplify a result by Mangasarian on the existence of solutions to the linear complementarity problem. The simplified condition gives a new geometric interpretation of the result. When used to characterize the matrix classesQ andQ0, our condition suggests a finitely checkable sufficient condition forP andP0.
Keywords
Linear complementarity problem  matrix classes  existence of solutions
