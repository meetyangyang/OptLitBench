A general descent framework for the monotone variational inequality problem
Jia Hao Wu;Michael Florian;Patrice Marcotte;
Abstract
We present a framework for descent algorithms that solve the monotone variational inequality problem VIP v  which consists in finding a solutionv*∈Ωv satisfyings(v*)T(v−v*)⩾0, for allv∈Ωv. This unified framework includes, as special cases, some well known iterative methods and equivalent optimization formulations. A descent method is developed for an equivalent general optimization formulation and a proof of its convergence is given. Based on this unified logarithmic framework, we show that a variant of the descent method where each subproblem is only solved approximately is globally convergent under certain conditions.
Keywords
Variational inequalities  descent methods  optimization
