Stochastic programming with simple integer recourse
François V. Louveaux;Maarten H. van der Vlerk;
Abstract
Stochastic integer programs are notoriously difficult. Very few properties are known and solution algorithms are very scarce. In this paper, we introduce the class of stochastic programs with simple integer recourse, a natural extension of the simple recourse case extensively studied in stochastic continuous programs.
Keywords
Simple integer recourse  expected value function  convex hull  algorithms
