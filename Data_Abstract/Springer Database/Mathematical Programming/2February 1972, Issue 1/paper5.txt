The complementarity problem
S. Karamardian;
Abstract
For a given mapF from then-dimensional Euclidean spaceEn into itself, we consider the problem of finding a nonnegative vectorx inEn whose imageF(x) is also nonnegative and such that the two vectors are orthogonal. This problem is refered to in the literature as thecomplemcntarity problcm. The importance of the complementarity problem lies in the fact that it is the unifying mathematical form for a wide range of problems arising in different fields such as mathematical programming, game theory, economics, mechanics, etc ⋯
