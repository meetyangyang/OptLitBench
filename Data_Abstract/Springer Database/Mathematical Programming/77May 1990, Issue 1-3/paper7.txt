Solving the linear matroid parity problem as a sequence of matroid intersection problems
James B. Orlin;John H. Vande Vate;
Abstract
In this paper, we present an O(r4n) algorithm for the linear matroid parity problem. Our solution technique is to introduce a modest generalization, the non-simple parity problem, and identify an important subclass of non-simple parity problems called ‘easy’ parity problems which can be solved as matroid intersection problems. We then show how to solve any linear matroid parity problem parametrically as a sequence of ‘easy’ parity problems.
Keywords
