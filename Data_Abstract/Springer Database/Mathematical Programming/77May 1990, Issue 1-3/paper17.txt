A new theorem of the alternative
Achiya Dax;
Abstract
Although the theorems of the alternative have been well known for a long time, their relationship to linear least squares problems and steepest descent directions was revealed only recently. This relationship was used by the author to derive a new theorem of the alternative. The present research extends this theorem to theℓ p  norm,p > 1.
Keywords
Alternative theorems  ℓp-norms  linear least-squares problem
