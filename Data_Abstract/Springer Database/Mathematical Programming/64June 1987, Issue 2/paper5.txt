Clarification of certain (α, β)-polarity results of Bachem and Grötschel
Soon-Kiong Sim;
Abstract
We give a description of the (α, β)-polar of a polyhedron. Based on the description, we correct and complete the table of the specific representation of the (α,β)-polar of a polyhedron, given by Bachem and Grötschel.
Keywords
Polyhedron  (α,β)-polar
