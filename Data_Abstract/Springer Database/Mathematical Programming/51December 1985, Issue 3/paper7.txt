Differentiability with respect to parameters of solutions to convex programming problems
Kazimierz Malanowski;
Abstract
We consider a family of convex programming problems that depend on a vector parameter, characterizing those values of parameters at which solutions and associated Lagrange multipliers are Gâteaux differentiable.
Keywords
Parametric Convex Programming  Differentiability of Solutions  Projection onto Convex Set  Clarke's Generalized Derivative
