Fractional and integral colourings
K. Kilakos;O. Marcotte;
Abstract
LetG=(V, E) be an undirected graph andc any vector in ℤ V(G) +. Denote byχ(G c) (resp.η(G c)) the chromatic number (resp. fractional chromatic number) ofG with respect toc. We study graphs for whichχ(G c)−[η(G c)]⩽1. We show that for the class of graphs satisfyingχ(G c)=[η(G c)] (a class generalizing perfect graphs), an analogue of the Duplication Lemma does not hold. We also describe a 2-vertex cut decomposition procedure related to the integer decomposition property. We use this procedure to show thatχ(G c)=[η(G c)] for series-parallel graphs andχ(G c)⩽[η(G c)]+1 for graphs that do not have the 4-wheel as a minor.
Keywords
Graph colouring  Chromatic number  Stable set  Integer rounding
