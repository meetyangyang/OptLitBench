A duality theorem for semi-infinite convex programs and their finite subprograms
Dennis F. Karney;
Abstract
In this paper, we first establish a general recession condition under which a semi-infinite convex program and its formal lagrangian dual have the same value. We go on to show that, under this condition, the following hold. First, every finite subprogram, with ‘enough’ of the given constraints, has the same value as its Lagrangian dual. Second, the weak value of the primal program is equal to the optimal value of the primal.
Keywords
Semi-infinite Programs  Finite Subprograms  Lagrangian Duality  Recession Theory  Weak Value of the Primal
