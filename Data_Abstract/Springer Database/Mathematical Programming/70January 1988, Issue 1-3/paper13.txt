Convergence of relaxation algorithms by averaging
Gerard G. L. Meyer;
Abstract
Adaptive relaxation algorithms use anti-jamming schemes that require either a large amount of computation or a large amount of memory. In this paper we present a non-adaptive approach that possesses substantial cómputational and memory advantages over the adaptive schemes. The approach uses averaging and may be applied whenever the relaxation algorithm's point-to-set maps satisfy appropriate assumptions.
