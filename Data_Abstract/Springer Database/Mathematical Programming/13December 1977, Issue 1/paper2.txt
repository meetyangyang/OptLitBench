Optimization of lipschitz continuous functions
A. A. Goldstein;
Abstract
This paper contains basic results that are useful for building algorithms for the optimization of Lipschitz continuous functionsf on compact subsets of E n . In this settingf is differentiable a.e. The theory involves a set-valued mappingx→δ∈f(x) whose range is the convex hull of existing values of ∇f and limits of ∇f on a closed∈-ball,B(x, ∈).
