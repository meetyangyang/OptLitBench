A note on the condition numbers of convex sets
Wen Song;Xiaomei Xu;Jen-Chih Yao;
Abstract
In this note, we give a counterexample to show that the characterization formula for the condition numbers of a convex set \(C\) of \(\mathbb R^n\) given in Coulibaly and Crouzeix (Math Program 116:79–113, 2009) is incorrect.
