On the equivalence of nonlinear complementarity problems and least-element problems
Siegfried Schaible;Jen-Chih Yao;
Abstract
Strictly pseudomonotoneZ-maps operating on Banach lattices are considered. Equivalence of complementarity problems and least-element problems is established under certain regularity and growth conditions. This extends a recent result by Riddell (1981) for strictly monotoneZ-maps to the pseudomonotone case. Some other problems equivalent to the above are discussed as well.
