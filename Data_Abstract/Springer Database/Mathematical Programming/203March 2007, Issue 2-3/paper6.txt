Dual extrapolation and its applications to solving variational inequalities and related problems
Yurii Nesterov;
Abstract
In this paper we suggest new dual methods for solving variational inequalities with monotone operators. We show that with an appropriate step-size strategy, our method is optimal both for Lipschitz continuous operators (
\(O({1 \over \epsilon})\) iterations), and for the operators with bounded variations (
\(O({1 \over \epsilon^2})\) iterations). Our technique can be applied for solving non-smooth convex minimization problems with known structure. In this case the worst-case complexity bound is
\(O({1 \over \epsilon})\) iterations.
