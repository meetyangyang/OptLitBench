Polyhedra for lot-sizing with Wagner—Whitin costs
Yves Pochet;Laurence A. Wolsey;
Abstract
We examine the single-item lot-sizing problem with Wagner—Whitin costs over ann period horizon, i.e.pt+ht⩾pt+1 fort = 1, ⋯,n−1, wherept, ht are the unit production and storage costs in periodt respectively, so it always pays to produce as late as possible.
Keywords
Lot-sizing models  Polyhedral combinatorics
