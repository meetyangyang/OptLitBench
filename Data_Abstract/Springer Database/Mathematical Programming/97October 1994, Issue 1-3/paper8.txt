On thek-cut subgraph polytope
Kalyan T. Talluri;Donald K. Wagner;
Abstract
We consider the problem of finding a minimum-cost set ofk pairwise-disjoint (s, t)-cuts in a graph. For non-negative costs, this problem was solved independently by Nishihara and Inoue, and Wagner. Both solutions involve formulating the problem as a linear-programming problem. In this paper, we give new polyhedral interpretations of the problem. Relationships to the path formulation of the maximum-flow problem are also established.
Keywords
Minimum cut problem  Network flows  Reliability
