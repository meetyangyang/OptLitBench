A complementarity partition theorem for multifold conic systems
Javier Peña;Vera Roshchina;
Abstract
Consider a homogeneous multifold convex conic system
$$Ax = 0, \quad x\in K_1\times \cdots \times K_r$$and its alternative system
$$A^t y \in K_1^*\times \cdots \times K_r^*$$, where K 1,..., K  r  are regular closed convex cones. We show that there is a canonical partition of the index set {1,...,r} determined by certain complementarity sets associated to the most interior solutions to the two systems. Our results are inspired by and extend the Goldman–Tucker Theorem for linear programming.
