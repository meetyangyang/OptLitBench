Mixed semicontinuous perturbations of nonconvex sweeping processes
Tahar Haddad;Lionel Thibault;
Abstract
In this paper we prove a theorem on the existence of a global solution of a differential inclusion governed by a class of nonconvex sweeping processes with unbounded pertubations. The perturbations are not required to be convex valued.
