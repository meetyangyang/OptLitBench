Primal separation for 0/1 polytopes
Friedrich Eisenbrand;Giovanni Rinaldi;Paolo Ventura;
Abstract
 The 0/1 primal separation problem is: Given an extreme point x¯ of a 0/1 polytope P and some point x*, find an inequality which is tight at x¯, violated by x* and valid for P or assert that no such inequality exists. It is known that this separation variant can be reduced to the standard separation problem for P.
