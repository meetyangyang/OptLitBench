A distance constrainedp-facility location problem on the real line
Arie Tamir;
Abstract
LetV = {v1,⋯, v n } be a set ofn points on the real line (existing facilities). The problem considered is to locatep new point facilities,F1,⋯, F p , inV while satisfying distance constraints between pairs of existing and new facilities and between pairs of new facilities. Fori = 1, ⋯, p, j = 1, ⋯, n, the cost of locatingF i  at pointv j  isc ij . The objective is to minimize the total cost of setting up the new facilities. We present anO(p3n2 logn) algorithm to solve the model.
