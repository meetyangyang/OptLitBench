The complex of maximal lattice free simplices
Imre Bárány;Roger Howe;Herbert E. Scarf;
Abstract
The simplicial complexK(A) is defined to be the collection of simplices, and their proper subsimplices, representing maximal lattice free bodies of the form (x: Ax⩽b), withA a fixed generic (n + 1) ×n matrix. The topological space associated withK(A) is shown to be homeomorphic to ℝ n , and the space obtained by identifying lattice translates of these simplices is homeorphic to then-torus.
Keywords
