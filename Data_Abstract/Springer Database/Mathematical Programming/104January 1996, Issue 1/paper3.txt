On the extended linear complementarity problem
M. Seetharama Gowda;
Abstract
For the extended linear complementarity problem (Mangasarian and Pang, 1995), we introduce and characterize column-sufficiency, row-sufficiency andP-properties. These properties are then specialized to the vertical, horizontal and mixed linear complementarity problems.
Keywords
Column-sufficiency  Row-sufficiency  P-property  Horizontal (vertical, mixed) linear complementarity problem
