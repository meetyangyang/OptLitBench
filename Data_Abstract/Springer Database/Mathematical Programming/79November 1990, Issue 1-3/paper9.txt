OnQ-matrices
M. Seetharama Gowda;
Abstract
Recently, Jeter and Pye gave an example to show that Pang's conjecture, thatL1 ⋂Q\( \subseteq  R_0 \), is false. We show in this article that the above conjecture is true for symmetric matrices. Specifically, we show that a symmetric copositive matrix is inQ if and only if it is strictly copositive.
