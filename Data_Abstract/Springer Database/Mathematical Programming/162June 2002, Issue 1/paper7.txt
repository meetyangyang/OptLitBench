A connection between cutting plane theory and the geometry of numbers
Gérard Cornuéjols;Yanjun Li;
Abstract
In this paper, we relate several questions about cutting planes to a fundamental problem in the geometry of numbers, namely, the closest vector problem. Using this connection we show that the dominance, membership and validity problems are NP-complete for Chvátal and split cuts.
Keywords
Key words: closest vector problem – dominance problem – membership problem – validity problem – Chvátal cut – split cut – disjunctive cut – Gomory cut  Mathematics Subject Classification (2000): 90C10
