On the discovery of the “good Broyden” method
C.G. Broyden;
Abstract
This short note traces the events that led to the unsymmetric rank one formula known as the “good Broyden” update [5, 6], which is widely used within derivative-free mathematical software for solving a system of nonlinear equations.
