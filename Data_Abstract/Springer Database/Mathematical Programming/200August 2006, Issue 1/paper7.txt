Solving discrete zero point problems
G. van der Laan;D.A.J.J. Talman;Z. Yang;
Abstract
In this paper we present two theorems on the existence of a discrete zero point of a function from the n-dimensional integer lattice ℤ n  to the n-dimensional Euclidean space ℝ n . The theorems differ in their boundary conditions. For both theorems we give a proof using a combinatorial lemma and present a constructive proof based on a simplicial algorithm that finds a discrete zero point within a finite number of steps.
