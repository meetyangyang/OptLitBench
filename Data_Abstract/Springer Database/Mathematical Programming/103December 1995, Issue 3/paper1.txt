Balanced 0, ±1-matrices, bicoloring and total dual integrality
Michele Conforti;Gérard Cornuéjols;
Abstract
A 0, ±1-matrixA is balanced if, in every submatrix with two nonzero entries per row and column, the sum of the entries is a multiple of four. This definition was introduced by Truemper (1978) and generalizes the notion of a balanced 0, 1-matrix introduced by Berge (1970). In this paper, we extend a bicoloring theorem of Berge (1970) and total dual integrality results of Fulkerson, Hoffman and Oppenheim (1974) to balanced 0, ±1-matrices.
Keywords
Combinatorial optimization  Integrality of polyhedra  Generalized set packing  Covering
