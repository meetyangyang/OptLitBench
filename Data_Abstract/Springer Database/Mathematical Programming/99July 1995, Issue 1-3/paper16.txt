An algebraic geometry algorithm for scheduling in presence of setups and correlated demands
Sridhar R. Tayur;Rekha R. Thomas;N. R. Natraj;
Abstract
We study here a problem of schedulingn job types onm parallel machines, when setups are required and the demands for the products are correlated random variables. We model this problem as a chance constrained integer program.
Keywords
Scheduling  Chance constrained programming  Integer programming  Decomposition  Gröbner basis  Polynomial ideals
