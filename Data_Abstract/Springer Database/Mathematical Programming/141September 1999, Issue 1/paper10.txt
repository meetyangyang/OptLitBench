A note on quadratic forms
Xin Chen;Ya-xiang Yuan;
Abstract
We extend an interesting theorem of Yuan [12] for two quadratic forms to three matrices. Let C1, C2, C3 be three symmetric matrices in ℜ n×n , if max{x T C1x,x T C2x,x T C3x}≥0 for all x∈ℜ n , it is proved that there exist t i ≥0 (i=1,2,3) such that ∑ i=1 3t i =1 and ∑ i=1 3t i C i  has at most one negative eigenvalue.
Keywords
Key words: quadratic forms – convex combination – matrix perturbation
