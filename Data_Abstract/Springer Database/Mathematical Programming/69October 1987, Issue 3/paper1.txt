Counting almost minimum cutsets with reliability applications
Aparna Ramanathan;Charles J. Colbourn;
Abstract
The numbern  1  ofi-edge network cutsets in a graph is an important parameter in reliability analysis. Using a theorem of Lomonosov and Polesskii, Ball and Provan have shown that the numbern  i  of minimum cardinality network cutsets can be determined in polynomial time. This paper gives a polynomial time algorithm for determiningn  r+k  for any fixedk, as a special case of counting cuts of specified maximum weight in an edge-weighted graph. The resulting improvement in existing reliability bounds is shown to be substantial.
Keywords
