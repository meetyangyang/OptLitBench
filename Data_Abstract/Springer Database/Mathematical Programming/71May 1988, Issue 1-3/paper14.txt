Approximation and decomposition properties of some classes of locally D.C. functions
J. -P. Penot;M. L. Bougeard;
Abstract
We study the connexion between local and global decompositions of some important subclasses of locally d.c. functions (functions which locally split as a difference of two convex functions). Then we tackle the problem of regularizing such functions by the Moreau-Yosida process and prove in particular that the class of lower-C2 functions fits well this approximation procedure.
Keywords
Approximation  d.c. functions  lipschitzian functions  lower-C2 functions  inf-convolution
