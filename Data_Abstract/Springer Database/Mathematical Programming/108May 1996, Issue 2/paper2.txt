On polynomiality of the method of analytic centers for fractional problems
A. Nemirovskii;
Abstract
We establish polynomial time convergence of the method of analytic centers for the fractional programming problemt→min |x∈G, tB(x)−A(x)∈K, whereG ⊂ ℝ n  is a closed and bounded convex domain,K ⊂ ℝ m  is a closed convex cone andA(x):G → ℝ n ,B(x):G→K are regular enough (say, affine) mappings.
Keywords
Interior point methods  Self-concordance  Polynomial time convergence  Analytic centers  Linear-fractional optimization  Generalized eigenvalue problem
