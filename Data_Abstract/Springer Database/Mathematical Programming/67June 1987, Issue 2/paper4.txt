On the global convergence of path-following methods to determine all solutions to a system of nonlinear equations
Immo Diener;
Abstract
In this paper we prove a general theorem stating a sufficient condition for the inverse image of a point under a continuously differentiable map from ℝ n  to ℝ k  to be connected. This result is applied to the trajectories generated by the Newton flow. Several examples demonstrate the applicability of the results to nontrivial problems.
