Helly’s Principle and Its Application to an Infinite-Horizon Optimal Control Problem
D. Bogusz;
Abstract

In this paper, we introduce locally absolutely continuous functions of two variables and functions of two variables of locally finite variation defined on an unbounded interval. We generalize Helly’s principle of choice to functions of locally finite variation and apply this principle to an infinite-horizon optimal control problem of the Goursat–Darboux type.

Keywords
Infinite-horizon optimal control  Goursat–Darboux problem  Helly’s principle  Existence of optimal solutions
