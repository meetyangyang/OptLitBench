Rate of convergence for an estimator in a portfolio and consumption model
T. E. Duncan;B. Pasik-Duncan;
Abstract
The rate of convergence in a sample path sense is given for a strongly consistent, recursive estimator. This estimator is for the unknown average return rate of the risky asset that is a parameter in a bilinear stochastic differential equation for the wealth in a portfolio selection and consumption model.
Keywords
Rate of convergence  recursive parameter estimator  identification  adaptive control
