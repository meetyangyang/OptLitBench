Sufficiency conditions for multiple integral control problems
H. Rund;
Abstract
The construction of multiple integrals which are independent, in the sense that they depend solely on the values of their integrands on the boundary of the domain of integration is described. These integrals are applied to the derivation of a sufficiency condition for multiple integral optimal control problems.
Keywords
Distributed control problems  sufficiency theorems  optimization theorems  calculus of variations
