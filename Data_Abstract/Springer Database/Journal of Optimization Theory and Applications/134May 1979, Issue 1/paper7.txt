Imbedding methods for the integral equations for the optimal control of time-lag systems, I: Parameter dependence
H. H. Kagiwada;R. E. Kalaba;
Abstract
The problem of the optimal control of systems with time lags can be reduced to a Fredholm integral equation. In this paper, it is shown that the Fredholm resolvent satisfies an initial-value problem in which a parameter serves as the independent variable. This reduction may be of computational utility.
Keywords
Time-lag systems  imbedding systems  integral equations
