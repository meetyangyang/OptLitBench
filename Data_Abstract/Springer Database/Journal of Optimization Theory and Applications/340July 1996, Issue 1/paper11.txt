An algorithm for minimizing a class of locally Lipschitz functions
L. S. Zhang;X. L. Sun;
Abstract
In this paper, we present an algorithm for minimizing a class of locally Lipschitz functions. The method generalized the ε-smeared method to a class of functions whose Clarke generalized gradients are singleton at almost all differentiable poinst. We analyze the global convergence of the method and report some numerical results.
Keywords
Nondifferentiable optimization  locally Lipschitz functions  generalized gradients  global convergence methods
