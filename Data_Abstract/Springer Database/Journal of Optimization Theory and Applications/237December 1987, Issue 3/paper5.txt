Suboptimal shape control for quasi-static distributed-parameter systems
L. A. Sievers;M. J. Balas;
Abstract
We propose an on-line control approach which will adjust the steady-state shape of a large antenna arbitrarily close to any achievable desired profile. The method makes use of distributed-parameter system theory and allows refocusing using a limited number of control actuators and sensors.
Keywords
Distributed parameter systems  control theory  partial differential equations  aerospace structures
