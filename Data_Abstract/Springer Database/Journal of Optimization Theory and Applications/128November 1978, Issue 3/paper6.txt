Solution of the nonlinear functional equations representing the roll gap relationships in a cold mill
M. J. Grimble;
Abstract
In the development of a roll force model for cold rolling, techniques were developed for solving the system equations which are of general interest. This paper gives a brief introduction to the physical model but concentrates on the solution of the model equations and the simulation. An unusual feature of the model was that the calculated profiles had to satisfy a number of boundary conditions at different points throughout the roll arc. A new method was developed for calculating these profiles and for determining the gradient functions which satisfied the boundary constraints.
