Distributed computing viewed as a team problem with constraints
Y. C. Ho;B. Kurtaran;
Abstract
The optimal distribution of the workload in a system of interconnected computer units is considered. Formulated as a team decision problem with a singular cost criterion and with equality and inequality constraints, it is shown that the problem admits always a unique piecewise linear strategy which is globally optimal. Some interesting particular cases are studied.
Keywords
Distributive computing  load optimization  team decision problems  decentralized control  quadratic programming with equality and inequality constraints  singular cost criterion  redundancy  Hadamard product
