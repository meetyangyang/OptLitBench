Target set reachability criteria for dynamical systems described by inaccurate models
B. R. Barmish;
Abstract
S is taken to be a dynamical system (described by Banach space operators) whose outputy we wish to regulate. The structural complexity ofS (nonlinearities, distributed parameters, etc.) forces us to design a controller forS using an approximate modelM ofS. A convex error bound ϑ describes the accuracy of the approximation ofS byM.
Keywords
Reachability  controllability  minimax problems  Banach spaces  convexity  reconstructed support functions
