Gap Functions and Existence of Solutions for a System of Vector Equilibrium Problems
N. J. Huang;J. Li;J. C. Yao;
Abstract

In this paper, a gap function for a system of vector equilibrium problems is introduced and studied. Some necessary and sufficient conditions for the system of vector equilibrium problems are established. Characterizations of the solutions set for the system of vector equilibrium problems are also derived. Furthermore, some existence results of solutions for the system of vector equilibrium problems are proved.

Keywords
Systems of vector equilibrium problems  Gap functions  Convex cones  Point-to-set mappings
