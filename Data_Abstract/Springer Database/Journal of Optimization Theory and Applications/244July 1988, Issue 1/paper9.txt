On simplified Tikhonov regularization
J. Guacaneme;
Abstract
Approximations to the minimal norm, least-square solution of a linear equation with positive semidefinite operator are defined in such a way that fewer computations are needed than in Tikhonov's approach. We establish necessary and sufficient conditions for convergence, and we provide a choice for the regularization parameter α that brings the optimal rate of convergence.
Keywords
Regularization  ill-posed problems
