Controllability of Nonlinear Differential Equations in Banach Space with Nonlocal Conditions
M. Benchohra;S. K. Ntouyas;
Abstract
In this paper, we establish sufficient conditions for the controllability of semilinear evolution systems in Banach space with nonlocal conditions. We rely on a fixed-point theorem for compact maps due to Schaefer.
Keywords
Nonlocal conditions  mild solutions  evolution  controllability  fixed points
