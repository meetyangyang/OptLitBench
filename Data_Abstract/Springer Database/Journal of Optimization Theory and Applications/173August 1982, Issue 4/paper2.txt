Nonexistence and nonuniqueness of open-loop equilibria in linear-quadratic differential games
T. Eisele;
Abstract
In this paper, we shall give a complete description of the set of all Nash equilibria in open-loop strategies for nonzero-sum differential games with linear differential equations and quadratic cost terms. Several examples are given, where nonexistence and nonuniqueness occur.
Keywords
Nonzero-sum differential games  quadratic cost functionals  open-loop strategies  Nash equilibrium
