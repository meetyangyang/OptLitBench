Optimal production mix
R. F. Hartl;J. Krauth;
Abstract
This paper deals with a production plant in which two different products can be produced. The plant consists of three subsystemsS i . Before or after a phase of separate processing in subsystemsS1 andS2, the two products have to be processed in subsystemS3. Each of these subsystems has a limited capacity.
Keywords
Optimal control  state constraints  production problems
