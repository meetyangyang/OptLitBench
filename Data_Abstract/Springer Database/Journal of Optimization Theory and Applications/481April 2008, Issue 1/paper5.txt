Contrasting Two Transformation-based Methods for Obtaining Absolute Extrema
D. F. M. Torres;G. Leitmann;
Abstract

In this note, we contrast two transformation-based methods to deduce absolute extrema and the corresponding extremizers. Unlike variation-based methods, the transformation-based methods of Carlson and Leitmann and the recent one of Silva and Torres are direct in that they permit obtaining solutions by inspection.

Keywords
Optimal control  Direct methods  Calculus of variations  Absolute extrema  Invariance  Symmetry
