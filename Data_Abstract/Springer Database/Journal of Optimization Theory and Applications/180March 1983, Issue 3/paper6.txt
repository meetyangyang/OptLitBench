Liapunov reachability and optimization in control
R. J. Stoner;
Abstract
In this paper, we find sufficient conditions that do not rely upon any Liapunov stability results for the reachability of the target set by an admissible control from ana priori specified set of states. The conditions found are less restrictive than those obtained by Sticht, Vincent, and Schultz in Ref. 1.
Keywords
Liapunov function  reachability  optimization  quantitative control  qualitative control
