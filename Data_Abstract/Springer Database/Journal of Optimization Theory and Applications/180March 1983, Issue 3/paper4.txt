Probabilistic prospects of Stackelberg leader and follower
A. Alkan;T. Brown;M. R. Sertel;
Abstract
In this paper, we consider the family of allm×n bimatrix games, whose payoff entries are the players' orderings of the outcomes, and count the fraction of games whose Stackelberg solution is the leader'shth best outcome and the follower'skth best outcome (h,k≤mn). We conclude that the average leader and follower enjoy symmetric prospects, and that the advantage lies not in the leadership role, but in the relative size of the player's strategy space.
Keywords
Bimatrix Stackelberg games  leadership-followership prospects
