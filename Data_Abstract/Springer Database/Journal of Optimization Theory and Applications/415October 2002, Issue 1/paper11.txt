Maximum Principle for State-Constrained Control of Some Semilinear Parabolic Differential Equations
G. Wang;C. Liu;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with the Pontryagin maximum principle for optimal control problems of some semilinear equations, which may be governed by nonmonotone operators and in particular might be singular. Some state constraints are considered.
Keywords
Optimal control  non-well-posed equations  maximum principle  state constraints
