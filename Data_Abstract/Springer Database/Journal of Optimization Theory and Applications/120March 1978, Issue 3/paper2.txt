Generalization of the Dubovitzky-Milutyn optimality conditions
I. Lasiecka;
Abstract
Various types of conical approximation of sets are discussed. The notions of an external cone and an internal cone result in a strengthened separation theorem. Based on this theorem, some generalizations of the Dubovitzky-Milutyn optimality conditions are given.
Keywords
Internal cone  external cone  admissible cone  tangent cone  dual cone  separation theorem
