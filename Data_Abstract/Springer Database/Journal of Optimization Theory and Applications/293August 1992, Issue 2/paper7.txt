Convexlike and concavelike conditions in alternative, minimax, and minimization theorems
S. Paeck;
Abstract
Convexlike and concavelike conditions are of interest for extensions of the Von Neumann minimax theorem. Since the beginning of the 80's, these conditions also play a certain role in deriving generalized alternative theorems of the Gordan, Motzkin, and Farkas type and Lagrange multiplier results for constrained minimization problems.
Keywords
Convexlike and concavelike conditions  minimax theorems  alternative theorems  constrained minimization problems
