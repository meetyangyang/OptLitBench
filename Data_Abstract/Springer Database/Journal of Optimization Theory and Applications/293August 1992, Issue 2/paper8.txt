Infinite-horizon investment consumption model with a nonterminal bankruptcy
S. Sethi;M. Taksar;
Abstract
This paper solves a general continuous-time consumption and portfolio decision problem for a single agent for whom there exists, upon bankruptcy, a possibility of recovery from his bankruptcy. The main contribution of the paper is in the modeling of the recovery process. Moreover, it is shown that the model with recovery has a one-to-one correspondence with the model with terminal bankruptcy treated in the literature.
Keywords
