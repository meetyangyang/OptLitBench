On a characterization of Clarke's tangent cone
G. Giorgi;A. Guerraggio;
Abstract
For a subsetM of a normed vector spaceX, it is shown that, in the characterization given by Elster and Thierfelder of Clarke's tangent cone toM at a pointx0∈X, there is a requirement which becomes superfluous whenx0 belongs to the closure ofM.
Keywords
Local conical approximations of sets  Clarke's tangent cone
