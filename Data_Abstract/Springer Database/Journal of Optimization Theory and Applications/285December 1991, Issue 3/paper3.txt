Control of partially observed diffusions
R. J. Elliott;H. Yang;
Abstract
The optimal control of a partially observed diffusion is discussed when the control parameter is present in both the drift and diffusion coefficients. Using a differentiation result of Blagovescenskii and Freidlin, and adapting techniques of Bensoussan, we obtain a stochastic minimum principle.
Keywords
Optimal control  partially observed diffusions  minimum principle
