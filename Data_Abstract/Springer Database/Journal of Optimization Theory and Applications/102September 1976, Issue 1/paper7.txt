Minimax approach to structural optimization problems
N. V. Banichuk;
Abstract
The problem of determining the shape of an elastic body which is optimal for a whole class of loads is formulated. Its general solution scheme, based on a minimax approach, is indicated. The optimization problem of an elastic beam is studied for both the simply supported case and the cantilevered case, and some specific properties of the optimal shape are determined.
Keywords
Structural optimization  minimax problems  beam design  inequality constraints
