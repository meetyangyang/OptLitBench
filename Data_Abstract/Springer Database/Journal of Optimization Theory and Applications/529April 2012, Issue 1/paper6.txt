Controllability of Neutral Evolution Integrodifferential Systems with State Dependent Delay
B. Radhakrishnan;K. Balachandran;
Abstract
In this paper, we address the problem of controllability of neutral evolution integrodifferential systems with state dependent delay in a Banach space. The results are obtained by using the theory of resolvent operators.
Keywords
Controllability  Integrodifferential system  Evolution operator  Resolvent operators  State dependent delay
