The Local Convergence Analysis of Inexact Quasi-Gauss–Newton Method Under the Hölder Condition
Y. Zhang;H. Sun;D. T. Zhu;
Abstract
In this article, we introduce the local convergence of the inexact quasi-Gauss–Newton method when the first Fréchet derivative of operator involved is Hölder condition. Furthermore, we give some results on the existence and uniqueness of the solution for a nonlinear function. Based on this study, the R-order of convergence is proved.
