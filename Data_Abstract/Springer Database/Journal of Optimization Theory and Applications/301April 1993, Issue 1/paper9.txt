First-order conditions for isolated locally optimal solutions
A. Shapiro;F. Al-Khayyal;
Abstract
There are well-known first-order sufficient conditions for a pointx0 to be a strict locally optimal solution of a nonlinear programming problem. In this paper, we show that these conditions also guarantee thatx0 is an isolated stationary point of the considered program provided a constraint qualification holds. This result has an interesting application to finite convergence of algorithms along the lines suggested by Al-Khayyal and Kyparisis.
Keywords
First-order optimality conditions  tangent cones  monotone multifunctions  finite convergence of algorithms
