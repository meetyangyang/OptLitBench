Interior-point algorithm for quadratically constrained entropy minimization problems
J. Ji;F. A. Potra;
Abstract
In this paper, we develop an interior point algorithm for quadratically constrained entropy problems. The algorithm uses a variation of Newton's method to follow a central path trajectory in the interior of the feasible set. The primal-dual gap is made less than a given ɛ in at most\(O(|\log   \in |\sqrt {m + m} )\) steps, wheren is the dimension of the problem andm is the number of quadratic inequality constraints.
