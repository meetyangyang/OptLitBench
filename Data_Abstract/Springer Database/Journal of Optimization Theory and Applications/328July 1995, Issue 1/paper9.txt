On the controllability of convex processes with delay
L. I. Minchenko;V. N. Tesluk;
Abstract
We study the controllability of differential-difference inclusions\(\dot x(t) \in A(t,x(t), x(t - \Delta ))\), where the graph of the multifunctionA(t, ·) is a closed convex cone.
Keywords
Differential inclusions  controllability  multifunctions  generalized gradients
