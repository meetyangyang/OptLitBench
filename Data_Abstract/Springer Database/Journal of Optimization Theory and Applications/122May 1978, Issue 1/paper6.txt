An existence theorem for Lagrange problems in Banach spaces
O. Caligaris;P. Oliva;
Abstract
We give an existence theorem for a Lagrange problem in a reflexive separable Banach space. We prove the main theorem by means of the direct method of the calculus of variations introduced by Tonelli.
Keywords
Lagrange problems  sufficient conditions  direct methods  semicontinuity  level sets compactness
