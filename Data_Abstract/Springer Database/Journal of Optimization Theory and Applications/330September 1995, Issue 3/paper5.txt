Convex composite minimization withC1,1 functions
V. Jeyakumar;X. Q. Yang;
Abstract
In this paper, we present second-order optimality conditions for convex composite minimization problems in which the objective function is a composition of a finite-valued or a nonfinite-valued lower semicontinuous convex function and aC1,1 function. The results provide optimality conditions for composite problems under reduced differentiability requirements.
Keywords
Convex composite minimization problems  generalized second-order directional derivatives  generalized Taylor expansions  basic constraint qualifications  second-order optimality conditions
