Convergence of bounds in optimization
A. W. J. Stoddart;
Abstract
Consider a sequence of extensionsI  n  of a real-valued functionI. Sufficient conditions for the convergence of the infimum ofI  n  to the infimum ofI are obtained. These conditions are applied to an eigenvalue problem.
Keywords
