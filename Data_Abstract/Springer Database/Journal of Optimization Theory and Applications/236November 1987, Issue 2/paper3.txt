Identification of classes of differential games for which the open loop is a degenerate feedback Nash equilibrium
C. Fershtman;
Abstract
In general, it is clear that open-loop Nash equilibrium and feedback Nash equilibrium do not coincide. In this paper, we study the structure of differential games and develop a technique using which we can identify classes of games for which the open-loop Nash equilibrium is a degenerate feedback equilibrium. This technique clarifies the relationship between the assumptions made on the structure of the game and the resultant equilibrium.
Keywords
Differential games  open-loop Nash equilibria  feedback Nash equilibria
