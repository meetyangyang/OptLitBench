Global convergence of quasi-Newton-type algorithms for some nonsmooth optimization problems
E. Sachs;
Abstract
We consider the global convergence properties for a class of quasi-Newton algorithms solving nonsmooth optimization problems. Stationary points are defined, and several relations with optimal points are proven. We show descent properties of the algorithm using approximations for the derivatives. The global convergence results are given for determining the stepsizes inexactly.
Keywords
Nonsmooth optimization  quasi-Newton methods  Chebyshev approximation  global convergence
