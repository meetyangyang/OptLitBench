On a Lagrangian sufficient optimality condition
L. Pellegrini;
Abstract
By exploiting the concept of image of a constrained extremum problem, a sufficient optimality condition of Lagrangian type is established. The result is achieved by applying the idea of coercivity in the image space; this produces new results with respect to the use of coercivity in the given space. The condition embeds a known one.
Keywords
Optimality conditions  image space  Lagrange multipliers  coercivity  separation theorems  convexlike functions
