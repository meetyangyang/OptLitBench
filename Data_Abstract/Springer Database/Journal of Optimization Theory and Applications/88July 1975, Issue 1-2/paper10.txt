A class of linear evasion problems
T. Matsumoto;
Abstract
A special class of linear evasion problems is discussed. It turns out that the evasion problems are more difficult than pursuit problems in that evader has to prevent capture on an infinite interval of time. Some of the basic facts and difficulties inherent to evasion problems are pointed out.
Keywords
Differential games  pursuit-evasion problems  min-max functions  directional differentiability  sequential upper-semicontinuity
