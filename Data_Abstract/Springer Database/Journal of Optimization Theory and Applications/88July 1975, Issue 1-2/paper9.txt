A new approach to optimum state estimation in optimum system control
G. Jumarie;
Abstract
We consider optimum system control, in which the controller has imperfect information on the availability of the state of the plant to control. The system is controlled via an estimation of the state. We propose a general estimation criterion, which should permit determination of the optimal estimator for a given dynamics and a given performance cost. This approach includes the separation theorem as a special case.
Keywords
Estimation theory  filtering theory  adaptive control  suboptimal control
