Novel Delay-Dependent  Open image in new window  Stabilization Criterion for Lur’e Systems with Sector-Restricted Nonlinearities and External Disturbances via PD Feedback Approach
Chun Yin;Shouming Zhong;Xiaoyun Liu;Zijian Liu;
Abstract
In this paper, the effects of time delay on  Open image in new window  control of Lur’e systems are considered. Using a convex representation of the nonlinearity, a novel delay-dependent  Open image in new window  stability criterion is derived for the Lur’e systems via proportional-derivative (PD) feedback law. The Lyapunov–Krasovskii functional based on the delay discretization approach is used for the purpose of obtaining the  Open image in new window  stability condition by utilizing Projection Lemma. The criterion is utilized to not only guarantee stability of systems but also reduce the effect of external disturbance to an  Open image in new window  norm constraint. Finally, two numerical examples show the effectiveness of the proposed method.
