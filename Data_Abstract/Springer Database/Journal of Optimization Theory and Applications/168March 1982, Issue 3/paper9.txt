A note on functions whose local minima are global
R. Horst;
Abstract
In this note, we introduce a new class of generalized convex functions and show that a real functionf which is continuous on a compact convex subsetM of ℝ n  and whose set of global minimizers onM is arcwise-connected has the property that every local minimum is global if, and only if,f belongs to that class of functions.
Keywords
Global optimality  nonconvex programming  mathematical programming  nonlinear optimization  generalized convexity
