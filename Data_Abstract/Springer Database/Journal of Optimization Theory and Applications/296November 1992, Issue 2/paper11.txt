Hölder continuity of the minimum-time function forC1-manifold targets
P. Soravia;
Abstract
We consider the minimum-time problem to reach aC1-manifold target. Using implicit function techniques, under conditions of order 0 and 1 on the vector fields, we prove that the minimum-time function is locally Lipschitz and respectively 1/2-Hölder continuous.
Keywords
Control theory  minimum-time problems  controllability  Lie brackets
