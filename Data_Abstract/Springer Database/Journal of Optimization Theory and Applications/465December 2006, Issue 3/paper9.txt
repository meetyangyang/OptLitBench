Exact Local Controllability of a One-Control Reaction-Diffusion System
G. Wang;L. Zhang;
Abstract
This work concerns the controllability of some parabolic systems with the control acting in one equation. The exact local controllability of a reaction-diffusion system with one control and the null controllability of a class of linear parabolic systems with one control are obtained. As an application, the existence of a time-optimal control for a reaction-diffusion system with one control is proved.
Keywords
Null controllability  exact local controllability  global Carleman inequality  time-optimal control
