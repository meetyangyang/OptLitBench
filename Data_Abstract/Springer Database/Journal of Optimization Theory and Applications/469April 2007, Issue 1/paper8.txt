First and Second-Order Lagrange Claims for Set-Valued Maps
M. Durea;
Abstract

In this note, we derive first and second-order necessary (resp. sufficient) conditions for local minimum (resp. strict local minimum) points of optimization problems governed by set-valued maps. This allows us to present and to solve a generalized Lagrange claim in a multivalued setting.

Keywords
Bouligand tangent cone  Set-valued maps  Derivatives  Optimality conditions
