Existence Theorems for Systems of Generalized Vector Quasiequilibrium Problems and Optimization Problems
L. J.  Lin;Y. H. Liu;
Abstract
In this paper, we use existence theorems for the equilibria of generalized abstract economies proved recently to establish existence theorems for systems of generalized vector quasiequilibrium problems. Then, these existence theorems for equilibrium problems are used to derive existence theorems for systems of generalized vector quasivariational-like inequality problems, and vector quasioptimization problems.
Keywords
Upper (lower) semicontinuity  transfer open values  system of generalized vector quasiequilibrium problems
