Intersections of extremals in linear-quadratic control problems
J. Kogan;
Abstract
In this paper, we study the structure of intersections of extremals in linear control systems with quadratic cost. The structure of the intersections is determined. In particular, it is shown that, in contrast with the classical calculus of variations, these points are not necessarily isolated. The results obtained extend a known criterion from the calculus of variations involving the Jacobi necessary condition.
Keywords
Calculus of variations  conjugate points  Jacobi condition  Hilbert invariant integral  optimal control problems
