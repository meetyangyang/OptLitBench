Feedback law design and the Liapunov approach
J. A. Walker;
Abstract
We study the problem of designing targeting-type feedback control laws for systems with state and control constraints. A systematic approach to this problem is described in terms of a number of Liapunov-like criteria. This approach is presented in tutorial form and is illustrated by means of two examples having some background in the literature.
Keywords
Feedback design  Liapunov criteria  targeting problems
