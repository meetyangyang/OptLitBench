A game locating a needle in a square haystack
V. J. D. Baston;F. A. Bostock;
Abstract
The following hider-seeker zero-sum game is considered. The hider hides a needle of length\(a, 0< a \leqslant \sqrt 2 \), in the closed unit square, and the seeker tries to locate it by shooting in a straight line across the square. The payoff to the seeker is 1 if he hits the needle and 0 otherwise.
