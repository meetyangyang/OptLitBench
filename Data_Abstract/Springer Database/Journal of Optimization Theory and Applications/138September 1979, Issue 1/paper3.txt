A commentary on the method of successive starts
S. M. Roberts;
Abstract
A number of comments are made on the method of successive starts. These include a derivation of the method, a discussion of it in its most general setting, remarks on its use, suggestions to improve its accuracy, and some open questions about the method. Several numerical examples are discussed illustrating both success and failure with the method.
Keywords
Invariant imbedding  method of successive starts  Scott's method  two-point boundary-value problems
