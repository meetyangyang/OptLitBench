Optimum design of four-bar function generators with minimum variance criterion
A. C. Rao;
Abstract
The closure equation of a four-bar linkage generally gives a nonzero result, which may be taken as an index of the error in the output for a given input. The actual output can be brought close to the desired value by specifying the mean error permissible in the entire range of operation and by minimizing the variance (standard deviation). The present method differs from the closed-form synthesis (i.e., precision-point synthesis), in the sense that it does not limit the number of precision points. The results show that the present method compares well with other methods.
