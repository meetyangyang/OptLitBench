Suboptimal control of nonlinear time-delay systems
G. Gopalakrishnan Nair;
Abstract
An interative method for the suboptimal control of nonlinear time-delay systems is presented. Polynomial forms are assumed for the state in cases with delay in the state only and for the control in other cases. The coefficients in the approximating polynomial are chosen by a direct search method.
Keywords
Suboptimal control  nonlinear time-delay systems  polynomial approximations  modified LJ search method
