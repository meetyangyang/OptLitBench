Weak lower semicontinuity of integral functionals
C. Olech;
Abstract
A lower semicontinuity theorem for integral functionals is proved underL1-strong convergence of the trajectories andL1-weak convergence of the control functions. An alternative statement is also proved under pointwise convergence of the trajectories.
Keywords
