The duality of convex functions and Cesari's property (Q)
G. S. Goodman;
Abstract
We examine the role of property (Q) in optimal control and the calculus of variations and, using lattice theoretical considerations, obtain a characterization of property (Q) in terms of Hamiltonian and conjugate functions. Connections with previous work of Rockafellar, Moreau, Brøndsted, De Giorgi, and others are established.
Keywords
Property (Q)  conjugate functions  duality  lattice theory  Fenchel's transforms  weak seminormality
