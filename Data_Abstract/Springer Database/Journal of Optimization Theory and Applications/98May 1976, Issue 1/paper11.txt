Remarks on lower semicontinuity and lower closure
M. B. Suryanarayana;
Abstract
In the context of a recent geometric condition of Cesari, used in the reduction of seminormality requirements in lower closure theorems, this paper shows that the existence of a strongly convergent selection from the sequence of orientor fields, under Kuratowski property (K), is adequate to guarantee lower closure theorems. This generalization is justified through examples. Several related remarks are made.
Keywords
Lower closure theorems  upper semicontinuity  control theory  existence theorems
