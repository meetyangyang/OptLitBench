Sufficiency in Nonsmooth Multiobjective Programming Involving Generalized (Fρ)-convexity
S. Nobakhtian;
Abstract
In this paper, we consider a generalization of convexity for nonsmooth multiobjective programming problems. We obtain sufficient optimality conditions under generalized (Fρ)-convexity.
Keywords
Multiobjective programming  efficient solutions  generalized Fρ)-convexity  nonsmooth analysis
