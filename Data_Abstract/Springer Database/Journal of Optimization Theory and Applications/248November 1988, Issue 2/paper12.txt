Remarks on convex cones
A. Sterna-Karwat;
Abstract
We point out in this note that the class of cones in a locally convex topological vector space satisfying property (Π) or piecewise relatively weakly compact cones is exactly the class of cones admitting weakly compact bases or the class of cones whose closures admit weakly compact bases.
Keywords
Convex cones  property (Π)  cones with bases  Banach spaces  locally convex spaces  vector optimization
