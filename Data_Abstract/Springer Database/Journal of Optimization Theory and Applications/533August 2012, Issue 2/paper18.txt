On the Rate of Convergence of Gradient Flow for Some Evolution Systems
Hadi Khatibzadeh;
Abstract
The rate of convergence of first- and second-order evolution equations governed by the subdifferential of a proper, convex, and lower semicontinuous function has been studied.
Keywords
Rate of convergence  Convex function  Subdifferential  Convex minimization problem  Evolution equation  Gradient flow
