Studies on Common Solutions of a Variational Inequality and a Fixed-Point Problem
Z. Y. Huang;M. A. Noor;
Abstract
In this paper, we construct a new projection method and prove the strong convergence for finding a common element of a set of fixed points of strict pseudo-contractions and a set of solutions of a variational inequality with inverse strongly monotone mappings in the setting of real Hilbert spaces. Our results improve and extend the recent results by Huang, Noor, and Al-Said (J. Optim. Theory Appl. 147(1):194–204, 2010), Takahashi and Toyoda (J. Optim. Theory Appl. 118(2):417–428, 2003).
Keywords
α-inverse strongly monotone mappings  β-strict pseudo-contraction  Common elements  Variational inequalities  Hilbert spaces
