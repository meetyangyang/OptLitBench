Return sets of a linear control process
R. Conti;
Abstract
Given a realn ×n matrixA and a nonempty set Γ of the Euclidean space ℝ n , the linear control process$$\dot x = Ax - c,c(t) \in \Gamma ,$$ defines several kinds of transferable (or reachable) sets. Among these, we study in detail return sets whose points are transferable into themselves after a certain period, and we consider the problem of minimizing such period.
Keywords
Linear control processes  minimum period
