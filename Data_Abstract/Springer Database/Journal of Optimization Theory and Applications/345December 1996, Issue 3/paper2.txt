Use of Pτ-nets for the approximation of the Edgeworth-Pareto set in multicriteria optimization
R. B. Statnikov;J. Matusov;
Abstract
Engineering optimization problems, design problems among them, are multicriteria in their essence. When designing machines, mechanisms, and structures, one has to deal with numerous contradictory criteria.
