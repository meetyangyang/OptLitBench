Optimal partitions
F. K. Hwang;
Abstract
Consider a set of numbersZ={z1≥z2≥...≥zn} and a functionf defined on subsets ofZ. LetP be a partition ofZ into disjoint subsetsSi, say,g of them. The cost ofP is defined as$$C(P) = \sum\limits_{i = 1}^g {f(S_i )} .$$
