On the existence of optimal controls for quasilinear parabolic partial differential equations
E. S. Noussair;S. Nababan;K. L. Teo;
Abstract
A class of systems governed by quasilinear parabolic partial differential equations with first boundary conditions is considered. Existence of solutions for this class of systems and theira priori estimates are established. Further, a theorem on the existence of optimal controls for the corresponding control problem is obtained. Its proof is based on Filippov's implicit functions lemma. The control restraint setU is taken as a measurable multifunction.
Keywords
Quasilinear parabolic partial differential equations  first boundary conditions  existence of optimal controls  Filippov's implicit functions lemma
