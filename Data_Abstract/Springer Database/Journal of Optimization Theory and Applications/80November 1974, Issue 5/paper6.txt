A lower closure theorem for abstract control problems withLp-bounded controls
L. D. Berkovitz;
Abstract
A lower closure theorem for an abstract control problem is proved. The functional isJ(ϕ,u)=∝ G f0(t, (Mϕ)(t),u(t))dt and the state equations areNϕ(t)=f(t, (Mϕ)(t),u(t)). It is shown that, if {(ϕ k ,uk)} is a sequence of admissible controlsuk and corre-sponding trajectories ϕ k  such that lim infJ(ϕ k ,uk)<+∞ and such that ϕ k  → ϕ weakly,Mϕ k  →Mϕ strongly,Nϕ k  →Nϕ weakly, and {uk} is bounded in someLp norm, then there is a controlu such that (ϕ,u) is admissible and lim infJ(ϕ k ,uk)≧J(ϕ,u).
Keywords
Lower closure theorems  generalized control theory  control theory  calculus of variations
