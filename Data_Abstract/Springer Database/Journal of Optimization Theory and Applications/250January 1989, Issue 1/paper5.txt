On a duel with time lag and equal accuracy functions
T. Kurisu;
Abstract
This paper deals with a duel with time lag that has the following structure: Each of two players I and II has a gun with one bullet and he can fire his bullet at any time in [0, 1], aiming at this opponent. The gun of player I is silent and the gun of player II is noisy with time lagt (i.e., if player II fires at timex, then player I knows it at timex+t). They both have equal accuracy functions. Furthermore, if player I hits player II without being hit himself before, the payoff is +1; if player I is hit by player II without hitting player II before, the payoff is −1; if they hit each other at the same time or both survive, the payoff is 0.
Keywords
