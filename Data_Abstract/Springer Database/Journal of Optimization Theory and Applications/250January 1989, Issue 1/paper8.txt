Minimal time function and viscosity solutions
V. Staicu;
Abstract
Two theorems in Ref. 1 are generalized. It is proved that, ifV(A,Γ) is the set of points that can be steered to the origin along a solution of the control systemx′=Ax−c, ifc(t)∈Γ, Γ is a compact subset ofR n , 0∈ intrelco Γ, and if a rank condition holds, then the minimal time functionT(·) is a viscosity solution of the Bellman equation$$\max \{ \left\langle {DT(x),\gamma  - Ax} \right\rangle :\gamma \varepsilon co\Gamma \}  - 1 = 0,x\varepsilon V(A,\Gamma )\backslash \{ 0\} ,$$ and of the Hàjek equation$$1 - \max \{ \left\langle {DT(x),\exp [ - AT(x)]} \right\rangle :\gamma \varepsilon co\Gamma \}  = 0,x\varepsilon V(A,\Gamma ).$$
