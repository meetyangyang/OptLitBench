Proper solutions of vector optimization problems
P. Q. Khanh;
Abstract
We define proper solutions in the Kuhn-Tucker sense for multiobjective mathematical programming problems with parameters in infinite-dimensional spaces and compare them with other definitions via suitable representatives: the Benson, Geoffrion, and Hurwicz properness. Necessary and/or sufficient conditions for proper solutions are proved. Problems with and without constraint qualifications are considered under relaxed convexity and differentiability assumptions.
Keywords
Kuhn-Tucker proper minimizers  Geoffrion, Benson, and Hurwicz proper minimizers  Kuhn-Tucker constraint qualifications  differentiable C-quasiconvexity  necessary and/or sufficient conditions for proper minimizers
