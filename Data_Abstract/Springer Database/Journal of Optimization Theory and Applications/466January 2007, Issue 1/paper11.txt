Global Uniqueness and Stability for an Inverse Plate Problem
Y. H. Wang;
Abstract
This paper deals with the global uniqueness and stability of an inverse problem for the plate equation. By means of a suitable transformation, the problem is reduced to the observability estimate for the plate equations with memory. The main tool that we employ is a global Carleman-type estimate.
Keywords
Observability inequalities  plate equations  Carleman estimates  exact controllability  inverse problems
