Convergence of the steepest descent method for minimizing quasiconvex functions
K. C. Kiwiel;K. Murty;
Abstract
To minimize a continuously differentiable quasiconvex functionf: ℝ n →ℝ, Armijo's steepest descent method generates a sequencexk+1 =x k −t k ∇f(x k ), wheret k >0. We establish strong convergence properties of this classic method: either\(x^k  \to \bar x,\), s.t.\(\nabla f(\bar x) = 0\); or arg minf = ∅, ∥x k ∥ ↓ ∞ andf(x k )↓ inff. We also discuss extensions to other line searches.
Keywords
Steepest descent methods  convex programming  Armijo's line search
