Outer ellipsoidal approximations of the reachable set at infinity for linear systems
J. P. Quinn;D. Summers;
Abstract
Outer ellipsoidal approximations to the reachable set at infinity for a linear control system with bounded scalar controls are obtained using a new method based on quadratic Lyapunov functions. These outer approximations are compared with those given by an algorithm due to Sabin and Summers, and also with certain tangential outer approximations, obtained using a fixed-point iteration scheme.
Keywords
Reachable sets  linear control systems  Lyapunov functions  maximum principle
