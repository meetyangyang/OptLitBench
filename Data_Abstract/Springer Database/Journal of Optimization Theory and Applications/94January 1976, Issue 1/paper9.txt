A sufficiency condition for coalitive pareto-optimal solutions
W. E. Schmitendorf;G. Moriarty;
Abstract
In ak-player, nonzero-sum differential game, there exists the possibility that a group of players will form a coalition and work together. If allk players form the coalition, the criterion usually chosen is Pareto optimality whereas, if the coalition consists of only one player, a minmax or Nash equilibrium solution is sought.
Keywords
Differential games  Pareto optimality  multicriteria optimization  games with coalitions  cooperative games
