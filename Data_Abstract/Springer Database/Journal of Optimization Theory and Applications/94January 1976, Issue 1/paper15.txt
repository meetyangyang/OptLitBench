A new approach toN-person, nonzero-sum, linear differential games
M. Vidyasagar;
Abstract
It is shown that there exist equilibrium strategies forn-person, nonero-sum, linear differential games if the cost to each player is convex. The approach used is believed to be novel, and is based on a theorem of Fan.
Keywords
Game theory  differential games  linear systems  nonzero-sum games  n-person games
