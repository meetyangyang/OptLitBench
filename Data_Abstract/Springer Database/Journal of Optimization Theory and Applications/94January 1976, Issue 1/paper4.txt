A note on nonzero-sum differential games with bargaining solution
A. Haurie;
Abstract
The extension of Nash's bargaining solution to differential games is discussed. It is shown that a closed-loop solution verifies very stringent necessary conditions and that an open-loop solution can present serious weakness from a normative point of view.
Keywords
Bargaining solutions  differential games  nonzero-sum games  Pareto optimality  linear-quadratic games
