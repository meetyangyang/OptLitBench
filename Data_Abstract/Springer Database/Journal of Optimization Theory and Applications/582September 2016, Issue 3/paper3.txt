Optimal Bilinear Control of Nonlinear Hartree Equations with Singular Potentials
Binhua Feng;Kai Wang;
Abstract
In this paper, we consider an optimal bilinear control problem for nonlinear Hartree equations with singular potentials. We show well-posedness of the problem and existence of an optimal control. In addition, the first-order optimality system is rigorously derived. In particular, we prove Fréchet-differentiability of the objective functional. Our results improve considerably some recent results.
