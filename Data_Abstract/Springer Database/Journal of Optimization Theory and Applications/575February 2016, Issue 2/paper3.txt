An Elementary Proof of the Existence of Solutions of a Monotone Variational Inequality in the Finite-Dimensional Case
Jean-Pierre Crouzeix;
Abstract
This short note shows that the existence of solutions of a finite-dimensional monotone variational inequality on a compact set can be proved with only very elementary tools.
