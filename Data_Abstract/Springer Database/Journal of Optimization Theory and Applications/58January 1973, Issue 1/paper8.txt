Optimization of functional differential systems
J. K. Aggarwal;D. H. Eller;
Abstract
A method of determining optimal control for functional differential systems is presented. This is a generalization of Krasovskii's results on the optimal control of time delay systems, which include several of the results published recently. A technique for the numerical solution of the resulting Riccati equations is developed, and an example is worked to illustrate the presented results.
