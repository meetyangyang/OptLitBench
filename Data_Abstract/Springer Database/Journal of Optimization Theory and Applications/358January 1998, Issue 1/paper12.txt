New Saddle Point Theorem Beyond Topological Vector Spaces
J. Yu;X. Z. Yuan;
Abstract
The purpose of this note is to prove a new topological saddle-point theorem, which in turn includes classical saddle-point theorems such as the Sion minimax theorem and others in topological vector spaces as special cases.
Keywords
Two-person zero-sum games  saddle points  minimax theorems  connectedness
