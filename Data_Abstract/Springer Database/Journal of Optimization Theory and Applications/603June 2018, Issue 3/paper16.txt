Images and Existence of Constrained Scalar and Vector Extrema
Carla Antoni;Franco Giannessi;
Abstract
By means of the image space analysis, a necessary and sufficient condition is established for the existence of vector extrema. In the scalar case, the sufficient part is shown to shrink to a known one.
Keywords
Image space analysis  Vector optimization problems  Multi-objective Problems  Existence of maxima and minima  Bi-level optimization  65K  90C
