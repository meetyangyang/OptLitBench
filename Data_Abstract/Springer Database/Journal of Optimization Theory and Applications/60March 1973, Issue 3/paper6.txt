A new necessary condition for optimality of systems with time delay
M. A. Soliman;
Abstract
A new necessary condition for optimality of nonsingular control problems with time delay is developed. This condition accounts for the interaction of the different delays in the control vector. A necessary condition for optimality of singular control problems with time delay is also obtained as a corollary of the new condition.
