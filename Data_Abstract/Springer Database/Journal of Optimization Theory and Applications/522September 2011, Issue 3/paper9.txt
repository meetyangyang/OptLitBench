On a Generalized Ky Fan Inequality and Asymptotically Strict Pseudocontractions in the Intermediate Sense
Xiaolong Qin;Lai-Jiu Lin;Shin Min Kang;
Abstract
In this paper, two iterative algorithms are considered for a generalized Ky Fan inequality and a fixed point problem of asymptotically strict pseudocontractions in the intermediate sense. Strong and weak convergence theorems are established in real Hilbert spaces.
Keywords
Asymptotically strict pseudocontraction in the intermediate sense  Fixed point  Monotone mapping  Nonexpansive mapping  Ky Fan inequality
