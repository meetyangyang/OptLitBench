An optimal control problem for systems described by partial differential equations of hyperbolic type with delay
M. E. Salukvadze;M. T. Tcutcunava;
Abstract
An optimal control problem of the Gourse type with delay is investigated. With a given aim functional, a necessary condition of optimality is formulated and proved in the form of a maximum principle. The proof is based on the reduction of a problem with delay to a problem without delay.
Keywords
Control theory  optimal control  partial differential equations  time-delay systems  necessary condition  maximum principle
