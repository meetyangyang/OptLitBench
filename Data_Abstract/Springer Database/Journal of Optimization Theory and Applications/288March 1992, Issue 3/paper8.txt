Direct solution of an optimal layout problem for isotropic and anisotropic heat conductors on a plane
K. A. Lurie;
Abstract
This paper suggests a procedure of direct construction of the minimal extension of a constrained optimization problem for a heat equation containing controls in the coefficients. For a somewhat simpler problem, this approach has been initiated in Ref. 1.
Keywords
Relaxation techniques  nonsaddle integrands  quasisaddle functions
