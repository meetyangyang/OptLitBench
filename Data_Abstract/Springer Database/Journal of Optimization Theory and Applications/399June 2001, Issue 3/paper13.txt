On the Conversion of Optimization Problems with Max–Min Constraints to Standard Optimization Problems
S. J. Li;X. Q. Yang;K. L. Teo;
Abstract
In this note, we obtain a sufficient and necessary condition for a point to be a local minimizer of a continuous optimization problem with maximin constraints via the local minimizers of its transformed standard nonlinear optimization problem.
Keywords
optimization  maximin constraints  nonlinear programs
