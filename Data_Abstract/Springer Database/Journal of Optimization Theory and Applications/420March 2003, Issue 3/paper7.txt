Observability Inequalities by Internal Observation and Their Applications
K. Liu;M. Yamamoto;X. Zhang;
Abstract
By means of a Carleman estimate, we obtain several observability inequalities by internal observation for hyperbolic equations with lower-order terms. Also, we apply our observability results to an exact controllability problem and an inverse wave source problem.
Keywords
Observability inequalities  wave equation  Carleman estimates  exact controllability  inverse problems
