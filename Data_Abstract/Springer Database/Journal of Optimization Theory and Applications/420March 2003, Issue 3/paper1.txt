Analysis of a Viscoelastic Unilateral Contact Problem Involving the Coulomb Friction Law
A. Amassad;C. Fabre;
Abstract
We give an existence result concerning the description of a contact problem between a viscoelastic body and a rigid foundation. We assume that a quasistatic process is valid. The contact is unilateral and involves friction between the two bodies. The friction law that we consider is a regularization of the Coulomb law. We present a weak formulation of the problem involving variational inequalities and establish an existence result, using a discretization method and a fixed-point property. The discretization method leads to the study of optimization problems on convex sets which depend on the discretization step.
Keywords
Quasistatic frictional contact  unilateral contact  Coulomb friction law  viscoelasticity  discretization  fixed points
