Determination of the optimal initial function and the parameters in a system described by delay-differential equations
P. C. Das;N. K. Patel;S. S. Prabhu;
Abstract
The determination of the initial function and the parameters in a system of delay-differential equations minimizing a cost functional is considered. The existence of a solution to the problem is discussed. A set of necessary optimality conditions is derived, and a computational method is presented to obtain a locally minimal solution.
Keywords
Parametric optimization  approximation identification  modelling
