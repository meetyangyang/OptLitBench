Numerical solution of a relaxed control problem by the epsilon method
K. Shmueli;A. M. Steinberg;
Abstract
A relaxed control problem is solved using the epsilon method of Balakrishnan. The singularity of the problem with respect to one of the controls presents no difficulty. However, solutions are sensitive to the initial guess of the states and controls. For a system with true dynamics, terminal conditions may generally be approximated, but cannot be satisfied exactly.
Keywords
Epsilon method  numerical solutions  relaxed controls  convergence
