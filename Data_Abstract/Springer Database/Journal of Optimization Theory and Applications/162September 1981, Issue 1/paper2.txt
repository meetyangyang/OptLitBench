An algorithm for the minimization of convex, nondifferentiable functionals in Hilbert space
E. A. Sideri;
Abstract
In this paper, an algorithm for minimizing a convex functionalF(x), defined by the maximum of convex smooth functionalsF i (x), is described. The sequence of valuesF(x n ) is monotonically decreasing, the algorithm is convergent, and it attains quadratic convergence near the solution.
Keywords
Nondifferentiable optimization  rate of convergence  convex functionals  minimizing sequences
