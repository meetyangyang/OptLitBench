Contour of complete nonuniqueness as a method of structural optimization with stability constraints
M. Markiewicz;M. Życzkowski;
Abstract
This paper develops the concept of a contour of complete nonuniqueness and shows its application to the optimal design (optimal layout) of simple trusses transmitting a concentrated force to a presscribed, arbitrary foundation contour. As an example, a symmetric two-bar truss with compressed bars is considered, and the constraints refer to various kinds of elastic instability of the system.
Keywords
Contour of complete nonuniqueness  trusses  stability  structural optimization
