The region of attainability of nonlinear systems with unbounded controls
A. Bacciotti;G. Stefani;
Abstract
In this work, we give a characterization of the topological boundary and the interior of the reachable set for a family of vector fields of the formX0+aX i , wherea∈ℝ andi belongs to an arbitrary set of indices. The methods considered in this article are applied to various problems in control theory.
Keywords
Families of vector fields  attainable set  nonlinear systems  unbounded controls
