Proof by Verification of the Greville/Udwadia/Kalaba Formula for the Moore-Penrose Inverse of a Matrix
K. C. Sivakumar;
Abstract
In this short note, we present a simple linear-algebraic proof, by verification, of the well-known Greville/Udwadia/Kalaba formula for computing the Moore-Penrose inverse of a matrix.
Keywords
Moore-Penrose inverse  Greville/Udwadia/Kalaba method
