Game Control Problem for a Phase Field Equation
Vyacheslav Maksimov;
Abstract
A game control problem for a phase field equation is considered. This problem is investigated from the viewpoint of both the first player (the partner) and of the second player (the opponent). For both players, their own procedures for forming feedback controls are specified.
