Absolutely cooperative solution for a linear, multiplayer differential game
G. Basile;T. L. Vincent;
Abstract
If the players of ann-player differential game agree to cooperate, then the solutions to the game should be confined to undominated ones. A property of an undominated or Pareto-optimal solution is that, when compared locally with any other solution, at least one player must do worse or all do the same if they use a solution other than the Pareto-optimal one.
