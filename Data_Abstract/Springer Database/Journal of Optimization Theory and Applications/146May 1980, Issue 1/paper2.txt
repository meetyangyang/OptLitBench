Optimization of nested polygons
B. A. Troesch;
Abstract
The optimization problem under consideration requires to find the largest regular polygon withk sides to be fitted into a regular polygon withk − 1 sides. If the sequence of these maximal polygons is started with an equilateral triangle, then the final nested polygon, a circle, possesses a radiusr=0.3414r3, wherer3 is the radius of the inscribed circle of the equilateral triangle. Lower bounds for the ratior/r3 are also obtained.
Keywords
Geometrical optimization  regular polygons of maximal area  infinite products
