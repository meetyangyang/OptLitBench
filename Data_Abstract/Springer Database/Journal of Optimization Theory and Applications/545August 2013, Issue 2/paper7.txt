Constructive Proofs of some Positivstellensätze for Compact Semialgebraic Subsets of ℝ d  
Gennadiy Averkov;
Abstract
In a broad sense, positivstellensätze are results about representations of polynomials, strictly positive on a given set. We give proofs of some known positivstellensätze for compact semialgebraic subsets of ℝ d , which are to a large extent constructive and elementary. The presented proofs extend and simplify arguments of Berr, Wörmann (Manuscripta Math. 104(2):135–143, 2001) and Schweighofer (J. Pure Appl. Algebra 166(3):307–319, 2002; SIAM J. Optim. 15(3):805–825, 2005).
