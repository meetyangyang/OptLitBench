Multivalued differential equations in separable Banach spaces
M. Kisielewicz;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with multivalued differential equations of the form\(\dot x\)∈F(t,x), whereF is a multivalued mapping taking as its values nonempty compact, but not necessarily convex, subsets in a separable Banach space. The main result is connected with the existence of solutions of these equations.
Keywords
Differential equations  Banach spaces  existence theorems  functional analysis
