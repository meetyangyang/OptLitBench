Optimal load frequency control with governor backlash
M. A. Abdel-Halim;G. S. Christensen;D. H. Kelly;
Abstract
A new technique is described for designing an optimal controller for a system whose dynamical equations contain a backlash element. The approach is applied to the problem of load frequency control (LFC) of a single area steam power system.
Keywords
Optimal control  parameter optimization  power systems  load frequency control
