On problems with bounded state variables
I. Bert Russak;
Abstract
A set of first-order necessary conditions is obtained for the general control problem of Bolza with bounded state constraints of the formψ α (t, x)≤0, α=1,...,m. With the solution required to satisfy the vector differential equationsx=f(t, x, u), whereu is control, an important feature of this paper is in relaxing the assumption on the rank of the matrixψ  x   α  fu generally made in attacking problems of this type. This is accomplished even though the solution may have an infinite number of intervals satisfyingψ α (t, x)=0 for various α.
