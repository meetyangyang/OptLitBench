Gauss-seidel method for least-distance problems
W. Li;P. M. Pardalos;C. G. Han;
Abstract
In this paper, we reformulate the least-distance problems with bounded inequality constraints as an unconstrained convex minimization problem, which is equivalent to a system of piecewise linear equationsA(a+A T y)  c   d  =b. The proposed Gauss-Seidel method for solving the problems is easy to implement and behaves very well when the number of rows ofA is much less than the number of columns ofA. Moreover, we prove that the Gauss-Seidel method has a linear convergence rate.
Keywords
Least-distance problems  linear Gauss-Seidel method  piecewise linear equations  unconstrained convex minimization problems  linear convergence
