A class of differential games with state-dependent closed-loop feedback solutions
D. Yeung;
Abstract
In this paper, a class ofN-person, nonzero-sum differential games with state-dependent closed-loop feedback solutions is presented. In particular, the system of Hamilton-Jacobi equations for a closed-loop feedback solution turns out to be a set ofN-independent linear equations with viscosity solutions.
Keywords
Differential games  closed-loop feedback solutions  Hamilton-Jacobi equations  viscosity solutions
