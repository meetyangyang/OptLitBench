Lagrange multiplier rules for extremals in linear spaces
Y. Nagahisa;
Abstract
The aim of this paper is to formulate extremals in real, linear spaces, and to derive necessary conditions in the form of Lagrange multiplier rules for the extremals. Using a separation of intrinsic cores in real, linear spaces, the multiplier rules are proved under some conditions.
Keywords
Lagrange multiplier rule  extremals  separation theorem  intrinsic core
