A decomposition of time scales for iterative computation of time-optimal controls
S. H. Javid;P. V. Kokotovic;
Abstract
An iterative procedure, exploiting the multiple time scale properties of systems with slow and fast modes, is presented. This procedure solves the time-optimal problem for higher-order systems iteratively via the solutions of simple second-order problems. Computational complexity is so reduced that fourth-order problems can be solved using a microprocessor or programmable calculator.
Keywords
Time-optimal control  singularly perturbed systems  iterative methods  time scale decomposition
