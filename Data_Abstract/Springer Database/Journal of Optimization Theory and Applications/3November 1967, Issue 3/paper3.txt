A descent algorithm for constrained stochastic extrema
Y. C. Ho;P. M. Newbold;
Abstract
In situations where it is not feasible to find an optimal feedback control law for a stochastic system, an open-loop law can often be derived by optimization. This article presents a method of finding the extremum of certain stochastic functionals analogous to the steepest descent method. Necessary conditions for the convergence of the algorithm are given. Two examples illustrate the use of the algorithm.
