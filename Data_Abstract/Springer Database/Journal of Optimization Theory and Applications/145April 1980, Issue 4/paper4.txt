Mixed strategy solutions forN-person quadratic games
R. J. Williams;
Abstract
Mixed strategy ∈-equilibrium points are given forN-person games with cost functions consisting of quadratic, bilinear, and linear terms and strategy spaces consisting of closed balls in Hilbert spaces. The results are applied to linear-quadratic differential games with no information and quadratic integral constraints on the control functions.
Keywords
N-person quadratic games  mixed strategies  ∈-equilibrium points  linear-quadratic differential games
