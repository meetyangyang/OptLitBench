Perturbation theory for games in normal form and stochastic games
S. H. Tijs;O. J. Vrieze;
Abstract
In this paper, the effect on values and optimal strategies of perturbations of game parameters (payoff function, transition probability function, and discount factor) is studied for the class of zero-sum games in normal form and for the class of stationary, discounted, two-person, zero-sum stochastic games.
Keywords
Game theory, games in normal form  stochastic games  nonstationary stochastic games  perturbation of payoffs  perturbation of transition probability function  perturbation of discount factor
