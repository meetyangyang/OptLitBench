Remark on the relationship between the biconjugate direction algorithm and the generalized conjugate direction algorithm
D. J. Van Wyk;
Abstract
In Ref. 1, a correspondence between the generalized conjugate direction method (Refs. 2 and 3) and the direction counterpart of the biconjugate gradient method (Ref. 4) is pointed out. This is a comment on the relationship.
Keywords
Quadratic optimization  conjugate directions  unconstrained optimization
