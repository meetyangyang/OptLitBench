An initial-value method for the determinantal equations of vibrations and flutter
R. E. Kalaba;E. Zagustin;
Abstract
In many investigations in mechanics, we must solve the equation detB(λ)=0, where the elements of the matrixB are general functions of λ. A method of solution is proposed, and results of numerical experiments are given.
Keywords
Determinantal equations  eigenvalues of matrices  eigenvectors of matrices  initial-value methods
