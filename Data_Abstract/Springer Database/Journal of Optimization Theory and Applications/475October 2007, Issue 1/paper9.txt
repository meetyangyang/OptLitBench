Necessary Optimality Conditions for a Dead Oil Isotherm Optimal Control Problem
M. R. Sidi Ammi;D. F. M. Torres;
Abstract

We study a system of nonlinear partial differential equations resulting from the traditional modelling of oil engineering within the framework of the mechanics of a continuous medium. Recent results on the problem provide existence, uniqueness and regularity of the optimal solution. Here we obtain the first necessary optimality conditions.

Keywords
Optimal control of distributed systems  Dead oil isotherm problem  Necessary optimality conditions
