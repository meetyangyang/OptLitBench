Optimal controls with equality state constraints
A. B. Schwarzkopf;
Abstract
In this paper, we derive necessary conditions for a measurable controlu with values in a compact convex subsetU ofR P  to minimize the integral$$\int_a^b {b^0 (s,x(s),u(s))} ds$$ subject to equality and inequality state constraints. The general conditions take the form of a maximum principle which involves an integration with respect to a finitely additive set function, but conditions are given under which this reduces to the classical maximum principle. These results are similar to results derived in Ref. 1 for relaxed controls.
Keywords
Control theory  Lagrange problems  bounded control problems  maximum principle  nonlinear programming
