A discretization method for systems of linear inequalities
K. H. Meyn;
Abstract
We investigate a method for approximating a convex domainC⊂Rn described by a (possibly infinite) set of linear inequalities. In contrast to other methods, the approximating domains (polyhedrons) lie insideC. We discuss applications to semi-infinite programming and present numerical examples.
Keywords
Inequalities  semi-infinite programming  polynomials  discretization methods
