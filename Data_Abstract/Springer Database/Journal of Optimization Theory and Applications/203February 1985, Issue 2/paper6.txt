Existence of an optimal control with sparse jumps in the state variable
A. Seierstad;
Abstract
The existence of an optimal control is proved in a problem where the criterion functional and the equation of motion contain a control that is a Lebesgue-Stieltjes measure. Due to nonlinearities in the problem, it is necessary to postulate a condition implying that large atoms of the control measures are sparse and that their derivatives, wherever they exist, have a uniform bound.
Keywords
Optimal control  control differential systems involving impulses  jumps in the state variables
