A strong variational algorithm for delay systems
G. S. Virk;
Abstract
In this paper, we present a convergent extension of the first-order strong-variational algorithm by Mayne and Polak (Ref. 1) for solving optimal control problems with control constraints to delay systems. Although the algorithm is similar to the one presented in Ref. 1, the proof of convergence is different, since the differential dynamic techniques used by Mayne and Polak are not applicable.
Keywords
Strong variational algorithms  optimal control  control constraints  nonlinear delay systems
