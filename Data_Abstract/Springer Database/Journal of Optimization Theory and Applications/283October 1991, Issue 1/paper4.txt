Impulsive control systems with commutative vector fields
A. BressanJr.;F. Rampazzo;
Abstract
We consider variational problems with control laws given by systems of ordinary differential equations whose vector fields depend linearly on the time derivativeu=(u1,...,u m ) of the controlu=(u1,...,u m ). The presence of the derivativeu, which is motivated by recent applications in Lagrangian mechanics, causes an impulsive dynamics: at any jump of the control, one expects a jump of the state.
Keywords
Commutativity of vector fields  adjoint systems  measurable functions  optimal controls  maximum principle
