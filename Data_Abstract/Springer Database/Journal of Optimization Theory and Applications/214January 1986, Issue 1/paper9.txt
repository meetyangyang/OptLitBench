Approximate first-order and second-order directional derivatives of a marginal function in convex optimization
J. -B. Hiriart-Urruty;
Abstract
Given a convex functionf:ℝ p ×ℝ q  → (−∞, +∞], the marginal function φ is defined on ℝ p  by φ(x)=inf{f(x, y)|y∈ℝ q }. Our purpose in this paper is to express the approximate first-order and second-order directional derivatives of φ atx0 in terms of those off at (x0,y0), wherey0 is any element for which φ(x0)=f(x0,y0).
Keywords
Convex optimization  marginal functions  approximate second-order directional derivatives of convex functions
