Deterministic optimal maneuver strategy for multi-target missions
N. P. Dwivedi;
Abstract
This paper presents an optimal strategy for making impulsive correction to a multi-target trajectory by a single maneuver. The concept of an optimal maneuver time is introduced. The choice of suitable weighting functions is explored to enable one to properly translate the subjective desire of mission success into an objective cost function whose minimization yields the optimal strategy.
Keywords
Optimal strategies  guidance theory  trajectories  terminal control  multistage problems
