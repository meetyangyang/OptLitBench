On qualitative differential games with two targets
R. J. Stonier;
Abstract
We consider a qualitative differential game between two players, each with their own respective target. A player's objective is to steer the response of a dynamical system under the control of both players into his own target before his opponent can steer it to his. The sufficiency conditions given by Getz and Leitmann (Ref. 2), which ensure termination on a particular target, are improved. The theory is illustrated by a pursuit-evasion problem.
Keywords
Differential games  first reachability of sets  playability  pursuit-evasion problem
