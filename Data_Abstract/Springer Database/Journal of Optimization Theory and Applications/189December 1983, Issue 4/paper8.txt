Nonconical optimality conditions: Some additional results
G. B. Hazen;T. L. Morin;
Abstract
The purpose of the present note is to provide a somewhat more flexible statement of the sufficiency conditions in Section 4 of Ref. 1 and to give explicit statements of the fundamental necessity and sufficiency results, which were only implicitly presented there. The additional flexibility is obtained by considering local, rather than global, pseudoconcavity requirements.
Keywords
Multiple-objective optimization  vector optimization  existence theorems  first-order optimality conditions  normed linear spaces
