Simple-motion pursuit-evasion differential games, part 2: Optimal evasion from proportional navigation guidance in the deterministic and stochastic cases
M. Pachter;Y. Yavin;
Abstract
The problem of the optimal evasion from a pursuer employing proportional navigation guidance is considered. Thus, we consider the problem of maximizing the time-to-capture when the problem parameters, namely, the speed ratio and the proportional navigation constant, are such that capturability of the evader is guaranteed.
Keywords
Pursuit-evasion  guidance  proportional navigation  optimal control
