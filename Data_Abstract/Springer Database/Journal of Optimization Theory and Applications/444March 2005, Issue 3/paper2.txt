Integration of Primal Lower Nice Functions in Hilbert Spaces
F. Bernard;L. Thibault;D. Zagrodny;
Abstract
In this paper, we obtain some integration results from subdifferential inclusions for primal lower nice functions by using the Moreau envelopes. A general result concerns an enlarged subdifferential inclusion. It says that, for g primal lower nice at x, the inclusion
\(\partial f \subset g + \gamma \mathbb{B}\) around x entails that, for any γ′]0; γ[, f − g is γ′∈- Lipschitz continuous on an appropriate neighborhood of x.
Keywords
Primal lower nice functions  Moreau envelopes  proximal mappings  subdifferentials
