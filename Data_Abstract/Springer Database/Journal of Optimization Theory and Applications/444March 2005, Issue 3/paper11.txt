Generalized Differential Properties of the Auslender Gap Function for Variational Inequalities
S. J. Li;S. H. Hou;G. Y. Chen;
Abstract
In this note, the Auslender gap function, which is used to formulate a variational inequality into an equivalent minimization problem, is shown to be differentiable in the generalized sense and has a lower contingent derivative under suitable conditions. This enables us to establish necessary and sufficient conditions for the existence of a solution to problems of variational inequalities.
Keywords
Variational inequalities  gap functions  lower contingent derivatives
