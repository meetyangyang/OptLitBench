The Necessary Conditions of Fractional Optimal Control in the Sense of Caputo
Tian Liang Guo;
Abstract
This paper deals with the optimal control problem of a fractional dynamic system in the sense of Caputo. The main result of this paper gives a second order necessary optimality condition for fractional optimal control problems, which has not been discussed before. An application is introduced to explain our main results.
Keywords
Fractional dynamic system  Optimal control  Necessary conditions  Calculus of variations
