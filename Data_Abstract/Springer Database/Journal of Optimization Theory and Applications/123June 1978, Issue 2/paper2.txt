A converse for disjunctive constraints
C. E. Blair;R. G. Jeroslov;
Abstract
A necessary and sufficient condition is given for the disjunctive constraints construction to provide all valid cuts for a system of logical constraints on linear inequalities. This condition is then applied to several commonly occurring situations of integer programming, and the issue of verification of the condition is discussed.
Keywords
Integer programming  disjunctive constraints  polyhedral annexation  cutting planes
