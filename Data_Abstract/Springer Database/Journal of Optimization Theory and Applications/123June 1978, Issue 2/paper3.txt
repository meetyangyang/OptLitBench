Simultaneous and sequential solution of a stiff differential equation system
S. M. Roberts;
Abstract
For the two-point boundary-value problem considered here with its system of stiff ordinary differential equations, the solution obtained by sequential computation is faster than that obtained by simultaneous computation with no loss in accuracy. In all cases, the equations are integrated by Gear's method using identical data and program control parameters.
Keywords
Simultaneous computation  sequential computation  stiff equations  two-point boundary-value problems
