On the asymptotic order of accuracy of Tikhonov regularization
C. W. Groetsch;
Abstract
In this paper, the asymptotic order of accuracy (with respect to the error level δ in the data) of Tikhonov's method for approximating the minimal norm least-square solution of an ill-posed operator equation is investigated. It is shown that, except for finite rank operators, the orderO(δ2/3) is best possible.
Keywords
Fredholm equations of the first kind  ill-posed problems  generalized inverses  regularization  asymptotic order of accuracy
