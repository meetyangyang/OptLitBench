Iterative Method for Set-Valued Mixed Quasi-variational Inequalities in a Banach Space
S. Schaible;J. C. Yao;L. C. Zeng;
Abstract
This paper introduces an iterative method for finding approximate solutions of a set-valued mixed quasivariational inequality in the setting of a Banach space. Existence of a solution of this rather general problem and the convergence of the proposed iterative method to a solution are established.
Keywords
Quasivariational inequalities  set-valued mappings  fixed-point problems  m-accretive operators  maximal monotone operators
