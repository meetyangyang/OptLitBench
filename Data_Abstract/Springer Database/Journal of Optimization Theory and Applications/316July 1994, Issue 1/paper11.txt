Yuan's alternative theorem and the maximization of the minimum eigenvalue function
J. E. Martinez-Legaz;A. Seeger;
Abstract
LetA1 andA2 be two symmetric matrices of ordern×n. According to Yuan, there exists a convex combination of these matrices which is positive semidefinite, if and only if the functionx∈R n  ↦ max {x T A1x,x T A2x} is nonnegative. We study the case in which more than two matrices are involved. We study also a related question concerning the maximization of the minimum eigenvalue of a convex combination of symmetric matrices.
Keywords
Alternative theorems  quadratic forms
