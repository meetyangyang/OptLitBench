New minimax inequality with applications to existence theorems of equilibrium points
K. K. Tan;J. Yu;
Abstract
A new minimax inequality is proved on a set which is the union of an increasing sequence of compact convex sets in a topological vector space. As applications, several existence theorems of equilibrium points for different games are obtained.
Keywords
Minimax inequalities  topological vector spaces  N-person games  qualitative games  equilibrium points  reflexive Banach spaces
