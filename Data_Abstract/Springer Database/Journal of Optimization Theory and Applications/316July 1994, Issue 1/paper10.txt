Remarks on the angle property and solid cones
Z. Q. Han;
Abstract
This paper is devoted to the investigation of the relations between cones satisfying the angle property and solid cones. The investigation shows that the two classes of cones are dual in some sense. As an application of our results, we improve some related results due to Cesari, Suryanarayna, Sterna-Karwat, and Yu.
