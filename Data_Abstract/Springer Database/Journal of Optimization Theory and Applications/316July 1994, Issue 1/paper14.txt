On differential games with a feedback nash equilibrium
D. W. K. Yeung;
Abstract
This note provides a lemma on differential games which possess a feedback Nash equilibrium (FNE). In particular, it shows that (i) a class of games with a degenerate FNE can be constructucted from every game which has a nondegenerate FNE and (ii) a class of games with a nondegenerate FNE can be constructed from every game which has a degenerate FNE.
Keywords
Differential games  feedback Nash equilibrium  openloop Nash equilibrium  degenerate feedback Nash equilibrium
