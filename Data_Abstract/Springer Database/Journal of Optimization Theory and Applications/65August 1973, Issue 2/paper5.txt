On observability and stability in infinite-dimensional linear systems
P. A. Fuhrmann;
Abstract
It seems to be generally known (Ref. 1) that, in the case of finite-dimensional systems, the state stability properties of the system may be inferred from the output stability properties if the system is completely observable.
