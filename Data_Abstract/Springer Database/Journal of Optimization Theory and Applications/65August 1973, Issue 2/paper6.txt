Min-max optimal control of systems approximated by finite-dimensional models: Nonquadratic cost functional
A. Negro;
Abstract
An open-loop control problem with nonquadratic performance criterion for a dynamical system, described by an abstract linear equation of evolution and approximated by a finite-dimensional model, is solved. A min-max approach is taken: the value of the cost functional in theworst-case output error between the system and the model, under the assumption that the norm of this output error is estimated by the norm of the input, is minimized.
Keywords
Hilbert Space  Control Problem  Euler Equation  Weak Topology  Output Error
