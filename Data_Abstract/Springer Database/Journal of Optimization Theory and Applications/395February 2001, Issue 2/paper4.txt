Error Bounds for R0-Type and Monotone Nonlinear Complementarity Problems
B. Chen;
Abstract
The paper generalizes the Mangasarian–Ren (Ref. 1) error bounds forlinear complementarity problems (LCPs) to nonlinear complementarity problems(NCPs). This is done by extending the concept of R0-matrixto several R0-type functions, which include a subset ofmonotone functions as a special case. Both local and global error bounds areobtained for R0-type NCPs and some monotone NCPs.
