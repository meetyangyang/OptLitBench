Coupling the Auxiliary Problem Principle and Epiconvergence Theory to Solve General Variational Inequalities
G. Salmon;V. H. Nguyen;J. J. Strodiot;
Abstract
Many algorithms for solving variational inequality problems can be derived from the auxiliary problem principle introduced several years ago by Cohen. In recent years, the convergence of these algorithms has been established under weaker and weaker monotonicity assumptions: strong (pseudo) monotonicity has been replaced by the (pseudo) Dunn property. Moreover, well-suited assumptions have given rise to local versions of these results.
Keywords
variational inequalities  auxiliary problem principle  nonsymmetric auxiliary operator  epiconvergence  barrier methods  generalized monotonicity  pseudo Dunn condition  Newton method
