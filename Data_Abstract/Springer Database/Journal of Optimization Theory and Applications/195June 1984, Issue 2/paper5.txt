ε-solutions in vector minimization problems
P. Loridan;
Abstract
This paper presents some properties of ε-solutions for vector minimization problems where the function to be optimized takes its values in the Euclidean space ℝ p . The results obtained generalize the classical ones for exact Pareto solutions.
Keywords
ε-efficient points  vector minimization problems  ε-Pareto solutions  Ekeland's variational principle
