A method for solving a quadratic optimal control problem
Russell D. Rupp;
Abstract
Hestenes' method of multipliers is used to approximate a quadratic optimal control problem. The global existence of a family of unconstrained problems is established. Given an initial estimate of the Lagrange multipliers, a convergent sequence of arcs is generated. They are minimizing with respect to members of the above family, and their limit is the solution to the original differentially constrained problem.
