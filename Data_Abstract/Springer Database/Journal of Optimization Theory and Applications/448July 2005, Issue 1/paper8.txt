Equilibrium Problems in the Quasimonotone Case
M. Fakhar;J. Zafarani;
Abstract
Existence results for quasimonotone vector equilibrium problems and quasimonotone vector variational inequalities are obtained starting from an existence result for a scalar equilibrium problem involving two quasimonotone bifunctions. These results are established under weaker conditions than in previous works.
Keywords
KKM maps  equilibrium problems  quasimonotone vector-valued bifunctions  variational inequalities.
