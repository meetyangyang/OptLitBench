Branch-and-bound decomposition approach for solving quasiconvex-concave programs
R. Horst;L. D. Muu;M. Nast;
Abstract
A class of branch-and-bound methods is proposed for minimizing a quasiconvex-concave function subject to convex and quasiconvex-concave inequality constraints. Several important special cases where the subproblems involved by the bounding-and-branching operations can be solved quite effectively include certain d.c. programming problems, indefinite quadratic programming with one negative eigenvalue, affine multiplicative problems, and fractional multiplicative optimization.
Keywords
Global optimization  convex-concave programming  branch-and-bound methods  optimization of differences of convex functions  fractional multiplicative programming
