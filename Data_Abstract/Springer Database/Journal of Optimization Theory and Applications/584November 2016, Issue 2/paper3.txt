Quaisidifferentials in Kantorovich Spaces
Elena K. Basaeva;Anatoly G. Kusraev;Semen S. Kutateladze;
Abstract
This is an overview of the quasidifferential calculus for the mappings that arrive at Kantorovich spaces. The necessary optimality conditions are derived also for multiple criteria optimization problems with quasidifferentiable data.
