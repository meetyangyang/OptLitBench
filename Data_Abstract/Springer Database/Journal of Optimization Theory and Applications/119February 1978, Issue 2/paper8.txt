A note on optimal fish harvest
J. Sanders;
Abstract
Some relatively simple techniques are applied to a singular control problem arising in bioeconomics. First, Stokes' theorem is applied to the fixed-endpoint problem. This reduces the free-endpoint problem to a problem in ordinary calculus. It is then possible to find an explicit expression for the switching manifold, which generalizes the results obtained by Cliff and Vincent (Ref. 1).
