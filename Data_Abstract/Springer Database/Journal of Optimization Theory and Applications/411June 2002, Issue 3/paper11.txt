Parameter Dependence and Controllability of Convex Processes with Delay
P. Lavilledieu;A. Seeger;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with the controllability of a delay-differential inclusion whose right-hand side is a parametric convex process and whose initial condition is described by a parametric convex cone.
Keywords
Convex processes  delay-differential inclusions  controllability  observability  adjoint systems  Painlevé–Kuratowski convergence
