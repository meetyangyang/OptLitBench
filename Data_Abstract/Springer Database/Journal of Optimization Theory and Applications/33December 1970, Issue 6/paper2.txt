On general problems with bounded state variables
I. Bert Russak;
Abstract
This paper serves as the sequel to a recent article by the author concerned with a certain canonical control problem. The present paper extends the first-order necessary conditions obtained there to a general form of the control problem of Bolza with inequality state constraints of the type ψα(t, x)≤0, α=1,...,m.
