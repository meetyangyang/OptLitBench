Optimization of heat flux in domains with temperature constraints
R. L. V. Gonzalez;D. A. Tarzia;
Abstract
In this paper, we deal with a heat flux optimization problem. We maximize the heat output flow on a portion of a domain's boundary, while on the other portion the distribution of the temperature is fixed. The maximization is carried out under the condition that there are no phase changes.
Keywords
Steady-state Stefan problem  mixed elliptic differential problem  constraint optimization problems  heat flux optimization problem  Lagrange multipliers theory  explicit solutions
