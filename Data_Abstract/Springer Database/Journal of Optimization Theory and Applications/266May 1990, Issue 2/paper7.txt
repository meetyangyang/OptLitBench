On the controllability of a class of differential inclusions depending on a parameter
O. Naselli Ricceri;
Abstract
In this paper, we deal with the following stability problem: given a differential inclusion of the formx'εF(t,x,λ), where λ is a parameter varying in a topological space Λ, find conditions under which the set of all λ ε Λ, such that the differential inclusion is controllable, is open in Λ. Applying Theorem 3.1 of Ref. 3, we get a result in this direction, assuming, as leader hypotheses, thatF(t,·,λ) is a convex process, from ℝ″ into itself, and thatF(t,x,·) is lower semicontinuous.
Keywords
Differential inclusions  convex processes  controllability  perturbations
