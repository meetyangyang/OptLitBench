Equilibrium points of a class of many-person games
K. T. Lee;K. C. Teo;
Abstract
This paper deals with the competitive equilibrium points of a class of (n+1)-person games, the players beingn sellers and a single buyer. Each seller offers a price for his product; depending upon the prices offered, the buyer then determines the amount he wishes to buy from each seller. Two types of equilibrium points in this class of games are discussed.
Keywords
Game theory  optimal strategies  equilibrium points  economics
