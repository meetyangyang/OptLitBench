Computational algorithm for a distributed optimal control problem of parabolic type with terminal inequality constraints
Z. S. Wu;K. L. Teo;
Abstract
A computational algorithm for optimal control problems with control and terminal inequality constraints involving first boundary-value problems of parabolic type is presented. The convergence properties are also studied.
Keywords
Parabolic differential equations  first boundary-value problems  optimal control problems with terminal inequality constraints  algorithms  convergence of algorithms
