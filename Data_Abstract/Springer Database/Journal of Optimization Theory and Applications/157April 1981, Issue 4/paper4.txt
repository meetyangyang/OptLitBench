The limiting Lagrangian as a consequence of Helly's theorem
J. M. Borwein;
Abstract
The perturbational Lagrangian equation established by Jeroslow in convex semi-infinite programming is derived from Helly's theorem and some prior results on one-dimensional perturbations of convex programs.
Keywords
Convex semi-infinite programming  limiting Lagrangians  Helly's theorem  recession directions  affine minorants  moment cones
