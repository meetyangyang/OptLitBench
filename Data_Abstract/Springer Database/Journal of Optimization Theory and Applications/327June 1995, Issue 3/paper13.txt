On strong pseudomonotonicity and (semi)strict quasimonotonicity
N. Hadjisavvas;S. Schaible;
Abstract
An example in Ref. 1 is corrected to show that indeed a strongly pseudoconvex function, which is only once but not twice differentiable, does not necessarily have a strongly pseudoconvex gradient.
Keywords
Strongly pseudoconvex functions  strongly pseudomonotone maps
