Numerical experience with a polyhedral-norm CDT trust-region algorithm
M. M. El-Alem;R. A. Tapia;
Abstract
In this paper, we study a modification of the Celis-Dennis-Tapia trust-region subproblem, which is obtained by replacing thel2-norm with a polyhedral norm. The polyhedral norm Celis-Dennis-Tapia (CDT) subproblem can be solved using a standard quadratic programming code.
Keywords
Trust-region methods  constrained optimization  Celis-Dennis-Tapia algorithm  successive quadratic programming algorithm  nonlinear programming  computational comparisons
