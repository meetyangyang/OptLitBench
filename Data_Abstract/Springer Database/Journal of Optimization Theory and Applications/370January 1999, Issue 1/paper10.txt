On Parametric Generalized Quasi-Variational Inequalities
X. P. Ding;C. L. Luo;
Abstract
In this paper, by using the projection method of Dafermos (Ref. 1), we study the behavior and sensitivity analysis of the solution set for a class of parametric generalized quasi-variational inequalities. Our approach and results are new and have a strong geometric flavor.
Keywords
Parametric analysis  generalized quasi-variational inequalities  sensitivity analysis  Hilbert spaces
