Global optimization and stochastic differential equations
F. Aluffi-Pentini;V. Parisi;F. Zirilli;
Abstract
Let ℝ n  be then-dimensional real Euclidean space,x=(x1,x2, ...,xn)T ∈ ℝ n , and letf:ℝ n  → R be a real-valued function. We consider the problem of finding the global minimizers off. A new method to compute numerically the global minimizers by following the paths of a system of stochastic differential equations is proposed. This method is motivated by quantum mechanics. Some numerical experience on a set of test problems is presented. The method compares favorably with other existing methods for global optimization.
Keywords
Global optimization  stochastic differential equations
