Controllability of Nonlinear Evolution Systems with Preassigned Responses
W. M. Bian;
Abstract
In this paper, we study the approximate controllability with preassigned responses of the nonlinear delay systems x′(t)=A(t)x(t)+f(t, x(t), x(δ(t)), u(t)) and L(x(t), x′(t))=A(t)x(t)+f(t, x(t), x(δ(t)), u(t)). The controllability is not governed by an associated linear system, but by conditions on f or A involving the domain of A(t). No compactness assumptions are imposed in the main results.
Keywords
Nonlinear systems  approximate controllability  preassigned responses  coercivity condition
