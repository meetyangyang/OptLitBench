A game locating a needle in a circular haystack
V. J. D. Baston;F. A. Bostock;
Abstract
A complete solution of the following Hider-Seeker zero-sum game is obtained. The hider hides a needle of lengtha, 0<a≤2, in the closed unit dise, and the seeker tries to locate it by shooting in a straight line across the disc. The payoff to the seeker is 1 if he hits the needle and 0 otherwise.
Keywords
