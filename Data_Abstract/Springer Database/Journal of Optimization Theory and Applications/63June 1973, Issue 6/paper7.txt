On the relative leadership property of Stackelberg strategies
T. Basar;
Abstract
The relative leadership property of Stackelberg strategies has been investigated via a scalar nonzero-sum, two-person differential game problem. It is shown that, depending on the parameters of the game, there exist three different types of solutions for his class of games.
