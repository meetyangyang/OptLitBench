Nonlinear time-domain models of human controllers
Lawrence W. TaylorJr.;
Abstract
Data from a compensatory tracking task are analyzed by using time-domain models. The linear time-domain results are transformed and compared with frequency-domain results. The nonlinear time-domain model of the same data reduced the remnant or residual power by only a small amount. The need for testing models on independent data is discussed. A novel, but attractive, method of generating functions for an efficient functional expansion of time-domain models is offered.
Keywords
