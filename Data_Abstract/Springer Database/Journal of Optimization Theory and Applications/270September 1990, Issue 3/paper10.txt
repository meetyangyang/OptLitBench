Controllability for parabolic equations with uniformly bounded nonlinear terms
M. Yamamoto;J. Y. Park;
Abstract
We consider a control system for a parabolic equation in a Banach space with uniformly bounded nonlinear termF,$$dz(t)/dt + Az(t) = F(t, z(t)) + Bf(t),       t > 0.$$ Here,Bf(t) corresponds to a finite-dimensional control. We prove the equivalence of approximate controllability for the above nonlinear system and that for the linear system$$dz(t)/dt + Az(t) = Bf(t),       t > 0.$$ Our method is based on results on approximate controllability for linear parabolic systems and estimates of solutions. A practical example is also given for a diffusion and reaction model.
Keywords
Approximate controllability  parabolic equations  semilinear control systems  uniformly bounded nonlinearities  reachable sets
