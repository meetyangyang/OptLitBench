Optimal interception with time constraint
N. X. Vinh;P. Lu;R. M. Howe;E. G. Gilbert;
Abstract
This paper considers the problem of minimum-fuel interception with time constraint. The maneuver consists of using impulsive thrust to bring the interceptor from its initial orbit into a collision course with a target which is moving on a well-defined trajectory. The intercept time is either prescribed or is restricted to be less than an upper limit.
Keywords
Orbital transfer  optimal interception  primer vector theory  hodograph theory  Lambert's problem  interception of ballistic missiles
