Rearrangement Optimization for Some Elliptic Equations
B. Emamizadeh;M. Zivari-Rezapour;
Abstract

We investigate an optimization problem related to an elliptic (linear and nonlinear) boundary-value problem. The competing objects are elements of a rearrangement class generated by a fixed positive function. The popular case where the generator is a characteristic function is also considered. In this case, the method of the domain derivative is used to obtain a free boundary result.

Keywords
Optimization  Rearrangements  Inequalities  Existence  Uniqueness  Free boundary  Domain derivative
