On the Solution Existence of Generalized Quasivariational Inequalities with Discontinuous Multifunctions
B. T. Kien;N. C. Wong;J. C. Yao;
Abstract

We study the following generalized quasivariational inequality problem: given a closed convex set X in a normed space E with the dual E *, a multifunction 
\(\Phi :X\rightarrow 2^{E^{*}}\) 
and a multifunction Γ:X→2 X , find a point 
\((\hat{x},\hat{z})\in X\times E^{*}\) 
such that 
\(\hat{x}\in \Gamma(\hat{x}),\hat{z}\in \Phi (\hat{x}),\langle \hat{z},\hat{x}-y\rangle \leq 0\)
, 
\(\forall y\in \Gamma(\hat{x})\)
. We prove some existence theorems in which Φ may be discontinuous, X may be unbounded, and Γ is not assumed to be Hausdorff lower semicontinuous.

Keywords
Generalized quasivariational inequalities  Lower semicontinuity  Hausdorff upper semicontinuity  Hausdorff lower semicontinuity  Multifunctions  Closed graphs  Open graphs
