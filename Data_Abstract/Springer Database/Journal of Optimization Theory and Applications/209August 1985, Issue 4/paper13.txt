On the use of Hamiltonian and maximized Hamiltonian in nondifferentiable control theory
G. Feichtinger;R. F. Hartl;
Abstract
In optimal control problems involving nondifferentiable functions of the state variable, the adjoint differential inclusion can be formulated by either use of the Hamiltonian or the maximized Hamiltonian. In this paper, we solve a production-employment model in which the latter approach must be utilized, since the former does not enable one to determine the optimal policy.
Keywords
Optimal control  envelope theorem  differential inclusions  nondifferentiable functions  production-employment model
