Duality Theory and Applications to Unilateral Problems
Patrizia Daniele;Sofia Giuffrè;Antonino Maugeri;Fabio Raciti;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with the problem of strong duality between an infinite dimensional convex optimization problem with cone and equality constraints and its Lagrange dual.
Keywords
Strong duality  Variational inequalities  Quasi-relative interior  Elastic–plastic torsion
