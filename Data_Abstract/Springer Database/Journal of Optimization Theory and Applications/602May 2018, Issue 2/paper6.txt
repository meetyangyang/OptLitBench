Stability Property in Bifunction-Set Optimization
Pham Huu Sach;
Abstract
This paper gives sufficient conditions for the lower and upper semicontinuities of the solution mapping of a model, called the parametric bifunction-set optimization problem, which provides a bridge between several parametric set optimization problems and parametric generalized vector Ky Fan inequality problems. Our main theorems, applied to the just mentioned problems, give some new or sharper results.
