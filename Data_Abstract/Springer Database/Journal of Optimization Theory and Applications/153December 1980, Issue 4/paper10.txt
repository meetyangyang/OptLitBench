Decentralized control by rational controllers
S. M. Meerkov;
Abstract
A strongly decentralized control problem is considered where only local information is available on both states and goals (the desired states) of each subsystem. A theory is presented which allows one to optimize the behavior of a system with such an information structure.
Keywords
Decentralized control  rational controllers  rules of interaction  optimal behavior  chaotic behavior  Nash equilibrium position  critical size of a system
