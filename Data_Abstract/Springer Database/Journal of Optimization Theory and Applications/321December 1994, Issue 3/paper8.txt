Two-step and three-step Q-superlinear convergence of SQP methods
B. Rustem;
Abstract
This paper investigates the convergence rates of the variable-multiplier pair (x, λ) in sequential quadratic programming methods for equality constrained optimization. The two main results of the paper are that the Q-superlinear convergence of {x k } implies two-step Q-superlinear convergence for {(x k , λ k )} and that the two-step Q-superlinear convergence of {x k } implies three-step Q-superlinear convergence for {(x k , λ k )}.
