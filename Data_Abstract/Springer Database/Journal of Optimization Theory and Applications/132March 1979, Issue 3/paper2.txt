A Filippov-type lemma for functions involving delays and its application to time-delayed optimal control problems
S. Nababan;
Abstract
A Filippov-type lemma for functions involving delays is derived. This lemma is used to prove the existence of an optimal control for a class of nonlinear control processes with delays appearing in both state and control variables.
Keywords
Delay-differential equations  cost-optimal problems  time-optimal problems  existence of optimal controls  nonlinear control processes
