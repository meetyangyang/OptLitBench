On the Bellman equation of the overtaking criterion: Addendum
R. A. Dana;C. Le Van;
Abstract
In a previous note, we discussed the properties of solutions to the Bellman equation of the Gale overtaking criterion. The purpose of this note is to show that the dynamic programming approach may also be used for the Brock criterion.
Keywords
Bellman's equation  value function  overtaking criterion  dynamical systems
