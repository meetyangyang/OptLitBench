Sufficiency theorems for optimal control
George Leitmann;
Abstract
Two sufficiency theorems, related to those of Kalman, Athans and Falb, and Boltyanskii, are derived. The derivation is based on the invariance of a certain line integral along a solution curve reaching the terminal state set. The theorems are applied to some simple problems.
Keywords
Terminal State  Simple Problem  Solution Curve  Sufficiency Theorem
