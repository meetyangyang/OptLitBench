Conventional penalty optimization methods
M. A. Ibiejugba;B. J. Olufeagba;
Abstract
In a recent study, the effects of large penalty constants on Ritz penalty methods based on finite-element approximations used in the solution of the control of a system governed by the diffusion equation were established.
Keywords
Ritz penalty methods  finite-element approximations  diffusion equation  convergence rate  multiplier method
