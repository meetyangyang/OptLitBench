The Whole Random Optimization with Application
Chuan-Gan Hu;
Abstract
On an open or a finitely open subset of a Fréchet space into a Fréchet space, the whole random optimization on large fields is investigated based on the concepts and properties of holomorphic and meromorphic random mappings using the topological products of Banach spaces. Examples on earthquake warnings are illustrated for explaining the application of the whole random optimizations.
Keywords
Fréchet space  Recognition of earthquake warning  Meromorphic random mappings  Random spaces  Whole random optimization
