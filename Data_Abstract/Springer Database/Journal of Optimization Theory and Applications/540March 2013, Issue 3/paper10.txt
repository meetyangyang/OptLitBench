The Hemivariational Inequalities for an Upper Semicontinuous Set-valued Mapping
Y. L. Zhang;Y. R. He;
Abstract
We establish some existence results for hemivariational inequalities of Stampacchia type involving an upper semicontinuous set-valued mapping on a bounded, closed and convex subset in ℝ n . We also derive a sufficient condition for the existence and boundedness of solution, without assuming boundedness of the constraint set.
Keywords
Hemivariational inequality  Clarke’s generalized gradient  Upper semicontinuous mapping  Coerciveness condition
