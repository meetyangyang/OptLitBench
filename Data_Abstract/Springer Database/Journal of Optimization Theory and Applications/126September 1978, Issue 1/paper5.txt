Fenchel's duality theorem in generalized geometric programming
E. L. Peterson;
Abstract
Fenchel's duality theorem is extended to generalized geometric programming with explicit constraints—an extension that also generalizes and strengthens Slater's version of the Kuhn-Tucker theorem.
Keywords
Fenchel's duality theorem  generalized geometric programming  convex programming  ordinary programming  Slater's constraint qualification  Kuhn-Tucker theorem
