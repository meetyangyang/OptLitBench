Controllability of nonlinear systems using a growth condition
Jerald P. Dauer;
Abstract
In this paper, we establish sufficient conditions for complete controllability of systems of the form\(\dot x = A(t)x + g(t, u)\). We assume that the inverse of the fundamental matrix solution of the homogeneous equationż=A(t)z is uniformly bounded on [t0, ∞). The criterion used is a growth condition similar to one used by LaSalle on linear control systems. Our results extend his concept of an asymptotically proper control system.
