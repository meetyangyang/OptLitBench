Approximation of Noncooperative Semi-Markov Games
A. Jaśkiewicz;A. S. Nowak;
Abstract
An approximation of a general V-ergodic semi-Markov game with Borel state space by discrete-state space strongly-ergodic games is studied. The standard expected ratio-average criterion as well as the expected time-average criterion are considered. New theorems on the existence of ∊-equilibria are given.
Keywords
Noncooperative semi-Markov games  Nash equilibria  approximation of dynamic games
