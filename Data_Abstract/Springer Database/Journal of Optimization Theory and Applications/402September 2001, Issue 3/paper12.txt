On the Geometry of Inner Approximations to the Set of Null Controllability for Constrained Linear Systems
J. P. Quinn;
Abstract
The problem of obtaining inner approximations to the set \(\mathcal{N}\) of null controllability for linear systems is considered, and the geometry of certain sets arising in the approximation of \(\mathcal{N}\) using linear feedback is explored. A two-dimensional example is worked in detail in order to delineate the limitations to the use of linear feedback.
Keywords
