Negative Answer to the Conjecture on the Convexity of the Greatest Eigenvalue
H. W. Liu;S. Y. Liu;
Abstract
In this note, we show by a counterexample that the conjecture of Seeger on the convexity of the greatest eigenvalue of a real symmetric matrix-valued function \((\alpha _{ij} (u))_{n \times n}\) does not hold.
Keywords
Convex functions  matrix-valued functions  greatest eigenvalue
