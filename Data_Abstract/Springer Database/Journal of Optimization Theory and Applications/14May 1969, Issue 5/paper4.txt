Optimal control problems with retardations and restricted phase coordinates
Sheng-Chao Huang;
Abstract
In this paper, we study necessary conditions for an optimal control problem both with and without restricted phase coordinates. The systems are specified by differential-difference equations with their allowed right-hand sides being a quasiconvex family. The multiplier rule developed by Neustadt is used, and maximum principles in integral form as well as in pointwise form are obtained.
