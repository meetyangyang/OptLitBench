Generic Existence of Solutions in Vector Optimization on Metric Spaces
A. J. Zaslavski;
Abstract

In this paper, we study a class of vector minimization problems on a complete metric space X which is identified with the corresponding complete metric space of objective functions 
\(\mathcal{A}\)
. We do not impose any compactness assumption on X. We show that, for most (in the sense of Baire category) functions 
\(F\in \mathcal{A}\)
, the corresponding vector optimization problem has a solution.

Keywords
Complete metric spaces  Generic properties  Minimal elements
