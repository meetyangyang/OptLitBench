Mosco approximation of integrands and integral functionals
J. Couvreux;C. Hess;
Abstract
It is shown that, given a lower semicontinuous convex integrandf satisfying a suitable integrability condition, there exists a sequence of Lipschitz simple integrands which Mosco converges tof and such that the sequence of conjugate integrands Mosco converges tof*. Moreover, this sequence can be chosen so that the sequence of associated integral functionals, respectively defined onL1(X) andL∞(X*), Mosco converges as well.
Keywords
Effors σ-field  Mosco convergence  Wijsman topology  approximation of integrands  approximation of integral functionals  asymptotic cones
