Relationship between solid cones and cones with bases
Z. Q. Han;
Abstract
This paper establishes a relation between Γ-based cones and solid cones in a separated locally convex topological vector space. The relation reveals that the properties of the base of a based cone affect deeply the topological properties of the dual cone.
Keywords
Vector optimization theory  topological vector spaces  convex cones  Γ-based cones  solid cones
