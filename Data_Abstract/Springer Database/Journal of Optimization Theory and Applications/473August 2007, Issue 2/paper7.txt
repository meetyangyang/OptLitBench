New Existence Result for Autonomous One-Dimensional Optimal Control Problems
P. Pedregal;J. Tiago;
Abstract

The approach to optimal control problems based on a purely variational reformulation may lead to new existence results by using fine, general existence theorems for variational problems without convexity assumptions. We illustrate this perspective here for autonomous one-dimensional problems and defer the study of more complex situations to later work.

Keywords
Variational reformulation  Existence under lack of convexity
