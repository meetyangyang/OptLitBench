Algebraic Interior and Separation on Linear Vector Spaces: Some Comments
Elham Kiyani;Majid Soleimani-damaneh;
Abstract
In this paper, some comments are given on recently published papers about algebraic (relative) interior, separation, and generalized convexity in linear vector spaces. Two drawbacks in the proofs of two essential theorems are addressed and are corrected. Also, it is shown that a condition assumed in some results is redundant and should be removed.
Keywords
Algebraic (relative) interior  Vectorial closure  Dual cone  Separation theorems
