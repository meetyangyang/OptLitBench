Simultaneous Vector Variational Inequalities and Vector Implicit Complementarity Problem
J. Fu;
Abstract
The concepts of monotone pair of mappings and vector implicit complementarity problem (VICP) in ordered Banach spaces are introduced. Existence theorems for simultaneous vector variational inequalities (VVI) and VICP are proved.
Keywords
Monotone pair of mappings  vector variational inequality  vector implicit complementarity problem  ordered Banach space
