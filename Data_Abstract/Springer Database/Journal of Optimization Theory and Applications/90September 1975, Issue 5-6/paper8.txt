Min-max control of systems approximated by simple models:L1-Type cost functionals
M. Milanese;A. Negro;
Abstract
A control problem for actual processes, modeled by simple dynamic linear systems, is studied. A min-max approach is taken, since the problem is reduced to the control of a model, whose output is affected by an input-dependent signal constrained in norm.
Keywords
Min-max controls  optimal controls  nondifferentiable functionals  two-point boundary-value problems
