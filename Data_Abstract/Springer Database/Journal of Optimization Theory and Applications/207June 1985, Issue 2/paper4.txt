Bimodal optimal design of vibrating plates using theory and methods of nondifferentiable optimization
A. Myslinski;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with an optimal design problem of vibrating plates. The optimization problem consists in maximizing the smallest eigenvalue of the elliptic eigenvalue problem describing the free plate vibration. The thickness of the plate is the variable subject to optimization. The volume of the plate is constant and the thickness of the plate is bounded.
Keywords
Optimal structural design  distributed elastic structures  multiple eigenvalues
