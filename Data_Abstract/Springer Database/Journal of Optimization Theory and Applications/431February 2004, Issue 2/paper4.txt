Degenerate Second-Order Identification Problem in Banach Spaces
M. Al Horani;A. Favini;
Abstract
We are concerned with a degenerate second-order identification problem in a Banach space. Suitable hypotheses on the operators involved are made in order to reduce the given problem to a nondegenerate problem. Some applications to partial differential equations are indicated extending well-known results in the regular case.
Keywords
Identification problems  degenerate equations  second-order equations  Banach spaces
