Strong duality for a special class of integer programs
R. R. Meyer;J. M. Fleisher;
Abstract
A pair of primal-dual integer programs is constructed for a class of problems motivated by a generalization of the concept of greatest common divisor. The primal-dual formulation is based on a number-theoretic, rather than a Lagrangian, duality property; consequently, it avoids the dualitygap common to Lagrangian duals in integer programming.
Keywords
Integer programming  duality  Euclidean algorithm  greatest common divisor  primal-dual formulations
