First-order strong variation algorithms for optimal control problems with terminal inequality constraints
E. Polak;D. Q. Mayne;
Abstract
This paper presents eight algorithms for solving optimal control problems with general constraints on the control and inequality constraints on the terminal state. These algorithms are of the differential dynamic programming type and bear a strong kinship to nonlinear programming algorithms of the feasible direction type.
Keywords
Computing methods  algorithms for optimal control  finite difference methods  differential equations  control theory
