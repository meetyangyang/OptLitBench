Weak and Strong Time Consistency in a Differential Oligopoly Game with Capital Accumulation
R. Cellini;L. Lambertini;
Abstract
We illustrate a differential oligopoly game with capital accumulation where the accumulation dynamics of productive capacity is modelled à la Ramsey. The model is solved under the open-loop information structure, to show that it admits an open-loop Nash equilibrium which is indeed a degenerate feedback one and therefore strongly time consistent, even if, by construction, the problem under consideration is not a linear state game.
Keywords
Differential games  Capital accumulation  Open-loop equilibria  Feedback equilibria
