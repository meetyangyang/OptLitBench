Initial-value methods in the theory of Fredholm integral equations: II
Michael Golberg;
Abstract
It is established that a recently developed initial-value method for solving a family of Fredholm integral equations is equivalent to an initial-value version of a classical method based on solving a set of linear equations. This enables the establishment of the convergence of the numerical method.
Keywords
