Relaxed Inexact Algorithm for Continuous Complementarity Problems on Measure Spaces
S. Y. Wu;X. Q. Yang;J. C. Yao;
Abstract
In this paper, we discuss a type of continuous complementarity problems posed over a measure space. A new relaxed inexact algorithm for the solution of these problems is introduced. Some convergence results are derived.
Keywords
Linear complementarity problems  continuous complementarity problems  inexact solutions
