Label Correcting Methods to Solve Multicriteria Shortest Path Problems
F. Guerriero;R. Musmanno;
Abstract
In this paper, we deal with the solution of the multicriteria shortest path problem. In particular, we present a class of labeling methods to generate the entire set of Pareto-optimal path-length vectors from an origin node s to all other nodes in a multicriteria network. The proposed methods are supported theoretically by the principle of optimality and they are defined on the basis of various innovative node and label selection strategies.
Keywords
Multicriteria shortest path problem  principle of optimality  Pareto optimal solutions  label correcting methods
