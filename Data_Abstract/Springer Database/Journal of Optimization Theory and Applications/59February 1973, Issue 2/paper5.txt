Solution of certain nonlinear programs involvingr-convex functions
M. Avriel;
Abstract
The solution of nonconvex nonlinear programs with sums ofr-convex functions is considered. An algorithm consisting of a sequence of approximating convex programs which converges to a Kuhn-Tucker point is described.
