Three kinds of generalized convexity
X. M. Yang;S. Y. Liu;
Abstract
This paper gives some properties of quasiconvex, strictly quasiconvex, and strongly quasiconvex functions. Relationships between them are discussed.
Keywords
Quasiconvex functions  strongly quasiconvex functions  strictly quasiconvex functions  lower semicontinuous function  convex sets
