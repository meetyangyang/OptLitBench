Discrete weighted residual methods: A sensitive nonlinear boundary-value problem
C. P. Neuman;D. I. Schonbach;
Abstract
The foundations, applications, and convergence properties of discrete weighted residual methods (DWRM's) are presented in Refs. 1–3. This paper serves to illustrate DWRM's for solving a sensitive nonlinear discrete boundary-value problem. The results indicate that DWRM's can be applied to provide models of increasing complexity which can then be utilized for the analysis and design of physical systems.
Keywords
Weighted residual methods  boundary-value problems  numerical methods  difference equations  discrete systems
