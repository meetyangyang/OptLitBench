On the Characterization of Preinvex Functions
H. Z. Luo;H. X. Wu;
Abstract
In Refs. [J. Math. Anal. Appl. 258:287–308, [2001]; J. Math. Anal. Appl. 256:229–241, [2001]], Yang and Li presented a characterization of preinvex functions and semistrictly preinvex functions under a certain set of conditions. In this note, we show that the same results or even more general ones can be obtained under weaker assumptions; we also give a characterization of strictly preinvex functions under mild conditions.
