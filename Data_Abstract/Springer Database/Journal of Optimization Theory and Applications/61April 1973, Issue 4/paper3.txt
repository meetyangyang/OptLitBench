The solution of linear equations by minimization
M. R. Hestenes;M. L. Stein;
Abstract
This paper considers the problem of solving linear equations of the formAx=b and replaces it with the construction of the minimum of the least-square function$$F(x)  =  (Ax - b)* (Ax - b)$$.
Keywords
Gradient Method  Iteration Scheme  Nonzero Vector  Group Iteration  Unique Vector
