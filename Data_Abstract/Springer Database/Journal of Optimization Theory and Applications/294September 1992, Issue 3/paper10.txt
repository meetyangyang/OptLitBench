MIMOl1-optimization with a scalar control
O. J. Staffans;
Abstract
In Ref. 1, the author showed that some scalar mixed sensitivity minimization problems have rationall1-optimal solutions and computed these solutions. Here, a similar approach is applied to a multi-input multi-output (MIMO) plant. The general flavor of the solution is the same as in Ref. 1, but there is one distinct new feature. We prove that some of the outputs may be ignored, in the sense that they have no influence on the optimal solution and that all the operators corresponding to the remaining outputs have the same optimal norm. This fact suggests alternative methods for solving the problem and it is employed in the construction of an exact rationall1-optimal solution of a particular problem.
Keywords
l1-optimal control  mixed sensitivity minimization  infinite-dimensional linear programming  finite-order compensators  discrete-time problems
