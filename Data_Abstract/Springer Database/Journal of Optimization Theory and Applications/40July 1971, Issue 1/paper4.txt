On the optimality of Contensou's singular alternating arcs in the orbital transfer problem
A. Archenti;J. P. Marec;
Abstract
Contensou's singular alternating arcs in the time-free, central-field, optimal transfer problem use a succession of infinitely small impulses, applied alternately at two different points symmetrical with respect to the major axis of the osculating orbit. It is demonstrated that, although these arcs satisfy the Pontryagin necessary condition for optimality, they do not satisfy the Kelley-Contensou supplementary necessary condition and, hence, they are not optimal.
Keywords
