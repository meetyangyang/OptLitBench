Optimal Control of the Periodic String Equation with Internal Control
C. Trenchea;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with the existence and the maximum principle for optimal control problems governed by the periodic vibrating string equation on (0, π)×(0, T) with Dirichlet boundary conditions. The case of internal controllers supported on ω⊂(0, π) is examined.
Keywords
Convex functions  weak solutions  self-adjoint operators  periodic conditions  null spaces  wave operators  internal controls
