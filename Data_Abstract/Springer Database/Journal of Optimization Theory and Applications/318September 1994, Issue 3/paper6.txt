On the closure of reachable sets for control systems
S. Perrotta;
Abstract
We prove the existence of a dense subsetD of continuous functions such that, forf∈D, the reachable set for the control system$$x_1^{(n)}  + a_{n - 1} x_1^{(n - 1)}  +  \cdot  \cdot  \cdot  + a_1 x'_1  \in \{ \phi _1 ,\phi _2 \} ,x'_2  = f(x_1 ),$$ is closed.
Keywords
Nonlinear control systems  reachable sets  bang-bang solutions
