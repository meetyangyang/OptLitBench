Moore-Penrose Inverse of a Gram Matrix and Its Nonnegativity
T. Kurmayya;K. C. Sivakumar;
Abstract
Let M=[A  a] be a matrix of order m×n, where A∈ℝ m×(n−1) and a∈ℝ m  is an m×1 vector. In this article, we derive a formula for the Moore-Penrose inverse of M * M and obtain sufficient conditions for its nonnegativity. The results presented here generalize the ones known earlier.
Keywords
