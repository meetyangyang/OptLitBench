Multitime Controllability, Observability and Bang-Bang Principle
C. Udrişte;
Abstract
Control problems for multitime first-order PDE arise in many different contexts and ways. The obstruction of complete integrability conditions (path independent curvilinear integrals) has determined the mathematicians to study such problems only in the discrete context, though thus they loose the geometrical character which is proper to the continuous approach.
Keywords
Multitime optimal control  Controllability  Observability  Bang-bang controls
