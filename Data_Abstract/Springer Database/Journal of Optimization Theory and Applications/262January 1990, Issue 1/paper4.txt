Controllability of nonlinear systems to affine manifolds
K. Balachandran;J. P. Dauer;
Abstract
Controllability to an affine manifold involves controlling a system to a target defined by the generalized boundary condition Γx=r, where Γ:C n →R n  is a bounded linear operator on the continuous functions, as defined for ordinary differential equations by Kartsatos. In this paper, sufficient conditions are obtained for such controllability for linear systems and for a class of nonlinear perturbations of linear systems.
Keywords
Controllability  affine manifolds  Leray-Schauder fixedpoint theorem  generalized boundary conditions
