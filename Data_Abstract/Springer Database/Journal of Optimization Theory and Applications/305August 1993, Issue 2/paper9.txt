Asymptotic behavior of optimal solutions to control problems for systems described by differential inclusions corresponding to partial differential equations
Z. Denkowski;S. Mortola;
Abstract
In the paper, we consider differential inclusions related to PDEs of parabolic type and some control problems with integral cost functionals associated to them. Given a sequence of such problems, we investigate first the asymptotic behavior of solution sets (mild solutions or more precisely selection-trajectory pairs) for differential inclusions, and we get some semicontinuity or continuity results (Kuratowski convergence of solution sets). Then, we prove the Γ-convergence of cost functionals, related to the above Kuratowski convergence of solution sets. Finally, applying the Buttazzo-Dal Maso abstract scheme, based on the sequential Γ-convergence, we obtain results concerning the asymptotic behavior (hence, also stability results) for optimal solutions to control problems as well as the convergence of minimal values.
Keywords
Differential inclusions  control problems  G-convergence of operators  Γ- convergence of functionals  Kuratowski convergence of sets  Kakutani-Fan fixed-point theorem
