An extended continuous Newton method
I. Diener;R. Schaback;
Abstract
This paper describes a numerical realization of an extended continuous Newton method defined by Diener. It traces a connected set of locally one-dimensional trajectories which contains all critical points of a smooth functionf:ℝ n →ℝ. The results show that the method is effectively applicable.
Keywords
Global optimization  continuous Newton method  trajectory method  multiple solutions  critical points
