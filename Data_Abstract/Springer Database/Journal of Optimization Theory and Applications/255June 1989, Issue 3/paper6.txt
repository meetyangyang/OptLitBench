Optimal scheduling of reservoir releases during flood: Deterministic optimization problem, part 1, procedure
R. Pytlak;K. Malinowski;
Abstract
In this paper, we are concerned with the optimal scheduling of water releases from retention reservoirs during flood, with the objective of minimizing flood damages at the important damage centers downstream of the reservoirs. Unlike in most other papers devoted to this subject, the flood routing equations are nonlinear. The performance index of the problem leads to a minimax optimal control problem. For this problem, the necessary optimality conditions are provided and a version of the feasible directions method is proposed.
Keywords
Nondifferentiable optimization  minimax optimal control problems  necessary optimality conditions  feasible directions methods
