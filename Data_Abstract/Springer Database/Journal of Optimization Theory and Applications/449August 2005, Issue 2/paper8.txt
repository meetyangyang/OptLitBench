On a Functional Operation Generating Convex Functions, Part 2: Algebraic Properties
P. Maréchal;
Abstract
Algebraic properties of the functional operation introduced in Part 1 of this paper (Ref. 1) are considered. In essence, the functional operation is shown to be associative, right distributive with respect to addition, and left distributive with respect to epigraphic sum.
Keywords
Convexity  Fenchel conjugacy  multiplicative potential functions
