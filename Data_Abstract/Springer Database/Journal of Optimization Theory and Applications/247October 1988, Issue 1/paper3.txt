Superlinear convergence theorem in the ABSg class of algorithms for nonlinear algebraic equations
J. Abaffy;
Abstract
In this paper, it is shown that, when two subclasses of algorithms in the ABSg family are applied to a set of nonlinear algebraic equations, then the convergence is superlinear. The conditions for the theorem to be true are essentially the same as those that apply to the Newton method.
Keywords
Nonlinear algebraic equations  conjugate direction methods  optimization  projection algorithms
