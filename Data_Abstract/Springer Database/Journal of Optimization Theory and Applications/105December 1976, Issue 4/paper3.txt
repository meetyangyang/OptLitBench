Characterization of optimality in convex programming without a constraint qualification
A. Ben-Tal;A. Ben-Israel;S. Zlobec;
Abstract
Necessary and sufficient conditions of optimality are given for convex programming problems with no constraint qualification. The optimality conditions are stated in terms of consistency or inconsistency of a family of systems of linear inequalities and cone relations.
Keywords
Convex programming  constraint qualification  optimization theorems
