Time-optimal control under state-dependent control restraints
R. J. Stern;
Abstract
We consider the control system$$\dot x = Ax + Bu,$$ subject to the state-dependent control restraint$$u(t) \in \Omega  \cap (\mathcal{H}{\text{ + }}Cx(t)\} ,$$ where Ω is a compact convex set, ℋ is a subspace, andC is a matrix. The existence of time-optimal maximal controls is proven. A natural application of this result is in solving time-optimal problems under the control restraintu(t)∈Ω and the requirement that the outputy=Sx be maintained at zero during the transfer, whereS is a given matrix. An example is provided.
