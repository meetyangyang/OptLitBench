Characterization of minimal elements in minimization problems with constraints
B. Brosowski;L. Wuytack;
Abstract
LetX be a compact Hausdorff space andC(X) be the set of all continuous functions defined onX. LetV⊂C(X), and consider the problem of minimizing supx∈XW[x,v(x)], withv∈V. The functionW is a generalized weight function and can be chosen such that certain constraints are included.
Keywords
Minimization with constraints  generalized weight function  characterization theorems  critical point  extremal signature
