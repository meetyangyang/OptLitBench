G-closure of a set of anisotropically conducting media in the two-dimensional case
K. A. Lurie;A. V. Cherkaev;
Abstract
This paper deals with the problem of the invariant description of a set of conducting materials which may be obtained from some given initial compounds by the process of mixing. Complete solution to this problem is provided for a two-dimensional case.
Keywords
G-closure  composite materials  anisotropically conducting media
