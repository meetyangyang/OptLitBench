Robustness of viability controllers under small perturbations
G. Colombo;V. Křivan;
Abstract
In this note, a perturbed control problem with state constraints depending on a parameteru is considered. Assuming that, for a certain value ofu, there exists a viability controller, we explicitly estimate the range of variations ofu for which the same controller gives viable solutions.
Keywords
Differential inclusions  state constraints  regulation maps
