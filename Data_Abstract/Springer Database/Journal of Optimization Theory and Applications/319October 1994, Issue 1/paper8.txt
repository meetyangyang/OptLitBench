Bang-bang property for Bolza problems in two dimensions
G. Crasta;B. Piccoli;
Abstract
Consider the following Bolza problem:$$\begin{gathered}  \min \int {h(x,u) dt,}  \hfill \\  \dot x = F(x) + uG(x), \hfill \\  \left| u \right| \leqslant 1,        x \in \Omega  \subset \mathbb{R}^2 , \hfill \\  x(0) = x_0 ,        x(1) = x_1 . \hfill \\ \end{gathered} $$
Keywords
Control theory  Bolza problems  bang-bang property  nonconvex optimization problems
