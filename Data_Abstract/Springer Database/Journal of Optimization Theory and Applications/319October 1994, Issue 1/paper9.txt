Asymptotic null controllability of nonlinear perturbed systems
J. P. Dauer;K. Balachandran;P. Balasubramaniam;
Abstract
In this paper, sufficient conditions are obtained for the asymptotic null controllability of the system$$\dot x(t) = g(t,x(t)) + B(t,x(t))u(t) + f(t,x(t),u(t)).$$
Keywords
Controllability  nonlinear systems  perturbations
