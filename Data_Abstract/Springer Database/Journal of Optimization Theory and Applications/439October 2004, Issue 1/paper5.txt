KKM Type Theorems and Coincidence Theorems with Applications to the Existence of Equilibria
L. J. Lin;W. P. Wan;
Abstract
In this paper, we obtain some coincidence theorems and some KKM-type theorems. We apply these results to establish the existence of the solution to generalized vector equilibrium problems, where a bimap f : X × Y → 2Z is involved and some sufficient conditions are imposed on f.
Keywords
Vector equilibrium problems  KKM-type theorems  coincidence theorems  transfer open (closed) maps  properly quasimonotone  lower(upper) inverses of multivalued maps
