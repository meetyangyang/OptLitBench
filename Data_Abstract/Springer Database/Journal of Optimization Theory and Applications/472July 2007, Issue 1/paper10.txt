Optimization Method for the Selection of the Appropriate Fuzzy Implication
B. K. Papadopoulos;G. Trasanides;A. G. Hatzimichailidis;
Abstract

In this paper, we introduce a method allowing us to choose the most suitable fuzzy implication in an inference system application. We introduce also a similarity measure, which we call degree of sameness of two fuzzy implications in an inference system application.

Keywords
Fuzzy implications  Similarity measures  Metric distances  Fuzzy sets
