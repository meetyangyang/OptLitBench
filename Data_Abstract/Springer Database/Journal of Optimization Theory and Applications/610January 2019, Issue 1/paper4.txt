On Preservation of Common Fixed Points and Coincidences Under a Homotopy of Mapping Families of Ordered Sets
Tatiana Fomenko;Dmitrii Podoprikhin;
Abstract
In the case of Banach spaces, there are theorems stating the invariance of the fixed point property of mappings under a homotopy. In this paper, we consider this problem in the case of ordered sets. As the main results, we present theorems on the preservation of the property of a family of mappings to have a common fixed point by an order homotopy.
