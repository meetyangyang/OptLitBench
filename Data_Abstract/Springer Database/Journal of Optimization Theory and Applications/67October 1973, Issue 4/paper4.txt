An initial-value method for the eigenvalue problem for differential equations with nonseparated boundary conditions
M. R. Scott;
Abstract
An initial-value technique is presented for the calculation of the characteristic lengths and characteristic functions of linear second-order ordinary differential equations subject to general nonseparated boundary conditions. Numerical examples are presented which demonstrate the efficacy of the method.
Keywords
