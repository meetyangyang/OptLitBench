Control of second-order systems at large amplitudes
E. Cumberbatch;
Abstract
Unconstrained optimal control of dynamical systems governed by second-order nonlinear differential equations is considered. Large amplitude behavior is investigated, and a control problem defined by the dominating nonlinearities is treated exactly. This asymptotic problem is reduced to a single differential equation by means of a similarity transformation, and control strategies are determined from the phase-plane behavior. The control of the Duffing equation is treated in detail.
Keywords
