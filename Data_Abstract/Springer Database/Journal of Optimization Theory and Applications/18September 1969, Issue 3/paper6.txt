On the regularization of a nonlinear ill-posed problem in Banach spaces
Massimo Furi;Alfonso Vignoli;
Abstract
In this paper, we present two theorems about the regularization of the nonlinear ill-posed problemT(x)=y, whereT is a weakly continuous mapping from a uniformly convex Banach spaceX into a normed spaceY.
Keywords
Banach Space  Continuous Mapping
