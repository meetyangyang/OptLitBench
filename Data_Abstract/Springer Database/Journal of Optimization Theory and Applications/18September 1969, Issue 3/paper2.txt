A multipoint method of third order
W. E. BosargeJr.;P. L. Falb;
Abstract
LetF be a mapping of the Banach spaceX into itself. A convergence theorem for the iterative solution ofF(x)=0 is proved for the multipoint algorithmxn+1=x n −ø(x n ), where$$\phi (x) = F\prime_x^{ - 1} \left[ {F(x) + F\lgroup {x - F\prime_x^{ - 1} F(x)} \rgroup} \right]$$ andF′x is the Frechet derivative ofF. The theorem guarantees that, under appropriate conditions onF, the multipoint sequence {x n } generated by ø converges cubically to a zero ofF. The algorithm is applied to the nonlinear Chandrasekhar integral equation$$\frac{1}{2}\omega _0 x(t)\int_0^1 {\frac{{tx(s)}}{{s + t}}ds - x(t) + 1 = 0}$$ where ω0>0. A discretization of the equations of iteration is discussed, and some numerical results are given.
