Nonlinear programming in Banach spaces
Youji Nagahisa;Yoshiyuki Sakawa;
Abstract
This paper is devoted to the formulation of generalized nonlinear programming problems in Banach spaces and to the derivation of the necessary conditions which the solutions of these problems must satisfy.
Keywords
