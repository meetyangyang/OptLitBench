A computational successive improvement scheme for adaptive optimal control processes
S. E. Dreyfus;H. L. Stalford;
Abstract
To computationally solve an adaptive optimal control problem by means of conventional dynamic programming, a backward recursion must be used with an optimal value and optimal control determined for all conceivable prior information patterns and prior control histories. Consequently, almost all problems are beyond the capability of even large computers.
Keywords
Control Problem  Control Process  Dynamic Programming  Optimal Control Problem  Prior Information
