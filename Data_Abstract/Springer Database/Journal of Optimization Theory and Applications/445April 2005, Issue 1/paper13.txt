Separate Necessary and Sufficient Conditions for the Local Minimum of a Function
L. R. Huang;
Abstract
This paper develops two necessary conditions for a local minimum of an arbitrary extended real-valued function (Theorem 2.2) and, quite separately, two conditions, each sufficient for such a function to have a strict local minimum (Theorem 2.3).
Keywords
Optimization  necessary and sufficient conditions  directional derivatives  second-order directional derivatives
