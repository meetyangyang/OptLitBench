Differentiability of Consistency Functions for DAE Systems
C. Büskens;M. Gerdts;
Abstract
In the present paper, parametric initial-value problems for differential-algebraic (DAE) systems are investigated. It is known that the initial values of DAE systems must satisfy not only the original equations in the system but also the derivatives of these equations with respect to time. Whether or not this actually imposes additional constraints on the initial values depends on the particular problem.
Keywords
Parametric DAE systems  consistent initial values  sensitivity analysis
