An explicit linear solution for the quadratic dynamic programming problem
W. R. S. Sutherland;H. Wolkowicz;V. Zeidan;
Abstract
For a given vectorx0, the sequence {xt} which optimizes the sum of discounted rewardsr(xt, xt+1), wherer is a quadratic function, is shown to be generated by a linear decision rulext+1=Sx t +R. Moreover, the coefficientsR,S are given by explicit formulas in terms of the coefficients of the reward functionr. A unique steady-state is shown to exist (except for a degenerate case), and its stability is discussed.
Keywords
Dynamic programming  discrete-time control theory  linear decision rules
