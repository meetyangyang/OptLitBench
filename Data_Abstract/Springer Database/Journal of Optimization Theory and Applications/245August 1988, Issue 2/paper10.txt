Existence of closed-loop policies for constrained discrete-time linear systems with bounded disturbances
J. B. Lasserre;
Abstract
We consider discrete-time linear systems with constraints on both control and state variables and bounded disturbances. We exhibit a closed-form expression of the necessary and sufficient conditions for existence of closed-loop policy. These conditions are linear constraints on the initial state and the bounds on the disturbances, control variables and state variables. A simple illustrative example is presented.
Keywords
Discrete-time linear systems  linear algebra  Farkas-Minkowski lemma
