Existence of a pareto equilibrium
S. Y. Wang;
Abstract
In this paper, we investigate the existence of Pareto equilibria in multicriteria games. The investigation is carried out in two ways: one follows the fixed-point technique, and the other utilizes other tools. Several sufficient conditions are presented to guarantee the existence of a Pareto equilibrium.
Keywords
Multicriteria games  Pareto equilibria  existence theorems  fixed points
