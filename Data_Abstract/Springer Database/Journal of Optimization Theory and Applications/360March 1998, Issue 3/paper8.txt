Acceleration of Superlinearly Convergent Sequences
R. Meyer;
Abstract
A method analogous to the Aitken extrapolation is proposed to accelerate the convergence of sequences of real or complex numbers with asymptotic behavior  where ei is the error of the ith element of the sequence, dj≥1 for all 1≤j≤r, and ∑dj > 1. The R-order of the resulting sequences is computed using methods which are also of independent interest.
Keywords
Convergence acceleration  root finding  R-order of convergence  nonlinear iterations
