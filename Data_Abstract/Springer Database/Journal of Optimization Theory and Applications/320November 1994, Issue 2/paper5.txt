Q-matrices and boundedness of solutions to linear complementarity problems
R. A. Danao;
Abstract
This paper is concerned with the existence and boundedness of the solutions to the linear complementarity problemw=Mz+q,w≥0,z≥0,w T z=0, for eachq∈ℝ n . It has been previously established that, ifM is copositive plus, then the solution set is nonempty and bounded for eachq∈ℝ n  iffM is aQ-matrix. This result is shown to be valid also forL2-matrices,P0-matrices, nonnegative matrices, andZ-matrices.
Keywords
