Optimal Shape Control Problem for the Navier–Stokes Equations, Part 2
B.A. Ton;
Abstract
Feedback laws for a closed-loop multicontrol inverse problem for a plane viscous nonstationary incompressible flow are established. The shape and the inflow are to be determined from partial measurements of the flow in a fixed subregion.
Keywords
Navier–Stokes equations  inverse problems  feedback laws  closed-loop multicontrol problems  shape design
