An extension of Jørgensen's differential game
D. Yeung;
Abstract
This note extends Jørgensen's exponential differential game (Ref. 1) to a more general type of game structure. In particular, the Hamiltonians of the players are not required to be linear in the state variables and the control variables may not be in exponential form.
Keywords
Differential games  open-loop strategies  closed-loop strategies
