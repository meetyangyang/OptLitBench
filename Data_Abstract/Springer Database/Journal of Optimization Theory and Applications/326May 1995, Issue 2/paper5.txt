Zero duality gap for a class of nonconvex optimization problems
D. Li;
Abstract
By an equivalent transformation using thepth power of the objective function and the constraint, a saddle point can be generated for a general class of nonconvex optimization problems. Zero duality gap is thus guaranteed when the primal-dual method is applied to the constructed equivalent form.
Keywords
Nonconvex optimization  nonlinear programming  saddle points  duality  primal-dual methods
