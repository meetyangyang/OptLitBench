Limit behavior of ellipsoids bounding attainable sets
A. I. Ovseevich;
Abstract
We study the asymptotic behavior of some ellipsoidal bounds for attainable sets of stable linear control dynamic systems. We show that, in a variety of situations, there is exactly one ellipsoid which can serve as a limit of the above bounds ast→∞. This ellipsoid is shown to be in many cases locally attractive.
