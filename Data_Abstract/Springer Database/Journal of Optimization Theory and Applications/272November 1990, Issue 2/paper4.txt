Time-axis decomposition of large-scale optimal control problems
T. A. Edmunds;J. F. Bard;
Abstract
Continuous-time optimal control problems can rarely be solved directly but have to be approximated with discrete analogues. Shorter time steps lead to more accurate approximations, but result in formulations that are often too big for computer memory. This paper presents a technique for decomposing the problem along the time axis and iterating toward a solution in a leader-follower framework.
Keywords
Optimal control  time-axis decomposition  Stackelberg games  bilevel programming
