Second-order characterizations of pseudoconvex quadratic functions
S. Schaible;
Abstract
The paper deals with strictly pseudoconvex quadratic functions on open convex sets. It presents second-order conditions in terms of an extended Hessian and in terms of bordered determinants. All of the conditions are both necessary and sufficient.
Keywords
Pseudoconvexity  quadratic functions  second-order criteria  extended Hessian  bordered determinants
