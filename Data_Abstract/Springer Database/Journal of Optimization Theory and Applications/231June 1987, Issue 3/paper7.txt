Existence of optimal controls for nonlinear systems in Banach spaces
N. S. Papageorgiou;
Abstract
Using a recent result of Castaing and Clauzure on the lower semicontinuity of integral functionals, we prove the existence of an optimal control for a broad class of nonlinear infinite-dimensional control systems. An example of a distributed parameter system is worked in detail.
Keywords
Evolution operators  optimal control  weak compactness  measurable functions  Gronwall's inequality
