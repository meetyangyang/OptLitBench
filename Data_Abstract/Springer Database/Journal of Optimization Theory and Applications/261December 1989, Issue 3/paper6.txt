On necessary and sufficient conditions in vector optimization
P. Q. Khanh;T. H. Nuong;
Abstract
It is shown that some general multiplier rules are necessary conditions for vector optimization in infinite-dimensional spaces. Under additional convexity assumptions, these conditions are sufficient. As an application, the Pontryagin maximum principle for cooperative differential games is examined.
Keywords
Pareto minima  weak minima  Slater minima  necessary and sufficient conditions  cooperative differential games  Pontryagin maximum principle
