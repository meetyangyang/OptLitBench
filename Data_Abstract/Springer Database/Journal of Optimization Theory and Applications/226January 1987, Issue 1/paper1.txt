Optimal trajectories for aeroassisted, coplanar orbital transfer
A. Miele;V. K. Basapur;W. Y. Lee;
Abstract
This paper considers both classical and minimax problems of optimal control which arise in the study of aeroassisted, coplanar orbital transfer. The maneuver considered involves the coplanar transfer from a high planetary orbit to a low planetary orbit. An example is the HEO-to-LEO transfer of a spacecraft, where HEO denotes high Earth orbit and LEO denotes low Earth orbit. In particular, HEO can be GEO, a geosynchronous Earth orbit.
