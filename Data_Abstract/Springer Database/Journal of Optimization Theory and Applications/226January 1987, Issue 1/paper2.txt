Contributions to the calculus of variations
S. J. G. Gift;
Abstract
In this paper, the theory of the calculus of variations for the simplest problem is reviewed. Simpler proofs are given for the classical conditions and a new necessary condition is presented which allows strong, necessary, and sufficient conditions to be stated for the first time.
Keywords
Calculus of variations  simplest problem  necessary conditions  sufficient conditions  strong conjugate point condition  minimum principle
