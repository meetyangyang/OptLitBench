Convergence Theorems for ℓ p -Norm Minimizers with Respect to p
K. Kido;
Abstract
Let C be a fixed compact convex subset of
\({\mathbb
R}_{++}^{n}\) and let x p  be the unique minimal ℓ p -norm element in C for any
\(p: \ 1<p<\infty\). In this paper, we study the convergence of x p  as p→ ∞ or
\(p\searrow 1\), respectively. We characterize also the limit point as the minimal element of C with respect to the lexical minimax order relation or the lexical minitotal order relation, respectively.
Keywords
ℓp-norm  minimum-norm problems  lexicographical orders  approximations
