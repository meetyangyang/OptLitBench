Separation theorems and minimax theorems for fuzzy sets
M. Takahashi;W. Takahashi;
Abstract
In this paper, we prove some theorems on fuzzy sets. We first show that, in order to demonstrate that the equality of shadows ofA andB implies the equality ofA andB, it is necessary to assume thatA andB are closed and thatSH(A)=SH(B) for any closed hyperplane hyperplaneH. We also obtain a separation theorem for two convex fuzzy sets in a Hilbert space. Finally, we investigate results relating to minimax theorems for fuzzy sets. We obtain a necessary and sufficient condition for compactness.
Keywords
Fuzzy sets  Banach spaces  Hilbert spaces  minimax theorems  separation theorems
