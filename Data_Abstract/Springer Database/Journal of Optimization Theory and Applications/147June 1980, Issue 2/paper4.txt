Optimal control systems with state variable jump discontinuities
W. M. Getz;D. H. Martin;
Abstract
Consideration is given to continuous-time, parameter-dependent optimal control problems with state-variable jump discontinuities atN variable interior times. A maximum principle involving known costate jump conditions is stated and is proved by transforming the problem into a standard Mayer control problem. An illustrative example for fisheries management is included.
Keywords
State-variable discontinuities  maximum principle  fisheries management  optimal control
