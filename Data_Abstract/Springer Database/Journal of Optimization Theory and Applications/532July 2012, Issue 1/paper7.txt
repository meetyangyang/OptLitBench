Calmness and Exact Penalization in Constrained Scalar Set-Valued Optimization
X. X. Huang;
Abstract
In this paper, we study a class of constrained scalar set-valued optimization problems, which includes scalar optimization problems with cone constraints as special cases. We introduce (local) calmness of order α for this class of constrained scalar set-valued optimization problems. We show that the (local) calmness of order α is equivalent to the existence of a (local) exact set-valued penalty map.
Keywords
Constrained scalar set-valued optimization  Calmness  Exact penalization
