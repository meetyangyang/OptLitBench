Optimum design method for four-bar function generators
W. Sun;
Abstract
This note uses the closure error function of Freudenstein's equation for a four-bar linkage to determine the initial dimensions of the links of a path-generating mechanism. The sum of the absolute values of the errors of the output angles is then taken as the cost function for optimization. Successive one-dimensional optimization in the coordinate directions is then used for the design of four-bar function generators. This method is simpler than other methods, and the work of calculation is less than for other methods.
Keywords
Function generators  four-bar function generators  closure error minimization  minimization of the sum of absolute errors
