Second-Order Degenerate Identification Differential Problems
M. Al Horani;A. Favini;A. Lorenzi;
Abstract
We are concerned with second-order degenerate identification problems related to closed operators in a Banach space. Suitable hypotheses on the involved operators are made in order to reduce the given problem to a solvable first-order problem of (weak) parabolic type.
Keywords
Degenerate second-order equations in Banach spaces  Inverse problems  Existence and uniqueness results  Applications to PDE’s
