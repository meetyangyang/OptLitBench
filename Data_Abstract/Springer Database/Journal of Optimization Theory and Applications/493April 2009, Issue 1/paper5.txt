Hölder Continuity of the Unique Solution to Quasiequilibrium Problems in Metric Spaces
L. Q. Anh;P. Q. Khanh;
Abstract
We extend and sharpen the result in Ait Mansour and Scrimali (J. Glob. Optim. 40:175–184, 2008) on the Hölder continuity of the solution sets of quasivariational inequalities in Hilbert spaces to the case of quasiequilibrium problems in metric spaces. Applications to some important problems are also provided.
Keywords
Quasiequilibrium problems  Quasivariational inequalities  Traffic network problems  Hölder continuity  Hölder monotonicity  Solution uniqueness
