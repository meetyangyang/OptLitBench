Onn-stage,m-reusableness rate production planning problems
C. J. Xiao;
Abstract
In this paper, the general solutions to the optimal policy and the limit theorem forn-stage,m-reusableness rate production planning are obtained for the problem with free terminal point. In addition, general solutions to the problem with fixed terminal point are obtained. The links between the solutions to these problems are discussed.
Keywords
Dynamic programming  linear programming  difference equations
