Identification and control in the partially known Merton portfolio selection model
T. R. Bielecki;M. Frei;
Abstract
Different aspects of the Merton two-asset portfolio selection model are studied in the case where the average rate of return for the risky asset α is not known to the investor. In particular, we investigate the question of estimation of α under arbitrary admissible allocation policy. The question of optimality of adaptive allocation policies is also discussed.
Keywords
Adaptive control  identification  partial observation  Merton model
