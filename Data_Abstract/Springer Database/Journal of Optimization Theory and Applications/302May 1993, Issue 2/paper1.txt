Continuous linear complementarity problem
E. J. Anderson;M. Aramendia;
Abstract
We investigate a form of linear complementarity problem posed over a space of measures onX, where the matrix which occurs in the finite-dimensional linear complementarity problem is replaced by a continuous functionM(x, y),x,y∈X. We give a number of conditions which ensure the existence of solutions, and we discuss the extension of Lemke's algorithm to this problem.
Keywords
Linear complementarity problems  Lemke's algorithm  infinte-dimensional programs  extreme points
