Analysis of switched linear systems in the plane, part 2: Global behavior of trajectories, controllability and attainability
K. A. Loparo;J. T. Aslanis;O. Hajek;
Abstract
The controllability and attainability properties of switched linear systems in the plane are investigated. A main result is the state-space decomposition theorem which classifies various convex regions inR2 according to their controllability properties. A preliminary investigation into the problem of determining minimum switch trajectories between two points inR2 is also presented.
Keywords
Variable structure systems  bilinear systems
