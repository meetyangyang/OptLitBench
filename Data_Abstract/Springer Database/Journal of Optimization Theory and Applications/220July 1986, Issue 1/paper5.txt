Stabilization of a class of hybrid systems arising in flexible spacecraft
S. K. Biswas;N. U. Ahmed;
Abstract
We consider the problem of rigorous modelling of flexible spacecraft and their stabilization. It is shown that the dynamics of the flexible spacecraft can be described by a coupled system of ordinary differential equations and partial differential equations (hybrid system). Lyapunov's approach is used to prove the stabilizability of the system. Simple feedback controls are suggested for stabilization of flexible spacecraft.
Keywords
Flexible spacecraft  dynamic modelling  coupled systems of ordinary and partial differential equations  system stabilization  Lyapunov's functionals  feedback control
