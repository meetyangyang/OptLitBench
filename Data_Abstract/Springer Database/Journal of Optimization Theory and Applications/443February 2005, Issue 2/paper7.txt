Essential Components of the Set of Weakly Pareto-Nash Equilibrium Points for Multiobjective Generalized Games in Two Different Topological Spaces
Z. Lin;
Abstract
In this paper, we study the existence and essential components of the set of weakly Pareto-Nash equilibrium points for multiobjective generalized games in two different uniform topological spaces. We obtain some new existence theorems. Examples show that the results are not identical in two different topological spaces.
