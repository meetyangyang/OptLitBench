Another application of Guignard's generalized Kuhn-Tucker conditions
J. W. Nieuwenhuis;
Abstract
Necessary conditions are given for a real-valued function to have a minimum, subject to a generalized inequality constraint. Under the appropriate hypotheses, the problem is demonstrated to be a special case of the type of problem to which Guignard's Kuhn-Tucker theorem can be applied.
Keywords
Banach spaces  Fréchet derivatives  convex cones  tangent cones  convex processes  linearization theorem
