Topological degree of a family of convex functions and localization of nash equilibrium points
C. Le Van;
Abstract
We define the degree of a family of convex functionsfx, wherex∈\(\bar \Omega \), and prove that, if this degree is different from zero, one has at least one Nash equilibrium point in Ω. This is a criterion of localization of such equilibrium points.
Keywords
Topological degree  Nash equilibrium points  subgradients  upper semicontinuous mappings  lower semicontinuous mappings
