Note on the nonexceptionality of Brunovský's counterexample
A. Battinelli;
Abstract
When dealing with the time-optimal problem for linear control systems, there may be a difference between optimal open-loop control and corresponding synthesized feedback control, since in the latter case one is led to allow for generalized (Filippov) solutions. In this note, it is shown that the set of two-dimensional linear control systems with a convex polyhedron as control domain, which exhibit such paradoxical behavior (completely characterized by Brunovský), has a nonempty interior, in a natural and appropriately defined topology on the space of all such linear control systems.
Keywords
Discontinuous differential equations  optimal control  Filippov solutions
