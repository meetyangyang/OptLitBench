On a modification of the Koenig theorem
L. Y. Atanassova;
Abstract
We present a modified Koenig theorem for the simultaneous determination of all distinct poles ofG(z)/F(z), whereG(z) is an analytic function inside a simple smooth closed contourC, F(z) is an analytic function inside and onC, with a known numbern of simple zeros insideC, andF(z), G(z) have no common zeros insideC. It turns out that complex and interval iterations of higher order can be constructed, and several algorithms are available for doing this. Some of them are well known and discussed in many papers.
Keywords
