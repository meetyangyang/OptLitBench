Topological existence and stability for stackelberg problems
M. B. Lignola;J. Morgan;
Abstract
The aim of this paper is to study, in a topological framework, existence and stability for the solutions to a parametrized Stackelberg problem. To this end, approximate solutions are used, more precisely, ε-solutions and strict ε-solutions. The results given are of minimal character and the standard types of constraints are considered, that is, constant constraints, constraints defined by a finite number of inequalities, and more generally constraints defined by an arbitrary multifunction.
Keywords
Stackelberg problem  multifunctions  Γ-limits  existence  stability  ε-solutions  strict ε-solutions
