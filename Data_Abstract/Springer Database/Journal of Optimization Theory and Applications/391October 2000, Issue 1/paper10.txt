Generalized Chi Square Method for the Estimation of Weights
Z. S. Zu;
Abstract
This paper presents a generalized chi square method for the estimation of weights in the analytic hierarchy process and studies its desirable properties such as invariance under transpose, harmony, and rank preservation. A convergent iterative algorithm is given and its practicality is shown by a numerical example.
Keywords
analytic hierarchy process  generalized chi square method  judgement matrix  harmony  rank preservation
