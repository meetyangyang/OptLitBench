On the convergence of the dual variables in optimal control problems
M. L. Bennati;
Abstract
The coefficients of the plant are perturbed in an abstract, linear, convex problem on a Hilbert space. Necessary and sufficient conditions are obtained about the perturbations for the continuous dependence of the adjoint variables on the coefficients of the plant. An application is given to the optimal control of ordinary differential equations.
Keywords
Hilbert spaces  convex functionals  Mosco convergence  theory of duality
