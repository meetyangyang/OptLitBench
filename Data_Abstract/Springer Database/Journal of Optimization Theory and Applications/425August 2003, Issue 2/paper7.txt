Arcwise Connected Cone-Convex Functions and Mathematical Programming
J.Y. Fu;Y.H. Wang;
Abstract
The concept of arcwise connected cone-convex functions in topological vector spaces is introduced. Optimality conditions and duality theorems for a vector-valued nonlinear programming problem involving arcwise connected cone-convex functions are discussed.
Keywords
Arcwise connected cone-convex functions  vector-valued Lagrangian  saddle points  optimality conditions
