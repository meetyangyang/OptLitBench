Weak Pareto optimality of multiobjective problems in a locally convex linear topological space
M. Minami;
Abstract
In this study, the Dubovitskii-Milyutin type optimization theory is extended to multiobjective programs in a locally convex linear topological space, producing necessary conditions for a weak Pareto optimum. In the case of an ordinary multiobjective convex program, generalized Kuhn-Tucker conditions by a subdifferential formula are necessary and sufficient for a weak Pareto optimum.
Keywords
