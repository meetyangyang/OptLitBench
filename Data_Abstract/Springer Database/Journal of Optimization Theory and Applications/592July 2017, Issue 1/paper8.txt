Mild Solution and Fractional Optimal Control of Semilinear System with Fixed Delay
Surendra Kumar;
Abstract
This paper considers fractional optimal control of a semilinear system with fixed delay in a reflexive Banach space. The existence and uniqueness of mild solution are obtained using the Weissinger’s fixed point theorem. The existence of optimal control for the system governed by fractional-order semilinear equation with fixed delay in state is presented. To show the effectiveness of the developed theory, an example is given.
