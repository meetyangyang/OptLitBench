Optimization of a class of pulse-frequency-modulated systems
R. J. Kuhler;H. H. Yeh;
Abstract
The problem of the optimal control of pulse-frequency-modulated (PFM) systems is investigated in this paper. Necessary and sufficient conditions for the linear-quadratic loss PFM case are developed. The basic problem with such conditions involves the computational implementation of the derived results. Here, sufficient conditions for the discrete-time case (DPFM) are obtained to provide ease in computation. It is shown that the conditions, which are developed under total variation constraints, can be utilized to define a simple scheme for solution of the resulting two-point boundary-value problem.
Keywords
Pulse frequency optimization  discrete control  two-point boundary-value problem
