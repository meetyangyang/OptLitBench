On a student-related optimal control problem
C. S. Lee;G. Leitmann;
Abstract
In a recent paper (Ref. 1), Cheng and Teo discussed some further extensions of a student-related optimal control problem which was originally proposed by Raggettet al. (Ref. 2) and later on modified by Parlar (Ref. 3). In this paper, we treat further extensions of the problem.
Keywords
Optimal control  mathematical modelling  study strategies
