Remark on the well-posedness of the problem of minimization of integral functionals
N. S. Papageorgiou;
Abstract
In this note, we study the well-posedness of the problem of constrained minimization of an integral functional and show that global well-posedness is equivalent to pointwise well-posedness almost everywhere. Our result extends an earlier one by Zolezzi.
Keywords
Well-posed optimization problems  measurable multifunctions  integrable selectors  normal integrands  minimizing sequences
