On the connectedness of the solution set to nonlinear complementarity systems
T. Rapcsák;
Abstract
This paper establishes sufficient conditions for the connectedness of nontrivial subsets of the solution set to nonlinear complementarity systems with twice continuously differentiable operators. In geometrical terms, the intersection of a Riemannian manifold, a pointed cone, and the level sets determined by nonlinear inequalities are studied.
Keywords
Nonlinear complementarity systems  geodesic convexity  variational inequalities  quasi-variational inequalities  image problems
