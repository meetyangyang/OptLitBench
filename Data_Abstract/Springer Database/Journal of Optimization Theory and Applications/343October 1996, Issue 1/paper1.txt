D.C. Representability of closed sets in reflexive Banach spaces and applications to optimization problems
P. T. Thach;H. Konno;
Abstract
A closed subsetM of a Hausdorff locally convex space is called d.c. representable if there are an extended-real valued lsc convex functionf and a continuous convex functionh such that$$M = \{ x \in X:f(x) - h(x) \leqslant 0\} .$$
Keywords
Generalized convexity  d.c. functions  optimization
