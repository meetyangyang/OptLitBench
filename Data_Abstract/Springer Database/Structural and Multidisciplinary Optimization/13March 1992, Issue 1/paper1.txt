Elemental reliability index-based system design for skeletal structures
D. M. Rosyid;
Abstract
A procedure is presented for the optimization of truss structures undersystem reliability constraints using anelemental reliability approach. The structure is weight-optimally designed by imposing on it a set of elemental reliability constraints. The optimum point is assumed to be on the boundary region and is reached through a recurrence relation based on anoptimality criterion.
