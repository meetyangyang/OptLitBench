Optimal structural design of a vibrating beam with periodically varying cross-section
A. Gajewski;Z. Piekarski;
Abstract
The aim of this paper is an application of the modified Pontryagin's maximum principle to the optimization problem of an elastic beam. It is assumed that a control variable (i.e. the cross-section of a beam) is to be periodic with a predeterminate period of the function.
Keywords
