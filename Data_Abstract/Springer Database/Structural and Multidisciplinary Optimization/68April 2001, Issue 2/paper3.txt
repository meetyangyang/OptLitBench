Stress ratio and compliance based methods in topology optimization – a critical review
G.I.N. Rozvany;
Abstract
Several currently popular methods of topology optimization are closely related to the classical Fully Stressed Design (FSD)/Stress Ratio (SR) or Minimum Compliance (MC)/Uniform Energy Distribution (UED) methods. The ranges of validity of the above techniques – and of recent variations on the same themes – are examined critically and possible extensions of their validity considered.
