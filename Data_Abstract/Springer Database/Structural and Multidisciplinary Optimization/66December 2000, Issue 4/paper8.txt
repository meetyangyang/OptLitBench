Optimal mean displacements of a loaded string (funicular)
S.S. Cheng;T.-T. Lu;K.-Y. Su;Y.H. Ko;
Abstract
Optimal mean displacements (such as the mean square displacements) of a loaded string funicular fixed at both ends are found for different classes of force distributions. Rearrangement inequalities and other related techniques are used to derive these optimal values.
Keywords
Key words: string, funicular, displacements, mean square
