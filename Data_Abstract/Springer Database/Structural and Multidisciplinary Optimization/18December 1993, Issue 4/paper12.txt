Optimal topology of trusses or perforated deep beams with rotational restraints at both ends
G. I. N. Rozvany;T. Lewiński;O. Sigmund;D. Gerdes;T. Birker;
Abstract
This note discusses the least-weight layout of a truss having support conditions similar to those for a clamped deep beam. Numerical solutions are presented and confirmed by analytical considerations based on Michell's optimality criteria.
