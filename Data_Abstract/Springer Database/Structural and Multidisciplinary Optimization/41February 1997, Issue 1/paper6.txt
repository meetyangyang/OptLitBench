On the optimal design of the form of hydroturbine impeller blades
V. I. Klimovich;
Abstract
When designing a hydraulic turbine, a machine of better performance characteristics and primarily of the highest possible efficiency should be created. In the process, a designer is given a numer of restrictions concerning dam height, impeller diameter, etc. The turbine efficiency can be increased by varying the impeller blade profile or the angular velocity of the impeller rotation and by some other design techniques.
