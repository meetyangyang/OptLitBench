Exact optimal grillage layouts — Part I: Combinations of free and simply supported edges
G. I. N. Rozvany;S. Liebermann;
Abstract
In the first instalment of this three-part study, a comprehensive treatment of analytically derived, exact optimal grillage layouts for combinations of simply supported and free edges is given. In part two, grillages with combinations of simply supported, clamped and free edges will be considered.
