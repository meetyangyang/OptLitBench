Optimal design of plastic annular plates of von Mises material in the range of large deflections
J. Lellep;J. Majak;
Abstract
An optimal design procedure is developed for circular and annular plates taking the post-yield behaviour of structures into account. Employing the variational methods of optimal control theory, the problem posed is transformed into a non-linear boundary value problem which is solved numerically.
Keywords
Optimal Design  Elastic Deformation  Design Procedure  Minimum Weight  Constant Thickness
