Structural control of a masonry vault
M. Brocato;A. De Domenico;F. Zanghì;
Abstract
Ancient buildings sometimes need to be supplied with reinforcing structures. Active reinforcements can be the right answer to quite complex, and widely unknown working conditions. Their design requires, in particular, the choice of a control policy and the optimal design of sensors-processors-actuators.
Keywords
Key words: brick masonry, intelligent structures, reinforcement, structural control
