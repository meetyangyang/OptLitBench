Multicriteria discrete optimization of large scale truss systems
S. Jendo;W. M. Paczkowski;
Abstract
The paper discusses optimization problems in civil engineering structural design. The following questions are discussed: continuous or discrete optimization, single- or multicriteria optimization, one- or multi-level optimization. The paper is illustrated with examples of the multi-criteria discrete optimization of large scale truss systems. The cross-sectional areas of truss bars are taken as design variables. Minimization of mass, labour cost and displacements are chosen as optimization criteria. Optimization constraints concern stresses, displacements and stability, as well as technological and computational requirements. The results of single- and multicriteria optimization of hall structures are presented in the form of diagrams and tables.
