Comments on the Authors’ Reply by T. Sokół and T. Lewiñski (2011) to a Discussion by Vazquez and Cervera (2011) on their Research Paper (Sokół and Lewiñski 2010)
George I. N. Rozvany;
Abstract
Sokół and Lewiñski (Struct Multidisc Optim 42:835–853, Sokół and Lewiñski 2010) published a paper on Michell trusses for three forces in the plane, with an application to a class of symmetric trusses. Vazquez Espi and Cervera Bravo (2011) have written a Discussion on the above paper, which was replied by Sokół and Lewiñski (2011). In the current Discussion, some comments on the above exchange are offered, because it involves some fundamental issues of topology optimization.
Keywords
Topology optimization  Trusses  Michell structures  Maxwell structures
