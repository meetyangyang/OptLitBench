Author’s reply to a discussion by Gengdong Cheng and Xiaofeng Liu of the review article “On symmetry and non-uniqueness in exact topology optimization” by George I. N. Rozvany (2011, Struct Multidisc Optim 43:297–317)
George I. N. Rozvany;
Abstract
It is explained in this reply that the author fully agrees with the writers of the discussion, because findings of similar nature were introduced already in the original article. Further counter-examples demonstrating the range of validity of the author’s propositions are also presented.
Keywords
Symmetry  Skew-symmetry  Non-uniqueness
