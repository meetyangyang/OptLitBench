Genetic algorithms and fictitious domain based approaches in shape optimization
J. Haslinger;D. Jedelský;
Abstract
The aim of this paper is twofold: on the one hand to present a new method for the numerical realization of optimal shape design problems, called a fictitious domain approach and on the other hand to describe the use of a genetic type algorithm in the above mentioned approach.
Keywords
Genetic Algorithm  Civil Engineer  Design Problem  Optimal Shape  Genetic Type
