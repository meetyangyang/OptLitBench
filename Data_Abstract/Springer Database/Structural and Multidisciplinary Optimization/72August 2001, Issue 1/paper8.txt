Rigidity of an annex building
Gy. Nagy;
Abstract
An annex building is a k×ℓ×m rectangular cube grid rod-joint framework and its joints on the base face and on the lateral face are attached by fixed joints to the planar ground and to a planar lateral wall, respectively. Inserting some diagonal braces the framework will be rigid. We give a fast algorithm for the annex building bracing problem.
Keywords
Key words: grids, space, rigidity, complexity, graphs
