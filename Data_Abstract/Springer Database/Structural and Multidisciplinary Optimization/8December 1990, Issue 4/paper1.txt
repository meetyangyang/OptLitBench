Arch shape optimization using force approximation methods
G. N. Vanderplaats;S. H. Han;
Abstract
The objective of this paper is to provide a method of optimizing areas of the members as well as the shape of both two-hinged and fixed arches. The design process includes satisfaction of combined stress constraints under the assumption that the arch ribs can be approximated by a finite number of straight members.
Keywords
Finite Element Analysis  Series Expansion  Taylor Series  Shape Optimization  Initial Structure
