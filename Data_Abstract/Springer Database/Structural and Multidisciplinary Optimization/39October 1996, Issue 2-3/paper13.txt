Global coordinative optimum design of structural systems and the performance synthesis technique
S. Chen;
Abstract
For a structural system composed of several substructures, which are designed and optimized respectively, in order to find the optimum design of the structural system automatically, it is necessary to globally coordinate the optimum designs of the substructures. The theory and methods of the global coordinative optimization of design for large scale structural systems are proposed, and applied to the optimization of satellite structural systems. A difficulty in solving the global coordinative optimization problem of structural systems is how to compute performance functions of the structural system from performance functions of its substructures, and how to compute their sensitivities. The author has developed a technique of dynamic modal synthesis and static displacement synthesis, overcoming this difficulty successfully. In this paper, the detail of the theory, methods, and the technique and its application example indicating excellent results are presented.
