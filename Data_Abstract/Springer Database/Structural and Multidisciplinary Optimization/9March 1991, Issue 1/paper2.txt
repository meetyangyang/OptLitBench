Shape optimization of an elastic thin shell under various criteria
M. Bernadou;F. J. Palma;B. Rousselet;
Abstract
The aim of this study is to propose a methodology for optimizing the shape (middle surface and thickness) of an elastic general thin shell (i) under different criteria: minimization of the weight, of the stresses, of the strain energy; (ii) with or without constraints: if any, these can be bounds on some displacements, on some stresses.
Keywords
Civil Engineer  Industrial Application  Mechanical Behaviour  Real Life  Thin Shell
