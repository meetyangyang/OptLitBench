Efficient reanalysis for topological optimization
U. Kirsch;
Abstract
An efficient reanalysis method for the topological optimization of structures is presented. The method is based on combining the computed terms of a series expansion, used as high quality basis vectors, and coefficients of a reduced basis expression. The advantage is that the efficiency of local approximations and the improved quality of global approximations are combined to obtain an effective solution procedure.
