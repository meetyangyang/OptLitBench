On a game approach to optimal structural design
V. Kobelev;
Abstract
A game problem of structural design is formulated as a problem of playing against external circumstances. The compliance plays the role of the pay-off function. Two players — the designer and nature — try to respectively minimize or maximize it, possessing limited resources. Discrete and continual optimization problems are considered.
Keywords
