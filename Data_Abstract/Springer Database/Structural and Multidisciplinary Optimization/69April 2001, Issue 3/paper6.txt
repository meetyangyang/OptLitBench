Accurate design sensitivities for closed-filled structures – application to plastic bottles

R. van Dijk;H. de Boer;F. van Keulen;
Abstract
Many efficient structural optimization algorithms require gradient information. Semi-Analytical (SA) design sensitivities are rather popular, as they combine ease of implementation with computational efficiency. For closed-filled structures also the effects of internal pressure changes and solubility of the gas in the fluid may be important. Typical examples for which these aspects are relevant can be found in packaging, e.g. edible-oil bottles. The present paper focuses on design sensitivities for such closed-filled structures. The design sensitivities are based on a refined SA formulation for the structure, whereas the contribution related to its contents is evaluated analytically.
Keywords
Key words: solubility, design sensitivities, pressure, optimization, thin-walled structures, semi-analytic
