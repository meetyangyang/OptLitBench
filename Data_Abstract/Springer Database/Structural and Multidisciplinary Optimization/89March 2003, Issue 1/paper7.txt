A note on stress-constrained truss topology optimization
M. Stolpe;K. Svanberg;
Abstract
The purpose of this brief note is to demonstrate that general-purpose optimization methods and codes should not be discarded when dealing with stress-constrained truss topology optimization. By using a disaggregated formulation of the considered problem, such methods may find also “singular optima”, without using perturbation techniques like the ε-relaxed approach.
Keywords
Key words: truss topology optimization, stress constraints, local buckling constraints
