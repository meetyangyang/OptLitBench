Mathematical modelling of processes in mechanics
A. Vásárhelyi;J. Lógó;
Abstract
Mathematical modelling processes are computed by the “path following” method from the literature. A new approximation is presented using parametrical optimization. The problems are solved as nonlinear programming problems by converting the problem from the function space into theℓ2 space.
