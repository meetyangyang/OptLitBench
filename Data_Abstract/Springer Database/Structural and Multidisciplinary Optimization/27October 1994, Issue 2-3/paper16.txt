Structural sensitivity analysis and optimization in the RIPAK system. Part 1
V. A. Zarubin;
Abstract
An approach to structural optimization within the framework of the RIPAK FEM package is presented. The first part of the paper is devoted to the description of sensitivity analysis and optimization methods in the RIPAK package. Structural mass is the goal function. A broad spectrum of constraints is considered. Both mass distribution and shape design variables are varied. The original algorithm of sequential linearization in the optimality criteria method is described. A numerical test is presented.
