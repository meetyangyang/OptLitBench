Optimal location of a circular support for nonaxisymmetric buckling of annular plates
Ü. Lepik;
Abstract
This paper deals with elastic stability of annular plates under uniform radial loads applied at its edges; emphasis is set on the analysis of nonaxisymmetric buckling forms. In order to increase the buckling strength a circular support is laid to the plate.
Keywords
Key words: optimal design, annular plate, elastic buckling, circular support, nonaxisymmetric deflections
