A confirmation of a conjecture on the existence of symmetric optimal solution under multiple loads
Xu Guo;Zongliang Du;Gengdong Cheng;
Abstract
In this note, a conjecture on the existence of symmetric optimal solution under multiple loads made in Rozvany (Struct Multidisc Optim 43:297–317, 2011, Conjecture 4) has been confirmed.
Keywords
Symmetry  Optimal solution  Multi-load case
