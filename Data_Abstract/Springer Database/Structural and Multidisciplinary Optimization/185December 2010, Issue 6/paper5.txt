Michell cantilevers constructed within a half strip. Tabulation of selected benchmark results
Cezary Graczykowski;Tomasz Lewiński;
Abstract
The paper delivers the benchmark results for the Michell cantilevers constructed within a half strip, for selected values of the σ  T /σ  C  ratio, σ  T , σ  C  being the admissible stresses in tension and compression, respectively.
Keywords
Michell structures  Minimum weight design  Topology optimization  Trusses
