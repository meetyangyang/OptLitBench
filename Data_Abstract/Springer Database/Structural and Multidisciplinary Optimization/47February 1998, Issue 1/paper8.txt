Shape optimization of dynamically loaded viscoelastic cylindrical shells
Ü. Lepik;
Abstract
Axisymmetric deflections of cylindrical shells of variable thickness are examined. The shell material is linear viscoelastic. The loading is of the impulsive type—it induces inside the shell a radial velocity field. The amount of kinetic energy is prescribed. The thickness function includes some design parameters, which must be calculated so that deflections of the beam are minimal. Only designs with a given volume are considered.
