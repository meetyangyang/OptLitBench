On the validity of ESO type methods in topology optimization
M. Zhou;G.I.N. Rozvany;
Abstract
It is shown on a simple test example that ESO’s rejection criteria may result in a highly nonoptimal design. Reasons for this failure are also discussed.
Keywords
Key words: “hard-kill” methods, evolutionary structural optimization (ESO), adaptive biological growth, compliance design, stress design, rejection criteria, inclusion criteria
