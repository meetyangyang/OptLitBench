State-Optimization Method for Order Reduction of Linear Models and of State Estimators
 Nguyen.   Ngoc San ;
Abstract
 Solution of the problem of optimal model reduction and that of optimal reduced-order state estimation are obtained by adopting a proposed state-optimization method. The method is based on the concept of nonsimilarity transformation on states. Factorization of the transformation in terms of a partial isometry enables the controllability and observability conditions of the optimal reduced versions to be expressed in optimal projection equations. The effect of the factorization is interpreted to be that of an additional constrained-condition to the reduction problems. The method is found applicable to systems of any nature with the possibility of preserving the physical significances of original modelled-states in the reduced ones  
Keywords
Order Reduction, Model, State-Estimator, State-Optimization Optimal Projection Equations
