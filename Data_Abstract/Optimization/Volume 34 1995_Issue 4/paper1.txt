Abstract
 The paper develops sufficient conditions for controllability and necessary conditions in different forms of the maximum principle for extremal solutions of discrete inclusions. The objective function is assumed to be locally lipschitzian only  
Keywords
Locally Lipschitz Function, Discrete Inclusions,  fLoca1 Controllability,  f-Extremal Solution, Maximum Principle
