Estimation of Parameters in a Particular Finite Capacity Priority Queueing Model
 C.   Muthu ; V.S.   Sampathkumar ;
Abstract
 In this paper, the maximum likelihood estimates of the parameters involved in a finite capacity priority queueing model are obtained. The precision of the maximum likelihood estimates is subsequently studied with the help of the likelihood theory for Markov processes  
Keywords
Imbedded Markov Chain, ML Estimation, Priority Queueing Model
