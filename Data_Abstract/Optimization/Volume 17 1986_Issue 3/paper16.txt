The restricted subordination problem for stationary sequences
   B.   Fritzsche ;   B.   Kirstein ;
Abstract
 This paper is a continuation of the classical work of Kolmogohov, Masani Rosenbebg and Brilliinger on the problem of subordination of stationary cross-correlated weakly stationary sequences. The problem is treated in the framework of linear filtering, Brillinger's restricted subordination problem is solved in the general case.
