A finite descent method for Steiner's problem of inpolygons with minimal circumference
   J.   Focke ;
Abstract
 A finite descent method is proposed to solve the problem of determining an inpolygon with minimal circumference. The algorithm is based on Schwabz' reflection principle and the idea of coordinate-wise descent. Four examples illustrate the efficiency of the algorithm.
