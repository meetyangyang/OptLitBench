On the minimization of quadratic functional on the set of solutions of a family of linear equations
   U.   Raitum ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with the optimal control problems for systems described by linear elliptic equations with controls appearing in second order terms and linear or quadratic cost functional. A new method for numerical approach is proposed. The method is based on the reduction of the original problem to another one and can be used in cases where a set of admissible controls has a complicate structure.
