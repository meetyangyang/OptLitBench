An objective-function ellipsoid-algorithm for convex quadraical programming
   P.   Reoht ;
Abstract
 An ellipsoid algorithm for convex, quadratical programming is given, based on the use of the objective function itself for ellipsoidal iterations. An implemented version is presented and numerical results are discussed.
