On locally-Lipschitz quasi-differentiate functions in Banach-spaces
   D.   Pallaschke ;   P.   Recht ;   R.   Urbański ;
Abstract
 In this paper quasi-differentiable functions in the sense of V. F. Demyanov and A. N. Rubinov [1] are studied. For locally Lipschitz functions defined on finite dimensional Banach-spaces, a characterization of the quasi-differentiability is given, which is closely related to the Fé-differentiability.
