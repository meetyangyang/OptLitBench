The limiting normal cone of a complementarity set in Sobolev spaces
Felix Harder;Gerd Wachsmuth;
Abstract
We investigate the limiting normal cone of the complementarity set associated with non-negative functions in the Sobolev space . By using results from homogenization theory, we provide lower estimates for this limiting normal cone. These estimates are unpleasantly large.
