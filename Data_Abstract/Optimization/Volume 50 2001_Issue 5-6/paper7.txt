A Linear optimization problem related to kronecker sums
 Uwe.   Prells ;
Abstract
 In this paper the problem is analyzed to approximate nm  complex numbers, by the sum  of components of a vector  in the 2-norm. This linear optimization problem is equivalent to solve  in the least square sense and is related to Kronecker sums. An analytical expression for  and of its Moore -Penrose inverse is given and the equation error is discussed in case of an inconsistent right-hand side  
Keywords
Finite-Dimensional Optimization, Least-Squares, Linear Approximation, Kronecker Sums
