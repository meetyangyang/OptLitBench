Optimizing of sums and products of linear fractional functions under linear constraints
   J.   Hirche ;
Abstract
 ln this paper.we consider nonconvex optimizationproblems whose objective functions are composed of parts easily to optimize on polyhedral sets. This allows to develop algorithms which take advantage of the special structure of the problems. We present a one–parametric algorithm – based on the approved simplex method – that yields a global solution to the problem of optimizing a sum or a product of two iinear iractionai functions under iinear constraints. Resuits of computationai experiments reported at the end of the paper show that our algorithm is promising in comparison with algorithms recently published.

Fractional programming, Composite Objective Functions, Charnes-Cooper Transformation, Parametrized Modified Simplex Method
