Multiplier rules for weak pareto optimization problems
   W. W.   Breckner ;   A.   Göpfert ;
Abstract
 The purpose of this paper is to obtain multiplier rules for local solutions of weak Pareto optimization problems by specializing a general multiplier rule. proved by Breckner [1] for the local solutions of weak multiobjective optimization problems in terms of K-derived convex cones. By using generalized derivatives there are constructed suitable K-derived convex cones that allow to derive readily verifiable necessary optimality conditions  
Keywords
