On Borel probability measures and noncooperative game theory
 J.B.G.   Frenk ; G.   Kassay ; V.   Protasov ;
Abstract
 In this article, the well-known minimax theorems of Wald, Ville and von Neumann are generalized under weaker topological conditions on the payoff function f and/or extended to the larger set of the Borel probability measures, instead of the set of mixed strategies. 
Keywords
Generalizations minimax theorems of von Neumann, Ville and Wald, Borel probability measures, Game theory, Mathematics Subject Classifications 2000:, 91A10, 60B05
