On controllability of convex differential inclusions in banach space  ∗  
   H. D.   Tuan ;
Abstract
 Given the convex differential inclusion (convex process, resp.)  where λ is a parameter in a first countable topological space A, we prove the stability of the set of all X for which this inclusion is locally controllable (globally controllable) in the separable Banach space X. This result includes well known results on controllability of perturbed linear systems. An application to the decomposition method in controllability problems is also considered. 
Keywords
Convex Inclusion, Convex Process, Local Controllability, Global Controllability
