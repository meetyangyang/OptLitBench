Adjacent and tangent regularity
   C.   Ursescu ;
Abstract
 In this paper we define regularity for general adjacency and tangency concepts and we study it in some particular cases. Most of the convex but sophisticated approximations used in optimization theory can be disconvexified using the regularity notion and their adjacent or tangent structure can be perceived looking at them under the regularity angle. 
Keywords
Adjacent sets, Tangent sets, Regularity
