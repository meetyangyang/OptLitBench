Zur abschläzung des nullsteuerbarkeitsbereiches nichtlinearer spsteme mir konzentrierten parametern
   A.G   Plochov ;   P   Burgmeier ;
Abstract
 For locally null-controllable autonomous nonlinear system the set of null controllability is estimated from the outside and the inside by use of Ljapunov-functions. 
Keywords
