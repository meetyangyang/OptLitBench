Zur analytischen und algorithmischen behandlung eines geometrisehem optimierungsproblems von j. steiner
   R   Klörzler ;   H   Rudolph ;
Abstract
 This article deals with the following geometrical problem of optimization. Given a convex m –gon  in the plane. Find the embedded convex domain which touches each side of  and having the smallest perimeter. The analytical and numerical investigation of this problem is effected by means of duality in optimal control and an infinite variant of the simplex algorithm. 
Keywords
