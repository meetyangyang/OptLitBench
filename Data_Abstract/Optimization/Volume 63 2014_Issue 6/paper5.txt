Online scheduling for jobs with nondecreasing release times and similar lengths on parallel machines
Rongheng Li;Xiayan Cheng;Yunxia Zhou;
Abstract
In this paper, we consider semi-online scheduling problems on parallel identical machine system which result from the online scheduling problem for jobs with arbitrary release times by knowing part information of the release times or processing sizes. The conditions are informed that the release times are nondecreasing sequence and the processing lengths are similar in  with . However, if the processing lengths are arbitrary, then no impact on the competitive ratio of the LS algorithm occurs after imposing the additional information.
