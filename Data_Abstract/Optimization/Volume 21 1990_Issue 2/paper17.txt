Linear nonstationary control systems: null-controllability with restrained controls in Barach spaces
 Vu   Ngoc Phat ; Nguyen   Khoa Son ;
Abstract
 In this paper are given new necessary and sufficient conditions for the local null-controllability of the nonstationary discrete-time systems  where X, U are infinite-dimensional Banach spaces. The proof of the main results is based on the theorem on the implicit function for the set-valued mapping. An application to non-stationary continuous-time systems with restrained controls is also given. 
Keywords
