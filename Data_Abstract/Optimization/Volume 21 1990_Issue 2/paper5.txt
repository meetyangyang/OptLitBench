On a DC-optimization-problem from statistical factor analysis
   J.   Focke ;   R.   Schulze ;
Abstract
 Howe's problem from statistical factor analysis is presented as a DC-optimization-problem and is carefully investigated. Thereby we form an always solvable closed version of this problem and give necessary optimality conditions. For numerical solution we describe a special iterative method. 
Keywords
