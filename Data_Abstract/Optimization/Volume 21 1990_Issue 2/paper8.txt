  

Abstract
 In the present paper the definitions of elementary and finite-created smooth multifunctions are introduced. These special multifunctions have fairly simple structure. It is proved the theorem about approximation in uniform metric of an arbitrary continuous convex-valued multifunction by smooth finite-created multifunctions with arbitrary exactness. Also the natural analogue of the Approximation Theorem of is obtained (see Theorem 2 and Remark 2).
