Splitting formulas for computing connectedness probability of undirected graphs
   E.   Manthei ;
Abstract
 This paper presents a general splitting formula for computing the connectedness probability (overall reliability) of connected undirected graphs. To be precise, let G split into two subgraphs H and K which are edge disjoint and meet in n common verticesU = V(H)⋂V(E). Let P(U) denote the set of all partitions of U.
