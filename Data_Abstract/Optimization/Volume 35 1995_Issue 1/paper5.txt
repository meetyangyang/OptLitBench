Maximum likelihood equilibria of random games
   P. E. M.   Borm ;   R.   Cao ;   I.   García-Jurado ;
Abstract
 In this paper we introduce the concept of random game in order to incorporate the possible random structure of a game in an explicit way. Two definitions of maximum likelihood Nash equlilbriul (mline) are given depending on the random structure of the game. Existence theorems of MLINE are proved in both setups  
Keywords
Game Theory, Nash Equilibrium, Random Game
