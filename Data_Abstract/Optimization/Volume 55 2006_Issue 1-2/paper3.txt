Smoothness of the costate and the target in the time and norm optimal problems
 H. O.   Fattorini ;
Abstract
 We show that, in the setting of general semigroups there is in general no relation between smoothness of the target and smoothness of the costate for time and norm optimal controls. However, there exist relations in particular cases, such as self adjoint and unitary semigroups.   
Keywords
