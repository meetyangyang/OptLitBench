Nonlinear impulsive integro-differential equations of mixed type and optimal controls
 W.   Wei ; X.   Xiang ; Y.   Peng ;
Abstract
 In this article, nonlinear impulsive integro-differential equations of mixed type on Banach spaces are considered. Existence of PC-mild solutions is proved. Existence of optimal controls of systems governed by impulsive integro-differential equations of mixed type is also presented. An example is given for demonstration.
