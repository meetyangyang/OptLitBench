Dual simplex algorithm with implicit representation of variable upper bounds
   W.   Ogryczak ;
Abstract
 In this paper we develop theoretical background for implementation of the dual simplex method with implicit representation of Variable Upper Bounds (VUBs). More precisely, we show how the standard techniques of the dual SUB algorithm can be extended to deal with VUB inequalities. The main simplex operations connected with pivoting are presented as ready-to-implement tables of exchange rules and basis transformations  
Keywords
Linear programming, Simplex method, Variable upper bounds, Simple upper bounds
