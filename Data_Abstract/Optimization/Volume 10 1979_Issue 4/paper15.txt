Ein lagerhaltungsmodell mit kontingentierten liefermengen
 Heinz-Uwe   Küenle ; Lutz   Walter ;
Abstract
 A dynamic inventory model with random demands is considered. Purchase costs and convex holding and shortage costs are assumed. The planning horizon is divided in intervals of the same length, and the cumulative stock on order is bounded by a constant R in every of these intervals. It is shown that a modification of an (S S) policy is optimal by these assumptions.
