Availability analysis of 1-server 2-unit system with slow switch. subject to maintenance
   M.N.   Gopalan ; Kalpana Y.   Marathe ;
Abstract
 This paper deals with the availability of 1-server 2-unit system with slow switch. The switch is taken up for maintenance after completing r (fixed number) switching operations. Explicit expression for the Laplace transform of the mean up-time of the system during (0t) is obtained. The steady-state availability to the system is discussed, xne case when the switch-over is instantaneous is also considered.
