  

Abstract
 Games in normal form with a finite set of players J and with a family of admissible coalitions  are considered. It is shown that the set of projection of -equilibrium points on the outcome set of the given game in each simple meta-game coincides with the core of a suitable cooperative game. The sufficient conditions of the existence of -meta-equilibrium points and symmetric -meta-equilibrium points are received by applying suitable results on the core in cooperative games if  is a quasipartition of J.
