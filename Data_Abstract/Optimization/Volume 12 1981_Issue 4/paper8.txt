Generalized regenerative processes with embedded regeneration periods and their application
   S.   Jolkof ;   V.   Rykov ;
Abstract
 The present paper deals with the class of regenerative processes. Thereby the ease is investigated that on its regeneration periods the considered process can again be seen as a regenerative process. In connection with this the notions of the decomposable regenerative Process and of the embedded regenerative process have been introduced. An example is given of application to priority systems of queueing theory.
