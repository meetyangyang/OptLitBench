Optimalitätsbedingungen fü ein parabolisehes randsteuerproblem mit steuer- und zustaridsbeschränkungen
 Fredi   Tröltzsch ;
Abstract
 In this paper an optimal control problem for the heat equation with constrains on control and state is investigated. The state constraint is an upper bound for the temparature gradient within the heated solid. A minimum principle and bang-bang-type properties for an optimal control are proved.
