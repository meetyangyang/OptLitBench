A mathematical programming approach to Strassen's theorem on distributions with given marginals
 Wolf-Rüdiger   Heilmann ;
Abstract
 Recently, it has been shown that V. Strassen's result on probability measures with given marginals can be derived via usual linear programming in finite subsets of the underlying metric space and a subsequent approximation procedure. In the present note, we indicate hew to attack the problem directly by linear programming in normed vector spaces.
