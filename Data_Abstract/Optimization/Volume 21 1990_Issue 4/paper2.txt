The use of metric graphical regularity in approximate subdifferential calculus rules in finite dimensions
   A.   Jourani ;   L.   Thibault ;
Abstract
 We apply our notion of metric graphical regularity in the study of the approximate subdifferential of a composition function defined on a finite dimensional space. 
Keywords
Approximate subdifferential, approximate coderivative, metric graphical regularityAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary:49 D 49, Secondary:90 C 30
