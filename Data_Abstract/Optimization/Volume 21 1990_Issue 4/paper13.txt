On the lower semicontinuity of the feasible region of nonlinear mixed-integer parametric programming
 Sira   Allende Alonso ;
Abstract
 We device sufficient conditions under which the constraint set mapping corresponding to a certain class of mixed-integer nonlinear parametric optimization problems is lower semicontinuous in Berge's sense.  
Keywords
