Operations on convergent families of sets and functions
   D.   Azé ;   J.P.   Penot ;
Abstract
 The lower limit of the images by a convex set-valued mapping of a family of convex sets is studied. Topological and metric results are given. Applications to variational convergence, in particular to epi-convergence of convex functions, are presented. 
Keywords
Kuratowski-Painlevé limits, convex set-valued maps, epieonvergence, Hausdorff metricAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary:49 A 50, Secondary:52 A 50
