General shock models with random threshold
   V.   Thangaraj ;   A.D.J.   Stanley ;
Abstract
 A general shock model associated with a correlated pair (X  n ,Y  n ) of renewal sequences is considered. The system fails when the magnitude of the shock exceeds a random threshold Zfollowing exponential law. The distribution of the system failure time T  Z is found and first two moments of T  Z are derived. A class of correlated cumulative shock models is also studied. As an application stochastic clearing system is studied in detail. 
Keywords
