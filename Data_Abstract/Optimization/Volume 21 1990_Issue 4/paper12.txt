On decomposition in problem of Markov chain optimal control calculation by means of linear programming
   V.G.   Sragovich ;
Abstract
 It is shown the possibility of decomposition in the problem of linear optimization for finding an optimal control of a Markov chain.of general form. The decomposition is based on the unique partition of the Markov chain into a sum of connected components and an inessential set, A corresponding control problem of lower dimension is considered for every summary. The obtained gain with regard to the number of necessary operations is estimated.
