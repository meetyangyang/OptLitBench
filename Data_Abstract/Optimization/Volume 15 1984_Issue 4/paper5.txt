Zum Satz von Tucker bei linearen optimalproblemen in halbgeordneten vektorraumen
   D.   Stolp ;
Abstract
 A generalization of the notion of Tucker solutions in infinite dimensional linear spaces using generating points of complementary cones is given. The existence of generating points is discussed, and a generalization of Tucker's complementary slackness theorem for linear programming problems in infinite-dimensional linear spaces is proved.
