A New Abb Theorem In Banach Spaces
 F.   Ferro ;
Abstract
 We give a new version of the Arrow, Barankin, and Blackwell theorem. Let X be a Banach space partially ordered through a closed, convex, and pointed cone C . We prove hat, if C is supposed to have a weakly compact base, the minimal points of a closed and onvex set S in X are in the strong closure of the minimum points of the linear continuous functionals which are strictly positive in C\{O}. We provide two proofs of this result; each of them, with only slight and canonical changes, furnishes as well the known ABB theorems in general normed spaces in a new unifying fashion. The first proof is based on the geometric property of Banach spaces expressed in the so-called drop theorem  
Keywords
Strictly positive supporting functionals, Vector optimization, The Arrow, Barankin, Blackwell theorem
