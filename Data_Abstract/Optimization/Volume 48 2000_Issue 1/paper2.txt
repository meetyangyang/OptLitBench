Duality theorems on an infinite network  *  
   W   Oettli ;   M   Yamasaki ;
Abstract
 We study conjugate duality for optimization problems on an infinite, but locally finite network with countable node set X and countable are set Y In contrast to earlier approaches we do not employ Hilbert or Banach space methods. Instead we work in the spaces RX and RY which are siven their Droduct toDolosv, As an application we obtain generalizations of some basic inverse relations from discrete potential theory  
Keywords
Locally Finite Networks, Conjugate Functions, Duality, Discrete Potential Theory
