Existence of nash equilibria and saddle points in infinite-dimensional spaces
   M.   Beatrice Lignola ;
Abstract
 In this paper a generalization of Ky Fan inequality is proved. As a by-product of this inequality, one obtains a new K.K.M. theorem for multifunctions whose values are not closed and new existence results for Nash equilibria and saddle points in infinitedimensional spaces  
Keywords
R-limits, Nash Equilibriumpoints, Saddlepoints, Ky Fan Inequalities, Existence Theorems
