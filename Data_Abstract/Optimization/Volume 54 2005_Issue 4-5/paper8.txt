First-order approximations of multivalued mappings and directional differentiability of maximum functions
 L.   Minchenko ; A.   Tarakanov ;
Abstract
 In the article we study directional differentiability of maximum functions generated by multivalued mappings. We introduce a new class of Γ-regular multivalued mappings that generalizes the class of mappings, which admit first-order approximation. We also prove directional differentiability of the maximum functions defined on the values of Γ-regular multivalued mappings. 
Keywords
Multivalued mapping, Maximum function, Directional differentiability, Mathematics Subject Classifications 2000: 41A50, 49K40, 90C31
