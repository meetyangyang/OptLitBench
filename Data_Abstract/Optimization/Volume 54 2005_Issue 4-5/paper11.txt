Continuous generalized convex functions and their characterizations
 X. Q.   Yang ;
Abstract
 The notions of continuous (strictly) pseudoconvex functions and set-valued (strictly) pseudomonotone maps are introduced. Relations between these notions are given using an asymptotic mean value theorem. Characterizations of a continuous quasiconvex function are derived. 
Keywords
Quasiconvex function, Pseudoconvex function, Pseudomonotone map, Mean value theorem, 1991 Mathematics Subject Classifications: 26A51, 47H04
