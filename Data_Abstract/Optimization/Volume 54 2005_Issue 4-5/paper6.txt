Identification of point sets by quasidifferentiable functions
 Anja   Herklotz ; Bernd   Luderer ;
Abstract
 The aim of this article is to realize some ideas of Demyanov (2001) by means of numeric algorithms designed for quasidifferentiable functions. 
Keywords
Quasidifferential calculus, Identification of point sets, Numerical algorithms, 2000 Mathematics Subject Classifications: 90C30, 49J52, 49M37, 65K05
