Transient behaviour of a simple queue with discouraged arrivals
   O.P.   Sharma ;   M.V.R.   Maheswar ;
Abstract
 The classical problem of single server Markovian queue with state dependent parameters is studied. Closed form computable transient state probabilities have been obtained for finite waiting space which can be easily extended to infinite waiting space model  
Keywords
State Dependent, Parameters, Positive Definite, Tridiagonal Matrix, Eigen Values, Numerical Computations
