Well-solved cases of the 2-peripatetic salesman problem
 M.J.D.   De brey ;   A.   Volgenant ;
Abstract
 The 2-peripatetic Salesman Problem (2-PSP for short) is the problem. where 2 edge-disjoint Hamiltonian cycles of minimal to tallength are required. We have investigated whether the 2-PSP, either the sum version or the bottleneck version can also be wellsolved in known well-solved cases of the TSP.  We show how to polynomially solve the 2-PSP in the following non-trivial cases:  — Problems on Small matrices with distinct values for the sum as wellx as for the bottleneck criterion;  — Problems on asymmetric Circulant matrices with a prime number of cities again for both criteria;  — Problems with the bottleneck criterion on max-distribution matrices with the number of negative a-values equal to the number of nonnegative b-values.  
Keywords
Traveling Salesman Problem, Peripatetic Salesman Problem, Edge Disjointness, Well-solved Cases
