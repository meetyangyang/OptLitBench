Variational-like inequalities
   M.   Aslam Noor ;
Abstract
 In this paper we prove that the minimum of non differentiable preinvex function on the invex set can be charaterized by a class of variational inequalities called variational-like inequalities Using the auxiliary principle technique we prove the existence of a unique solution of general variational-like inequality and suggest a novel iterative algorithm Several special cases are also discussed 
Keywords
Preinvex Function, Invex Sets, Variational-Like Inequalities, Auxiliary Principle, Iterative Algorithm
