On helly's theorem for functions of several variables and its applications to variational problems  ∗  
   D.   Idczak ;   S.   Walczak ;
Abstract
 In this paper prove Helly's principle of choice for functions of two variables. This theroem is applied to the investigation of some variational problems. An application is given to a gas absorption problem  
Keywords
Helly's theorem, Variational problems, Optimal control
