Analysis of M|M|2|N queue with correlated servers
   O. P.   Sharma ;   M. V. R.   Maheswar ;
Abstract
 A computable matrix approach is developed to study a correlated two server Markovian queue with finite waiting space capacity. Steady state oribabilities have been worked out analytically and a procedure is given to obtain the transient probabilities numerically. The waiting time distribution for steady state has also been derived  
Keywords
Correlation, Matrix Approach, Laplace Transforms, Difference Equations, Eigen Roots, Polynomial
