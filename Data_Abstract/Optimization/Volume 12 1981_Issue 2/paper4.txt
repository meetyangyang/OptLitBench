 R n-optimierungsprobleme und R mxR n-transformationen
   E.A.   Pforr ;   R.H.   Günther ;
Abstract
 To each optimization problem B (of the R  n ) is associated an optimization problem T(B) (of the R  m ) by means of an transformation T (from R  m  into R  n ). The investigations have the aim to find relations between convex and non convex optimization problems. From this point of view the CREMONA-transformations play an essential role.
