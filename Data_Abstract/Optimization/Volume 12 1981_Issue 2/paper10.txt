On characterizing properties of the value sets and the equilibrium point sets of non-cooperative two-person games
   M.J.M.   Jansen ;   S.H.   Tijs ;
Abstract
 In this paper, for certain subfamilies of the family of bounded measurable two-person games in normal form, the value sets are characterized by three properties, called the maximum property, the minimum property and the adjunction property. Furthermore, one of the papers of Vilkas is critically discussed. Finally, for bimatrix games, two systems of characterizing properties for the equilibrium point sets are given.
