On 3xn sequencing problem involving equivalent-job for a job-block with transportation-times of jobs
   P.L.   Maggu ; Ghanshiam   Das ; Mahabir   Singh ;   M.L.   Singhal ;
Abstract
 This paper deals with a 3 xn (three-machine n-job) sequencing problem in which (i) transportation times of jobs from one machine to a subsequent machine and (iii) Equivalent-jobs for job-blocks are assumed to occur. The object of this paper is to obtain a schedule rule minimizing the total elapsed time.
