Measuring initiative and attraction by means of deviations from the shapley value
   T.   Radzik ;   A.   Wieczorek ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with a modification of the Shapley value in which the process of coalition formation is not necessarily uniform (as in the case of usual value) hut it depends on individual initiative and attraction towards already existing coalitions. The value is given an axiomatic treatment and methods are proposed how to measure the indicators of initiative and attraction of individuals in a given population by comparison of the expected and actual outcomes in a sequence of games played within this population. We present an example of calculations based on results of an experiment  
Keywords
Shapley value, measuring initiative and Attraction
