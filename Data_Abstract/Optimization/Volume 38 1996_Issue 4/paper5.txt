Abstract
 In tile last years a weak version of a maximum principle for control problems with multiple integrals was developed ([KP]). This maximum principle holds without restricting assumptions, however a numerical use of this principle quite difficult since in one of its two formulations the adjoint multipliers y are measures, and in the other formulation the maximum condition holds in the so-called integral form only ([P2]). In this paper we are able to verify a pointwise maximum condition with multipliers , for a subclass of problems
