A Note on roughly convex functions
 Helga.   Hartwig ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with several kinds of roughly. globally and y-convex functions on the real line. In particular. an interesting property of roughly convex functions in the sense of Klötzler (it suffices to consider points with a distance equal to the roughness degree) is verified, a sharpened y-convexity condition (which is satisfied by Favard’s “fonction penetrante“) is introduced, and some continuous counterexamples are glven  
Keywords
Generalized convexities, rough convexity, global convexity,  y-convexity A Note on roughly convex functions
