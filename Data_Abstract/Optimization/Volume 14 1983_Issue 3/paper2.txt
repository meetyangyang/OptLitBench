 Reinhard   Mandel ;
Abstract
 For classes of (mixed-) integer optimization problems of the form    where  are polynomials with national coefficients, the following questions are considered:  1. Is there an algorithm for deciding if an arbitrary problem of the considered class has a solution?  2. Is there an algorithm for determining a solution of an arbitrary problem of the considered class, which has a solution?  Classes are presented for which the answer to question 1 or 2 is positive.
