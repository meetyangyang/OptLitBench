Ein erweitertes effizienztheorerm
   J.   Ester ;   B.   Schwartz ;
Abstract
 A parametric problem is associated the classic multiobjective optimization problem and a generalized efficiency theorem is proved under rather weak conditions. Neither convexity nor continuity of the functions involved are required. Based on this generalized efficiency theorem an algorithm is designed and is applied to a nonconvex problem.
