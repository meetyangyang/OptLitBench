On statistical control of optimization
   K.A.   Ariyawansa ;   J.G.C.   Templetoh ;
Abstract
 We consider the general, deterministic, finite-dimensional Mathematical Programming Problem. There is no numerical procedure which is guaranteed to find the global solution to this problem. We examine in this paper a group of probabilistic approaches to this problem which may be termed Statistical Control of Optimization. In these approaches a global solution to the deterministic Mathematical Programming Problem is given a probabilistic characterization by considering a suitable Statistical Inference Problem. We examine in detail the generation of these Statistical Inference Problems together with the requirements on solutions to the Statistical Inference Problems.
