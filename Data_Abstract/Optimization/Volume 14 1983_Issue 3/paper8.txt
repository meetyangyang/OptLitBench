Abstract
 We are concerned in the vector optimization problem how to characterize the minimum of a set V. First we this result. Only relative interior- points of the cone K are needed. Then we sharpen this result by the concept of stepwise Bayes-rules. In finite dimensional spaces L this characterization is an equation.In the case of a polynomial cone KWe get a slightly nicer result. We give an Example of a prescription how to choose a minimal point from a compact set V, if you do not know about situation ill and the prescription is an order how to act in this situation).
