On an approach to the estimation of the state-variable descriptive parameters for linear continuous-time models
   N. N.   San ;
Abstract
 The concept of a state-optimisation is presently made available and is used in an approach proposing to the estimate of parameters for linear, continuous–time models in the state–variable space. The concept of a partial isometry is utilised for obtaining an additionally constrained condition to the optimal problem for parameter estimation. The estimation process is found to be successfully carried out with any form of the system input. The approach is also used for treating a robustness of the problem of parameter estimation  
Keywords
State-Optimization, Parameter Estimation, Robustness
