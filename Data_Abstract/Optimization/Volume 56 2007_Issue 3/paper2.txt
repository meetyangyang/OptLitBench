Monotonic analysis over ordered topological vector spaces: I
 H.   Mohebi ; H.   Sadeghi ;
Abstract
 The theory of increasing and positively homogeneous (IPH) functions defined on a convex cone in a topological vector space X, is well developed. In this article, we present a suitable extension of this theory for IPH functions defined on the whole of the space X. We also examine important notions of abstract convex analysis such as subdifferential and polarity of these functions and sets.
