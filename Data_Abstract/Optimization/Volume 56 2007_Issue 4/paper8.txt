An interior-point method for minimizing convex functions on the convex hull of a point set
 Josef   Stoer ; Nikolai D.   Botkin ; Oleg A.   Pykhteev ;
Abstract
 The article describes an interior-point method for minimizing a smooth strictly convex function f: Rn  → R,  , on the convex hull P of m points in Rn . The algorithm uses barycentric coordinates for representing points in P and generates points in P with positive coordinates. In particular, the algorithm can be used to compute the orthogonal projection of a point xc∈ Rn   to P.
