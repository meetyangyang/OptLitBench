A new algorithm for minimax and l 1-norm optimization
 Ekaterina A.   Kostina ; Svetlana V.   Prischepova ;
Abstract
 Minimax and L 1-norm optimization problems are well-known in nonlinear programming. These problems are widely applied in different areas of science, technology and economy. This paper presents a new algorithm for the solution of these problems. The basic constructions of the algorithm are theoretically justified and proved. The results of numerical experiments are also included in the paper  
Keywords
Nonlinear Programming, Nonsmooth and Smooth Support, Support Feasible Solution, Test Examples
