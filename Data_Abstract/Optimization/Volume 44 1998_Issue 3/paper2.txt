A quadratically convergent scaling newton’s method for nonlinear programming problems
 Zhenghua.   Lin ; Daicai.   Song ;
Abstract
 Similar to [6], we present a Q-quadratically convergent scaling Newton's method for nonlinear programming problems. At each iteration, the multiplier variables and slack variable generated by this algorithm all keep in positive orthant of Rm, we only compute a Newton step and determine the step length with at most 2 m division operations, where m is the number of constraints. In Section 4. three examples also support the Q-quadratic convergence  
Keywords
Interior Point Algorithm, Nonlinear Programming Problem,  Q-Quadratic Convergence, Scaling Newton's Method
