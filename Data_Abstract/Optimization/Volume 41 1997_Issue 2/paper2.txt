Abstract
 We establish the Kuhn-Tucker sufficient optimality conditions for a class of generalized minimax fractional programming involving pseudoconvex and quasiconvex functions. Subsequently, these optimality criteria are utilized as a basis for constructing three dual models and certain duality results have been derived in the framework of generalized convex functions  
Keywords
Fractional Minimax Programming, Pseudoconvex, Quasiconvex, Duality
