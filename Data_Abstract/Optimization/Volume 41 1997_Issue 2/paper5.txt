Multivariate Optimal Replacement Policies for a System subject to Shocks
   V.   Thangaraj ; Revathy.   Sundararajan ;
Abstract
 A system subject to shocks is considered. Some multivariate replacement policies based on number of shocks, cumulative (or maximum of) damage caused due to shocks and the working age of the system are proposed and optimal replacement policies based on long-run average cost are obtained  
Keywords
Optimal Replacement Policies, Geometric Process, Renewal Reward Theorem
