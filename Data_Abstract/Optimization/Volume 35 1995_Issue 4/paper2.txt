On the solution of determinantal systems of linear inequalities
   F.   Werner ;
Abstract
 In this paper an algorithm of checking determinantal systems of linear algebraic inequalities for compati-bility is presented. It is based on solving a sequence of systems of linear algebraic equations constructed on the principle of bordering. The time complexity of the proposed algorithm coincides with the complexity of solving a system of linear equations of the same dimension.  
Keywords
Systems of Inequalities, Systems of Equations
