Increasing maps
   F.   Beccacece ;   M.   Cigola ;
Abstract
 In this note a concept of monotonicity for maps from ℜ n  into ℜ m  is introduced. It can be seen as an extension of monotonicity for maps from ℜ n  into ℜ n . Moreover, a first-order characterization in the differentiable case is provided and the relationships between increasing maps and componentwise increas-ing ones are studied  
Keywords
Monotonicity, Increasing Maps, Componentwise Increasing Maps, First-Order Characterization, Differentiable Case
