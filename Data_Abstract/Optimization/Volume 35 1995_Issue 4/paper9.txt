Time discretization of a setvalued stochastic dynamic system
   H.   Fritsch ;
Abstract
 For a controlled stochastic dynamic system with a setvalued drift coefficient a time discretization is considered. The existence of e-optimal controls for the continuous original problem is shown and a discrete version of a minimum principle is derived for the discretized setvalued control problem.  
Keywords
Controlled Stochastic Differential Inclusion, Time Discretization, Discrete Minimum Principle, ε-optimal control
