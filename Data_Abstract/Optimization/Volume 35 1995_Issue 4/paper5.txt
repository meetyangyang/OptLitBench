Extremal solutions of some constrained control problems ∗  
   H. X.   Phu ;   H. G.   Bock ;   J. P.   Schölder ;
Abstract
 In this paper, a class of absolutely continuous functions with fixed end points and restrictions to their value and their derivative is considered. In particular, the upper and lower bounds of these functions are determined. They are solutions of some optimal control problems with state constraints. The result is applied to some practical problems: optimal control of hydroelectric power plants, inventory problems, and manipulator trajectory planning.  
Keywords
Optimal Control, State Constraints, Extremal Solutions, Hydroelectric Power Plants, Inventory Problems, Robotics, Manipulators, Trajectory Planning
