Necessary optimality conditions with a modified subdifferential
   X.Q.   Yang ;   B.D.   Craven ;
Abstract
 The concepts of the τ-subgradient and τ-subdifferential are introduced for local Lipschitz functions. It is proved that the generalized invex functions have a nonempty τ-subdifferential. The generalized directional derivate related to the τ-subdifferential is given. The necessary conditions of inequality constrained problems are clarified by the τ-subdifferential.
