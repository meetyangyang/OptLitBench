A single unit cumulative damage model with correlated damage and repair time
   A.   Subramanian ;   N.   Eagambaram ;
Abstract
 In this paper we study the correlation between the life time and the repair time of a device which is subjected to a damage process. The correlation is obtained in section 2 when there is a repair facility which repairs the damages one at a time. In section 3 the marginal down time distribution is obtained when there exists a repair facility for every damage (i.e. damages are repaired simultaneously).
