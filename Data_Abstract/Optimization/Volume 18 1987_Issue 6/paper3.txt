Convexity and closedness of sets with respect to cones
 Dinh   The Luc ;
Abstract
 The purpose of this paper is to investigate the cone-convexity, cone-compactness and cone-closedness of sets in finite dimensional spaces. Some sufficient conditions for the sum of sets to be closed or compact are provided.  
Keywords
Cone-convexity, cone-closedness, cone-compactness, direction of recessionAMS Subject Classifications: Primary: 52 A 01, Secondary: 09 C 31
