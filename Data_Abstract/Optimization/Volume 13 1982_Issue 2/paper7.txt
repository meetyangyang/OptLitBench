Untersuchungen über eine günstige verzweigungsreihenfolge der variablen bei branch and bound-verfahren
 Frank   Körner ;
Abstract
 A branch and bound algorithm for the nonlinear integer programming problem is investigated. A condition in which order the variables should be ramified to make the number of knots being studied to a minimum is given. The results obtained are discussed for the knapsack problem.
