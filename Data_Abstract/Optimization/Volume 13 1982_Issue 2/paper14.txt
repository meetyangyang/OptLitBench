Regenerative processes with Markov-dependent cycles and their applications
   G.P.   Klimov ;   R.   Nagapetjan ;   S N.   Smirnov ;
Abstract
 There exist many papers about semi-Markov processes. The paper of E.Çinlar [1] can be offered as a good introduction into this problem. There is a detailed bibliography in the survey of Koroljuk, Brody, Turbin [2], in the book of Koroljuk and Turbin [3]; see also J. Teugles [4]. This paper presents a brief outline of the subject and a number of sufficient conditions for ergodicity, convenient for applications. The M/GI/1 type queueing system with batch processing is considered as an example, and the limiting distributions of some characteristics are derived.
