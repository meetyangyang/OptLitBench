A GI/M/1 queue with rest periods Jacob
 Jacob K.   Daniel ;   A.   Krishnamoorthy ;
Abstract
 In this paper we consider a single server queueing model in which the interarrival times have a general distribution and service times are exponentially distributed. The server takes rest for a random duration when the system is empty or when the service of k units are over, whichever occurs first. Using the Imbedded Markov chain, the stationary behaviour of the process is studied. We investigate the waiting time distribution of unit in the system. Some numerical examples are also given.
