Sensitivitätsanalysen in entscheidungsmodellen
   U.D.   Holzbaur ;
Abstract
 For the decision models considered in [6], we give a sensitivity analysis and show that it is possible to formulate and to solve these models over an ordered field with an infinitesimal element. The evaluation of Blackwell optimal politics in Markovian decision models is a special case of the investigations.
