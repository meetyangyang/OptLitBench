Optimality conditions for a class of nondifferentiable minmax programming problems
   G.J.   Zalmai ;
Abstract
 In this paper, utilizing a minmax theorem due to Sion, we establish necessary and sufficient optimality criteria for a class of nondifferentiable minmax programming problems with generalized convexity conditions. Applications of these results to some fractional minmax problems and to a particular type of nonlinear nondifferentiable programming problem are discussed.
