Sufficient conditions for ε-minimum
   D.   Zagrodny ;
Abstract
 The paper contains sufficient conditions for a point x ∈ U, function f X→ R ∪ ∪ {-∞,∞}, and ε≧ 0 to hold the inequality f(x) ≦ inf f=ε on the subset U of a topological vector space X.
