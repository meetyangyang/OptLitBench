  

Abstract
 This paper focuses on the problem of optimal control of ecological systems with reproduction and age dependence. The model is a, boundary-value problem for divergency partial differential equations with operator coefficients and nonlocal boundary conditions. Necessary optimality conditions are established in terms of the maximum principle.
