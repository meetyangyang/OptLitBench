On the differentiability properties of the infimal regularisation of a prox-regular function
 A.   Eberhard ;
Abstract
 A short proof of the fact that the infimal convolution of a prox-regular function possesses the C 1,1 property is provided. The approach here is based on techniques from generalised convexity and thus avoids the use of maximal monotone operator theory as used is in (Prox-regular functions in variational analysis. Transactions of the American Mathematical Society, 348(5), 1805–1838).
