Optimality of (σS)-type policies for a stationary multi-product inventory model with a canonical diffusion process as demand process
   V.   Barche ;
Abstract
 The presented multi-product inventory problem is a special impulse-control problem of canonical diffusion processes and is considered within a general, time-discrete, Markovian decision model. A proof of the optimality of a (σS) type policy is given by using a policy iteration method  
Keywords
Impulse-Control Problem, Inventory Model, (σ, S)-Policy
