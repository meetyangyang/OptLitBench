Sufficient conditions of weierstrass type for the generalized problem of bolza
   A.   Nowakowski ;
Abstract
 Sufficient conditions for a strong relative minimum in the generalized problem of Bolza are described. The method used further generalizes Young’s concept of “geodesic coverings”—chain of flights, by casting off the synchronization principle from it  
Keywords
Sufficient Conditions, Strong Relative Minimality, Field of Extremals
