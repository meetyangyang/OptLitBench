Limit properties of distance functions via semigroup techniques
   M.   Turinici ;
Abstract
 A differential consequence of the Bbezis-Bbowder attraction test is used to get the following statement:For each (nonempty) subset X of the Banach space V the quantity    depends on the couple (z, w) ∈ X × V but not on the subset W of V including X A parameter enlargement of the Bbezis-Bbowder theorem is being performed then, as a preliminary step in extending the obtained result.
