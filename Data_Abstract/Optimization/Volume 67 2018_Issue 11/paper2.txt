Subdifferentials of perturbed distance function in Riemannian manifolds
A. Barani;
Abstract
The notions of perturbed optimization problem and perturbed distance function are introduced in Riemannian manifolds. Some characterizations of Fréchet and proximal subdifferentials of perturbed distance function in the context of Riemannian manifolds are given.
Keywords
Fréchet subdifferential, proximal subdifferential, perturbed distance function, well-posedness, Riemannian manifoldsAMS SUBJECT CLASSIFICATIONS: 58C05, 58C99
