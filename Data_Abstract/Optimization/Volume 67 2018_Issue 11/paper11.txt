A variational derivation of a class of BFGS-like methods
Michele Pavon;
Abstract
We provide a maximum entropy derivation of a new family of BFGS-like methods. Similar results are then derived for block BFGS methods. This also yields an independent proof of a result of Fletcher 1991 and its generalization to the block case.
Keywords
Quasi-Newton method, BFGS method, maximum entropy problem, block BFGS
