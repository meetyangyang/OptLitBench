Nonsmooth minimax programming problems with V − r-invex functions
 S.K.   Mishra ; Kalpana   Shukla ;
Abstract
 In this article, we consider a class of nonsmooth minimax programming problems in which functions are locally Lipschitz. Sufficient optimality conditions and duality results are obtained under V − r-invexity assumptions. These results extend several known results to a wider class of problems. 
Keywords
