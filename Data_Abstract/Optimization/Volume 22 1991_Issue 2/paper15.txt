Some relations between neighbourhood graphs for a permutation problem
   F.   Werner ;
Abstract
 Many discrete optimization problems belong to the class NP-hard. Therefore heuristics have been developed for such problems. As regards iteration methods, the determination of a suitable neighbourhood plays an important role. In this paper we derive structural relations between different neighbourhood graphs where the set of solutions is characterized by the set of permutations of the integers 1,…m For two neighbours in a graph G 1 we calculate the mean shortest length in some other graph G 2 We also derive the maximum difference of shortest lengths in G 2 and G 1between vertices which are connected in G 1.
