Optimality conditions and duality for multiobjective measurable subset selection problems
   G.J.   Zalmai ;
Abstract
 Optimality conditions and several duality results are established under convexity and generalized ρ convexity assumptions for constrained multiobjective measurable subset selection problems  
Keywords
Finite Atomless Measure Space, Lebesgue Integrable Functions, Efficient Solutions, Properly Efficient Solutions, Generalized ρ Convex nSet Functions, Multiobjective Programming, Optimality Conditions, Duality Theorems
