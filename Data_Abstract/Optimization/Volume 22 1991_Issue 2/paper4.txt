On the singularities in linear one-parametric optimization problems *  
 Darinka D.   Pateva ;
Abstract
 The stability properties of linear one-parametric optimization problems are studied via classification of the solutions of the problem under different regularity assumptions. We will describe the classes of linear one-paramteric optimization problems determined by some regularity assumptions introduced in the literature as well as present an extension of them. A notion of regularity will be introduced, which determine the largest class of linear one-parametric problems where it is possible to classify the solutions. 
Keywords
Linear programming, piecewise smooth mapping, regular value, critical point, smooth manifold with boundary
