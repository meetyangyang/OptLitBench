Directional K-epiderivatives and error bound
 Marco   Castellani ; Massimo   Pappalardo ;
Abstract
 We give a sufficient condition for a function depending on a parameter and defined on a Banach space to have an error bound; such a result is given in terms of directional K-epiderivatives and does not require any particular property of the space. 
Keywords
parametric inequality system, generalized epiderivative, error boundAMS Subject Classifications:: 49J52, 90C26, 90C31
