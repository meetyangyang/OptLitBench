Conic separation for vector optimization problems
 Giandomenico   Mastroeni ; Letizia   Pellegrini ;
Abstract
 In this article, we analyse the relationships between conic and vector separation of two sets. Applying such results in the image space associated with a vector optimization problem (VOP), we show that the existence of a particular instance of vector separation is equivalent to the existence of a vector saddle point for the Lagrangian function associated with the VOP.
