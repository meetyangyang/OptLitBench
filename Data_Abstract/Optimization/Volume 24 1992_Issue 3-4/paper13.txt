Implementation of goal programmingin long-range resource planning in university management
   D.   Ghosh ;   B. B.   Pal ;   M.   Basu ;
Abstract
 This paper presents a resource allocation model via Goal Programming (GP) in a long-range planning horizon in university management. In the proposed model, the introduction of new courses in the academic units and the allocation of available budget within the academic units as well as the forecast of the budget which is actually needed for better academic performance are taken into consideration. An illustrative example is presented to expound the model  
Keywords
Resource Allocation, Goal Programming, Pre-emptive Priority
