A class of generalised minimum effort problems in locally convex linear topological spaces
   G.   Chakraborty ;
Abstract
 In this paper a class of generalised minimum effort problem has been formulated in locally convex linear topological spaces. Some necessary conditions has been obtained. Existence and uniqueness of the solutions are discussed 
Keywords
Optimal Control, Minimum Effort Control, Locally Convex Linear Topological Spaces, Weak∗-Topology, Supporting Hyperlane, Semi-Reflexive
