Optimal impulse correction problem
   B. N.   Pshenicheny ;   S. B.   Kirichenko ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with the problems of impulse correction of an object where the behaviour is described by the system of differential equations , and join relations , The optimum of various control criteria is attained due to variation of the moments τi and values v i The principal distinguishing feature of the problem is the possibility of obtaining necessary extremum conditions under constraints , and different quality criteria  
Keywords
Impulse, Optimal Control, Necessary Optimal Conditions, Cone, Conjugate Cone
