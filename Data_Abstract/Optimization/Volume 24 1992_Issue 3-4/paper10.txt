Linear control systems with additive disturbances: constrained controllability to a subset
   V. N.   Phat ;   T. C.   Dieu ;
Abstract
 The paper studies constrained controllability to a subset of linear discrete-time systems with additive disturbances. Several necessary and sufficient conditions for local controllability to a subset of linear infinite-dimensional systems with constraints on both control and state are given. The results are applied to study the pursuit problem in linear discrete-time game 
Keywords
Controllability, Discrete-Time Systems, Disturbances, Discrete Game
