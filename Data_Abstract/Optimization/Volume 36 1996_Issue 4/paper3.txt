Optimality and duality for multiobjective fractional programming involving nonsmooth($si:F$esi:ρ)–convex functions
   J. C.   Liu ;
Abstract
 We establish the Kuhn-Tucker necessary and sufficient conditions for an efficient optimum of multiobjective fractional programming problems containing ($si:F$esi:ρ)-convex functions. Bector type dual for multiobjective fractional programming problem is introduced and certain duality results have been derived in the framework of ($si:F$esi:ρ)-convex functions.  
Keywords
Multiobjective Fractional Programming, nonsmooth ($si:F$esi:ρ)-convexity, Duality
