General nonlinear mixed variational-link inequalities
   M.   Aslam Noor ;
Abstract
 In this paper. we introduce and study a new class of variational inequalities which is called the general strongly nonlinear mixed variational-like inequalities. This class is the most general and unifying one , The auxiliary principle technique is used to prove the unique existence of a solution of the variational-like inequality and to suggest a novel and innovative iterative algorithm. We also study the convergence analysis of the iterative algorithm. Several special cases. which can be obtained from our main results, are also discussed  
Keywords
Variational-like Inequalities, Auxiliary Principle, Iterative Algorithm
