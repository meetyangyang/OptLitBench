Optimality and duality for generalized fractional variational problems involving generalized (fρ)-convex functions
   J.C.   Liu ;
Abstract
 Using a parametric approach. we establish sufficient conditions and derive duality theorems for a class of generalized minimax Iracuonal variational problems containing generalized (fρ)-convex functions  
Keywords
Generalized Fractional Variational Problem, Generalized (fρ)-convex Functions, Duality
