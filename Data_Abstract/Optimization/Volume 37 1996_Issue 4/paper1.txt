Sufficient conditions for generalized convex set-valued maps
   P.H.   Sach ;
Abstract
 The paper discusses conditions under which a set-valued map F between Banach spaces Xand Y is K-quasiconvex and K-convex. where K is a convex cone in Y These conditions are given in terms of nutions of K-quasimonotnicity and Kmonotonicity of the Bouligand and Clarke deriva tives for the extended map   
Keywords
Set-valued map, Convexity, Quasiconvexity, Bouligand contingent derivative, Clarke derivative, Quasimonotonicity, Monotonicity, Bifunction
