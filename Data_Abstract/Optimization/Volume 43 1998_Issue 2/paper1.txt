On a calculation of an arbitrary separating hyperplane of convex polyhedral sets
   L.   Grygarová ;
Abstract
 Separation theorem is a basic theorem of convex analysis and convex programming. Its applicability to nonlinear programming methods depends on whether we can also describe a separating hyperplane of two or more convex sets. In this article the results obtained for two convex polytopes are extended to the case of two convex polyhedra, to  the case of a convex polytope and a convex polyhedron, and further to the case of a finite number of convex polyhedral sets. In the courbe of solving the problem it was derived also an implicit representation of the convex hull of a finite number of collvex polyhedral sets and a simple necessary and sufficient condition for the emptiness of the intersection  of the interiors of two n-dimensional convex polyhedral sets  
Keywords
Separating hyperplane, convex polyhedron, convex polytope, convex polyhedral set, projection cone, polar cone, convex hull
