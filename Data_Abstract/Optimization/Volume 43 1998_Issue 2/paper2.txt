Separating support hyperplanes for a pair of convex polyhedral sets
   L.   GrygarovÁ ;
Abstract
 A calculation of all separating hyperplanes of two convex polyhedral sets (polytopes and polyhedra), which are for them also the supporting ones, is dealt with in this article. In the theory of optimization this means of find all linear objective functions, which attain simultaneously their minimum over one and their maximum over the other one of two feasible sets. The feasible sets, which are described by a finite number of linear inequalities, can be bounded or unbounded, but they can have at most boundary points in common. Using the derived theory we try to find an approximate solution of a special nonlinear programming problem.
