About controllability and controllability sets of higher order goursat systems
 P.   Burgmeier ;
Abstract
 This paper is concerned with controllability questions of GOURSAT stems of m-th order (m≧2) Occurring for the first time the concept of degree of controllability is derived. for a distributed parameter system and successfully used to describe a controllability set, which is of a more general type than the usual Null-controllability set. Moreover, a special case of higher order GOURSAT systems is introduced, which allows an especially simple representation of its solution and with that a simple characterization of the corresponding controllability set  
Keywords
Controllability, Controllability Set, GOURSAT System
