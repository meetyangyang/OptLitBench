On Pontryagin maximum principle for linear systems with constraints
 Phan   Quoc Khanh ;
Abstract
 The note considers a case in which Pontryagin Maximum Principle for linear systems with constraints implies the closedness of the reachable set and shows a class of systems for which there is an answer to the question of S. Rolewicz in [6].
