Identifikation der verlustwahrscheinlichkeit aus beobachtungen des abgangsprozesses
   W.   Warmuth ;
Abstract
 For single -server loss systems G/M/1/0 with stationary ergodic input it is shown, that the loss probability and the one-dimensional interarrival time distribution can be identified by the service intensity and the one-dimensional interdeparture time distribution.
