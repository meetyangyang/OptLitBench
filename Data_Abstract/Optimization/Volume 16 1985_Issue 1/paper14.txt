Abstract
 A condition of starshapedness has been shown in the article [1] to be sufficient for the existence of  equilibrium points in a class of noncooperative games with compact strategy sets and continuous payoff functions which for some players are quasi concave with respect to the strategy sets of coalitions from . In this paper the condition of starshapedrless is shown to be necessary for the existence of  equilibrium points on the above mentioned class of noncooperative games.
