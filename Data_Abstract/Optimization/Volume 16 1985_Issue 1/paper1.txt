On non-differentiable convex functions
   B.D.   Craven ;   B.M.   Glover ;
Abstract
 The well-known first order characterization of real-valued convex functions is here extended to a class of non-differentiable functions which are convex with respect to the ordering induced by a closed cone in a locally convex topological vector space.  
Keywords
