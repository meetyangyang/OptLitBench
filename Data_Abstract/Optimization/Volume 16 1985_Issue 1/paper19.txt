Diffusion approximation for a tandem queue with blocking
   T.   Kimura ;
Abstract
 By using a diffusion approximation model, this paper discusses the steady-state behavior of a queueing system consisting of two single server stations in series. On account of finite capacity of an intermediate waiting room, a phenomenon called blocking occurs in this system. The number of customers at each station and the waiting time at the first station are analyzed by appropriately selected diffusion processes. The diffusion parameters of these processes are provided by some asymptotic properties of the arrival and service processes and of blockings. To check the accuracy of the approximations, they are numerically compared with simulation results.
