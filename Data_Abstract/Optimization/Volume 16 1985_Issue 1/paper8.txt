Characterization of all optimal solutions and parametric maximal mows in networks
   G.   Ruhe ;
Abstract
 In the paper two extensions of the classical max-flow problem are considered. At first, an approach to solve the case of parametric are capacities is presented. And secondly, a constructive description for the set of all optimal solutions of the max-flow problem is given.  
Keywords
