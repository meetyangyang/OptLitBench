Modèles stochastiques de redistribution et certain aspect optimal
 Bui-Trong   Lieu ;
Abstract
 The aim of this paper is the presentation of the notion of stochastic redistribution models (definitions, existence theorems and general properties) and of their optimal aspects (stochastic centred models and bivalued stochastic models). As a special case of stochastic redistribution models we can understand the fiscal programs, which are described in [2] in the nonstochastic case.
