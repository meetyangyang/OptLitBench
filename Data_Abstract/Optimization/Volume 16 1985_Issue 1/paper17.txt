Charakterisierung dynamischer leontief- systeme bei unbestimmten koeffizientenmatrizen
   R.   Fahrion ;
Abstract
 By abstracting mathematically dynamic Leontief-systems with inaccurate knowledge of the matrices of technology, of capital and consumption coefficients, we are led to point-to-set correspondences whose mathematical properties permit a characterization of the dynamic production model under uncertainty. The matrices representing the processes of technology and capital formation are to be classified in such a way that the domain of uncertainty for the production output at time t + 1 is contained in the one at time t. This invariance problem will be investigated for different topologies defined on the (n—1)-simplex. We suggest the application of a metric which is based on a concept measuring entropies on convex polyhedra, and sufficient conditions for the invariance property of the production paths are given.
