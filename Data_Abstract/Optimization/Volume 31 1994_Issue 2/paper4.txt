Constrained controllability and reachability of linear descriptor systems *  
   V. N.   Phat ;
Abstract
 The Paper derives new necessary and sufficient conditons for globally controllability and reachability of linear discrete-time descriptor systems with restrained controls. The main tool of the proofs is based on some extensions of the Banach open mapping theorem to set-valued convex closed functions. Illustrative examples are given. 
Keywords
Descriptor Systems, Controallability, Reachability, Set-valued Functions, Banach Open Mapping Theorem
