A note on a class of cones ensuring the existence of efficient points in bounded complete sets 1  
   T. X. D.   Ha ;
Abstract
 We study a class of cones with a special property and prove that under some additional conditions a cone had this property if and only if any nonempty bounded complete set has efficient points 
Keywords
Multiobjective Optimization, Efficient Point, Completely Regular Cone, Cone-bounded (cone-closed) Set
