Generalized (F, ρ)-convexity and duality for non smooth multi-objective programs
   D.   Bhatia ;   P.   Jain ;
Abstract
 (F, ρ)-convexity and related definitions are extended for non-smooth functions which are then used to establish duality results for multi-objective programs 
Keywords
Multiobjective Programming, Non-smooth(F, ρ)-Convexity, Duality
