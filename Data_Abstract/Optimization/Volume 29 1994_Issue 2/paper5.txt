Some remarks on controllability and reachability of nonstationary discrete-time processes  *  
   V.N.   Phat ;
Abstract
 In this paper. some criteria for controllability and reachability of nonstationary discrete-time systems described by difference inclusions in Banach spaces are given. The results are applied to obtaining necessary and sufficient conditions for global controllability and reachability of linear discrete-time nonautonomous systems with restrictions not only on controls, but also on states  
Keywords
Controllability, Reachability, Constraints, Nonstationary Discrete-time Systems, Set-valued Functions, Banach Spaces
