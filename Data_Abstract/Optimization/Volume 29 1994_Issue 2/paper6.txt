An inventory model with wiener demand process and positive lead time
   D.   Beyer ;
Abstract
 In the paper there is presented a special one-product inventory model with Wiener demand process, a fixed positive lead time and a (r Q)-strategy. For generai cost functions ive give a method of computation of the long run average cost. In the case of iinear cosi functions we explicitly determine the optimal strategy parameters
