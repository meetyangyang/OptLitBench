Generating properly efficient points in multi-objective programs by the nonlinear weighted sum scalarization method
 M.   Zarepisheh ; E.   Khorram ; Panos   M.   Pardalos ;
Abstract
 This article deals with the scalarization techniques in multi-objective optimization problems (MOPs). In this article, the nonlinear version of the weighted sum method is considered as the scalarization problem, and the relation of the set of properly efficient points and the set of scalarization solutions (optimal solutions of the scalarized problem) is studied. In particular, conditions are determined under which the optimal solutions of the scalarized problem are properly efficient points of the MOP, and the conditions under which the properly efficient points could be characterized in terms of the scalarization solutions are presented.
