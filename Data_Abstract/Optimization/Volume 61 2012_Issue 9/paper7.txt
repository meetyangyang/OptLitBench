Geodesic G-invex sets and semistrictly geodesic η-preinvex functions
 R.P.   Agarwal ; I.   Ahmad ; Akhlad   Iqbal ; Shahid   Ali ;
Abstract
 In this article, we introduce semistrictly geodesic η-preinvex functions on Riemannian manifolds, geodesic G-invex sets and study their properties. Our results extend and improve the results of Yang and Li [X. Yang and D. Li, Semistrictly preinvex functions, J. Math. Anal. Appl. 258 (2001), pp. 287–308]. Example is constructed in support of our defnition. 
Keywords
geodesic G-invex sets, geodesic η-preinvex functions, semistrictly geodesic η-preinvex functions, Riemannian manifolds
