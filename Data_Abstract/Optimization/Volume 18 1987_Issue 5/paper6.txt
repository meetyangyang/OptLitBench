Lokale konvergenzeigenschaften einer klasse von iterationsverfahren der nichtlinearen optimierung
   R.   Schulze ;   K.   Tammer ;
Abstract
 We show that every method of nonlinear optimization satisfying the conditions that every iterated point x  k-1belongs to the same component of the level set of the objective function as x k-1and that every accumulation point is a stationary point converges to a strong local minimal point if it starts in a neighbourhood of it. These conditions are satisfied by a modified version of the P1-algorithm of Zoutendijk. 
Keywords
Components of a level set, local convergence in nonlinear optimization, methods of feasible directionsAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary:90 C 30, Secondary:65 K 05
