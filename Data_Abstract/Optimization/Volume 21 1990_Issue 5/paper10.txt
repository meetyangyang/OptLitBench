Support algorithm for linear discrete minimax control problem
 Nguyen   Chan ; Tran   Hoang Yen ;
Abstract
 In this paper Gabasov-Kirillova's support method is developed to derive an optimality criterion for a linear discrete minimax control problem. This is then used to construct an algorithm for numerical solution of the problem.
