Quasi-differentiability: a formal approach. first and second gradient classes
 Beatriz   Margolis ;
Abstract
 The present paper inserts quasi-differentiability into the frame of a certain normed Riesz space. Completion of the spaces dealt with gives rise to almost quasi-diferentiability. This allows the introduction of first and eventually second order gradient classes generalizing subgradients and second subgradients of convex functions.
