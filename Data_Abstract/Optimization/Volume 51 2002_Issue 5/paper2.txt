A General Gordan Alternative Theorem with Weakened Convexity and Its Application
 Renying   Zeng ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we present a general Gordan alternative theorem for presubconvexlike functions in a Hausdorff topological linear space. This is then applied to discuss the saddle point theorem, concave-convex condition and minimax theorem for vector extremum problems. 
Keywords
Hausdorff Topological Linear Space, Alternative Theorem, Vector Extremum Problem, Presubconvexlike Function
