Generalized Level Method with Approximated Data
 Thomas   Unger ; Evgenij G.   Gol'stejn ;
Abstract
 We describe a generalization of the level method for solving a nondifferentiable program where the problem data (feasible set, function values, and subgradients) are not known explicitely and may be approximated using an oracle only up to an appropriately chosen accuracy. We show that it produces an approximately feasible and optimal solution after a finite number of oracle calls. 
Keywords
Nondifferentiable Optimization, Convex Optimization, Level Method, Cutting Plane Method, Inexact Data
