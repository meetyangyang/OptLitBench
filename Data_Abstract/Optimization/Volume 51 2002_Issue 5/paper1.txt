Lipschitz Continuity and Interpolation Properties of Some Classes of Increasing Functions
 E.V.   Sharikov ;
Abstract
 We study local Lipschitz continuity and interpolation properties of some classes of increasing functions defined on the cone Rn ++ of n-vectors with positive coordinates. We also study the so-called self-conjugate increasing positively homogeneous functions. 
Keywords
Lipschitz Continuity, Increasing Functions, Abstract Convexity
