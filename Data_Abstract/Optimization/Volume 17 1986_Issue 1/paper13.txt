A lower bound for mean characteristics in E k /G/1 and GI/E k /1 queues 1  
   D.J.   Daley ;
Abstract
 The mean stationary waiting time EW  of a stable E k /G/1 queueing system depends on the service distribution through its first two moments and k–1 roots of an equation involving its transform. Vainshtein's (1983) work on delimiting the region where such roots lie is improved and leads to a bound on EW  that is always tighter than other known lower bounds. The methods can also be applied to Gi/E k /1 systems but the bounds are not as useful  
Keywords
Single Server Queueing System, Mean Waiting Time Inequality
