Optimization with set-functions described by functions
   B.D.   Craven ;   D.V.   Luu ;
Abstract
 Constrained optimization problems for set-valued vector objective and constraints are studied, where the multifunctions take convex polyhedra as their values. This allows necessary conditions for an optimum, also sufficient conditions, to be specified in terms of conventional functions  
Keywords
Set-valued functions, Vector functions, Weak set-valued minimum, Optimality
