Abstract
 For Piecewise Smooth Morse functions Defined on an Open subset of Rn the normal forms around a nondegenerate critical point have been determined in [2] and [7]. The non- smooth part of these normal forms is given by a max-min combination of linear functions with integer coefficients. The additive group generated by these functions is a combina- torial Picard group (see [4]) corresponding to a certain fan  in Rn In this paper we show that the generators of this group are the support functions of the face complex of a polytope and give an explicit representation for the two-dimensional case.  
Keywords
Combinatorial Picard group, Generalized Convexity, Nonsmooth Morse Theory
