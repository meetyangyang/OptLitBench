Zur c-konvexität und c-subdifferenzierbarkelt von funktionalen
   H.   Dietrich ;
Abstract
 In this paper by means of the generalized Fexchel conjugacy in the ease of a certain quadratic coupling functional a c-subdifferential calculus is given. The relationship to the ”lower C 2” functions in the nonconvex and nondifferentiable optimization is proved. 
Keywords
 C-convexity,  C-subdifferentiabilityAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary:49 A 55, Primary:49 B 27
