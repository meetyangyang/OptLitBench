Topological stability results about approximate solutions of parametrized minimization problems
   J.P.   Penot ;   M.   Volle ;
Abstract
 The useful and classical question of the stability of approximate solutions to a parametrized minimization problem is revisited and examined under various facets. New notions of continuity for functions and multifunctions are involved in this study. Incidentally, a characterization of variational convergence is presented. Some persistence results complete this circular tour.
