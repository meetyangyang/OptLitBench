Zur lösung einiger minimax-aufgaben über dem quadrat
   H.   Voigt ;   L.V.   Wolfersdorf ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with the minimax problems (2), (3) for probability distribution functions G or bounded measurable functions g and bounded measurable or quadratic summable functions f with the restrictions (6), (7) and (8), respectively. Under suitable assumptions apart from elementary cases the optimal solutions G or g and f can be calculated by means of certain Fredholm integral equations.
