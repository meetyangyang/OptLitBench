Higher-order necessary and sufficient conditions for strict local pareto minima in terms of Studniarski's derivatives
 Do   Van Luu ;
Abstract
 In this article, we establish higher-order necessary and sufficient conditions for strict local Pareto minima of nonsmooth multiobjective programing problems in terms of Studniarski's derivatives of higher order. 
Keywords
strict local pareto minimum, studniarski derivatives, higher-order optimality conditions2000 Mathematics Subject Classifications:: 90c46, 90c29
