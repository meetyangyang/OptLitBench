Degrees of maximums of linear mappings
 E.   Hajilarov ;
Abstract
 In this article, we introduce the notion of (topological) degree for maximums of linear mappings (MLM) and study its properties. It is shown, that in  the only possible values for the degrees of MLMs are 0 and ± 1, whereas for  with n > 2 the degrees can take arbitrary integer values.
