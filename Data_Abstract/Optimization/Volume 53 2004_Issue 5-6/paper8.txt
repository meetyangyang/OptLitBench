Continuity of the Legendre–Fenchel transform for some variational convergences
 Jean-Paul   Penot ; Constantin   Zălinescu ;
Abstract
 We present in a self-contained way some results about the continuity of the Legendre–Fenchel transform. They will be used for a study of the convergence of explicit solutions to the evolution Hamilton–Jacobi equations. 
Keywords
Bounded convergence, Convergence, Duality, Fenchel transform, Legendre transform, Mosco convergence, Mathematics Subject Classifications 2000: 46N10, 46A20, 46A55, 90C31
