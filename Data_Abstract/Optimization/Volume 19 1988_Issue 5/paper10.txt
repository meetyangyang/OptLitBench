A Schur parametrization of non-negative Hermitian and contractive block matrices and the corresponding maximum entropy problems
   B.   Fritzsche ;   B.   Kirstein ;
Abstract
 In continuation of the paper [28] Schur parametrizations will be constructed for non-negative Hermitian block matrices and for contractive block matrices.This admits a clear geometrical approach to special problems of entropy optimization.
