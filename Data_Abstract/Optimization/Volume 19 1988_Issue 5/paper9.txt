Variablenfixierungen in gemischt-ganzzahligen linearen 0-1-Optimierungsaufgaben
   J.   Sommerschuh ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with the reduce of dimension size in the linear mixed integer problem by variables fixing. These criteria are generalizations of the known results for Knapsack-problems. Furthermore strategies of finding feasible solutions are discussed. Numerical tests show the usefulness of these results.
