Existence of solutions and error bound for vector variational inequalities in Banach spaces
Jen-Chih Yao;Xi Yin Zheng;
Abstract
Without monotonicity and convexity assumptions, we provide the existence results of solutions for weak vector variational inequalities and vector variational inequalities under some kinds of continuity assumptions. We give a gap function for a weak vector variational inequality and present a sufficient condition to guarantee that the vector variational inequality has error bound property with respect to such a gap function.
Keywords
Vector variational inequality, fixed point, gap function, error bound
