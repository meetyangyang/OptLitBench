Abstract
 In [1, 4] the author has given an ε-approach for the approximate solution of discrete linear programming problems. He derives estimations on the effectiveness of the multi-dimensional knapsack-problem. The present paper deals with a special class of problems, namely the multidimensional Knapsack-problem ,wit,h sufficiently rapidly decreasing coefficients of the objective function. For this class of problems are derived un estimation on the number of iterations for the Aε ′-algorithm. This estimation only depends on the rate q of decreasing of the coefficients and on the given relative derivation ε of the approximate solution of the objective function from the exact solution.
