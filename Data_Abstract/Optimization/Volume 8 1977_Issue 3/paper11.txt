Abstract
 In the present paper generalizations are investigated of piecewise-linear Markov processes introduced by Kovalenko in 1964. For this processes, an ergodic theorem is proved, and iteration formulae are given. Furthermore, some method is proposed in order to calculate approximatively state probabilities by means of compare models.
