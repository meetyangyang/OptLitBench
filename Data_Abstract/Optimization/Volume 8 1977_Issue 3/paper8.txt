Über die lösung diskreter steuerungsproblerne
 Horst   Hollatz ;
Abstract
 In this paper a method for finding stationary controls of discrete control problems is founded. This method is based on a method of feasible directions derived in [2]. Whereas in previous applications of these methods it was necessary to slove the variational problem of all stages for each iteration step, here it is proved that only one optimal suitable variation of one stage for each iteration step is necessary.
