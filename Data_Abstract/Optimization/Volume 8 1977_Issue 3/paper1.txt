Über eine methode zur kopplung dualer schnittebenenverfahren in der ganzzahligen linearen optimierung 1  
 Jörg   Seeländer ;
Abstract
 A method for synthesis of dual cutting plane algorithms in integer linear programming is given. The background of this are Gomory's fractional experiences are communicated. Finally, some questions connected with the presented method are mentioned and remarks about further studies are given.
