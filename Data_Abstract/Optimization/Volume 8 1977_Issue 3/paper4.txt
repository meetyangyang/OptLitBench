Das tourenproblem mit zentralem fuhrpark bei unterschiedliehen fahrzeugtypen
   G.   Grundler ;   M.   Schoch ;
Abstract
 In [1], [2] the authors considered the problem of optimal routs for vehicles of the same type from a central starting point. In the present paper the problem is generalized to the case of different type of vehicles, for which case a mathematical model and a related transportation problem are developed. Some remarks are added concerning the modification of the algorithm presented in [1], [2].
