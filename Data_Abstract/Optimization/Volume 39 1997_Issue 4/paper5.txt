A class of self-dual updating formulae in the broyden family
   Z.Y.   You ;   C.X.   Xu ;   X.F.   Ma ;
Abstract
 In this note, at first me give 3 survey of self-dual quasi-Newton updates in the Broyden family, and then we discuss the relations between the choices of the parameter and the self-dual updating formulae. A general sufficient and necessary condition on the selection of the parameter for self-dual updating formulae is presented  
Keywords
Quasi-Newton Method, Self-Dual
