Countably orderable sets and their applications in optimization
   L.   Gajek ;   D   Zagrodny ‡ ;
Abstract
 A special class of relations is common in many of optimization problems. We extract it in a general form and provide sufficient conditions for the existence of maximal points. The general results are next applied to Nonsmooth Analysis and Optimization.  
Keywords
Multiobjective optimization, maximal points, optimality conditions
