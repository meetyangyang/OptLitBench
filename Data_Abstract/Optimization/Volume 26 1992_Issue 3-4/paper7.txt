A parallel global optimization method and its implementation on a transputer system
   V.P.   Gergel ;   Ya.D.   Sergeyev ;   R.G.   Strongin ;
Abstract
 This paper deals with a new parallel method for solving one-dimensional global optimization problems. We present the formulation of the decision rules of this method, the conditions for its nonredundant parallelization, the generalization for solving multi-dimensional problems and its implementation on a transputer system. There is also an account of some numerical experiments.  
Keywords
Global optimization, parallel numerical methods, speed up, transputer system
