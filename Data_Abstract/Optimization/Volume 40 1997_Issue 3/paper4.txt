A Note On Kalmanson Matrices ∗  
 V.M   Demidenko ; R   Rudolf ;
Abstract
 In this note we derive an additive characterization of Kalmanson matrices by investigating the cone of this matrix class. With the help of this characterization, a simple proof for a result obtained by Kalmanson (1975) on the symmetric travelling salesman problem is presented. Moreover, this characterization can also be used to generate distance matrices for which the optimal solution is known in advance  
Keywords
Travelling Salesman Problem, Kalmanson Matrix, Cone
