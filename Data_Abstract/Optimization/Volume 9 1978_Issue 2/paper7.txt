Theorie zur numerischen behandlung einer erweiterten Bellmanschen funktionaigleichung der diskreten dynamischen optimierung
 Hans-jürgen   Sebastian ;
Abstract
 For the parameter dimension problem of the extended Bellmanean functional equation, we have to obtain appropriate reduction methods. For this purpose, we define the concept “minimal parametric computation set of a functional equation” and develop approximation methods on the basis of this concept. Furthermore, a general conception and examples of error estimates of these approximation methods are given.
