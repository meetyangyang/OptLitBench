On a method of L. bittner for deriving maximum principles to optimal control processes
   L.V.   Wolfersdorf ;
Abstract
 By means of the known multiplier rule of M.R. Hestenes there is given an extension of a method of L. Bittner for deriving maximum principles to general control processes in Banach spaces. This extension is applied to a class of optimal control problems for processes governed by Hammerstein integral equations with weakly singular kernels.
