Über die optimale lagerhaltung in einem system von lagern: eine näherugslösung
 Peter   Köchel ;
Abstract
 This paper investigates the N-Warehouse-Problem under the condition of linear cost-functions and constant profit made from transport (per unit stored) from warehouse i to warehouse j, i ≠ j. Applying this model we obtain and approximation for the optimal decision in a model where transportation profit does not remain constant. A new expression for the expected costs in introduced, which facilitates the comparison of the costs with those of N independent warehouse. In the appendix several problems are solved.
