A lagrangian approach to duality for complex nonlinear fractional programming over cones
   C.R.   Bector ; Suresh   Chandra ;   T.R.   Gulati ;
Abstract
 A Largrangian function approach to duality for a complex nonlinear fractional programming problem over cones is presented and under certain convexity type assumptions a saddle point theorem, with other duality results, is proved.
