On correlated life and repair times
   G.   Sankaranrayanan ;   R.   Ramanarayanan ;
Abstract
 In this paper we study the correlation between the life time and repair time of the device that arises when two types A and B of shocks that can occur to it in the case when it is exposed to a cumulative damage process due to type A shocks and it can with-stand damage of type B shocks, the repair starts immediately after the failure and all damages are repaired one at a time. We also investigate the cases when the worker avoids damages due to type B shocks whenever he is alert.
