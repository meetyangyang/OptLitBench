Durchschnittsoptimale strategien in Markovschen entscheidungsmodellea bei unbeschränkten kosten
 Christine   Küenle ; Heinz   Uwe Küenle ;
Abstract
 Markovian decision processes with unbounded one-period costs and average cost criterion are treated. For a inventory modal the optimality of (s, S)-policies is shown and a computational method is given. Similar results for a replacement problem are presented.
