  

Abstract
 N-stage control processes with parameters which depend on the time are considered, and only incomplete information about these parameters is available. It is shown that for such a class of processes an extended Bellmanean functional equation of discrete dynamic programming is a sufficient conditions of optimality.  Furthermore, sufficient conditions of continuity, concavity, and monotonicity of the optimal return functions in the extended Bellmanean functional equation are given. In the discrete stochastic case (the objective function is then the expected value with respect to stochastic vectors), we obtain an approximation method with error estimates for the reduction of the parameter dimension problem.
