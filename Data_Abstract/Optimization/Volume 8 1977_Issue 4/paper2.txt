  

Abstract
 For solving problems having block-structure a method is presented which is based on the application of a modification of the Lagrangian. Doing this the given problem with block-structure is transformed into a saddle point-problem with a function which is implicitly given being concav-convex and differentiable.  This saddle point-problem is solved by gradient projection method. It is proved that the presented method is convergent to a solution of the problem given before; when the problem is linear the convergence takes place with geometric progression.  A new method for solving usual (non-structured) problems of linear programming is given which is based on the approach described.
