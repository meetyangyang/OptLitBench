Stochastic behaviour of a 2-unit standby redundant system with two switching failure modes
 ashok   Kumar ;
Abstract
 A 2-Unit standby redundant system with switching failure modes:Transfer switch and connect switch is considered. Failure distributions of units and connect switch are exponential and repair time distribution are general.Stochastic behaviour of the system has been studied by semi-Markov process and parameters of interest, e.g/., MTSF, expected numbers of visits to a state, steady-state availability, net profit have been obtained MTSF has been studied for its behaviour. Several earlier models are shown as particulate cases.
