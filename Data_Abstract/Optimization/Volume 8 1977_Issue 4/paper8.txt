Zur Konvergenz des Algorithmus von Krylov und Ĉernous'ko II
 Klaus   Nolte ; Joachim   Bergmann ;
Abstract
 The optimization algorithm of Krylov and Ĉernous'ko is applied to discrete processes. Convergence statements are given for linear problems as for problems involving linear differece equations and a quadratic objective function. The behaviour of the algorithm at the approximation of continuous processes by discrete ones is investigated.
