Optimality, minimax and duality in nonlinear programming
 Ş   Mititelu ;
Abstract
 In this paper we consider a nonlinear program with mixed constraints and we establish that the following theorems are true in more weak conditions as those known in literature:1) the sufficiency of the Karush-Kuhn-Tucker optimality theorem, 2) the Fritz John and Kuhn-Tucker minimax necessary optimality theorems and 3) the Hanson and Huard converse duality theorems.
