Ein beitrag zur theorie der linearen parametrischen optimierung
   M.   Stefek ;
Abstract
 A linear optimization problem with parameters as right-hand sides of the restrictions and as coefficients of the objective function is considered. In some special cases one can discern stability domains from an explicit optimal simplex tableau. Furthermore it is proved that the set of vertices of the solvability cone is a stability domain.
