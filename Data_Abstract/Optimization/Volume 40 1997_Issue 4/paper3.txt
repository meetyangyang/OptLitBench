Generalized F-convexity and its classification
 R.N.   Mukherjee ; CH.   Purnachandra Rao ;
Abstract
 Several types of F-convexity (FC), pseudo F-convexity (PFC) and quasi F-convexity (QFC) of a nonlinear function are described. Direct implications of the relations existing between various types of F-convexity and generalized F-convexity are presented as well as a block diagram has been proposed which depicts the implications reflected in this paper.  
Keywords
(Fρ)-convexity, Clarke's Generalized Directional Derivative, Smooth and Nonsmooth Functions
