Duality for a sum of convex ratios
 C.H.   Scott ; T.R.   Jefferson ;
Abstract
 Fractional programming models involving a function plus a ratio or a sum of ratios are considerably more difficult to deal with than a single ratio, since the result is generally nonconvex. In this paper, we consider a particular form of this problem which is convex and for which a duality theory can be derived. Explicit form of the dual is given in terms of conjugate transforms. Several particular cases are developed in detail.  
Keywords
Convex Ratio, conjugate Duality, fractional Programming
