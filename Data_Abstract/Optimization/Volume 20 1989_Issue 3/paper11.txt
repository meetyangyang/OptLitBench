Initial busy period analysis for a multichannel Markovian queue
   O.P.   Sharma ;   J.   Dass ;
Abstract
 The analysis for the initial busy period generated by k-servers in a multichannel Markovian queueing system initiated by an arbitrary number of customers is carried out and the expression of its density function is obtained in closed form which helps in evaluating its important parameters and the results for particular cases have also been derived.  
Keywords
Initial busy period, Cramer's rule, recurrence relation, tridiagonal matrixAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary:90C40
