Optimal solution of a deterministic transportation problem by the duality in goal programming technique under a preemptive priority structured approach
   N.N.   Nayak ;   M.   Basu ;   P.K.   Tripathy ;
Abstract
 An attempt has been made to give an insight into the dual method which can be best represented for decision making in goal programming under an assumption of a preemptive priority structured approach. This model has been used to apply it to a transportation framework with deterministic constraints.  
Keywords
