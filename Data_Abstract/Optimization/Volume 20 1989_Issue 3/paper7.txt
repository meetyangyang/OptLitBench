Factorizations of m-matrices in input-output analysis  1  
 Anna   Torriero ;
Abstract
 The aim of this paper is to examine the effects of arbitrary changes in technological coefficients on the prices in Input-output models. This analysis has been performed by splitting M-matrices into the product of suitable matrices.
