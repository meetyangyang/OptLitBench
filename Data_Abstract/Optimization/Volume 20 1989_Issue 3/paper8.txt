Some inequalities for bin packing
   W.T.   Rhee ;
Abstract
 We describe a general method to improve a result in [2] and obtain a better bound on P(|Bn −E(Bn )|≧t) where Bn  is the objective function value of the bin packing problem with n items.  
Keywords
