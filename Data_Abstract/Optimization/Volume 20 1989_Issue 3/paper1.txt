Finite dimensional second order optimality conditions
   C.E.   Nahum ;   H.M.J.   Cantalloube   II ;
Abstract
 We consider in this paper a non-linear mathematical program (P) over R n  The functions are assumed twice continuously differentiable. We show that the well known BBZ conditions for optimality, which can be found in [3], are the best attainable when only the first and second derivatives of the functions are available.
