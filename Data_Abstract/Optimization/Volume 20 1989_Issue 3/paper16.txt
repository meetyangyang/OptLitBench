Optimale nutzung von bergbauabfallen zur produktion von baiimateriallen
   R.F.   Hartl ;   G.T.   Kirakossian ;
Abstract
 In the present paper we consider the problem of optimal using the dead rock which is interesting in ore mining. Through that it is possible to avoid costs for storage and protecting the environment on the one hand and to produce useful products, like different kinds of building materials (gravel, sands, cement, brick etc.) on the other hand. By using Pontryagin's Maximum Principle the structure of optimal policy can be found.  
Keywords
