Anmerkungen zu einem abstrakten maximumprinzip von halkin
 Manfred   Schäuble ;
Abstract
 It is shown that a well-known maximum principle of A. Halkin can be characterized as a special case of a Lagrange-multiplier rule for nonconvex programming problems in quasiordered locally-convex topological vector spaces, established by the author, using a certain concept of subtangential approximations.
