On generalized convex functions
 Helga   HartWig ;
Abstract
 This paper deals with an extension of Beckenbach's classical generalized convexity notion, called F-convexity, in such a manner that much of, “modern”convexities of functions on the Euclidean R n(as log-, ω-, [explicit] quasi- or K- convexicity can be included. General theorems concerning connections between several kinds of convexity, convexity properties of composite functions, first and second order characterizations of differentiable F-convex functions are presented.
