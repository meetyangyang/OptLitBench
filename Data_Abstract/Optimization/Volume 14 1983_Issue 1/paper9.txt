Combinatorial theory of transportation polyhedrons
   V.A.   Emeičev ;   M.K.   Kravcov ;
Abstract
 This paper gives a survey of some results of the combinatorial theory of transportation polyhedrons. A classification of transportation polyhedrons with respect to facets (these are faces of maximal dimension) is stated. Furthermore, there is a representation of a criterion of the membership of a non-degenerated polyhedron with a given number of facets to a class of polyhedrons with minimal and maximal number of vertices. The paper gives a solution of two suggestions of Bolker concerning a formula for the maximal number of vertices of a transportation polyhedron and its asymptotic behaviour. Some results with respect to estimations of diameters of transportation polyhedrons are represented. Finally there are formulated some unsolved problems and suggestions.
