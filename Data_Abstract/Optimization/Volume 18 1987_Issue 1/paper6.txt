Properties of the general quadratic optimization problem and the corresponding linear complementarity problem
   J.   Sommerschuh ;
Abstract
 The quadratic nonconvex function  (C symmetric, arbitrary) is considered on the convex polyhedral set  and neighbourhoods of local minima are described, Using the corresponding Kuhn-Tucker-conditions we find a cut excluding a neighbourhood of a previously determined local minimum.  
Keywords
