Piecewise affine functions as a difference of two convex functions
 Anita   Kripfganz ;   R.   Schulze ;
Abstract
 We give different representations of piecewise affine linear functions as a difference of convex piecewise affine linear functions.  
Keywords
Nonlinear programming, nnonsmooth optimization, representation of continuous functions, convex functions, DC-functionsAMS 1980 Subject Classifications: Primary: 29B40, Secondary: 90C30
