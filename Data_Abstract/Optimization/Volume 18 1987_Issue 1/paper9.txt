Existence, characterization and application of interpolating minimal polynomials
   L.   Berg ;
Abstract
 The paper continues former work on completely monotonous minimal polynomials, interpolating a given set of points. A pair structure conjectured by B. Thielcke on the basis of numerical experiments is proved for these polynomials together with their existence and two inclusion theorems about the exponents, which are useful for their numerical calculation. Knowledge of the structure of these polynomials is also advantageous with regard to the more general problem of fitting data by completely monotonous polynomials.
