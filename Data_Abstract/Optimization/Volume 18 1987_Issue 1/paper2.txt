On a general duality result for moment problems
   E.J.   Balder ;
Abstract
 A general duality result for moment problems due to Kemperman is shown to fit into Rockafellar's perturbation scheme for convex duality theory.  
Keywords
Convex programming, duality theory, moment problemsAMS 1980 Subject Classifications: Primary: 90C25, Secondary: 49A55
