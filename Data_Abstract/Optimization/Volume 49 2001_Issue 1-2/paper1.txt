Optimal control for dynamic versions of the leontief and other matrix models ∗  
 Bittner.   Leonhard ;
Abstract
 In this paper dynamic versions of the Leontief model are formulated as two kinds of optimal control problems where the control regions are given by linear inequalities. Applying Pontryagins maximum principle a practicable form of an optimality test is presented. Finally, an extension to the case of multistage models is developed.  
Keywords
Leontief model, Optimal control problem, Optimality Conditions, Optimal Solution, Multistage optimal control problem
