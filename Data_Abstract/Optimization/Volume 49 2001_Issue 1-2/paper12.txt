Abstract
 On the basis of a work by B.Döring for ℝ1→ ℝ1maps we give a thourough proof of the convergence and on the convergence speed of the Newton Method for C 1αmaps in Banach spaces. We describe the application of this classical method for the existence (and local uniqueness and approximation) of nonlinear eigenvalue problems - abstractly - and for the concrete case of nonlinear Dirichlet problem which was for- merly attacked by variational methods
