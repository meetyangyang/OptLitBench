Abstract
 The paper is a first test to derive necessary optimality conditions for problenls of optimal control with an abstract quasivariational inequality. The state restriction i!. represented by a multifunction K:Y→Y, with a convex and closed set K⊂Y and a nonlinear operator g Y→Y, In this case there exists a smooth gap fumction to describe the quasivariational inequality problem. By means of a penalty method one can derive necessary optimality conditions for this control problem
