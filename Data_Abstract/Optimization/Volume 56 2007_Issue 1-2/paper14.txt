Semicontinuity of vector-valued mappings
 M. Ait   Mansour ; C.   Malivert ; M.   Théra ;
Abstract
 We first provide, for a given vector-valued mapping f, a lower semicontinuous (l.s.c.) regularization. Further, we present a new characterization of lower semicontinuity, from which we derive some other type of l.s.c. regularizations. A quasi-l.s.c. regularization is thereafter presented.
