The asymptotic turnpike property and the uniqueness of a periodic minimal solution for one-dimensional variational problems 
 Alexander J.   Zaslavski ;
Abstract
 In the article we consider the asymptotic behaviour of extremals for a class of second order infinite horizon variational problems arising in continuum mechanics. We analyze the uniqueness of a periodic minimal solution and the asymptotic turnpike property which means that all good functions  have the same set of limit points. 
Keywords
Turnpike property, An (f)-good function, A minimal long-run average cost growth rate, The representation formula, Mathematics Subject Classification 2000: 49J99
