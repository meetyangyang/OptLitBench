A minimax approach to semicoercive hemivariational inequalities 
 Dumitru   Motreanu ; Zdzisław   Naniewicz†  ;
Abstract
 A new minimax approach for treating semicoercive hemivariational inequalities is presented. An elliptic Dirichlet problem at resonance and with discontinuous nonlinearties is solved under a recession condition 
Keywords
Hemivariational inequalities, Nonsmooth analysis, Critical point theory, Mathematics Subject Classificatins 2000: Primary: 49J40, Secondary: 49J35
