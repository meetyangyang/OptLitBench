  

Abstract
 Extrenal algebra, i.e. a mathematical system with fundamental operations taking the maximum and multiplication of nonnegativ real numbers has a lot of analogies with linear; algebra. Discovery  of analogy is prolonging in this paper by proof of a theorem corresponding to the theorem of Hamiltion-Caley in the linear algebra. It is introduced the concept of extremal sign of numbers by means of which one can define determinant and the root of polynomial.
