Methods for a class of discrete location problems
   I.   Martinec ;
Abstract
 A generalized discrete location problem on a finite set is defined. An implicit enumeration method and two approximative ones for this problem are presented. Application to a special class of mixed 0-1 integer linear programming problems are outlined.
