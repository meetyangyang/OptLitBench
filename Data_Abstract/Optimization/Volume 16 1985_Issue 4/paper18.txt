A note on sequential decomposition in Maekovian decision models
   P.   Köchel ;
Abstract
 We complete investigations in [3] on sequential decomposition in Markovian decision models (MDM). An example for non-equivalence of a stationary MDM with his so-called k-step decomposition is given and an equivalence theorem under compactness and continuity assumptions is proved.
