Epiderivatives of the marginal function in nonsmooth parametric optimization
   D. E.   Ward ;
Abstract
 In this paper, upper and lower bounds are calculated for the epiderivatives and parabolic second-order epiderivatives of the marginal function of an abstract parametric mathematical program. The main tools in the proofs of these bounds are "intersection theorems" for tangent cones and second-order tangent sets. The special case of a nonlinear program with right-hand-side perturbations of inequality and equality constraints is examined in detail. 
Keywords
Marginal Function, Tangent Cone, Normal Cone, Second-order Tangent Set, Epiderivative, Parabolic Second-order Epiderivative
