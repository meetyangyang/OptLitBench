An alternative derivation of the projective interior point method for linear programming through the least squares approach
 Zhao-Yang.   Cheng ; J.E.   Mitchell ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we use a least squares problem to derive the affine-scaling direction and a modification of this problem to derive the affine-centering direction of the interior point method for linear programming. The least squares approach provides us with some useful tools in analyzing the algorithm's progress and makes the reasoning of a scaling transformation, as well as the centering modification, more intuitive and understandable. We derive an algorithm which is equivalent to the projective algorithm proposed by Ye and Kojima through the least squares approach and some convergence analysis is also given
