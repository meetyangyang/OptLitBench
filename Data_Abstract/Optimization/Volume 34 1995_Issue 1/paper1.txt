Compactly epi-lipschitzian sets and a-subdifferentials in wt-spaces
   A.   Jourani ;
Abstract
 This paper studies some properties of compactly epi-Lipschitzian sets in Weak Trustworthy spaces. These properties are obtained by using the A-normal cones. As consequences. intersection formulae and A- subdifferential calculus rules are derived. Some applications are investigated 
Keywords
Compactly epi-Lipschitrian sets, Locally compact sets, WT-space, A-subdifferential
