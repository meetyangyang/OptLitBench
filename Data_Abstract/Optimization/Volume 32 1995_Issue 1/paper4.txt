On measure functions for the self-scaling updating formulae for quasi-newton methods  ∗  
   M.   Ai-Baali ;
Abstract
 This paper is concerned with the choice of parameters in the self-scaling Broyden family of formulae, defined on the basis of minimizingr two type of measurc functions. Onc measure is the $si:l$esi:2 condition number of a certain matrix, while the other one is suggested by Dennis and Wolkowicz, which has some useful properties and acts like the former measure. We extend the class of self-scaling updates of Oren and Spedicato to intervals containing those of the Oren–Luenberger's intervals. We motivate, in a sense to be defined, that desirable members of the self-scaling Broyden family are self-scaling symmetric rank-one updates
