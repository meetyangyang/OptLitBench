Controllability of Nonsmooth Discrete Systems with Delay
 L.   Minchenko ; S.   Sirotko ;
Abstract
 This paper is concerned with a study of local controllability to discrete inclusions with delay. The sufficient conditions for relative and complete local controllability are obtained on the base of using Mordukhovich's nonsmooth constructions. 
Keywords
Local Controllability, Discrete Control System, Multifunction, Inclusion, Normal Cone, Nonsmooth Analysis
