Transformation of Some Mixed Approximation Problems by Optimization Methods
 Miguel A.   Jiménez ; Estela L.   Juárez ; Francisco   Guerra ;
Abstract
 The present paper considers the approximation of a function in the L 2 -norm by functions in a Haar space subject to a constraint set defined by the sup norm. This problem is examined from developments of the Karush-Kuhn-Tucker theorem for semi-infinite programming as well as from the multiobjective optimization approach. An extreme feasible set is characterized with the help of a version of the Chebyshev alternation theorem. 
Keywords
Semi-infinite Optimization, Multiobjective Optimization, Chebyshev Alternation Theorem, Haar System, Best Approximation
