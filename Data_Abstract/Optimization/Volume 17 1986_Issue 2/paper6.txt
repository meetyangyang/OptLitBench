Complexity investigations on the ellipsoid algorithm
   J.   Terno ;   G.   Scheithauer ;
Abstract
 It is shown, that the by Khačian in 1979 published Ellipsoid-algorithm for the solution of linear optimization problems is also polynomial on the set of operation { +, −, *, /, <}. 
Keywords
