Ein produktionsplanungsmodell bei horizontaler und vertikaler verflechtung
   K.   Beer ;   J.   Käschel ;   K.   Richter ;
Abstract
 A model for developing and optimal production plan of several products, workshops, plants and stages of production under certain discreteness conditions is investigated. If the number of products is essentially greater than the number of plants in all workshops and stages of production, an appropriate twice-dual linear programming problem yields a feasible solution of the original problem. Some computational and economic results obtained from the application of the model are presented.
