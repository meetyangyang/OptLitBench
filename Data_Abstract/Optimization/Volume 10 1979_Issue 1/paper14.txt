One - processor scheduling with nonuniform ready times
 Milan   Vlach ;
Abstract
 This paper is concerned with one-machine scheduling problems allowing non-simultaneous jobs arrivals. For problems in which all jobs are simultaneously available Held and Karp developed a dynamic programming algorithm for finding an ordering that minimizes the total sum of arbitrary cost functions. This paper extends the approach of Held and Kabp to the problems with nonsimultaneous arrivals and arbitrary non-decreasing cost functions. The algorithm is illustrated by a problem of minimizing the number of tardy jobs.
