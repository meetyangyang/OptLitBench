Linearität and verallgemeinerungen der konvexität
 Karel   Zimmermann ;
Abstract
 An attempt to develop an axiomatics for a generalized convexity concept is made. The concept of a hyperplane is introduced axiomatically. A new concept of a generalized linearity is considered and some properties of optimization problems that are linear in this sense, are studies. These properties were made use of in literature in the process of solving certain class of optimization problems. The properties give us also a methodology for the construction of algorithms for the solving of some further optimization problems of his kind.
