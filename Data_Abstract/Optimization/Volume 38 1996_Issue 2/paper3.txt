Optimal stopping in a shock model
   A.   Rangan ;   G.   Sarada ;   V.   Arunachalam ;
Abstract
 In this article we consider two optimal stopping problems in a general shock model associated with a correlated pair {Xn, Yn} of renewal sequences, {Xn} and {Yn} denoting respectively the time and magnitude of the nth shock. A control limit policy is proved to be optimal based on the working age of the system  
Keywords
Shock Model, Cumulative Damage, Optimal Stopping, Control Limit Policy Optimal stopping in a shock model
