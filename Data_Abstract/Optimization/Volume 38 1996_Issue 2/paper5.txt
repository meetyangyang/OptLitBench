Pdm time cost trade-off:activities are splittable or non-splittable
   M.   Haidu ;
Abstract
 In this paper we deal with some special problems concerning least cost scheduling problem in precedence diagramming. The idea of PDM time cost trade-off comes from the CPM time cost trade-off problem. Solutions of PDM/cost problem when activity splitting is not allowed and solutions when splitting is allowed for all the activities can be found in the author's earlier papers. The reality is, that in a project, activity splitting is allowed for certain activities but for others are not. This model is more realistic than the two we mentioned before. In this paper we give an algorithm for the PDM/cost problem using the assumption that both splitting and non-splitting of activities are allowed. 
Keywords
Project Management, Network Techniques, Precedence Diagramming, Time Cost Trade-Off Pdm time cost trade-off:activities are splittable or non-splittable
