Anwendung der dualitätstheorle auf probleme der optimalen steuerung III
   H.   Benker ;   H.   Dietrich ;
Abstract
 Optimal control problems described by operator equations are considered. A duality theorem for norm-minimal problems is given and lower bounds are calculated. Furthermore, a duality theorem for nonconvex problems is derived by generalization results of Rocks-Fellar.
