Über die optimalität von strategien in stochastisehen dynamischen minimax-entscheidungsmodellen II
 Heinz-Uwe   Küenle ;
Abstract
 In this paper a modified stochastic dynamic game is considered, where the opposite player chooses an action not simultaneous but next the first player, knowing his last decision. It is shown, that there are stationary deterministic optimal or ε-optimal strategies if some semi-continuity and compactness conditions rare fulfilled. We obtain optimality conditions also for Shapley's usual stochastic two-person zero-sum game with hidden choice of actions by reducing to the former case.
