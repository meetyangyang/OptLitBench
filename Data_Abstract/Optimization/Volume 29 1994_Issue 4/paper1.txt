Convexity along curves and indunvexity
   R.   Pini ;
Abstract
 The properties of invex functions on manifolds with respect to particular maps are investigated to throw a new light on the relationship between invexity and convexity along curves: geodesic convexity on Riemannian manifolds is obtained as a particular case 
Keywords
Generalized Convexity, Invexity on Manifolds, Nonconvex Optimization
