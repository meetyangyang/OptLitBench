Ein 2-stufen Netwton-Dem'yanov algorithmic zur lösung eines diskreten minimaxproblemes
 Roland   Hornung ;
Abstract
 For a discrete minimasproblem a 2-stage algorithm is presented. This algorithm, a combination of gradient-type algorithms of Dem'Yanov and Newton method, adds the good behavior of the global convergence of descent methods to the fast final  convergence of the Newton method.
