Abgleich einer matrix bei änderung ton zeilen- und spaltensummen
   M.   Hellwich ;   S.   Lehmann ;
Abstract
 In input-output schemes the problem of generating a matrix of non-negative elements with given sums of rows and columns often occurs which agrees with the structure of a given matrix of the same type. After presenting criterions for measuring the equivalance of structures the paper deals with three methods of generating matrices of the characteristics required. The equivalence of structures obtained by these techniques is compared by means of some examples.
