Dualität und algorithmische anwendung beim vektoriellen standortproblem
 Christiane   Gerth ; Karin   Pöhler ;
Abstract
 In this paper we present duality theorems for the vector valued locational problem. These results are used in an algorithm that constructs all efficient locations. 
Keywords
Efficient locations, Duality in vector-optimization, Multiple-criteria decision makingAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary: 90 C 31
