Existenz durelisehnittsoptimaler Strategien in einem Markoffschen Entscheidungsmodell mit unbekaimter Parameterfolge
   A.   Sokolichin ;
Abstract
 Finite state controlled Markov chains with transition probabilities depending on a parameter are considered. The parameter values converge to a limit. Conditions are established for the existence of a policy which is average optimal uniformly in the parameter.
