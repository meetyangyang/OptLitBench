Intersection Properties of Tangent Cones and Generalized Multiplier Rules
 Ş.   Miric[acaron] ;
Abstract
 We prove that the quasitangent (Q)-intersection property of tangent cones in [14] is stronger and provides better generalized multiplier rules in finite-dimensional Mathematical Programming than the well known Dubovitskii – Milyutin (DM)-intersection property in [9-10], as extended and refined by Girsanov [ll] and Boltyanskii [3 - 5]  
Keywords
Mathematical Programming, Multiplier Rule, Tangent Cone, Local Approximation, Intersection Property
