On Generalized Vector Quasi-Variational Inequalities
 Won.   Kyu Kim ; Kok-Keong.   Tan ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we shall prove two generalized vector quasi-variational inequalities which generalize many existence results on VVI and GVQVI in the literature  
Keywords
Generalized Vector Quasi-Variational Inequality, Generalized Quasi-Variational Inequality, Equilibrium, Upper Semicontinuous, Cone, Order
