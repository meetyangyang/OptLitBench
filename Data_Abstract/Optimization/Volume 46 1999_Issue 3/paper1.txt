Continuous approximations to generalized jacobians
   H.   Xu ;   A.M.   Rubinov ;   B.M.   Glover ;
Abstract
 Here we discuss approximations to the Clarke generalized Jacobian of Lipschitz mappings from ℝn, to ℝm. A calculus for such approximations is outlined for both inner and outer compositions of Lipschitz mappings and continuously differentiable mappings. Typical examples of the approximations are presented. For real-valued Lipschitz functions these approximations reduce to approximations to the Clarke subdifferential. Applications of such approximations are outlined  
Keywords
Uniform Approximations, Strong Approximations, Generalized Jacobians
