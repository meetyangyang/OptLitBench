Generalized PC 1-functions
 Dempe ;   T.   Unger ;
Abstract
 In this paper we consider a new class of functions, so-called generalized PC 1-functions which are composed by a finite number of continuously differentiable functions. Such functions naturally appear as marginal functions in bilevel programming problems if the lower level problem is not assumed to have unique optimal solutions for all parameter values. We investigate them using the radial-directional derivative and a radial subdifferential and give criteria for optimality for minimizing such a function  
Keywords
Generalized PC 1-functions, Necessary and Sufficient Optimality Conditions, Radial-Continuous Functions
