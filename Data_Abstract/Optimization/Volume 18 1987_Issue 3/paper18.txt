Arbitrary state semi-Markov decision processes
   K.   Wakuta ;
Abstract
 We study the problem of maximizing the expectation of the total discounted rewards in semi-Markov decision processes with unbounded rewards. Both the state space and the action space are uncountable. We give a short and simple proof of the Bellman's principle of optimality and present sufficient conditions for the existence of an optimal stationary policy.
