Hamiltonian circuits with generalised cost
   O.   Brudaru ;
Abstract
 In this paper it is considered a special transportation problem with a decreasing charge along a Hamiltonian circuit. The are costs are linear functions of the transported charge. A dynamic programming formulation is proposed and some lower bounds are given, too.
