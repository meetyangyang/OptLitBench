Parallelschnitte in der ganzzahligen optimierung
   J.   Piehler ;
Abstract
 It is shown that a pure integer linear programming problem can be transformed by a unimodular matrix in such a manner, that all Gomory cuts defined by the rows of the optimal simplex tableau are parallel to the coordinate planes. With methods of cut strengthening we have a simple possibility for finding the solution.
