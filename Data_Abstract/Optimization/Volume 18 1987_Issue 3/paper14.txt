A distribution stability result for a stochastic optimal control problem
   P.   Doschkinov ;
Abstract
 A stability result for a stochastic optimal control problem is presented, where the distribution of the random input in the differential equation is perturbed in the sense of weak convergence of distributions. 
Keywords
Distribution stability, Stochastic optimal control, Weak convergence of measuresAMS 1980 Subject Classifications: Primary: 49360, Secondary: 49 A 60
