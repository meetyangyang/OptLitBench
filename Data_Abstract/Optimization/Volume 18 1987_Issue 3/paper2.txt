Ein konstruktives lösungsverfahren für das roblem des inpolygons kleinsten Umfangs Von J Steiner
 Hoàn   Xaä Phú ; Vien Toàn   Hoc ;
Abstract
 We develop a constructive method to solve the problem of finding inpolygons with minimal circumference. For this, we Schwarz reflection principle and the idea of oriented curves. The optimal inpolygon of an arbitrary m-polygon can be determined by a construction with compasses and ruler only.
