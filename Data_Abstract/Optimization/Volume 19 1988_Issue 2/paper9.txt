  

Abstract
 In [2] a general theory of duality for multicultural problems is developed. The theory is based on conjugate set-valued mappings and duality relations for set-valued mappings. In the present paper the main notions and a duality theorem for set-valued .mappings from [2] are given without proof. With the aid of this theorem a duality theorem for some general multicultural problem is obtained which includes the convex programming and optimal control problems. This result is applied to prove a duality theorem for quasi linear optimal control problems. The theory is applied to find the set of all Pare to-minimum points in some multi criteria linear control problems.
