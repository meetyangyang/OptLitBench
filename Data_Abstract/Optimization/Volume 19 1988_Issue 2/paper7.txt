Bemerkungen zu einem sonderfall glatt-konvexer optimlerungsaufgaben
   S.A.   Zinzadse ;
Abstract
 Convex-like optimization problems are considered under equality and inequality constraints. For a special ease of [2] a necessary optimality condition is supplied on the Lagrangian. 
Keywords
Smooth-convex and convex-like, optimization functions, Lagrangian, separation theoremAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary: 52 A 40, Secondary: 49 C 30
