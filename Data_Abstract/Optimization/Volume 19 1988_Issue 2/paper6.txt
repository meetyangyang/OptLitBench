Minimizing a sum of staircase functions under linear constraints
   C.T.   Bornstein ;   R.   Rust ;
Abstract
 We present a branch -and-bound algorithm to minimize f(x) for x∈G∩C where G are network flow constraints is a sum of staircase functions. We minimize the separable staircase functions over a polyhedron making successive linearizations of the objective function. Computational results are presented for the capacitated warehouse location problem.
