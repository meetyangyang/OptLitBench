Über lösungsmengen spezieller konvexer parametrisher optimierungsaufgaben
 Libuše   Grygarová ;
Abstract
 In the boundary of a n-dimensional strong convex set which represents a smooth manifold in En  the so called affine-geodetical manifolds of different dimension are investigated and some of their global properties are proved. It appeared, that these manifolds are to be considered as the set of all optimal points of certain parametric convex problems. 
Keywords
Parametric convex problem, affine-geodetical manifold, spherical mappingAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary:90 C 31, Secondary:53 A 15
