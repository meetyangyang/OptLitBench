Non-convex infinite games
   V.   Jeyakumar ;
Abstract
 A minimax theorem is proved for non-convex infinite games, using a theorem of the alternative. Infinite games are introduced as two person zero-sum games in which players have infinite number of strategies. Conditions for the existence of a finite value of an infinite game, and the existence of optimal strategies for players are also discussed.
