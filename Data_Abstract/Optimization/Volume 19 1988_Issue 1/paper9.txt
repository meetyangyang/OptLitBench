On the characterization of efficient points by means of monotone functionals
 Petra   Weidner ;
Abstract
 Sets of efficient, weakly efficient and properly efficient points are studied in linear topological spaces. We investigate interdependencies between, such optimal point sets of vector optimization and the optima of monotone functionals. Known results in partially ordered spaces are extended to linear spaces with a preference structure.
