Parametric approach to a class of nonconvex global optimization problems
 Phan Thien   Thach ; Hoang   Tuy ;
Abstract
 We present a parametric approach to the following nonconvex problem encountered in certain design calculations: Given a compact set S in  R  n and a symmetric positive definite n × n-matrix A, find a point x∊ R  n and a positive number r such that the ellipsoid  is contained in S and is largest possible We also discuss a problem in some sense dual to the above: find x∊  R  n and f >0 such that the ellipsoid containsS and is smallest possible.
