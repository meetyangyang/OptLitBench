Guillotine cutting of defective boards
   G.   Scheithauer ;   J.   Terno ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with the problem to cut rectangular pieces from a defective board using Guillotine cuts. To solve this problem dynamic programming is used and it is shown how the solution process can be improved. Practical examples show the quality of the investigations. The CPU-time needed corresponds to the theoretical complexity.
