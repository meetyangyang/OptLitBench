Branch- and bound algorithms for solving global optimization problems with Lipschitzian structure
   J.   Pintér ;
Abstract
 Adaptive partition and search methods are proposed to solve mathematica programming problems which are formulated with Lipschitz-continuous functions; the problems considered may typically have a multiextremal objective function and a non-convex feasible set. The suggested methods are globally convergent; besides, their structure permits efficient numerical realizations. 
Keywords
