Maximizing the sum of integers when their sum of squares is fixed
   S.   Kounlas ;   A.M.   Mathai ;
Abstract
 A complete solution is given for the maximization of the sum of integers when their sum of squares is fixed. Strict upper bounds are derived and an algorithm leading to all optimal solutions is given.
