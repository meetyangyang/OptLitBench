The constrained equal loss rule in problems with constraints and claims
 Leticia   Lorenzo ;
Abstract
 In this article I study how to distribute a resource among different agents claiming on it, according to the equal loss principle, when there are some constraints in the problem. I introduce one single-valued rule. Some properties of this rule are given as well as an axiomatic characterization. 
Keywords
