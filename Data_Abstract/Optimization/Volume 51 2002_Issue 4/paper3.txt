On the Pseudoconvexity of a Quadratic Fractional Function
 Alberto   Cambini ; Jean-Pierre   Crouzeix ; Laura   Martein ;
Abstract
 In this paper we give a necessary and sufficient condition for the pseudoconvexity of a function f which is the ratio of a quadratic function over an affine function. The obtained results allow to suggest a simple algorithm to test the pseudoconvexity of f and also to characterize the pseudoconvexity of the sum of a linear and a linear fractional function. 
Keywords
Generalized Convexity, Fractional Programming
