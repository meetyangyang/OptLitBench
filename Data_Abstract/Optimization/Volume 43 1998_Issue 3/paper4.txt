A feasible trust region method for nonlinear inequality constrained optimization
 Naihua.   Xiu ; Fang.   Xiu ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we present a feasible trust region method for the solution of inequality constrained optimization problem. The global convergence is established under certain conditions. It is also shown that, under reasonable assumptions, the algorithm reduces to the famous Nocedal-Overton method [17] for equality constraints after finitely many iterations, allowing two-step superlinear rate of convergence for linear constraints.  
Keywords
Nonlinear inequality constrained optimization, feasible trust region method, global convergence, two-step superlinear rate of convergence
