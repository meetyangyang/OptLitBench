Refinement of proper equilibrium points
   E.   Marchi ;   A.   Neme ;
Abstract
 In this paper we refine the concept of proper equilibrium point for n-person normal games due to Myerson 1978. We introduce the g-proper equilibrium points which we prove the existence under the condition of [ILM 0001]. They compare the strategy weight compounded not homogeneously. We prove that under some restrictive conditions of g g-proper equilibrium point is a proper equilibrium point. Relation with strictly proper equilibrium point is also considered  
Keywords
 n-Person Normal Game, Proper Equilibrium Points,  g-Proper Equilibrium, Existence Theorem, Kakutani Fixed Point Theorem, Eilenberg-Montgomery Fixed Point Theorem
