On the symmetric affiine scaling algorithm for line programming *  
   J.   Amaya ;
Abstract
 We propose a central trajectory motivation to derive the symmetric (primal-dual) interior point algorithm for linear programming. The central trajectory is chosen in such manner as the current solution lies on it. That is to say, the current point is the center of the polyhedron defined by the feasible region and a bound on the objective function. The weighted potential function defining the central path is given by an appropriate choice orweights, depending on the current primal-dual feasible solution. The direction of change is given by a first order approximation of the central path  
Keywords
Linear Programming, Interior Point Algorithm, Central Path, Primal-dual Feasible Solution, First Order Approximation
