Inventory returns and special sales in am order-level system
   U.   Dave ;
Abstract
 The problem of returning or of selling the inventory excess to optimal stock level is considered for an order-level system. Two inventory models, viz. the infinite and the finite horizon models, are developed; in which lead time is zero and shortages are allowed. Examples are given to illustrate the derived models.
