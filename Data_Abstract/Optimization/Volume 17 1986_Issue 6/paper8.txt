Transformations and consecutivities on a king for computational applications
   G.   Gambarelli ;
Abstract
 Aim of this work is to explore a new way to study polyhedra with minimal number of integer points, generalizing integer programming to more general algebraic structures. 
Keywords
Networks on commutative rings, integer transformations, polyhedra, integer programmingAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary:90 C 10, Secondary:52 A 25
