Multivalued strongly nonlinear variational inequalities
   M.   Aslam noor ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we consider a new class of variational inequalities. Using essentially the projection method, we establish the equivalence between the multivalued strongly nonlinear variational inequalities and the multivalued Wiener-Hopf equations. We used this equivalence to suggest and analyze a number of iterative algorithms for solving the multivalued strongly nonlinear variational inequalities. We also derive several special cases from our main results  
Keywords
Variational inequalities, Wiener-Hopf equations, Iterative algorithms, Convergence criteria
