Generalized quasimonotonicity and strong pseudomonotonicity of bifunctions
   M.   Bianchi ;
Abstract
 We extend some recent definitions of generalized monotonicity of maps such as strong pseudomonotonicity and (semi) strict quasimonotonicity, to real bifunctions h(x,d). Under a suitable continuity assumption on a function f we show that (semi) strict quasimonotonicity of the Dini derivatives D +(x,d) and D +(x,d) is equivalent to (semi) strict quasiconvexity off. By means of a majoring criterion we extend these results to other kind of generalized derivatives. Finally we show that under a regularity condition, strong pseudomonotonicity of the lower Dini derivative D +(x,d) is equivalent to strong pseudoconvexity of the function f.  
Keywords
Generalized monotonicity, Generalized convexity, Dini derivatives
