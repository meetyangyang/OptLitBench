A non-classic optimality condition in the problem of control by boundary value conditions of a semi-linear hyperbolic system
 A.V.   Arguchintsev ;
Abstract
 A non-classic optimality condition of variational maximum principle type is presented for optimal control problems by initial-boundary conditions of first-order hyperbolic systems. The optimal starting or boundary control provides the maximum in special problems of control by initial values of a system of ordinary differential equations. The optimality condition is illustrated by an example.
