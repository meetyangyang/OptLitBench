Imbedding and cutting methods for global optimization and their applications
 Valerian   Bulatov ;
Abstract
 The article addresses some versions of cutting methods for global optimization, the Chebyshev points of convex sets and applications of the Chebyshev points for global optimization and control theory. 
Keywords
global optimization, the Chebyshev points, cutting methods, theory of control, imbedding methodsAMS Subject Classification: 90C30
