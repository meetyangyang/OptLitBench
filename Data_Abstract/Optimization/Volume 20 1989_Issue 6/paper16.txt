Approximation scheduling algorithms: a survey
   M   Ya Kovalev ; Ya. M   Shafranskij ;   V.A   Strusevich ;   V.S   Tanaev ;   A.V   Tuzikov ;
Abstract
 The paper gives a state-of-art survey in the field of developing approximaion scheduling algorithms. Main attention is concentrated on algorithms with determined worst-case performance guarantees.  
Keywords
