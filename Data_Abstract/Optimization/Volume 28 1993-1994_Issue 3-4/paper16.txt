The convergence of entropy-based approximations for moment problems
   A.S.   Lewis ;
Abstract
 Given the first n moments of a density 1/y, one very popular estimation technique approximates y by the polynomial p n of degree n whose recipricol 1/p n matches the given moments. The approximant l/p n is the Burg entropy estimate: it solves the problem of maximizing  under the given moment constraints on x We show that if y is a positive. bounded, measurable function, bounded away from 0, then p n approaches y in L, norm. This is a special case of a result for more general approximation schemes based on moment data Some omputational evidence is also presented  
Keywords
Moment Problem, Nonlinear Approximation, Burg Entropy, Duality
