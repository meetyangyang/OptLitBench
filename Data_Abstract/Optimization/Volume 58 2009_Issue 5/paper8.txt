On generalized conjugations and subdifferentials in abstract convex analysis
 Evgeny   Sharikov ;
Abstract
 We generalize the notion of a duality , where U and V are two complete lattices, to arbitrary partially ordered sets U and V. Then we consider abstract subdifferentials corresponding to such type of dualities and formulate conditions for the global minimum of a function  in terms of these subdifferentials. We also define a conjugation in the case, when the sets U and V are upper complete semilattices, and examine some of its properties.
