Einfluß der vertemnngsfunktionen der vorgangsdauern auf die ergebnisse von netzplanberechnungen
   G.   Merkel ;
Abstract
 In this paper it is shown that in networks with given means and dispersions of activities the effect of the distribution-type is small. It follows the great importance of Clarks method and of Monte-Carlo-method for practical solving stochastic time-problems.
