Stability of approximations in optimal nonlinear control
   E.   Jacewicz ;   A.   Nowakowski ;
Abstract
 Stability of an approximation of a minimum for a control problem of Bolza is investigated. The dual dynamic programming method is used. An ε-value function and a dual ε-value function are defined. Several properties of the functions are presented. The verification theorems are proved. A concept of an £-optimal dual feedback control is described 
Keywords
Calculus of variations and optimal control, stability of approximations, approximate minimum, dual dynamic programming, ε-optimal dual feedback control
