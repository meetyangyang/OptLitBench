Abstract
 In this paper based on the nonmonotone linesearch technique of Grippo, et al ([4]) we propose a class of nonmonotone linesearches which includes many linesearches used in practice. Applying this general linesearch model to the well- known BFGS algorithm. we prove. under mild conditions. its global convergence.
