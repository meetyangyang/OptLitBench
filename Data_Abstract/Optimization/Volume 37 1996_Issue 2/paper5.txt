A note on the paper multi-step-prox-regularization methods for solving convex variational problems
   A.   Kaplan ;   R.   Tichatschke ;
Abstract
 In [∗] we have investigated the application of iterative Multi-Step-Proximal-Methods to ill-posed variational problems and have described conditions on convergence and rate of convergence for the methods suggested. The present notice improves essentially the statement given there concerning converge nce of the methods  
Keywords
Convex variational problems, III-posed problems, Iterative prox-regularization, Adaptive Approximation, Convergence and rate of Convergence
