New relaxed hybrid-extragradient method for fixed point problems, a general system of variational inequality problems and generalized mixed equilibrium problems
 Yonghong   Yao ; Yeong-Cheng   Liou ; Jen-Chih   Yao ;
Abstract
 In this article, we present a new iterative method based on the relaxed hybrid method and the extragradient method for finding a common element of the set of solutions of generalized equilibrium problems and the set of fixed points of a strictly pseudocontractive mapping and the set of a general system of variational inequality with inverse strongly monotone mappings in a real Hilbert space. Furthermore, we prove that the proposed iterative method has strong convergence under some mild conditions imposed on algorithm parameters.
