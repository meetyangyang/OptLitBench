Approximation der optimalen strategieparameter von (σS)-Lagerhaltungsmodellen
 Manfred   Miethe ;
Abstract
 We consider a stochastic multicommodity periodic review inventory system with proportional costs plus a single set-u cost. The replenishment policy is of the (σS)-type (either nothing is ordered or all commodities are ordered simultaneously). For two classes of (σS)-policies and Erlang demand distributions of the order one and two we obtain approximations to optimal ordering parameters. Numerical examples are given.
