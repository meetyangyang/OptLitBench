Necessary optimality conditions in some problems of paramete control 1  
   L.V.   Wolfersdorf ;
Abstract
 A theory of necessary conditions'for optimal control problems with Noether-ian operator equations in Banach spaces developed by M. Goebel and the author is applied to two problems of parameter control in nonlinear boundary value problems for an ordinary differential equation and for the plane Laplace equation.
