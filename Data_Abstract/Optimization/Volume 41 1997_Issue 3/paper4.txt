Optimality and Duality in Fractional Programming Involving Semilocally Convex and Related Functions
   V.   Lyall ;   S.   Suneja ; S.   Aggarwal ;
Abstract
 Necessary and sufficient optimality conditions are obtained for a nonlinear fractional programming problem involving semidifferentiable functions introduced by Ewing, Kaul and Kaur. A dual is formulated and duality results are proved using concepts of semilocally quasiconvex and semilocally pseudoconvex functions. An equivalent program using generalized Charnes Cooper transformation is given  
Keywords
Generalized Convexity, Nonlinear Programming, Optimality, Duality
