Input-error approach to parameter estimation of alinear,e-invariant, continuous-time model in state-variable description
 Nguyen.   Ngoc San ;   N. G.   Nath ;
Abstract
 The input-error approach to estimate the state-variable descriptive parameters of the controllable and observable part of the model is described. With reference to the parameters of an assumed model, the parameters of the model of either known or unknown order are estimated from the presently developed optimal projection equations applicable to either of the cases. Introduction of the input-error in the proposed approach enables one to avoid direct use of the system inout for estimation purpose removing thus the existing restriction on the system input in the estimation process. 
Keywords
Parameters estimation, continous-time model, input-error, output-error, cost function, stability theory
