Regularity properties of the optimal value function in non linear programming
   B.D.   Craven ;   R.   Janin ;
Abstract
 For perturbed constrained optimization, the optimal value function is shown to have the semismooth property, and expressions are given for its directional derivative, not assuming the optimum to be unique  
Keywords
Nonlinear Programming, Perturbation Problems, Semismoothness, Inverse Function, Lower Semicontinuous Differentiability
