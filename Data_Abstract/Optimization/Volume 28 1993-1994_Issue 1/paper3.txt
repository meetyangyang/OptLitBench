Duality in nonlinear programming involving semilocally convex and related functions
   S.K.   Suneja ; Sudha.   Gupta ;
Abstract
 A nonlinear programming problem is considered where the functions involved are nondifferentiable. Fritz John type and Kuhn-Tucker type necessary optimality conditions are given in terms of the right differentials of the functions. Wolfe's type and Mond-Weir type duals are formulated and duality results are obtained by using the concepts of semilocally quasi-convex and semilocally pseudoconvex functions  
Keywords
Generalized Convexity, Semilocally Convex, Semilocally Quasiconvex, Semilocally Pseudoconvex, Mathematical Programming
