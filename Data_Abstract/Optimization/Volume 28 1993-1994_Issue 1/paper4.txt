Abstract
 Some improved projected quasi-Newton methods are presented in which a nonmonotone line search technique is employed. Although these methods allow the sequence of the merit functions, nondifferentiable and differentiable, to be nonmonotonic, the convergence properties similar to those for the usual Armijo's rule are proved under certain conditions  
Keywords
Constrained Optimization, Nonmonotone, Convergence
