Abstract
 We formulate a pair of symmetric dual variational problems different from the one presented by SMART and MOND [8]. And we prove the weak, strong and converse duality for these problems, using the continuous version of pseudo-invexity which is a generalization of that of invexity, and give the weak, strong and converse duality under the pseudo-invexity assumptions for the static case of our continuous results  
Keywords
Symmetric Duality, Pseudo-Invexity, Variational Problems
