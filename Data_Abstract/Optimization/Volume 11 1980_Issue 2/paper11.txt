Starke konvexität helm standortproblem
 Joachim   Focke ;
Abstract
 It is shown, that the location-allocation problem posses a strongly convex objective function with a bound of convexity of m=(F/d 2)2/d. Here F and d are the area and the diameter of the convex hull formed by the "points" of the problem. In the case of the four-points-problem a better estimation is valid giving a sharp bound of convexity greater by the factor two. As means of proof the appendix contains two new, elementary geometric inequalities about the relations between the area and the and the diameter of a convex planar region with vertices lying in a certain angle-section.
