The integrated distribution function and its application for some inventory problems with linear demand pattern, arbitrary demand distribution and without fixed ordering costs
 Karl   Hinderer ;
Abstract
 We give a detailed analysis of some one-Item, Multi-stage inventory problems with linear demand pattern, arbitrary demand distributions, with backlogging and without fixed ordering costs. The main tool for deriving an equation for the optimal order level is the integrated distribution function, a notion which seems to be of independent interest.
