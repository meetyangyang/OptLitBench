Über vollmonotone polynome dureh vier gegebene punkte
   L.   Berg ;
Abstract
 For the existence of the polynomials mentioned in the headline there are established necessary and sufficient conditions. Especially, their uniqueness is investigated. Under an additional optimality condition we determine the structure of these polynomials and construct an algorithm for their calculation.
