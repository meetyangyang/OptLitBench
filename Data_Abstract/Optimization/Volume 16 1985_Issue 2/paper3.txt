Monotonie géneraliséd et sousdifférentiels de fonetions convexes vectorielles
   M.   Jouak ;   L.   Thibault ;
Abstract
 In this paper we establish some results about maximal monotonicity and maximal cyclic monotonicity of subdifferentials of convex operators.  
Keywords
