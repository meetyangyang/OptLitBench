The three dimensional bottleneck assignment problem and its variants
 Rita   Malhotra ;   H.L.   Bhatia ;   M.C.   Puri ;
Abstract
 The three dimensional bottleneck assignment problem and some of its variants are studied in the present paper. A procedure is developed for each of these bottleneck assignment problems.
