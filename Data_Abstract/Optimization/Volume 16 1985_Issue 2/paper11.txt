Non-discrete silent duels with complete counteraction
   T.   Radzik ;   K.   Orlowski ;
Abstract
 The paper considers a modification of the so-called non-discrete silent duels. Namely, it is assumed that each player has a possibility of counteraction at the moment of a success of the opponent. The players are in possession of the resources of the identical amounts.  The solution is given in the case of equal accuracy functions, being nondecreasing and continuous, from<0,1> onto <0,1>.
