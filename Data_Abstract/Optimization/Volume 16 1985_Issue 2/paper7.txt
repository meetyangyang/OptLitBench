Komplementaritätsprobleme für punkt-menge-abbildungen
   J.   Willöper ;
Abstract
 Let be given a closed convex cone K○.;R  n , the polar cone K *and the point-to-set-mapping F:K→𝔓(Rn ). We consider the generalized complementarity problem to find a z∈Ksuch that z*∈K *=0 for some z*∈F(z). Using Browder-sets we prove theorems for the existence of solutions of complementarity problems in a unique manner for various classes of point-to-set-mappings.
