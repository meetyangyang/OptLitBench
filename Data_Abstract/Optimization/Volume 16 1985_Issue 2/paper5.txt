The behaviour of the objective function on the Kuhn-Tucker set
 H.Th.   Jongen ;   K.   Tammer ;   S.   Wolf ;
Abstract
 We consider a real valued function ∫ of maximum type on a subset of R nconstrained by nonlinear (in)equalities. Under certain differentiability conditions we prove that the image under ∫ of the Kuhn-Tucker set ∑KThas Lebesgue measure zero. It should be emphasized, that we do not assume any constraint qualification. As a corollary we obtain that ∫ is constant on a path connected subset of ∑,KT.
