Recursive branch and bound
   A.   Hartwig ;
Abstract
 The context describes a method for solving integer programming problems. Using branch and bound the solution of a problem is transformed into the solution of a series of problems, which belong to the same recursive class like the problem being solved. To determine series of bounds special types of the recursive strategy can be given.
