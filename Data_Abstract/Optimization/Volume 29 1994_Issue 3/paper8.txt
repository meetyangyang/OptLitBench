Sojourn times in queues with a pair of instantaneous independent bernoulli feedback
   V.   Thangaraj ;   A.   Santhakumaran ;
Abstract
 In this paper we study the total sojourn time in a queueing system with a pair of instantaneous independent Bernoulli feedback processes. As the details of the computations are difficult, we give only the structure of the sojourn time process for the M/G2/l queue with a pair of Bernoulli feedback. We have also carried out a numerical study in this paper  
Keywords
Queueing Theory, Queueing Networks, Bernoulli Feedback Process
