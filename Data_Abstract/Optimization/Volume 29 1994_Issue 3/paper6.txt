On the improvement of allocation rules for multi-armed bandit problem
   H.J.   Girlich ;   S.   Pohlenz ;
Abstract
 In this note we present a method to modify allocation rules which is useful for multi-armed bandit problems also for the finite-horizon case. The method is based on monotonicity properties of the value function and the structure of certain optimal policies. It is applied to the well-known allocation rule of Bather (1985). The performance of improvement is investigated by a numerical case study  
Keywords
Bandit problem, Bayes Analysi, Beta-distributed Prior, Bather's Randomized Policy, Dynamic Programming
