Ein überlinear konvergentes mehrschrittvcrfahren mit fester schrittweite zur funktionsminimierung
   J.   Ehlert ;
Abstract
 For unconstraint minimization problems having a known optimal value a multistep method with a fixed step-length is given. The local convergence and a superlinear rate of convergence is proved. This method is inserted in a general one which is global convergent under the condition that the optimal value is only known approximately.
