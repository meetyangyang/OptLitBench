Optimal solutions for a class of infinite horizon variational problems with extended-valued integrands
 Alexander J.   Zaslavski ;
Abstract
 In this work, we study the structure of optimal solutions of infinite horizon autonomous variational problems with a lower semicontinuous integrand f : R  n  × R  n  → R 1 ∪ {∞}, where R  n  is the n-dimensional Euclidean space. We also show that all the optimality notions used in the literature are equivalent for the problems with the integrand f.
