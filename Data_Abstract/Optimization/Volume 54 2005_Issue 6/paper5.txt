An approach to classification based on separation of sets by means of several ellipsoids
 A. V.   Demyanov ; M.   Gaudioso ;
Abstract
 We state the problem of separating two finite point sets in  by means of a given number of ellipsoids. The problem reduces to the solution of a mathematical program with a nonconvex, nonsmooth objective function of the sum-min type. We discuss two algorithms to tackle the problem and present numerical results on several testing datasets. 
Keywords
Classification, Separation of sets, Nonsmooth optimizationKeywords:  AMS Subject Classifications: 90C, 65K
