Competitive equilibrium:walras meets darwin
 Sjur   Didrik Flåm ; Børn   Sandvik ;
Abstract
 Prime objects of this note are (I) excess demandgenerated by priee-taking economic agents, and (II) an alternative version of tàtonnementWe relate laws of demand, axioms of revealed preferences, and other notions of generalized monotonicity to “evolutionary stable” prices. Focus is on localstability of competitive equilibrium. Specifically, we establish that evolutionary stable prices are asymptotically attractive under so-called replicator dynamics
