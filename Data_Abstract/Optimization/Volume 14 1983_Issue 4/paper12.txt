Die direkte suche - ein heurlstisches verfahren zur behand-lung ganzzahliger quadratischer optimierungspromeme
   R.   Sessner ;
Abstract
 This paper presents a heuristic method for solving a quadratic integer programming problem. This method is tested for 18 examples and compared with the branch and bound algorithm by ABADIE. Because of the numerical tests the approximating method is preferred to the exact branch and bound algorithm.
