Anisotropic best τ C  -approximation in normed spaces
 Xian-Fa   Luo ; Chong   Li ; Jen-Chih   Yao ;
Abstract
 The notions of the τ C  -Kolmogorov condition, the τ C  -sun and the τ C  -regular point are introduced, and the relationships between them and the best τ C  -approximation are explored. As a consequence, characterizations of best τ C  -approximations from some kind of subsets (not necessarily convex) are obtained. As an application, a characterization result for a set C to be smooth is given in terms of the τ C  -approximation. 
Keywords
