A new hybrid method for hierarchical fixed points problems and variational inequality problems
 Yeong-Cheng   Liou ;
Abstract
 In this article, we introduce a new hybrid method which includes some algorithms in the literature as special cases. We prove that the proposed algorithm strongly converges to an element which solves some hierarchical fixed point problem and some variational inequality problem. 
Keywords
