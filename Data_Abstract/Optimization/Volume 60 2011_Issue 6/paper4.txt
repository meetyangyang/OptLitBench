Topological methods in some optimization problems for systems governed by differential inclusions
 Valeri   Obukhovskii ;
Abstract
 This is the survey article devoted to some applications of the topological degree theory for noncompact multivalued maps to certain control systems governed by differential inclusions in Banach spaces.
