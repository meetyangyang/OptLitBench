Extremal problems for the generalized heat equation and optimal recovery of its solution from inaccurate data
 K.Yu.   Osipenko ;
Abstract
 We consider some extremal problems for the solution of the generalized heat equation similar to the well-known Hadamard three-circle theorem. These problems are connected with optimal recovery problems. We construct a family of optimal recovery methods for the solution of the generalized heat equation using the information about inaccurate data.
