Long-time behaviour for a model of porous-medium equations with variable coefficients
 Tran   Dinh   Ke ; Ngai-Ching   Wong ;
Abstract
 By analysing the uniform attractor for multi-valued processes, we study the long-time behaviour of the solutions of a model of non-autonomous porous-medium equations. The result is obtained by using the a priori estimates and suitable compactness arguments. 
Keywords
