Markov renewal queues of M/M(a,d)/l/(b,N) system-part i
   K.   Senthamari Kannan ;   R.   Sivasamy ;
Abstract
 In this Part-II, using the filtering technique developed by Cinlar (1969), the queue length processes embedded at any of the ‘arrivals’ and ‘batch departures’ of M/M(a,d)/l/(b,N)type Markov renewal queues, are shown as Markov renewal. Then using the Markov renewal theory, all possible semi-Markov processes (SMP) associated with each of the embedded MRP are examined. Further, for all these processes, stationary distributions associated with their irreducible sub-spaces are obtained. Such results are then verified with a numerical illustration  
Keywords
Filtering Procedure, Sub-Spaces, Stationary, Distributions
