Approximating a common zero of finite family of monotone operators in Hadamard spaces
Mohammad Taghi Heydari;Amene Khadem;Sajad Ranjbar;
Abstract
In this paper, a common zero of a finite family of monotone operators is approximated in Hadamard spaces. Some applications in convex minimization and fixed point theory are also presented.
Keywords
Hadamard space, proximal point algorithm, monotone operators, -convergence, convex minimization
