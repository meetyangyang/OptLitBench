The role of commitment in repeated games
 Ignacio   García-Jurado ; Julio   González-Díaz ;
Abstract
 We model the role of commitment in non-cooperative games by means of what we call unilateral commitments. We study their impact within the framework of repeated games with complete information. To do so, we revisit the main folk theorems for repeated games with complete information and check up to what extent the assumptions needed in the classic models can be relaxed in the model with unilateral commitments. 
Keywords
Repeated games, Unilateral commitments, Delegation games, Folk theorems, Complete information
