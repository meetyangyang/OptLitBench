Ein kapazitiver enumerationsalgorithmus für lineare opti- mierungsprobleme mit ganzzahligkeitsbedingungen
   E.P.   Beisel ;   M.   Mendel ;
Abstract
 An enumeration algorithm is presented for the solution of (mixed-) integer linear programming problems. It comprises the methods of Land and Doig (1960), Balas (1965) and Dbiebeek (1966) to a common procedure. The algorithm distinguishes itself by numerical stability, general applicability and little need of the central memory.
