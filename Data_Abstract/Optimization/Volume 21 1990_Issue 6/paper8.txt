A schur analysis approach to maximum distance problems in hubert space and prediction
   W.   Apitzsch ;   B.   Fritzsche ;   B.   Kirstein ;
Abstract
 In continuation of former investigations by Ben-Artzi, Ellis, Gohbebg and Lay several versions of maximum distance problems in Hilbert space will be studied with the aid of Schur analysis of non-negative Hermitian block matrices. Moreover, the results will be interpreted in the light of prediction theory. 
Keywords
