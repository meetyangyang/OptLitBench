Approximation of prior estimation games with threshold loss functions by matrix games
   L.B.   Merkulovich ;
Abstract
 The paper concerns the games on the unit square with the payoff functions of the form  where h is a continuous and 1 a threshold function. A method of the approximation of these games by matrix games is provided. The relationship between the solutions of these games and the properties of their values as functions of the parameter a are studied.
