On equivalent quasidifferentials in the two dimensional case
   M.   Handschug ;
Abstract
 The quasidifferential defined by Demyanov in [1] isn't unique. In this paper we give for the two dimensional case an algorithm for the construction of an quasidifferential, which is unique except for displacement and which has some external properties. 
Keywords
