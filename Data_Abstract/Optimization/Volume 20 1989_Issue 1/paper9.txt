On decomposition in bilinear programming
   U.   Würker ;
Abstract
 In this paper, the bilinear optimization, problem and the method of primal decomposition for solving it are considered. The convexity and concavity properties of the arising optimal value function are investigated, and in a special case an algorithm based on the Dantzig-Wolfe decomposition principle is introduced. 
Keywords
