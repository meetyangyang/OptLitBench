Sufficient conditions for the stability of local minimum points in nonsmooth optimization
   M.   Studniarski ;
Abstract
 In this paper a stability theorem of D. H. Hyers [11] is extended to include arbitrary perturbations of the set of feasible points for a nonsmooth mathematical programming problem.
