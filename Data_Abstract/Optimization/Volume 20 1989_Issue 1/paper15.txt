On the dimension of polytopes of planar three-index transportation problems
   A.D.   Korsnikov ;   E.E.   Burkard ;
Abstract
 Polytopes arising from planar three-index transportation problems of order m × n × k are investigated. Theorems concerning the regularity, the dimension and the representability of such polytopes are shown. In particular it is shown that any number of the form  where  occurs as dimension of such a polytope.
