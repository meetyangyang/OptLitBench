Two algorithms to get strong Gomory cuts
   B.   Vlzvári ;
Abstract
 In this paper we investigate two ways to get strong Gomory outs. In Section 1 we give an improved version of Nijenhuis dynamic programming method to solve the diophantine problem of Frorenius and we apply it to determine the rank of a Gomory cut. In Section 2 a new method is given to find a strong cut among the multipliers of a cut. In the last section we illustrate on an example how deep the strong cuts can be. The ex ample is that of Schrijver for showing that a cutting plane proof can have an arbitrary great length.
