A note on multiple stopping rules
 Holger   Kösters ;
Abstract
 We consider multiple stopping problems as introduced by Stadje [W. Stadje (1985). On multiple stopping rules. Optimization, 16, 401–418.]. We provide counterexamples to show that Theorem 1 in [W. Stadje (1985). On multiple stopping rules. Optimization, 16, 401–418.] fails to hold in general, and we prove a corrected version of this theorem. Finally, we explain why similar problems cannot arise in the situation of Theorems 2 and 3 in [W. Stadje (1985). On multiple stopping rules. Optimization, 16, 401–418.]. 
Keywords
k-Stopping problem, Optimal k-stopping problem, St. Petersburg Paradox, Mathematics Subject Classifications 2000: Primary: 60G40, Secondary: 90C39
