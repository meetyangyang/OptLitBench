Solvability of fully nonlinear functional equations involving Erdélyi-Kober fractional integrals on the unbounded interval
JinRong Wang;Chun Zhu;Michal Fečkan;
Abstract
In this paper, we study the existence of solutions to fully nonlinear functional equations involving Erdélyi-Kober fractional integrals on the unbounded interval. By constructing a closed and convex subset of a Fréchet space, sufficient conditions are presented by using a fixed point theorem via the Kuratowski measure. Finally, an example is given to illustrate the obtained result.
