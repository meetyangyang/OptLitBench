On the structure of the maximum entropy extension
   B.   Fritzsche ;   B.   Kirstein ;
Abstract
 In continuation of the papers [10] and [11] a permanence principle for the so called central extension will be verified. Moreover, it will be shown that the maximum entropy extension can be characterized by a slight modification of the notion band extension, too. 
Keywords
