On strong quasiconvex functions and boundedness of level sets
   M.   Jovanović ;
Abstract
 It is shown that the level sets  are bounded for any x∈C if the function f:C → R  is strong quasiconvex and C⊆ R  n is a convex set with nonempty interior. Thus we have generalized one of the results from Vial [3]. 
Keywords
