Ein überlinear konvergentes verfahren mit modifizierten Lagrangefunktionen
   C.   Grossmann ;   C.   Vanselow ;
Abstract
 By means of augmented Lagrangians some dual optimization problem is constructed and the properties of this dual problem are investigated. An approximated Newton's method is applied to solve the senerated auxiliary problems. This paper deals with the investigation of the convergence of the presented algorithm.
