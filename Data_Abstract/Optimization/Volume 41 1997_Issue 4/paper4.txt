Duality for Multiobjective Variational Problems With Pseudo-Invexity
   C.   Nahak ;   S.   Nanda ;
Abstract
 Wolfe and Mond-Weir type duals for multiobjective variational problems are formulated.Under pseudo-invexity assumption on the functions involved Weak and converse duality theorems are proved to relate efficient solutions/properly efficient solutions of the primal and dual problems. A close relationship between these variational problems and non-linear multiobjective prograrnmlng problems is also indicatcd  
Keywords
Variational Problem, Pseudo-Invexity, Efficiency, Proper Efficiency, Duality
