Approximate feasible direction method for stochastic programming problems with recourse. linear inequality deterministic constraints
 Li   Xiaoliu ; Jinde   Wang ;
Abstract
 In this paper we design a new algorithm–approximate feasible direction method, which can be used to solve stochastic programming problems with recourse efficiently, This algorithm is obtained by combining feasible direction method and approximation in stochastic programming. The presented algorithm is completely different from existing methods 
Keywords
Approximate Feasible Direction Method, Stochastic Programs With Recourse, Differential Stability of Function Sequence
