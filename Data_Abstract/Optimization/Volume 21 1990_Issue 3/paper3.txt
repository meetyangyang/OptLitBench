A dual problem for convex programming in ordered vector spaces
 Li   Shizheng ;
Abstract
 In this paper we consider a dual problem of nondifferentiable convex programming in ordered vector spaces which is different from well known dual problems and prove the weak duality and the strong duality. We give the necessary and sufficient conditions of strong duality using saddle point criterion and deduce a converse duality theorem 
Keywords
Saddle Point Criterion, Lagrangian Function, Algebraic Subdifferential, Directional Derivative, Weak Duality, Strong Duality, Slateb’s Condition, Converse Duality
