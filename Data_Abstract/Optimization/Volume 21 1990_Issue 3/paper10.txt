Erweiterung von mehrdimensionalen steuerungspromemen und dualität
   H.   Kraut ;   S.   Piekenhain ;
Abstract
 Multidimensional control problems and corresponding dual problems are investigated. Results about strong duality between dual and generalized control problems are obtained and weak compactness of the set of generalized controls is proved 
Keywords
Multidimensional Optimal Control Problems, Strong Duality, Generalized Control Problems
