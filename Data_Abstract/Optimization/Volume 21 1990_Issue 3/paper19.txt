A new approach for evaluating the Shapely value
   G.   Gambarell ;
Abstract
 A new formula for the Shapley value of a game is given. This formula offers a reduction in the time of computation. A generalization of a result by Shapley on the value as a Barycenter is also given 
Keywords
Barycenter, Shapley Value, Superadditive Games, Subadditive Games
