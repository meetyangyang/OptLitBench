  

Abstract
 In the first part of this article a new method of solution for the games on the unit square with a payoff function depending of the difference of strategies is proposed. In this paper, the solution of games is given, the payoff function of which can be represented in the form of the convolution of two functions of a special type. For the games of the considered class the formulae are given which let obtain the game's value and optimal strategies.
