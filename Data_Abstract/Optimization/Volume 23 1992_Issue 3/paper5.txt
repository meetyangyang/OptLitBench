On a class of continuous games with limited spectrum of optimal strategies
   K. V.   Sagatelian ;
Abstract
 Two-person games at unit square are considered, where payoff functions are the continuation of any Tschebishev functions system. Using one theorem of Krein the existence of discrete optimal strategy is proved. Estimations of quantity of spectrum points and also some examples are given  
Keywords
Tchebishev Systems, Two-Person Games, discrete strategies
