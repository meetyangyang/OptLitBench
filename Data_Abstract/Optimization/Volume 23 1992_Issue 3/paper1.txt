Difference of compact sets in the sense of demyanov and its application to non-smooth analysis
   A.M.   Rubinov ; I.S.   Akhundov ;
Abstract
  Operation of the difference of pairs of convex compacta introduced by Demyanov and the related operation proposed by the authors are investigated. Using these operations the relationship between the Clarke subdifferential and quasidifferential is clarified  
Keywords
Difference of Convex Compacta, The Clarke Subdifferential, Quasidifferential
