Saddle-point-type optimality conditions and lagrangian-type duality for a class of constrained generalized fractional optimal control
   G.J.   Zalmai ;
Abstract
 Kecessary and sufficient saddle-point-type optimality conditions and Lagrangian-type duality relations are established for a class of state- and control-constrained generalized fractional optimal control problems. These optimality and duality criteria also contain.  as special cases. similar results for optimal control problems with fractional. discrete max, and conventional objective functions  
Keywords
Optimal Control, Generalized Fractional Objective Function, Constraints, Saddle Point Optimality Conditions, Lagrangian Dual Problems, Duality Theorems
