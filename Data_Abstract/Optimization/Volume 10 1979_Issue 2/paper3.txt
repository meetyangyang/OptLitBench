Glättung von ungleiehheitsrestriktionen bei konvexen optimierungsproblemen
 Fritz C.   Pohl ;
Abstract
 Considering the convex optimization problem min {f(x)∣, g  i (x)≦0, i=1, …, n} we call a smooth convex function G a convex smoothing, if{x∣G(x)≦0} includes the feasible set, and if the resulting problem min{f(x) ∣ G(x≦0)} is equivalent to the initial problem. A relation is developed to the Lagrangian functions: with the involved multiplier functions we are able to define such convex smoothings in some cases.
