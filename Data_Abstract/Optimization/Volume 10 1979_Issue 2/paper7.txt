Eine yerallgemeinerung der theorie der linearen optimierung
   R.   Thron ;   L.   Teschke ;
Abstract
 Let f: X→Y a function, R a relation defined by R⊂X×X×X and S a partial order in x. To each relation R there exists an R-system consisting of subsets of X with an selected subset X 0. If f possesses the property of (R, S)-maximality the set of S-maxima off(X) is equal to the set of S-maxima of f(X 0. If possesses the property of (R, S)-minimality the set of S-minima of f(X) is equal to the set of S-minima of f(X 0).
