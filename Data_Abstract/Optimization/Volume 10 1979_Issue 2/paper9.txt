Eine existenzbedingung für multiströme
   L.   Hempel ;   D.   Würbach ;
Abstract
 In this paper a necessary and sufficient condition for the existence of a stream in a finite, connected graph is given, when the flow in each are of the graph is to be taken from a convex and closed subset of R  k . The condition is a generalization of the well known condition of A. Hoffman.
