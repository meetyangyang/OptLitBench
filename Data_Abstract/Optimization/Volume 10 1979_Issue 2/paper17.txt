Abschä tzungen der erneueriwgsfunktion
   G.   Siegel ;   S.   Wünsche ;
Abstract
 The authors give a survey on bounds of the renewal function. By means of an integral inequality it is possible to give unknown bounds, weaker conditions and elementary proofs. Further, the regarded bounds can be monotonically improved. Generalizations and applications to other integral equations are given too.
