Stability property of a class of variational inequalities
 Le Dung   Muu ;
Abstract
 For a given multivalued mapping F : X → Y and a function g : X× X → R this paper considers continuity properties of the (multivalued) mapping S(v) : ={[xbar] ∈ F -1(v)|g ([xbar],x) 0 for all x ∈ F -1 (v)}.
