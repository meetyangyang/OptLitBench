ε-optimal control of random parabolic differential equations by an elliptic approximation
   W.   Grecksch ;
Abstract
 We consider an optimal control problem for an abstract ITO equation on a Gelfand triple of Hilbert spaces. This control problem is approximated by means of a family of optimal control problems for elliptic systems  
Keywords
