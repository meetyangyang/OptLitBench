Simultaneous location-allocation of facilities and servers. a generalized heuristic
   A.   Makris ;   G.   Tziafetas ;
Abstract
 This paper is concerned with an extention of the original p-median problem, where, for a given set of ndemand points of a network and a given number of servers Y, the location of pcenters, the simultaneous distribution of servers and the allocation of each of the remaining nodes to just one center, are asked, in order to minimize the aggregate sum of the aggregate travel times (from all nodes to their centers) and the aggregate total waiting and service time at each center. The heuristic proposed in this paper seems to provide more effective solutions than other known methods, at least for some specific problem instances.
