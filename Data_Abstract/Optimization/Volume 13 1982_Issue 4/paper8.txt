Zur Abhängigkeitsfortpflanzung in einlinigen Verlustsystemen
   T.   Raykov ;   W.   Warmuth ;
Abstract
 For the multiple filtering by one server loss systems /GI/1/O deriving explicit formulae it is shown that inter-departure distribution only depends on the one-dimension inter-arrival distribution in the case GI =M respectively on the Palm distribution (with respect to served costumers) of the inter-arrival interval.
