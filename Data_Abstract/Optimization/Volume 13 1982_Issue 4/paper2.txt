Abstract
 In this contribution we are going to discuss the extension of the method of feasible directions which is described for semi-infinite optimization in [8] for to linear continuous Cheese approximation problems. We shall be working with an extension of the sub differential for the nondifferentiable error function which implies the convergence of this method.
