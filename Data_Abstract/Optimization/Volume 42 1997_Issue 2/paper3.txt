Reducing a nonlinear dynamic programming equation to a kolomogorov equation
 Mario.   Lefebvre ;
Abstract
 A theorem that enables us to compute the optimal control of a diffusion process from a mathematical expectation for the uncontrolled process when a certain relation holds is generalized: the optimal control is obtained from a mathematical expectation for the (or another) uncontrolled process, even if the relation in question is not satisfied. The cost criterion takes the risk-sensitivity of the optimizer into account. An example is Presented 
Keywords
Optimal Stochastic Control, Riccati Equation, Risk-Sensitivity, Brownian Motion
