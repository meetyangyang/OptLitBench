Zur lösung linearer ungleichungssysteme bei störimg der rechten seite und der koeffizientenmatrix
   W.   Gerlach ;
Abstract
 A condition is given for the compatibility of a solution of a system of linear inequalities with given error bounds for the coefficients and right hand sides. This condition is a generalization of a lemma of PRAGER/OETTLI. Application of the condition are given for optimization problems with linear inequality constraints and the revised simplex algorithm of linear optimization.
