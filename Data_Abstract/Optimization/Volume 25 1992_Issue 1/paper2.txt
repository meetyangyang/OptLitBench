On the notion of tangent cone in mathematical programming
   G.   Giorgi ;   A.   Guerraggio ; A.   Guerraggio ;
Abstract
 In the present paper, several different definitions of the contingent cone, of the attainable cone and of Clarke's tangent cone are examined in order to state some equivalence relations and the links among them  
Keywords
Tangent Cones, Local Cone Approximations, Nonsmooth Optimization
