Vector approximation problems in geometric vector optimization
   K. H.   Elster ; R.   Elster ;
Abstract
 Using the geometric vector inequality, introduced by Elster/Elster/Gö pfert [9], we consider a special case of that inequality which is a basis for treating certain classes of (nonsmooth) vector optimization problems: vector curve fitting problemslp -vector approximation problems and vector regression problems. By the introduced geometric vector inequality we obtain in a natural way dual problems for such special-structured vector optimization problems, which can be solved in an efficient manner, since the dual constraints are linear.  
Keywords
Vector Optimization, Vector Approximation Problems, Duality, Geometric Vector Inequality, Legendre Vector Transform
