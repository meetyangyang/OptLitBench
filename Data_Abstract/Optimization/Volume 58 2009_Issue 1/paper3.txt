Global optimality conditions for mixed nonconvex quadratic programs†View all notes 
 Z.Y.   Wu ; F.S.   Bai ;
Abstract
 In this article, we present some global optimality conditions for mixed quadratic programming problems. Our approach is based on a L-subdifferential and an associated L-normal cone. Unlike most subdifferentials, the L-subdifferential is formed by functions that are not necessarily linear functions. We derive some sufficient and necessary global optimality conditions for mixed quadratic programs with box constraints and binary constraints.
