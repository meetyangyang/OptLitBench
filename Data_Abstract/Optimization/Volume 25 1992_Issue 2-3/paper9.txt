Solving a class of regular optimal control problems with state constraints by the method of orienting curves
   N.   Dinh ;   H.X   Phu ;
Abstract
 In this paper, the Method of Orienting Curves for solving optimal control problems with state constraints, which was introduced by H. X. Phu in 1987, will be extended for a class of regular optimal control problems with one state and one control variable (with state constraints), where the state equation is linear in the control variable  
Keywords
Optimal Control, Solving Method, Orienting Curve, Final Curve
