Monotone convex and first order maps in optimization of nonsmooth lipschitz functions  ∗  
   A.   Peretti ;   C.   Sutti ;
Abstract
 Looking for optimization of nonsmooth Lipschitz functions, the paper considers quasidifferential and subdifferential mappings for this class of functions. Namely, a generalized subdifferential is introduced for Lipschitz functions and discussed with respect to the Demyanov’s quasi- and the Clarke’s subdifferential. Then a new minimization algorithm is defined and numerical results are reported. Moreover. someaspects of operations and relations among sets. relevant within the nonsmooth analysis, are pointed out  
Keywords
Nonsmooth Optimization, Lipschitz Functions, Quasi-Differentials, Qeneralized Subdifferentials, Minimization Algorithm
