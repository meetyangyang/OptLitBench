 Harald   Günzel ;
Abstract
 A structured jet transversality theorem is presented, dealing with functions possibly depending differently on any variable involved. A method for modelling highly complex situations to fit within the framework of the structured jet transversality theorem is described. Some applications to optimization theory are given.
