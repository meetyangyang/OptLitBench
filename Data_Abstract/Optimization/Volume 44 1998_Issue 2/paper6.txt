The banzhaf – coleman index for games with r alternatives
   R.   Amer ;   F.   Carreras ;   A.   Maga[Nbar]a ;
Abstract
 A natural extension of the Banzhaf – Coleman Index to games with n players and r alternatives is defined using an axiomatic approach. In fact. a game with r alternatives splits into components. and this enables us to introduce r-games and concentrate on them. The computation of the extended index by means of multilinear extensions is also provided and. moreover. both the axiomatic system and the use of multiliner extensions are transferred to Bolger's generalization of the Shapley value Finally, two examples are discussed where r-games natural1y arise  
Keywords
Banzhaf Index, Games With Alternaitves, Multilinear Extension
