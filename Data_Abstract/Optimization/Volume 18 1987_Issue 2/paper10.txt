Generalized Lipschitz-continuity of integrals with respect to a parameter of the intergrating probability measure
   M.   Kolonko ;
Abstract
 For a suitable function f and probability measures Pθ we consider the integrals  as a function of the parameter θ as it often appears e.g. in decision theory. We give conditions (mainly on densities of Pθ) under which is Lipschitz continuous in a generalized sense. The results allow to construct bounds for which depend on θ,θ1 only through the difference |θ-θ'|.  
Keywords
