Zur Lösung eines zermelosehen nayigationsproblems
 Hoá   Xuá Phú ;
Abstract
 In this paper, a new “method of orienting curves” for solving some regular optimal control problems is represented and then applied on the navigation problem of Zermelo for constant wind distribution and restriction of the domain.
