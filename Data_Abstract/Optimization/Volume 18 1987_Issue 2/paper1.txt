Nonconvex theorems of the alternative and minimization
   B.D.   Craven ;   J.   Gwinner ;   V.   Jeyakumar ;
Abstract
 Theorems of the alternative, in particular, Gordan and Motzkin type theorems for non-convex functions are presented, based on the Fuchssterner and König's extended minimax theorem. As applications of these theorems, optimality conditions and duality theorems are obtained for non-convex programming problems.
