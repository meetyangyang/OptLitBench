Partielle linearislerang einer steueraufgabe für eine parabolisehe Itogleiehuug
 Gabriele   Wallura ;   W.   Greoksch ;
Abstract
 We consider a problem of optimal control for a nonlinear Ito-equation with a martingal-term. The original nonlinear problem is approximated by means of a family of linear problems. 
Keywords
Martingale, stochastic integral, evolution equations, optimal control, linearizationAMS 1980 Subject Classification: Primary: 93 E 20, Secondary: 60 H 15
