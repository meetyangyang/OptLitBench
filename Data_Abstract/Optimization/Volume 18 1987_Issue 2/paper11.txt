A unifying diffusion model for state-dependent queues
   T.   Kimura ;
Abstract
 A diffusion model is developed for a class of state-dependent queues arising in computer communications and in switching systems. A diffusion process with piece wise constant infinitesimal parameters is investigated as a unifying approximation model for such queues. Its steady-state probability density is derived in a convenient recursive form for computation. The accuracy and fitness of the model is tested by some computational results.
