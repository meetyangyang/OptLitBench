The linearization method
   B.N   Pshenichnyj ;
Abstract
 The linearization method for solving the general problem of nonlinear programming and its various modifications is considered. The basic algorithm of the method and its variants are constructed. The method convergence is proved, its rate is estimated, the methods providing the superlinear convergence are given.
