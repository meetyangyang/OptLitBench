A method for the solution of fixed charge problems by Benders' decomposition
   J.S.   Mashford ;
Abstract
 A method of solving fixed charge problems by Benders' Decomposition is described. The sub-problem of the Benders' Decomposition process is solved by solving a smaller equivalent problem. Extremal optimal solutions and extreme rays associated with the dual of the sub-problem are obtained from those associated with the smaller problem.
