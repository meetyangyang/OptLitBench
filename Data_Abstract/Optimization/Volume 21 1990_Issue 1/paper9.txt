On the surrogate constraint and the core of a linear mixed-integer 0–1 program with few constraints
   J.   Sommerschuh ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with relaxations of the linear mixed integer 0–1-problem, A heuristic for the reduce of dimension size is given. Furthermore the relation between multipliers defining the relaxations and the objective function value of the relaxations is investigated. 
Keywords
