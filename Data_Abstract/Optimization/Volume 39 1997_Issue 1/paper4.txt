A Modified fixed point iteration method for solving exchange models
   E. I.   Nenakhov ;   M. E.   Primak ;
Abstract
 An exchange model with linear utilities functions is considered. The proposed method for solving this model can produce a divergent sequence. But it can be corrected in such a way that the new sequence approaches solution of the model. A pure exchange model with periodic points has a vector of equilibrium prices  
Keywords
Exchange Model, Total Supply, Budget, Equilibrium Vector, Utility Function, Periodic Point
