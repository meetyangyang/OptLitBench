Abstract
 Two distinct pairs of nondifferentiable minimax mixed integer mathematical programming problems are formulated and symmetric duality theorems are established. Self duality for these pairs has also been discussed  
Keywords
Symmetric Duality, Minimax, Integer Programming Nondifferentiable Programming, Self Duality
