Contex singularly perturbed optimal control problem with fixed final state, controllability and convergence
 Asew L.   Dontchev ; Todob R.   Gioĉev ;
Abstract
 An optimal control problem with singularly perturbed linear dynamics and convex cost is considered. Sufficient conditions for controllability of the perturbed system and uniform convergence of the optimal solution upon the singular parameter are given. Extensions of known results are presented.
