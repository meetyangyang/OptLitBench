Regularlsiernngsmögliclikeiten in der optimierung von nnendlichdimenslonalen linearen systemen 1  
 Eva V.   Nagy ;
Abstract
 The optimization of a functional under restrictions given by an infinite dimensional linear system of differential equations is considered. A method is given for construction of an optimal process.  At first, the given problem is transformed by means of the ε-method by Balakrishanan into a nonrestricted extremum problem. By the application of a generalization of the regularization method by Tychonow with respect to this (non-correctly formulated) problem a method can be given which generates the optimal process as a limit of norm-convergent sequences.  Following this a result obtained by De Julio is generalized.
