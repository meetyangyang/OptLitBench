 A p-committee (where O≦p≦1) for the system  of sets Dj  on the class Y is a finite set K ⊂ Y such that  where |K| denotes the number of members of K.  A review of results for the method of committees is given: definitions of committee constructions for systems of sets and inequalities, definition of discriminant committees and existence theorems of committee constructions. Applications of committee constructions are given in pattern recognition, in decision making problems with contradictory systems of restrictions and with multicriterial objective functions.
