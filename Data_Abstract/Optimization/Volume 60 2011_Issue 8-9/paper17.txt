A new duality theory for mathematical programming
 B.F.   Svaiter ;
Abstract
 A new duality theory for mathematical programming is presented. Dualization is performed using sets of functions from the primal space into the extended real numbers. A Lagrangian is proposed and generalized KKT conditions are discussed. As an application, the theory is used to design a new strongly convergent algorithm for finding a zero of a maximal monotone operator. A formalization of the theory via generalized conjugations is also presented.
