Equilibrium problems and generalized Nash games
 Mostafa   Nasri ; Wilfredo   Sosa ;
Abstract
 In this article, we use Ky Fan's lemma to obtain a new existence result for equilibrium problems which does not invoke monotonicity of the objective function. Then we focus on reformulating of generalized Nash equilibrium problems (GNEPs) in terms of an equilibrium problem under reasonable assumptions. Finally, using our existence result and the reformulation, we obtain an existence result for GNEPs. 
Keywords
equilibrium problem, generalized Nash equilibrium problem, Ky Fan's lemma, point-to-set operator
