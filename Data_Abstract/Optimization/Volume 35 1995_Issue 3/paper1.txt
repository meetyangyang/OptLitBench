A Generalization of the lasry-lions method for functions in reflexive banach spaces
   H.   Dietrich ;
Abstract
 The Lasry–Lions regularization method is extended to arbitrary functions in reflexive Banach spaces The regularization effect of the method arises from the Fréchet-differentiability of the conjugate f ∗ for an uniformly convex function f in a reflexive Banach space By means of Toland s and Singers duality theory one can derive the first and second regularization of an arbitrary function in a natural way  
Keywords
Moreau-Yosida Approximation, Regularization Approximation, D c Functions, Duality Theory of Toland and Singer
