Optimal control of a setvalued stochastic dynamic system
   H.   Fritsch ;
Abstract
 For a controlled stochastic dynamic system with a setvalued drift coefficient a necessary optimality condition is deduced. Assuming the property of maximal monotonicity for the multifuntion going into the drift we show the uniquencess of the solution of our system  
Keywords
Controlled Stochastic Differential Inclusion, Stochastic Minimum Principle, Optimal Control, Maximan Monotonicity
