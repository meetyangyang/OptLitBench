Morse Theory And Invariant Concepts of Convexity
 B.D.   Craven ; V.   Gershkovich ; D.   Ralph ;
Abstract
 In optimization, the literature on invariant definitions of convexity has largely centered on the notion of invexity. However, the connection with Morse theory and related geometric trends has not been recognized and results do not appear to be as impressive as those in topology. Our purpose is to improve the situation in optimization, and to clarify the relationship between these two trends. The main result is that the problem can be reduced to the case of functions of one variable.  
Keywords
Convexity, Invexity, Morse theory, Optimization
