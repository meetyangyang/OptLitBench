Transient Analysis of Multiple Unit Redundant Systems—A Continued Fraction Approach
 P.R.   Parthasarathy ; V.   Vijayalakshmi ;
Abstract
 Important characteristics of the multiple unit redundant repairable system like availability, reliability and mean time of system failure are discussed through computational approach of continued fractions. The effectiveness of the technique is demonstrated by tables and graphs. The steady state behaviour is also discussed.  
Keywords
Multiple redundant systems, Reliability, Eigen values, Symmetric tridiagonal matrix
