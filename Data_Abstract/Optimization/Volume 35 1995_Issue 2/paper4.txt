An Optimal Broyden Updating Formula And Its Application To Nonliner Least Squares
 C.-X.   Xu ; X.-F.   Ma ;
Abstract
 In this paper an optimal quasi-Newton updating formula in the positive definite Broyden family is presented. The optimal parameter is determined by minimizing the function  for positive definite matrices A. It is proved that the function c(A) is strictly convex for positive definite matrices and that the optimal quasi-Newton formula is another self-dual updating formula. The application of the optimal updating formula to factorized quasi-Newton updating formulae for nonlinear least squares is considered and numerical results of the optimal factorized quasi-Newton method for standard test nonlinear least squares problems are presented.  
Keywords
Quasi-Newton method, Self-dual, Optimal updating, Factorized quasi-Newton method, Nonlinear least squares
