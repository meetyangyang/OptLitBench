Minimal doubly resolving sets of prism graphs
 Mirjana   Čangalović ; Jozef   Kratica ; Vera   Kovačević-Vujčić ; Milica   Stojanović ;
Abstract
 In this paper, we consider the problem of determining the minimal cardinality of double resolving sets for prism graphs . It is proved that the minimal cardinality is equal to four if  is even and equal to three if  is odd.
