Parametric properties of the transportation problem and relations to supermatroids
   R.   Hildenbrandt ;
Abstract
 The changes of optimal slack-variables and optimal dual variables under the assumption of parametric changes of requirements or availabilities are investigated for non–balanced transportation problems  The “set of optimal slack-vectors” results in a set of maximal elements of a poly(super-) matroid for a (integer) transportation problem  
Keywords
Transportation Problem, Parametric Relations, Slack-Variables, Supermatroid (Polymatroid), Distance Properties
