Arcwise connected functions and applications in multiobjective optimization
   R. N.   Mukherjee ;
Abstract
 We have given a generalization of arcwise connected functions, the concept of which was originally introduced by Avriel and Zang, taking values in the Euclidean Space with a given preference order. Such a generalization has the potential of obtaining local-global optimal properties of solutions of the multiple objective optimization problem in a more general setting. The results of this work can be specialized to give quite a few earlier results on such functions used in the case of single objective optimization problem obtained by several authors mentioned in the introduction  
Keywords
Arcwise Connected Functions, Preference Order, Multiple Objective Optimization
