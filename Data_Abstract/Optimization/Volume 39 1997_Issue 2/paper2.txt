Sufficiency criteria and duality in complex nonlinear programming involving pseudoinvex function
   J. C.   Liu ;
Abstract
 Necessary conditions for optimality in polyhedral-cone constrained nonlinear complex programs are shown to be sufficient under the assumption of a particular form of generalized invexity. Duality results are thus extended for a Mond-Weir type dual 
Keywords
Complex Nonlinear Programing, Pseudo-Invex, Quasi-Invex, Duality
