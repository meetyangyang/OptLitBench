Ordinally Quasiconcave Functions §  
   M.   Cigola ; M.   Licalzi ;
Abstract
 A traditional assumption in the definiion of generalized concave function f X→Y is that both X and Y are subsets of vector spaces. often endowed with some topology. We define and study six classes of quasiconcave functions arising when the algebric and topological structures of X and Y are replaced with partial or complete orders  
Keywords
Ordinal Quasiconcavity, Generalized Quasiconcavity, Partiality Ordered Sets, Lattices
