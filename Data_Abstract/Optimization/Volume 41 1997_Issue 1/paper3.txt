A Calculation of all Separating Hyperplanes of two Convex Polytopes
   L.   Grygarová ;
Abstract
 Separation theorem is a basic theorem of convex analysis and convex programming. Its applicability to nonlinear programming methods depends on whether we can describe a separating hyperplane of two or more convex sets. A characterisation and simple description of all separating hyperplanes of two convex polytopes is given in this article  
Keywords
Separating Hyperplane, Convex Polytope, Projection Cone, Polar Cone
