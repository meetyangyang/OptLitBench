Genetic Optimization of Supports in Vibrating Structures
 J.L.   Marcelin ;
Abstract
 This work gives an example of the application of stochastic techniques (genetic algorithms) to the optimization of supports in vibrating structures. It describes a combined genetic algorithm and finite element method for obtaining the optimal boundary conditions (with respect to their type and location) of vibrating structures. Optimization consists of seeking the optimal nodes of the finite element grid to ensure an optimal location of supports with the objective of either maximum frequency or maximum difference between two consecutive frequencies. 
Keywords
Vibrations, Supports, Structural Optimization, Genetic Algorithms
