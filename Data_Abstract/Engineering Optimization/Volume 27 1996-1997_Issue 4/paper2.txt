INVENTORY SYSTEMS FOR DETERIORATING ITEMS TAKING ACCOUNT OF TIME VALUE
 KUN-JEN   CHUNG ; JOSEPH   LIU ; SUI-FU   TSAI ;
Abstract
 This paper discusses the inventory replenishment policy over a finite planning horizon for a deteriorating item taking account of time value and presents a line search technique to decide the optimal interval which has positive inventories. Numerical examples are given to explain how the line search technique works. 
Keywords
EOQ models, deterioration, shortages, discounted cash flow
