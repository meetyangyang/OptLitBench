PARETO OPTIMAL SERIAL DYNAMIC PROGRAMMING
 M. A.   ROSENMAN ; J. S.   GERO ;
Abstract
 A method for generating the Pareto optimal set, or an approximation to it for multi-criteria (multi-objective) problems capable of being formulated as serial stage-state discrete dynamic programs, is presented. It is shown that this set may be produced by using Pareto optimality as the optimization selection mechanism in dynamic programming, Worked examples are presented.
