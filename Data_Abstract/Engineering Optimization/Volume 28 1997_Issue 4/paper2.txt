EFFICIENT ROUTING OF SERVICE VEHICLES
 MICHEL   GENDREAU ; GILBERT   LAPORTE ; SYLVAIN   YELLE ;
Abstract
 Are routing problems arise in a number or settings where vehicles must traverse streets for the purpose of maintenance, garbage collection, snow plowing, etc. Such problems can often be solved as node routing problems through a suitable graph transformation. The paper discusses the solution of some practical problems using this type of methodology. 
Keywords
Routing, networks, service vehicles
