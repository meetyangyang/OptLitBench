COMPUTATIONAL ASPECTS OF GEOMETRIC PROGRAMMING 1.INTRODUCTION AND BASIC NOTATION
 A. B.   TEMPLEMAN ;
Abstract
 This paper briefly summarizes the history of geometric programming and shows that efficient computational algorithms are necessary if GP is to retain its place as a major tool for optimal engineering design. Basic notation is established for use by the authors of the succeeding papers in this issue of the journal which is wholly devoted to the algorithmic and computational aspects of geometric programming.
