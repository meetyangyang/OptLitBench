FORECASTING THE LOAD DURATION CURVE USING BOX-JENKINS TIME SERIES ANALYSIS
 NOEL D.   URI ; JOHN S.   MAYBEE ;
Abstract
 This paper endeavors to develop a forecasting model for discrete approximations to the load duration curve using Box-Jenkins time series analysis. A model is structured and estimated for six, twenty. and fifty discrete approximations for two electric utility regions in the United States. The forecast results are extremely good. indicating that while a larger number of approximations performs marginally better, there is nothing to mitigate using only a six-step approximation.
