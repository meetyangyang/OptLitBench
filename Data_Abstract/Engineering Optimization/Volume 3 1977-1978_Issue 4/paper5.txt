RELIABILITY—CONSTRAINED OPTIMUM STATIC DESIGN FOR RANDOM LOAD SEQUENCES
 K. V.   BURY ;
Abstract
 This paper considers the optimum static design of a device or structure that is exposed to a finite sequence or loads. The number of loads in that sequence is assumed known. Both loads and relevant material properties are treated as random variables. Information is assumed available to specify the distribution of load values, and the first two moments of the material property distribution. The optimization problem is stated as a weight minimization subject to a constraint on the device's probability of mission survival. A simple formula is developed that relates device reliability to an optimal, dimensional design quantity. The method is illustrated by a stability problem of a structural element.
