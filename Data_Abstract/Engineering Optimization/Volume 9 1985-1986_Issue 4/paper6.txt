A NOTE: EFFECTS OF LOGARITHMIC TRANSFORMATIONS OF NONLINEAR GOALS IN THE GOAL PROGRAMMING PROBLEM
 CARLOS   ROMERO ; FRANCISCO   AMADOR ;
Abstract
 This note points out that in the context of goal programming the linearization of nonlinear goals by logarithmic transformations, as it is usually done in the field of mechanical engineering applications, is not in general a valid method and can lead to wrong results. 
Keywords
goal programming, nonlinear programming
