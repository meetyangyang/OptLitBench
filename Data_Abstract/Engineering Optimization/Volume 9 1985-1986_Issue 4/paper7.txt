AN IMPROVED TEMPLEMAN'S ALGORITHM FOR THE OPTIMUM DESIGN OF TRUSSES WITH DISCRETE MEMBER SIZES
 DUAN   MING-ZHU ;
Abstract
 The algorithm for the optimum design of trusses using discrete member sizes which was given by Templeman and Yates7 is improved in this paper. A 0-1 simplex method is proposed and some numerical examples are reported. 
Keywords
trusses, optimum design, discrete variables, linear programming
