OPTIMAL DESIGN OF STRUCTURES WITH LARGE DEFORMATIONS ALLOWED FOR
 KRZYSZTOF   SZUWALSKI ;
Abstract
 The paper points out the necessity of taking into account large deformations in the process of optimal design, whenever they may be expected. The problem is presented via two examples of bars under nonuniform tension and of a Mises truss. Two cases of the constitutive laws: independent of time, and rheological, are considered, 
Keywords
Structural optimization, large deformations, rheology
