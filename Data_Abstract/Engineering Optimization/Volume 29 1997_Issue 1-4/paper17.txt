EIGENPAIR SENSITIVITY OF MEMBRANE WITH RESPECT TO PERTURBATION OF BOUNDARY SHAPE
 ZHONG-SHENG   LIU ;
Abstract
 This paper presents a method to evaluate the eigenpair sensitivity of a membrane with respect to perturbation of the boundary shape. Analytic expressions for eigenpair sensitivity are obtained. Treatment of the boundary condition is facilitated by using a δ-function. An example of a rectangular membrane is given to illustrate the method and its validity. 
Keywords
Sensitivity, eigenvalue, eigenfunction, boundary shape, perturbation
