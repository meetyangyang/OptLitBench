DYNAMICS AND CONTROL OF SPACE STRUCTURES
 V. B.   VENKAYYA ; V. A.   TISCHLER ; N. S.   KHOT ;
Abstract
 Vibration control as applied to large space structures is the subject or this study. A number of computational issues have to be resolved before integrating the disciplines of optimal structural design and modern control theory. Control of free-free structures and the effects of model order reduction are the two issues addressed in this paper. Results are presented for a two dimensional truss structure. 
Keywords
Space structures, dynamics, control, optimization, active control, free-free structures
