OPTIMUM DESIGN OF ELASTIC PLATES FOR A GIVEN DYNAMIC DEFLECTION
 FUAT   ERBATUR ;
Abstract
 This paper is concerned with the minimum-weight design of elastic plates subjected to harmonically varying dynamic loading, under a deflection constraint. In the derivation of the optimality condition a generalized form of the principle of stationary mutual potential energy including the inertial force has been used. Numerical results are presented for simply supported circular plates under rotationally symmetric loading.
