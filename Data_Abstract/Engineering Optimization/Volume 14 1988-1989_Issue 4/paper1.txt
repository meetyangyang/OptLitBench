THE EFFECTIVENESS OF FOURIER SERIES IN OPTIMAL DESIGN FOR TRANSIENT RESPONSE
 W. R.   SPILLERS ; Y.   TEBOUB ; F.   ROFOOEI ;
Abstract
 This paper attempts to assess the effectiveness of the method of truncated Fourier series in problems of structural optimization. That is done by studying the transient vibrations for a shear beam subjected to a uniform load which is applied as a step function in time. First, second, and third order solutions are constructed; their performance is compared to “exact” solutions obtained by numerical integration. 
Keywords
 Structural optimization, Fourier series, vibrations, beams, transient response
