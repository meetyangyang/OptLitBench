OPTIMIZATION OF STRUCTURES WITH REPEATED NORMAL-MODE FREQUENCIES
 P.   BARTHOLOMEW ; N.   PITCHER ;
Abstract
 Problems encountered in the optimization of structures with repeated eigenvalues are introduced in the context of a simple system. The indeterminacy of the normal modes of vibration is shown to prevent update methods based on first derivatives of eigenvalues from converging to the correct optimum. It is then shown how this can be overcome by introducing an additional constraint on the optimization problem. Appropriate optimally conditions are derived by the method of Lagrange multipliers, and solved by an adaptation of Newton's method. Computed results indicate the effectiveness of the resulting method.
