OPTIMUM CIRCULAR SHAFTS IN FORWARD PRECESSION
 R.D.   PARBERY B ; L.   KARIHALOO ;
Abstract
 The problem of a rotating shaft carrying a disc of given geometry at one end is solved to find the distribution of the material along its length that gives the highest natural frequency of forward precessional vibrations. It is shown that optimization can lead to a subslanial increase in the frequency in comparison with a uniform circular shaft of equal volume. 
Keywords
 rotating shaft, disc, forward precession, optimum fundamental natural frequency
