THE NONINFERIOR SET ESTIMATION (NISE) METHOD FOR THREE OBJECTIVE PROBLEMS
 M.   BALACHANDRAN ; J. S.   GERO ;
Abstract
 The noninferior set estimation (NISE) method is a powerful technique for the generation of the Pareto optimal set for multicriteria optimization problems with two dimensions. This paper extends the NISE method to three-objective problems. The concepts which allow this extension are presented and an algorithm described. The method is applied to a problem drawn from the literature to produce numerical results.
