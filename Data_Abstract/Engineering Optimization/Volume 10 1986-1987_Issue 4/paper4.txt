COST OPTIMIZATION OF INTZE TANKS ON SHAFTS USING NONLINEAR PROGRAMMING
 MANU   SAXENA ; S. P.   SHARMA ; C.   MOHAN ;
Abstract
 Minimum cost design parameters for container-shaft-foundation systems of Intze type water tanks of various capacities are obtained using a computational technique of nonlinear optimization in conjunction with a computer code developed by SERC, Roorkee for the design of Intze tanks. 
Keywords
Structures, tanks, reinforced concrete, design, nonlinear programming, optimization
