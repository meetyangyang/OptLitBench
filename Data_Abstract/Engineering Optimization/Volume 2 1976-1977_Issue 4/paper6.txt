ON THE PERFORMANCE OF A COMBINED GENERALIZED REDUCED GRADIENT AND DAVIDON-FLETCHER-POWELL ALGORITHM: GRGDFP
 LEO J.   LAFRANCE ; J. F.   HAMILTON ; K. M.   RAGSDELL ;
Abstract
 An algorithm for the constrained non-linear programming problem is described. The algorithm, GRGDFP, is based upon the Generalized Reduced Gradient Method of Abadie1 and employs the Davidon-Fletcher-Powell2 Method for the unconstrained optimization. The performance of the method on the Eason and Fenton,3 and Colville4 test problems is reported. GRGDFP is shown to be one of the most efficient algorithms currently available.
