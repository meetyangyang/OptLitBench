CONTROLLED WEAR PROCESSES: A RISK-SENSITIVE FORMULATION
 MARIO   LEFEBVRE ; JOHN   GASPO ;
Abstract
 Optimal control problems set up for wear models by Rishel are given a risk-sensitive formulation. An example is presented and explicitly solved. Moreover, the critical value of the risk parameter is computed. 
Keywords
 Optimal control, risk sensitivity, tribology, stochastic differential equation, Brownian motion
