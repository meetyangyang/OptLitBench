MODELING AND SOLUTIONSTRATEGIES FOR HIERARCHICAL DESIGN OPTIMIZATION
 ALEJANDRO R.   DÍAZ ; BRADLEY   BELDING ;
Abstract
 Amodeling strategy for optimization or engineering design problems decomposed inhierarchical fashion ispresented. The decomposition issuch that each level inthe hierarchy corresponds to a different level of detail in thedesign description. Thehighestlevel involves optimization of gross measures of designperformance while lowerlevels correspond to optimization of increasingly more detailedaspectsof the design. The modeling is based onfuzzy set theory, whichprovides the operational connection between problems atdifferent levels. A formal optimizationalgorithm to solve the decomposed problem is presented.An example is provided for illustration. 
Keywords
 Multilevel system optimization, hierarchical decomposition, fuzzy optimization.
