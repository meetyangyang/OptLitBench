OPTIMALITY CONDITIONS FOR TRUSSES WITH NONZERO MINIMUM CROSS-SECTIONS
 GEIR A.   GUNNLAUGSSON ;
Abstract
 This paper is concerned with the optimal design of an elastic truss carrying a single system of loads, and subject to stress constraints and nonzero minimum stiffness constraints. Necessary and sufficient conditions for local optimality of a fully stressed design are derived by the method of Lagrange multipliers. The form of the optimality conditions for some special cases of the general problem are considered and their application is illustrated by examples.
