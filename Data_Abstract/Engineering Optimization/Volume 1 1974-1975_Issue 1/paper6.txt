THE CIRIA OPTIMIZATION STUDY OF SEWAGE TREATMENT
 K.   BOWDEN ; D. E.   WRIGHT ;
Abstract
 This paper describes a feasibility study carried out under contract to the Construction Industry Research and Information Association (CIRIA) by the Local Government Operational Research Unit (LGORU) between June 1971 and December 1972. The aim of the feasibility study was to examine whether a mathematical model could be built that would enable the optimal (i.e. most cost-effective) design of a sewage treatment works to be found for a prescribed standard of effluent. The paper outlines the underlying principles of a simple prototype model that was constructed during the study. The paper is a condensed version of the full report on the study issued by CIRIA.
