MIXED INTEGER NON-LINEAR PROGRAMMING AND ITS APPLICATION TO THE MANAGEMENT OF UTILITY NETWORKS
 B.   KALITVENTZEFF ;
Abstract
 This paper will focus on the application of MINLP (Mixed Integer Non Linear Programming). For the sake of application, we have chosen the utility network management problem which is important lo mechanical and chemical engineers. 
Keywords
 Utility networks, chemical plants, heat exchangers, mixed integer variables, non-linear programming
