DUALITY-BASED DECOMPOSITION METHODS FOR OPTIMAL OPERATION OF THE EDF GENERATION SYSTEM
 P.   SANDRIN ;
Abstract
 The paper surveys operations planning methods for power generation used by Electricité de France. Decomposition of the generation system into sub-units and the coordination of the sub-units is planned by means of Lagrangian dual methods. The paper describes these methods and current research into developing their accuracy and efficiency.
