MINIMUM COST SOLAR THERMAL ELECTRIC POWER SYSTEMS: A DYNAMIC PROGRAMMING BASED APPROACH
 WILLIAM S.   DUFF   Assistant Professor ;
Abstract
 A dynamic programming methodology is presented for finding minimum cost solar thermal electric power systems ’(STEPS). The objective of the study for which this technique was developed was to identify designs of STEPS concepts that were most promising in terms of projected cost per kilowatt hour of electricity generated.
