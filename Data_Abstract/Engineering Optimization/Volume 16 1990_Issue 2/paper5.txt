OPTIMAL I-SECTION OF AN ELASTIC ARCH UNDER STABILITY CONSTRAINTS
 BOGDAN   BOCHENEK ; MICHAL   YCZKOWSKI ;
Abstract
 The optimal I-section dimensions of a prismatic circular arch loaded by uniform radial pressure are sought with a view to maximize the lowest critical load for a given total arch volume. Both overall and local instability are considered. A so-called bound formulation of the min-max problem is applied and the resulting mathematical programming problem is solved using the Method of Moving Asymptotes. Numerical results are presented. 
Keywords
 Structural optimization, arches, stability, buckling
