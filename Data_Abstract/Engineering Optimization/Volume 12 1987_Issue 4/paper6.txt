A HEURISTIC ALGORITHM FOR THE TRAVELLING SALESMAN PROBLEM WITH TIME-VARYING TRAVEL COSTS
 ATTAHIRU   SULE   ALFA ;
Abstract
 A heuristic algorithm for the travelling salesman problem with time-varying travel costs is developed in this paper. The algorithm is based on the insertion technique. Illustrative examples are solved using the algorithm. The algorithm does not demand high computation time or storage. 
Keywords
travelling salesman problem, time-varying travel costs, heuristics, vehicle routing
