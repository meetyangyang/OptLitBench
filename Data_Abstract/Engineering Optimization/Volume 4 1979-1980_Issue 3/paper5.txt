TECHNICAL NOTE: LYAPUNOV THEORY IN STRUCTURAL DESIGN
 D. G.   CARMICHAEL ;
Abstract
 The design of structures to a given performance or response index while ensuring certain dynamic stability properties may be based on Lyapunov's second or direct method. For structures subject to initial disturbances and modelled in state equation form, the stiffness/damping/mass requirements may be optimally chosen according to an integral square error (or deviation) criterion.
