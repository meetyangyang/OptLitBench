OPTIMAL DESIGN OF CIRCULAR PLATES AGAINST CREEP BUCKLING
 ADAM   WRÓBLEWSKI ;
Abstract
 The paper deals with the problem of optimal design of circular plates against creep buckling. The design variable, i.e. the plate thickness function, is determined so as to minimize the total volume of a plate under given external load (radial pressure) and critical lime. An axisymmetric thickness function is analysed bul unsymmetric buckling modes are also admitted. The problem is solved by the use of Pontryagin's maximum principle. 
Keywords
 Structural optimization, plates, buckling, creep
