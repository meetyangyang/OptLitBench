OPTIMAL PRODUCTION POLICY FOR PERISHABLE ITEMS WITH PARTIAL BACKORDERING
 H. M.   WEE ;
Abstract
 The optimal production lot-size policy for perishable items has been developed for a situation when shortage is partially buckordered and time is treated as a discrete variable. The total cost consists of cost due to deterioration, ordering cost, holding cost, cost due to backordering and cost due to lost sales. A numerical example, solved by a computer search approach, is shown to illustrate the derived model 
Keywords
 Optimal lot-size, discrete-in-time, deterioration, partial backordering
