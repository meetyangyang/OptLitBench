OPTIMUM DESIGN OF SHOCK AND VIBRATION ISOLATION SYSTEMS USING GAME THEORY
 S. S.   RAO ; S. K.   HATI ;
Abstract
 The multicriteria optimization a shock and vibration isolation systems is considered. The concept of cooperative solution from game theory is used for trading of one criterion versus another. Results are obtained for a three degree-of-freedom system with an exponentially decaying type base disturbance. A single degree-of-freedom system is also considered to evaluate the relative worth of a multidegree-of-freedom system.
