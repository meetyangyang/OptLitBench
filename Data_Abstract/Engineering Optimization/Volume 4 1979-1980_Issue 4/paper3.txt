MINIMUM WEIGHT DESIGN OF BEAM-COLUMNS
 B. L.   KARIHALOO ;
Abstract
 The paper considers the minimum weight design of elastic, pin-ended members which are acted upon simultaneously by an axial compression and a transverse midspan concentrated load. The design has to be such that the midspan transverse deflection does not exceed a prescribed value. Simple approximate analytical expressions for the minimum weight design are given for members of sandwieb and solid construction. It is shown that substantial material economy is achieved by optimization.
