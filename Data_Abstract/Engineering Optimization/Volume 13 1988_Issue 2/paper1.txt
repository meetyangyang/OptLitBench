MAXIMIZATION OF NATURAL FREQUENCIES OF CYLINDRICAL SHELLS
 DAVID P.   THAMBIRATNAM ; V.   THEVENDRAN ; S. L.   CHANG ; S. L.   LEE ;
Abstract
 Maximization of the natural frequencies of a cylindrical shell is treated herein by considering the lowest frequency corresponding to an axisymmetric mode. For a given volume of the shell, the inner radius is kept constant, while piecewise linear variation of the outer radius is allowed. Results indicate considerable elevation in the frequency. The problem of determining the minimum volume for a prescribed frequency is also treated and the study shows that a reasonable amount of saving in material is possible. A numerical procedure incorporating the complex optimization technique and the finite element method is used. 
Keywords
Structural optimization, cylinders, shells, vibrations, natural frequencies, finite elements
