COMPUTATIONAL ASPECTS OF THE GRADIENT PROJECTION METHOD FOR TRUSS OPTIMIZATION
 C.   LAKSHMANA   RAO ; C. S.   KRISHNAMOORTHY ;
Abstract
 The State-Space Gradient Projection Method is a versatile method for structural optimization. The convergence or otherwise of the: above algorithm is a function of many optimization parameters that are used in the algorithm. An attempt has been made to evolve suitable guidelines to the choice of these parameters for easy applicability to real life truss structures. 
Keywords
computers, nonlinear programming, Structural optimization, trusses
