CELLULAR ARRANGEMENT PROBLEMS WITH RANDOM FLOWS
 J.   MACGREGOR   SMITH ;
Abstract
 In VLSI design, manufacturing systems, architectural floorplan generation, urban and suburban neighborhood configurations, the problem of integrating the layout of cell nodes and rouling links is extremely complex. In this paper, configuration of the topology of a set of cells in the plane where random ( lows occur between the cells is analyzed. The problem is formulated as a Quadratic Set Packing Problem on a graph and a branch and bound algorithm together with supporting computational experience is presented. 
Keywords
 Cellular arrangement, quadralic assignment, stochastic networks
