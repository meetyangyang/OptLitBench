MICHELL FRAMEWORK FOR A FORCE IN ANY DEFINITE DIRECTION AT THE MID-POINT BETWEEN TWO SUPPORTS
 W. S.   HEMP ;
Abstract
 A half-plane Michell framework is derived for the title problem. This generalizes the solution given by A. G. M. Michell for the case when the force is at right-angles to the line of supports. The volume of the optimum structure is determined as a function of the angle between the force and the normal to the line of the supports.
