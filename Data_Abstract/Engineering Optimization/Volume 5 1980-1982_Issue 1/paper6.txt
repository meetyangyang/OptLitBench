A NUMERICAL PROCEDURE FOR THE SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS OF GENERALIZED GEOMETRIC PROGRAMMING PROBLEMS
 C.   MOHAN ; ABBAS Y. E.   Al-BAYATY ;
Abstract
 In Ihis paper a numerical procedure is presented which can be used to carry out the sensitivity analysis in the case of generalized geometric programming problems. The present procedure is a generalization of the procedure proposed by Dinkel and Kochenberger2 for the sensitivity analysis of geometric programming problems. The numerical procedure presented in the paper has been applied to a few illustrative problems and its limitations and scope discussed.
