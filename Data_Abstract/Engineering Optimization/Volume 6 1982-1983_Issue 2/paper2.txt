THE SOLUTION OF LARGE-SCALE GAS PIPELINE DESIGN PROBLEMS
 F. I.   SOLIMAN ; B. A.   MURTAGH ;
Abstract
 The solution of large-scale gas pipeline design problems involving compressible fluid flow through tree networks is described. The problems are posed as large-scale nonlinear optimization problems. Two design situations are considered: with and without the possibility of compressors in the network. Computational experience on a number of problems is discussed.
