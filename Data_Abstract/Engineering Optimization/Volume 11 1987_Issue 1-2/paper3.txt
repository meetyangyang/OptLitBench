OPTIMIZATION OF DIES FOR AXISYMMETRIC COLD FORWARD EXTRUSION
 SØREN   GYDE   THOMSEN ;
Abstract
 Breakdown of tools for cold forging is often very expensive. Therefore, we will show in this paper how tools for axisymmetric cold forward extrusion can be optimized with respect to tool life. First, a design criterion based on fracture mechanics is formulated. For the stress and design sensitivity analysis a finite difference scheme is adopted and the optimization problem is solved through a sequence of linear programming problems. It is shown that this approach leads to designs with improved properties with respect to fracture strength compared to tools designed on the basis of traditional calculation methods. 
Keywords
 Cold forging, die design, optimization, tool life.
