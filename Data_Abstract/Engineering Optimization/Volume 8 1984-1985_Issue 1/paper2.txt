STRUCTURAL SYNTHESIS WITH REDUCED DESIGN SPACE
 LI   WEIJI ;
Abstract
 The proposed method of this paper is based on a combination of the mathematical programming method and the optimality criteria method. It attempts to give satisfactory approximate engineering optima of large-scale structures. The concept of reduced design space is adopted to reduce time-consuming computation. The dimensionality of the reduced design space is made equal to the number of behavioral aspects of the structural system. In reduced design space the feasible direction method is employed to optimize structures and only the most critical constraint is considered, therefore, the calculation is much simplified. Basis vectors relate the original design variables to the new independent design variables in reduced design space. The quality of the basis vectors should be as good as possible and their generation should not be time-consuming. The simplified optimality criteria method is used to generate basis vectors. The method is effective for not only truss structures but also wing box structures of fixed geometry under multiple load cases and with stress, displacement and minimum-size constraints.
