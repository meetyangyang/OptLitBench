TIMING PLANS FOR A SIGNALIZED INTERSECTION DURING THE PEAK PERIOD
 ATTAHIRU   SULE   ALFA ; MINGYUAN   CHEN ;
Abstract
 A procedure is presented for determining signal timing plans for a signalized intersection during the peak period when vehicle arrivals are time-dependent. It considers each arm of the intersection as an infinite dam and then extends some of the existing results to evaluate queue lengths. An optimization model is presented and a heuristic searching approach is then used for determining the signal timing plans 
Keywords
 Traffic signals, timing plans, signalized intersection, queueing
