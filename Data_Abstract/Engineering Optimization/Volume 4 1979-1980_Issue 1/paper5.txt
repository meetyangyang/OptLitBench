A DUAL BOUND USED FOR MONITORING STRUCTURAL OPTIMIZATION PROGRAMS
 P.   BARTHOLOMEW ;
Abstract
 A linear programming method is employed to provide an initial active set of constraints for a class of structural optimization problem and to create lower bound estimates of the optimal weight. Results are presented which illustrate how the method may be used to give initial dual variables for an optimality criterion routine and to monitor subsequent progress of the optimization.
