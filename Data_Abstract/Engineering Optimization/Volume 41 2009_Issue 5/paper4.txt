The aerodynamic shape optimization of airfoils using unconstrained trust region methods
 Jaehun   Lee ; Kyungjin   Jung ; Jang   Hyuk   Kwon ;
Abstract
 In this article, unconstrained trust region methods are studied and applied in aerodynamic shape optimization. Trust region methods are high-performance gradient-based optimization methods. The theory of unconstrained trust region methods is explained, and a trust region subproblem is solved using the dogleg method and the Moré–Sorensen method. After verifying the trust region methods via analytical test problems, they are applied to the aerodynamic shape design optimization for transonic and subsonic flows. 
Keywords
aerodynamic shape optimization, trust region method, dogleg method, Moré–Sorensen method
