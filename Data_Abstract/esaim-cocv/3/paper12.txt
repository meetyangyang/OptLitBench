Controllability and observability of linear delay systems: an algebraic approach
M. Fliess1 and H. Mounier2;
Abstract
        (fliess@cmla.ens-cachan.fr)       2         (mounier@ief.u-psud.fr)       AbstractInterpretations of most existing controllability and observability notions for linear delay systems are given. Module theoreticcharacterizations are presented. This setting enables a clear and precise comparison of the various examined notions. A new notion ofcontrollability is introduced, which is called pi-freeness. RésuméNous interprétons dans le cadre de la théorie des modules la plupart des notions existantes de commandabilité et d'observabilité dessystèmes linéaires à retards. Ce formalisme permet une comparaison précise de ces notions. Nous introduisons un nouveau type decommandabilité, appelée pi-liberté.
Keywords
Linear delay systems, controllability, observability, modules, polynomial rings, Serre's conjecture, localization.
