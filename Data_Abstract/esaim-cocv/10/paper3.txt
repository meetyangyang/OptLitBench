The pseudo-p-Laplace eigenvalue problem and viscosity solutions as p → ∞
Marino Belloni1 and Bernd Kawohl2;
Abstract
We consider the pseudo-p-Laplacian, an anisotropicversion of the p-Laplacian operator for . We studyrelevant properties of its first eigenfunction for finite p andthe limit problem as p → ∞.
