Monotonicity properties of minimizers and relaxation for autonomous variational problems
Giovanni Cupini1 and Cristina Marcelli2;
Abstract
We consider the following classical autonomous variational problemwhere the Lagrangian f is possibly neither continuous, nor convex, nor coercive.We prove a monotonicity property of the minimizers stating that they satisfy the maximum principle or the minimum one. By virtue of such a property, applying recent results concerning constrained variational problems, we derive a relaxation theorem, the DuBois-Reymond necessary condition and some existenceor non-existence criteria.
