Everywhere regularity for vectorial functionals with general growth
Elvira Mascolo and Anna Paola Migliorini;
Abstract
We prove Lipschitz continuity for localminimizers of integral functionals of the Calculus of Variationsin the vectorial case, where the energy density depends explicitlyon the space variables and has general growth with respect to thegradient. One of the models is with h a convex function with general growth (also exponential behaviouris allowed).
Keywords
Minimizers, regularity, nonstandard growth, exponential growth
