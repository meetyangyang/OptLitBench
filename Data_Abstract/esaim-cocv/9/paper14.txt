The Nonlinearly Damped Oscillator
Juan Luis Vázquez;
Abstract
We study the large-time behaviour of thenonlinear oscillatorwhere m, k>0 and f is a monotone real function representingnonlinear friction. We are interested in understanding thelong-time effect of a nonlinear damping term, with specialattention to the model case    withα real, A>0. We characterize the existence and behaviourof fast orbits, i.e., orbits that stop in finite time.
Keywords
