On the characterization of some classes of proximally smooth sets
Graziano Crasta1 and Ilaria Fragalà Fragalà2;
Abstract
We provide a complete characterization of closed sets with empty interior and positive          reach in R2. As a          consequence, we characterize open bounded domains in R2 whose high ridge and cut locus          agree, and hence C1 planar domains whose normal distance to          the cut locus is constant along the boundary. The latter result extends to convex domains          in Rn.
Keywords
Distance function, proximal smoothness, positive reach, cut locus, central set, skeleton, medial axi
