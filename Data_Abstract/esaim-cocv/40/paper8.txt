Strong unique continuation for the Lamé system with Lipschitz coefficients in three dimensions
Hang Yu;
Abstract
This paper studies the strong unique continuation property for theLamé system of elasticity with variable Lamé coefficientsλ, µ in three dimensions, where λ and μ are Lipschitz continuous and V ∈ L∞. The method is based on the Carleman estimate with polynomial weights for the Lamé operator.
