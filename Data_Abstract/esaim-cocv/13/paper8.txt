Resonance of minimizers for n-level quantum systems with an arbitrary cost
Ugo Boscain1,2 and Grégoire Charlot1;
Abstract
We consider an optimal control problem describing a laser-inducedpopulation transfer on a n-level quantum system. For a convex cost depending only on the moduliof controls (i.e. the lasers intensities),we prove that there always exists a minimizer inresonance. This permits to justifysome strategies used in experimental physics. It is also quite importantbecause it permits to reduce remarkablythe complexity of the problem (and extend some of our previous resultsfor n=2 and n=3): instead of looking for minimizers on thesphere  one is reduced to look just forminimizers on the sphere . Moreover, for the reduced problem, we investigate on the question of existence of strict abnormal minimizer.
