Links between Young measures associated to constrained sequences
Anca-Maria Toader;
Abstract
We give necessary and sufficient conditionswhich characterize the Young measures  associated totwo oscillating sequences of functions, un on  and vn on  satisfying the constraint . Our study is motivated by nonlinear effects induced by homogenization. Techniques based on equimeasurability and rearrangements are employed.
Keywords
Young measures, rearrangements, doubly stochastic operators
