Oscillations and concentrations in sequences of gradients
Agnieszka Kałamajska1 and Martin Kružík2,3;
Abstract
We use DiPerna's and Majda's generalization of Young measures to describe oscillations and concentrations in sequences of gradients, , bounded in  if p > 1 and  is a bounded domain with the extension property in .Our main result is a characterization of those DiPerna-Majda measures which are generated by gradients of Sobolev maps satisfying the same fixed Dirichlet boundary condition. Caseswhere no boundary conditions nor regularity of Ω arerequired and links with lower semicontinuity results by Meyers andby Acerbi and Fusco are also discussed.
