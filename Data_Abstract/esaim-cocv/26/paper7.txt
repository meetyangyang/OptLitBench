Minimizers with topological singularities in two dimensional elasticity
Jonathan Bevan1 and Xiaodong Yan2;
Abstract
For a class of 2-D elastic energies we show that a radial equilibrium solutionis the unique global minimizer in a subclass of all admissible maps. Theboundary constraint is a double cover of S1; the minimizer u is C1and is such that  vanishes at one point.
