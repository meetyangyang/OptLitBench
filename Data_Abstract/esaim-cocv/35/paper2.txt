Projective Reeds-Shepp car on S2 with quadratic cost
Ugo Boscain1,2 and Francesco Rossi2;
Abstract
Fix two points  and two directions (without orientation)  of the velocities in these points. In this paper we are interested to the problem of minimizing the cost   along all smooth curves starting from x with direction η and ending in  with direction . Here g is the standard Riemannian metric on S2 and  is the corresponding geodesic curvature.The interest of this problem comes from mechanics and geometry of vision. It can be formulated as a sub-Riemannian problem on the lens space L(4,1).We compute the global solution for this problem: an interesting feature is that some optimal geodesics present cusps. The cut locus is a stratification with non trivial topology.
