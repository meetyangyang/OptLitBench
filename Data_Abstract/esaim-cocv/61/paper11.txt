Spectral inequality and optimal cost of controllability for the Stokes system
Felipe W. Chaves-Silva and Gilles Lebeau;
Abstract
In this paper we present a new proof of the null controllability property for the Stokes          system. The proof is based on a new spectral inequality for the eigenfunctions of the          Stokes operator. As a consequence, we obtain the cost of the null controllability for the          Stokes system of order eC/T, when            T is small,            i.e., the same order in time as for the heat equation.
Keywords
Stokes system, null controllability, Carleman estimates, spectral inequalit
