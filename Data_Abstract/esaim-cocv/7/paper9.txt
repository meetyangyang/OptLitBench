On a Volume Constrained Variational Problem in SBV²(Ω): Part I
Ana Cristina Barroso1 and José Matias2;
Abstract
We consider the problem of minimizing the energyamong all functions u ∈ SBV²(Ω) for which two level sets  have prescribed Lebesgue measure . Subject to this volume constraintthe existence of minimizers for E(.) is proved and the asymptotic behaviour of the solutions is investigated.
Keywords
Special functions of bounded variation, level sets, 
lower semicontinuity
