An example in the gradient theory of phase transitions
Camillo De Lellis;
Abstract
 We prove by giving an example that when n ≥ 3 the asymptotic behavior of functionalsis quite different with respect to the planar case. In particular weshow that the one-dimensional ansatz due to Aviles and Giga in theplanar case (see [2]) is no longer true in higher dimensions.
