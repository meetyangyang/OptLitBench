Well-posedness and sliding mode control
Tullio Zolezzi;
Abstract
Sliding mode control of ordinary differential equations isconsidered. A key robustness property, called approximability, is studied froman optimization point of view. It is proved that Tikhonov well-posedness of asuitably defined optimization problem is intimately related to approximability.Making use of this link, new approximability criteria are obtained for nonlinear sliding modecontrol systems.
Keywords
Sliding mode control, Tikhonov well-posedness, 
approximability
