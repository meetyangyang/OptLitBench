On the controllability and stabilization of the linearized Benjamin-Ono equation
Felipe Linares1 and Jaime H. Ortega2,3;
Abstract
In this work we are interested in the study of controllability andstabilization of the linearized Benjamin-Ono equation withperiodic boundary conditions, which is a generic model for  thestudy of weakly nonlinear waves with nonlocal dispersion. It iswell known that the  Benjamin-Ono equation has infinite number ofconserved quantities, thus we consider only controls acting in theequation such that the volume of the solution is conserved. Westudy also the stabilization with a feedback law which gives us anexponential decay of the solutions.
Keywords
exact controllability, stabilization, Benjamin-Ono
equation, dispersive equation
