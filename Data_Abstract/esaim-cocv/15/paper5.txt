Monge solutions for discontinuous Hamiltonians
Ariela Briani and Andrea Davini;
Abstract
We consider an Hamilton-Jacobi equation of the form where H(x,p) is assumed Borel measurable and quasi-convex inp. The notion of Monge solution, introduced by Newcomb and Su,is adapted to this setting making use of suitable metric devices.We establish the comparison principle for Monge sub andsupersolution, existence and uniqueness for equation ([see full text])coupled with Dirichlet boundary conditions, and a stability result. Therelation among Monge and Lipschitz subsolutions is also discussed.
