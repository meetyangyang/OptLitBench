Uniqueness of stable Meissner state solutions of the Chern-Simons-Higgs energy
Daniel Spirn1 and Xiaodong Yan2;
Abstract
For external magnetic field hex ≤Cε–α, we provethat a Meissner state solution for the Chern-Simons-Higgs functional exists. Furthermore, if the solutionis stable among all vortexless solutions, then it is unique.
Keywords
Chern-Simons-Higgs theory, superconductivity, uniqueness, Meissner solutio
