Exact Boundary Controllability of a Hybrid System of elasticity by the HUM Method
Bopeng Rao;
Abstract
We consider the exact controllability of a hybridsystem consisting of an elastic beam, clamped at one end and attached at the other end to arigid antenna. Such a system is governed by one partialdifferential equation and two ordinary differential equations. Using theHUM method, we prove that the hybrid system is exactlycontrollable in an arbitrarily short time in the usual energy space.
Keywords
Hybrid system, weak solution, exact controllability, singular control, unique continuation
