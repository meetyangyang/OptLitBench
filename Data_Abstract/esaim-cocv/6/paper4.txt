Relaxation of optimal control problems in Lp-SPACES
Nadir Arada;
Abstract
We consider control problems governed by semilinearparabolic equations with pointwise state constraints and controls in anLp-space (p < ∞). We construct a correct relaxed problem, prove some relaxationresults, and derive necessary optimality conditions.
Keywords
Optimal control problems, relaxation, generalized Young measures, 
stability properties, Pontryagin's principle
