On the Instantaneous Spreading for the Navier–Stokes System in the Whole Space
Lorenzo Brandolese1,2 and Yves Meyer3,2;
Abstract
We consider the spatial behavior of the velocity field u(x, t)of a fluid filling the whole space  () for arbitrarily small values of the time variable.We improve previous results on the spatial spreading by deducing the necessaryconditions  under more general assumptions on thelocalization of u.We also give some new examples of solutions which have a stronger spatial localizationthan in the generic case.
Keywords
Navier–Stokes equations, space-decay, symmetries
