On a variational problem arising in crystallography
Alexander J. Zaslavski;
Abstract
We study a variational problem which was introduced by Hannon,Marcus and Mizel [ESAIM: COCV 9 (2003) 145–149] todescribe step-terraces on surfaces of so-called “unorthodox” crystals.We show that there is no nondegenerate intervals on which the absolutevalue of a minimizer is  identically.
Keywords
Minimizer, surfaces of crystals, unorthodox crystal, 
variational problem
