Null controllability of the heat equation with boundary Fourier conditions: the linear case
Enrique Fernández-Cara1, Manuel González-Burgos1, Sergio Guerrero2 and Jean-Pierre Puel3;
Abstract
In this paper, we prove the global null controllability ofthe linear heat equation completed with linear Fourierboundary conditions of the form. We consider distributed controls with support in a small set andnonregular coefficients . For the proof of null controllability, a crucial tool will be a new Carleman estimate for the weak solutions of the classical heatequation with nonhomogeneous Neumann boundary conditions.
Keywords
Controllability, heat equation, Fourier conditions
