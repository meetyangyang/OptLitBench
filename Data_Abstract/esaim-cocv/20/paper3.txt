An estimation of the controllability time for single-input systems on compact Lie Groups
Andrei Agrachev1 and Thomas Chambrion2;
Abstract
Geometric control theory and Riemannian techniques are used to describe the reachable set at time t of left invariant single-input control systems on semi-simple compact Lie groups and to estimate the minimal time needed to reach any point from identity. This method provides an effective way to give an upper and a lower bound for the minimal time needed to transfer a controlled quantum system with a drift from a given initial position to a given final position.The bounds include diameters of the flag manifolds; the latter arealso explicitly computed in the paper.
Keywords
