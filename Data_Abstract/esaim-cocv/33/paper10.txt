Multibump solutions and asymptotic expansions for mesoscopic Allen-Cahn type equations
Matteo Novaga1 and Enrico Valdinoci2;
Abstract
We consider a mesoscopic model for phase transitions in a periodic mediumand we construct multibump solutions.The rational perturbative case is dealt with by explicitasymptotics.
Keywords
Oscillatory solutions of PDEs, phase transitions, asymptotic expansion
