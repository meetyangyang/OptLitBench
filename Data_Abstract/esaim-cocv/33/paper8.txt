Structure of approximate solutions of variational problems with extended-valued convex integrands
Alexander J. Zaslavski;
Abstract
In this work we study the structure of approximatesolutions of autonomous variational problems with a lowersemicontinuous strictly convex integrand f : Rn×Rn R1 , where Rn is the n-dimensional Euclideanspace. We obtain a full description of the structure of theapproximate solutions which is independent of the length of theinterval, for all sufficiently large intervals.
