Upper bounds for a class of energies containing a non-local term
Arkady Poliakovsky;
Abstract
In this paper we construct upper bounds for families offunctionals of the formwhere Δ  = div { u}. Particular cases of such functionals arise inMicromagnetics. We also use our technique to construct upper boundsfor functionals that appear in a variational formulation ofthe method of vanishing viscosity for conservation laws.
