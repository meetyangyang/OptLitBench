A regularity result for a convex functional and bounds for the singular set
Bruno De Maria;
Abstract
In this paper we prove a regularityresult for local minimizers of functionals of the Calculus of Variations of thetypewhere Ω is a bounded open set in , u ∈ (Ω; ), p > 1, n ≥ 2 and N ≥ 1.We use the technique of difference quotient without the usual assumption on the growth of the second derivatives of the function f. We apply this result to givea bound on the Hausdorff dimension of the singular set of minimizers.
Keywords
Partial regularity, singular sets, fractional differentiability, variational integral
