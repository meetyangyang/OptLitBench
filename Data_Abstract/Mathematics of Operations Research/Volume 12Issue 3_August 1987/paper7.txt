A Posteriori Error Bounds for the Linearly-Constrained Variational Inequality Problem
Jong-Shi Pang;
Abstract
In this paper, we derive a posteriori error bound of an approximate solution to a linearly-constrained variational inequality problem. Three measures of error are used, with each depending on a particular characterization of an exact solution and related to a certain class of iterative methods.
Keywords
error bounds variational inequality complementarity problem iterative methods
