An Axiomatization of the Core of Market Games
Bezalel Peleg;
Abstract
We provide an axiomatization of the core of market games (totally balanced coalitional games). We prove that the core is fully characterized by the following five independent properties: (i) nonemptiness; (ii) individual rationality; (iii) superadditivity; (iv) the weak reduced game property; and (v) the converse reduced game property. Also, we consider a possible alternative characterization.
