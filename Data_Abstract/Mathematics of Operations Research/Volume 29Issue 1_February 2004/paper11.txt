The Mean Number-in-System Vector Range for Multiclass Queueing Networks
B. Curtis Eaves;Peter W. Glynn;Uriel G. Rothblum;
Abstract
In a multiclass network of queues, natural sufficient conditions are given for the mean number-in-system vector to cover the nonnegative orthant as the arrival rate vector varies over the stable domain.
Keywords
