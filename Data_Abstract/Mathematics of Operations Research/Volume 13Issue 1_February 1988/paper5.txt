Convergence of Lagrange Multipliers and Dual Variables for Convex Optimization Problems
Kerry Back;
Abstract
Sufficient conditions are given for the limit of a net of Lagrange multipliers for convex optimization problems to be a Lagrange multiplier for the limit problem. The distinct issue of convergence of solutions to dual problems is also addressed, using a continuity property of the Fenchel transform.
