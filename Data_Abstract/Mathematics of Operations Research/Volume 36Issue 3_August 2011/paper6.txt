Perfect Information Games with Upper Semicontinuous Payoffs
Roger A. Purves;William D. Sudderth;
Abstract
It has been shown that every n-person, perfect information game with no chance moves and bounded, lower semicontinuous payoffs has a subgame perfect ϵ-equilibrium in pure strategies. Here the same is proved when the payoffs are bounded and upper semicontinuous.
