Necessary Conditions for the Bolza Problem
Halina Frankowska;
Abstract
In this paper we provide necessary conditions for the Bolza problem which are obtained by using a generalization of Frechet derivative and by reducing the original problem to a convex problem. The investigation of this convex problem is carried out by approximating the given functions by smooth ones.
Keywords
generalized gradient nonsmooth Bolza problem subdifferential calculus
