Moment-Preserving Shifts and Stochastic Dominance
Peter C. Fishburn;
Abstract
Let Mn be the set of signed measures on the line with nonempty finite supports whose first n − 1 moments vanish and which satisfy a specified positivity condition. Let Mn* be the minimum-support measures in Mn. Then Mn is the convex cone spanned by Mn* if n ≤ 3 but not if n ≥ 4.
