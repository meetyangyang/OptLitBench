On the Existence of Moments for the Busy Period in a Single-Server Queue
S. Foss;A. Sapozhnikov;
Abstract
We obtain sufficient conditions for the finiteness of the moments of the single-server queue busy period in discrete and continuous time. In the cases of power and exponential moments, the conditions found coincide with the known necessary ones.
Keywords
single-server queue busy period moments random walk first passage time
