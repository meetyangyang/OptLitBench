A Light Traffic Approximation for a Single-Server Queue
D. J. Daley;T. Rolski;
Abstract
It is shown that in the GI/G/1 queue, with W(S, T) denoting a stationary waiting time random variable that is determined by the generic service and interarrival times S and T, $$ \lim_{\gamma \rightarrow \infin} EW(S,\gamma T)/E(S - \gamma T)_+ = 1/\mbox{Pr}\{T >0 \} $$ whenever the right-hand side exceeds one. While equality need not necessarily hold when Pr{T > 0} = 1, sufficient conditions are given for the limit result to remain true.
