Sets of Generalized Complementarity Problems and P-Matrices
George J. Habetler;Michael M. Kostreva;
Abstract
The existence and uniqueness of solutions for a set of 2n simultaneous generalized linear complementarity problems (one per orthant of Rn) is studied. If all the complementary points are nondegenerate, each problem has a unique solution if and only if the coefficient matrix M is a P-matrix.
Keywords
linear complementarity problem generalized complementarity problem P-matrix characterization
