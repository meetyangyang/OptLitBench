Measure-Based Values of Nonatomic Games
Dov Monderer;
Abstract
A complete characterization of μ-symmetric linear continuous operators from pNA(μ) to FA is given. This is used to prove the existence of a unique μ-value on pNA(μ), and to deduce the general form of a μ-semivalue and the general form of a degenerate μ-value.
