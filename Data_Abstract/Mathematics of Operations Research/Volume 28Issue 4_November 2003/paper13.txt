Note On: N. E. Aguilera, M. S. Escalante, G. L. Nasini, “A Generalization of the Perfect Graph Theorem Under the Disjunctive Index”
A. M. H. Gerards;Gábor Maróti;Alexander Schrijver;
Abstract
We give short elementary proofs of two results by N. E. Aguilera, M. S. Escalante, and G. L. Nasini on the disjunctive index of the clique relaxation of the stable set polytope.
Keywords
Perfect graphs clique relaxation disjunctive procedure antiblocker duality
