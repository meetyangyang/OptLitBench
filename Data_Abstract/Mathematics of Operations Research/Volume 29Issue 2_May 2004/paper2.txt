Error Bound Moduli for Conic Convex Systems on Banach Spaces
Xi Yin Zheng;Kung Fu Ng;
Abstract
We give two explicit formulas which express the error bound moduli for conic convex systems, one in terms of directional derivative, and the other in terms of the coderivative. As applications, we study error bounds for systems of infinitely many convex inequalities.
Keywords
