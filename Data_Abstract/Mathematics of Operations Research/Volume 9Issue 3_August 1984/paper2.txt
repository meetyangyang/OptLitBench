Axioms and Examples Related to Ordinal Dynamic Programming
Charles E. Blair;
Abstract
This note is intended as a continuation of Sobel's work on ordinal dynamic programming (Sobel, M. J. 1975. Ordinal dynamic programming. Management Sci.21 967–975.). We generalize several results of that paper. Also, some examples are given to clarify differences between the treatment of ordinal dynamic programming in (Sobel, M. J. 1975. Ordinal dynamic programming. Management Sci.21 967–975.) and Sobel's later treatment (Sobel, M. J. 1980. Ordinal sequential games. Econom. et SocietiesXIV 1571–1581.).
