Sensitivity Analysis for Stochastic Scheduling Problems
K. D. Glazebrook;
Abstract
We seek to evaluate the effect which departures from assumed parameter values have upon the performance of scheduling strategies. Most of the material is presented in terms of a single machine scheduling model, denoted {(Rj, ρj, α), 1 ≤ j ≤ N}. The theoretical basis of the sensitivity analysis is developed from previous work by the author and Gittins.
Keywords
