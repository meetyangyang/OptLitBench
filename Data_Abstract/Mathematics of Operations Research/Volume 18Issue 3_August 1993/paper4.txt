A Bound on the Proportion of Pure Strategy Equilibria in Generic Games
Faruk Gül;David Pearce;Ennio Stacchetti;
Abstract
In a generic finite normal form game with 2α + 1 Nash equilibria, at least α of the equilibria are nondegenerate mixed strategy equilibria (that is, they involve randomization by some players).
