Inequalities for Bin Packing—II
Wansoo T. Rhee;
Abstract
Consider the minimum number Bn of unit size bins needed to pack items X1, …, Xn that are independently distributed according to a given distribution μ concentrated on [0, 1]. We establish a sharp inequality for the deviation of Bn from its expectation.
Keywords
stochastic bin packing tail deviation Poisson point processes
