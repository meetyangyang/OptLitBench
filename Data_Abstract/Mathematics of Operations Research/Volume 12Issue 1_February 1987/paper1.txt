The A-Forest Iteration Method for the Stochastic Generalized Transportation Problem
Liqun Qi;
Abstract
The stochastic generalized transportation problem (SGTP) is a stochastic program with simple recourse where the master problem is a generalized transportation problem. The graph corresponding to an optimal solution of an SGTP can be characterized as an A-forest each of its connected subgraphs is either a tree or a one-loop tree. We propose here a finitely convergent method to solve the SGTP. The iteration techniques constitute some modifications of those for the forest iteration method for solving the stochastic transportation problem, which was given in a previous paper. A small numerical example is also presented.
