On Harris Recurrence in Continuous Time
Haya Kaspi;Avi Mandelbaum;
Abstract
We show that a continuous-time Markov process X is Harris recurrent if and only if there exists a nonzero α-finite measure ν on its state space such that X surely hits sets with positive ν-measure. This simple criterion is applied to some nonparametric closed queueing networks.
Keywords
Markov processes which are Harris recurrent Borel right piecewise-deterministic nonparametric or generalized Jackson queueing networks
