Three-Player Absorbing Games
Eilon Solan;
Abstract
An n-player absorbing game is an n-player stochastic game where all the states but one are absorbing (a state is absorbing if once it is reached, the probability to leave it is zero, whatever the players play).We prove that every three-player absorbing game has an undiscounted equilibrium payoff.
Keywords
