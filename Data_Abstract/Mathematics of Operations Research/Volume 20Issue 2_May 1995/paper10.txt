Generalized-Quasi-Variational Inequality Problems with Discontinuous Mappings
Jen-Chih Yao;
Abstract
We derive several existence results for generalized quasi-variational inequality problems where the mappings under consideration need not have any continuity properties. As applications, we obtained several existence results for equilibrium problems involving abstract economies.
Keywords
generalized quasi-variational inequalities point-to-set mappings minimax inequalities abstract economy
