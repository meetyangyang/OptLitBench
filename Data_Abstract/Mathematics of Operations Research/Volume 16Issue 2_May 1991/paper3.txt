Small Travelling Salesman Polytopes
Sylvia C. Boyd;William H. Cunningham;
Abstract
We introduce a new class of valid inequalities for the polytope of the symmetric travelling salesman problem. We also gave complete characterizations of the polytope for 6 and 7 cities. For the latter case, the new inequalities are needed. These results are related to work of R. Z. Norman in the 1950's.
