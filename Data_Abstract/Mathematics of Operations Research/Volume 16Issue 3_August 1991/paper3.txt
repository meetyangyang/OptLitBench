On the Fluctuations of the Stochastic Traveling Salesperson Problem
Wansoo T. Rhee;
Abstract
Consider n points X1, …, Xn independently and uniformly distributed on the unit square [0, 1]2. Denote by Tn the length of the shortest tour through X1, …, Xn. We prove that for some universal constant K, we have P(|Tn − E(Tn)| ≥ t) ≥ K−1 exp(−t2K) whenever t ≤ K−1n1/2.
