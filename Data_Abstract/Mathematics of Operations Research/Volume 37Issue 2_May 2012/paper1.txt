In Memoriam

Abstract
INFORMS is saddened to announce that Editor-in-Chief Uriel G. Rothblum passed away on March 26, 2012, after a sudden illness. He was an important scholar and dedicated leader of the INFORMS journal Mathematics of Operations Research. Our thoughts are with his family.  An obituary on Professor Rothblum can be found at http://mor.pubs.inform.org.J. G. “Jim” Dai, MOR Area Editor for Stochastic Models, will serve as Interim Editor-in-Chief through the end of 2012.
