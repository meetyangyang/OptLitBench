Erratum
E. J. Anderson;P. Nash;A. B. Philpott;
Abstract
In (Anderson, E. J., P. Nash, A. B. Philpott. 1982. A class of continuous network flow problems. Math. Oper. Res.7 501–514, §5), we state two corollaries to a continuous maximum flow-minimum cut theorem. The second corollary is incorrect, owing to an implicit assumption that the optimal cut is unique.
